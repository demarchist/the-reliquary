Lady Faye is a noblewoman in Baldur's Gate who serves as [[Aliyah Faen Tlabbar|Patches]]' benefactor and employer. She commands a network of champions — including Patches and the Moon Elf warrior [[Zhink]] — and maintains connections throughout the city's upper society.

## The Vault Job

Lady Faye hired the Spellforged to infiltrate Lord Fendrel's vault as a test of its security system, presenting the mission through her associate Lord Hufflebram. The job was accessed through a masquerade party at the lord's manor. However, the entire operation turned out to be a [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] trap — the deep dwarf [[veygar|Veygar]] had been impersonating Lord Faendral, and both Lady Faye and Patches were captured and held as bait in the deepest chamber of the ancient ruins beneath the estate.

The group fought through the ruins, battled Veygar's forces, and ultimately rescued both Lady Faye and Patches. After returning to [[baldurs-gate|Baldur's Gate]], Lady Faye paid the group seventy-five hundred gold for her rescue and the amethyst from the Stone Golem. She sent [[zhink|Zhink]] to accompany the group while Patches ran one last mission for her mistress.

## Champions

Lady Faye employs champions to carry out her interests. Patches serves as one such champion, and [[zhink|Zhink]] — an eccentric Moon Elf warrior — serves as another. At the Council of Helm's Hold, Lady Faye appeared with [[lemerlais|Lemerlais]] on the outskirts. She reported that an agent had a lead on another fragment of [[the-last-word|The Last Word]] in the [[underdark|Underdark]] — the agent would meet the party in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]], the city of the Duergar.
