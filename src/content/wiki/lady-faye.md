Lady Faye is a noblewoman in Baldur's Gate who serves as Patches' benefactor and employer. She tasks the party with infiltrating Lord Fendrel's vault, but is later captured by the Duergar. Her connections in Baldur's Gate's upper society make her a useful but sometimes demanding patron.
