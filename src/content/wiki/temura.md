Temura is a high-elven Cleric of [[selune|Selune]] who arrived at the [[fane-of-selune|Fane of Selune]] with three human acolytes — Yuri, Clemence, and Gilga — to officially bring [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus']] temple into the wider religion. Bronze-skinned with bleached blonde hair, Temura has a sharp, predatory smile that never quite reaches his eyes and an eagerness to please that borders on insistence. He pushed [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] for a proper sermon and reading from the Astronomican, the holy book of [[selune|Selune]], and voiced his disapproval when the warlock strayed from the texts.
