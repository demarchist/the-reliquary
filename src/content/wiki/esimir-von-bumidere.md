Esimir Von Bumidere is the patriarch of the Bumidere family and father of Horatio, Ernst, Kurt, and Emma. A red-haired man greying at the temples with a strong build developing a bit of a belly, he walks with a darkwood cane and bears a striking resemblance to his son Buumi. He met his wife Sera through the Harpers in Waterdeep.

A political figure in Neverwinter, Esimir was present at the dinner with Lord Neverember and Mordenkainen and was tasked with preparing the family estate to host the Amnian delegation. His relationship with Buumi is formal — they exchange nods rather than embraces. He recognised the Thayan Drop Box by description and noted that the Red Wizards of Thay had been invited to the Council but never responded.
