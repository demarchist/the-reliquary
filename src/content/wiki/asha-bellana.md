Asha Bellana, called the Sage of the Swamp, is an Ancient Green Dragon from the hidden Empire of Shou Lung and a member of the Dragonflight. Her humanoid form appears as a human woman with green highlights.

She has a marked affinity for natural magic and nesting instincts — upon encountering the walled Rock Gnome city, she immediately began selecting a tower to mend into a lair alongside Kalphena, engaging the locals in enthusiastic conversation about architecture. Her dreams, while the group slept in an abandoned Blue Dragon lair, produced a surge of plant growth that supercharged The Black Arrow's oasis. She also shielded a mountainside vale from encroaching lava when Vakuun's mysterious nocturnal visitor triggered a volcanic eruption.

Asha was among those who slipped into a local Green Dragon's lair to steal treasure and eggs while the other dragons kept the host occupied.
