Magistrate Delroy is a [[drow-faction|Drow]] magistrate in [[dokkar|Dokkar]], the gleaming city of [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]] in [[svartalfheim|Svartalfheim]]. His skin is plum-coloured, his eyes vibrant pink, and his hair tinged blue. He described himself as a long, long dead Elf, and as a magistrate holds no clan affiliation.

When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] and [[finn|Finn]] arrived in Dokkar, Delroy confronted them about carrying the Curse of Wendonai — the taint borne by the [[lolth|Lolthsworn]] [[drow-faction|Drow]] of [[toril|Toril]]. After [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] summoned [[eclipse|Eclipse]] to prove her devotion to [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]], Delroy took both under his personal protection. He later ruled that [[finn|Finn]] could remain in the city while [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] was tasked with ridding herself of the curse — and advised her to look to history from before the Crown Wars.
