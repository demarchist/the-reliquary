## Background

The Goblin King is the ruler of Goblin Town, a sprawling settlement in the Bog beneath the Tavern of Doors. He is a cunning and dangerous entity who feeds on the minds of those who wander into his domain.

## Role in the Campaign

The Goblin King becomes a major antagonist when he consumes JmJm's mind, seeking to use the bard's body as a vessel to gain access to the prime material plane. This threat prompts the Harper agent Lorde to intervene and drives the party's adventures through Goblin Town and the Bog.

## Notable Traits

- Possesses the ability to consume and inhabit minds
- Rules over Goblin Town with absolute authority
- Seeks escape from his planar imprisonment through JmJm's body
