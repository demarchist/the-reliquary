Lady Isabel is an Adult Copper Dragon who has lived for years in [[helioglabulus|Helios]] under the guise of a wealthy and respected human merchant and socialite. To the city — and to the [[crimson-dawn-knights|Crimson Dawn Knight]] [[ishin|Ishin]], who recognised her at distance through a spyglass — she is a familiar figure of the capital's commercial upper society: voluptuous, sturdy, beautifully dressed, and well-mannered enough to move through noble salons without comment.

## Revelation

The party encountered her on the slopes above the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]], sprinting barefoot down the mountainside in a tattered low-cut evening gown, covered in bruises and small cuts, with part of her gleaming, curled hair caked dark with blood. She carried a small, foreign-looking [[gnome-of-the-vale|Gnome]] in one hand by the back of her clothing, draped limp like a suitcase. The Gnome bore two black-edged puncture wounds on her neck — a vampire bite.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] tried to intercept her in disguise. Isabel hurled herself sideways at sprint speed to avoid trampling the rogue, struck a substantial tree trunk shoulder-first, splintered it, knocked it down, and was on her feet again before she had finished rolling. She turned on the disguised drow with a glare that was about to become a shout — then sniffed the air, paused, and identified Patches by *scent*.

"You've been in my shop," she said. She said nothing more about which shop.

She and Patches sprinted together to the estate carrying the dying Gnome. As the Ashen White Dragons and the Vampire Knight closed on her position, she handed the Gnome to [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] for delivery to the chapel, refused Patches' offer of cover, and then — in a ripple of warm copper that ran up her body — shifted into her true Adult Copper Dragon form. Her first action in the open was a Slow Breath blast that took two of the Ashen Whites mid-stoop and brought their dive down in a fall.

## The Shop

Patches has visited Isabel's shop in [[helioglabulus|Helios]] at some point during the group's time in the city. Isabel recognised her by scent through a human disguise on a mountainside, which suggests either an unusually attentive proprietor or an unusually distinctive customer — possibly both. The shop's name, location, and inventory have not yet been disclosed.

## Connection to the Wounded Gnome

That an Adult Copper Dragon was carrying a wounded, vampire-bitten Gnome down a mountain at sprint speed — in a torn evening gown, through hostile dragon territory, to *hallowed ground* — suggests a relationship that is not merely commercial. Whoever the Gnome actually is, Isabel risked exposure of her true form to save her life. [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]]'s comment after laying on hands — that the strength required to heal the small body was *not the strength he would need to heal a Gnome* — only deepens the question.
