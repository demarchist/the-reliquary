Lady Isabel is an Adult Copper Dragon — Izmakella by her true name — who has lived for years in [[helioglabulus|Helios]] under the guise of a wealthy and respected human merchant and socialite. To the city — and to the [[crimson-dawn-knights|Crimson Dawn Knight]] [[ishin|Ishin]], who recognised her at distance through a spyglass — she is a familiar figure of the capital's commercial upper society: voluptuous, sturdy, beautifully dressed, and well-mannered enough to move through noble salons without comment.

## Revelation

The party encountered her on the slopes above the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]], sprinting barefoot down the mountainside in a tattered low-cut evening gown, covered in bruises and small cuts, with part of her gleaming, curled hair caked dark with blood. She carried a small, foreign-looking [[gnome-of-the-vale|Gnome]] in one hand by the back of her clothing, draped limp like a suitcase. The Gnome bore two black-edged puncture wounds on her neck — a vampire bite.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] tried to intercept her in disguise. Isabel hurled herself sideways at sprint speed to avoid trampling the rogue, struck a substantial tree trunk shoulder-first, splintered it, knocked it down, and was on her feet again before she had finished rolling. She turned on the disguised drow with a glare that was about to become a shout — then sniffed the air, paused, and identified Patches by *scent*.

"You've been in my shop," she said. She said nothing more about which shop.

She and Patches sprinted together to the estate carrying the dying Gnome. As the Ashen White Dragons and the Vampire Knight closed on her position, she handed the Gnome to [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] for delivery to the chapel, refused Patches' offer of cover, and then — in a ripple of warm copper that ran up her body — shifted into her true Adult Copper Dragon form. Her first action in the open was a Slow Breath blast that took two of the Ashen Whites mid-stoop and brought their dive down in a fall.

## In the Battle

In dragon-form Izmakella was the centre of the engagement on the valley floor. She tore the smaller [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen White]] wyrmlings apart in close combat — three of them within a minute, the fourth slowed out of the air by a second column of her breath — and then turned her gaze on the two surviving adults. A second sweep of Slow Breath rolled up the slope as the [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Vampire Knight]] broke free of [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s force cage and called the retreat. The two adults peeled away under her breath and limped for the ridge, one of them already bleeding heavily from [[cassian|Cassian]]'s arrows.

## The Sister's Game

After the battle, [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] approached the Copper on his own lawn. Isabel shifted back to her human shape mid-conversation — startling the lord, who had known the merchant Lady Isabel for years and had not known. She passed off the disguise with the brittle ease of a socialite caught in a small embarrassment: *a game my sister and I have been playing for some time*. The party recognised the sister at once. The old woman with the slitted catlike pupils running the going-out-of-business sale in the merchant ring — [[tazmikella|Tazmikella]] — was the other Copper. Two adult dragons had been quietly resident in the capital the entire time.

## Bahamut's Call

In the chapel, while the bitten Gnome lay between them, Isabel filled in the rest of her week. She had been called to action by [[bahamut|Bahamut]] in the same hours that [[vulkan|Vulkan]] and [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] had been pursuing the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]] from their [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] above ruined [[palischuk|Palischuk]]. She had answered the call and joined with [[protanther|Protanther]], the Ancient Gold Wyrm who tended the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree of Bahamut]], at the rite that was supposed to be the Dragonflight's ascension. She had fought at his side.

She had watched Protanther fall before Vulkan.

She and [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo]] had got away. She did not know what had become of the other Shou Lung dragons, though she believed most had scattered alive. The Vampire Knight's flight had cut Azoo off from her route to the Tree and harried her south through the foothills for days, and Isabel had ended up running through the trees above Frostmoore in a torn evening gown with her ward over one shoulder, looking for hallowed ground.

## The Resurrection

When the party proposed killing the [[gnome-of-the-vale|Gnome]] cleanly on the chapel stone in order to break the bite, Izmakella refused — once, twice, three times. It was [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] who turned her, with a gentle account of the ritual and the [[anduriel|Planetar]] whose body had given the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] its grace. She agreed. The Gnome was killed by a knife and resurrected by [[selune|Selûne]]'s power into the brilliant adult body of [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] — and Isabel, watching her ward breathe again, rested one bruised hand on the Copper's foreleg and let out the breath of someone who had been holding it for days.

## The Shop

Patches has visited Isabel's shop in [[helioglabulus|Helios]] at some point during the group's time in the city. Isabel recognised her by scent through a human disguise on a mountainside, which suggests either an unusually attentive proprietor or an unusually distinctive customer — possibly both. The shop's name, location, and inventory have not yet been disclosed.
