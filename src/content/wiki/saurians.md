The Saurians were an ancient and technologically advanced race that once dominated the world of Al Askar. They possessed capabilities far beyond what other civilizations could achieve, including the construction of the Ringed Veils (fargates) that enabled interplanar travel, the creation of Firelance weapons, and the mining of spell-infusable crystals. Murals depict them as humanoid-reptilian beings from a planet with two suns, and their ruins are scattered across Al Askar.

## The Phaerimm War

The Saurians were destroyed approximately a thousand years before the party's arrival, breaking their world in the process and unleashing the chaos that entangled magic and sorcery ever since. Their downfall was tied to a devastating war against the Phaerimm — ancient aberrations capable of consuming and rerouting magic. The conflict spanned multiple worlds, with the Saurians encountering their enemy even in Faerûn.

In the frozen ruins of Thueba Swamp, the party witnessed ghostly echoes of the war's final days. Saurian and elven soldiers — wearing armour of gleaming metal adorned with feathers, wielding firelances and curving swords — fought desperately against the Phaerimm invaders. When conventional forces failed, an elven mage named Avitus summoned an Aldjinn, a celestial of ice and radiance, to turn the tide. But the Phaerimm adapted, and a desperate second summoning — a shadow spirit — went catastrophically wrong, imprisoning the celestial and condemning every soul in the fortress to a thousand years of undeath.

## Legacy

The ancient Saurian engineer Hasrala, preserved as a soul in a phylactery crystal, provided the party with crucial information and combination keys to access Saurian technology. Their Machina Factory in Thueba Swamp remained partially functional, containing a database of Ringed Veil coordinates and manufacturing capabilities for machina bodies.
