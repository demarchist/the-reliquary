The Bluelake District is the affluent quarter of Neverwinter, situated north of the city centre past the keep. Its cobbled streets are lined with fine houses, upscale merchant establishments, and the best inns in the city. Guards patrol the area and are known to be unfriendly toward common folk who wander in uninvited — wagon drivers refuse to enter for fear of being roughed up.

The Bumidere family estate is located in the district, set behind wrought-iron gates with a long paved drive leading to the red-brick manor.
