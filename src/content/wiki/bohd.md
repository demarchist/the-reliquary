Bohd is the captain of Bohd's Band, a mercenary company hired by Silverymoon to shore up the defences of the Barony of Thunder Bluff. A half-orc with a mane of crimson hair, red and gold armour, and a massive battleaxe, he is competent, boisterous, and full of bravado.

He rides a blood-drinker wyvern named Kim, which he houses in a makeshift aviary atop the Temple of Helm. He can extract venom from Kim on request. When Patches tested him in the arena, he proved good — able to glance her repeatedly and learn from her trip attacks — but ultimately no match for any of the Heralds. He was deemed a solid addition to the Faerûn Defense Force.
