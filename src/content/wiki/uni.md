Uni is the celestial mount of the ranger [[cassian|Cassian]] — a silver-coated winged unicorn with a single spiraling horn at her brow, luminous and proud, who bears him into battle and travels at his side. When Cassian first introduced her to the group, [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] chuckled that she certainly was *Unique*; the name stuck, and the rest of the party took it for what it was.

## Temperament

Uni carries herself with the fastidious dignity of a creature of the upper planes. She is wary of strangers — she snorted and shuffled away when [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] first reached out to pet her — and warms only at gestures she approves of, such as the dot of moonlight [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] once played for her off the mirror-disc of [[eclipse|Eclipse]]. Her celestial nature puts her in open, mutual dislike of the party's fiendish mounts; on the road to [[helioglabulus|Helios]] she kept as far from [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s nightmare [[steden|Steden]] as the road would allow, and the two traded withering looks the whole way. Among the procession of wartigers, mechanical lions, owlbears, and spectral steeds the group rides, Uni stands out as the one unmistakably *holy* presence.

## In the Field

Uni is a genuine warhorse for all her grace. At the relief of the Helios road she carried Cassian into a charge against an enemy commander, letting the ranger knock the man from his mount and drive him into cover. She does not shy from a fight, but she takes hurts like any mount — and Cassian tends her with more care than he gives himself.

After the dusk battle at the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]], Cassian spent the quiet hours of the night in the stable yard seeing to her wounds, having the [[crimson-dawn-knights|paladins]] lay their healing hands on her flanks until the worst had closed, and then brushing her down in the lamplight with long, unhurried strokes while she leaned into the brush. It was one of the few peaceful moments of a night that did not stay peaceful — the [[the-thayans-attack|Thayan attack]] fell on the estate before it was through.
