MCAS Mythalmar — Mythalmar Combat Arcane Station — is the Alliance's top-secret military research facility, hidden in a sheltered mountain valley near [[Silverymoon]]. It serves as the headquarters of [[Project STARFALL]] and the development and training centre for the [[r-14-thunderchaser|R-14 Thunderchaser]] programme.

## Cover and Access

The facility's surface presence is a seemingly abandoned watchtower with a weathered stone exterior and a deliberately neglected appearance. Staffed by silent figures in grey robes, the receiving room gives no indication of the complex beneath. A hidden lift disguised as a wine cellar descends through the mountain on enchanted rails, passing through multiple security wards — each triggering different magical screening effects — on a twenty-minute journey into the heart of the installation.

## The Complex

The valley itself is protected by permanent illusion magic and anti-detection wards. Multiple runways are carved into the valley floor, with magical landing arrays and navigation beacons supporting flight operations. The architecture blends dwarven engineering with elven arcane design — a physical expression of the partnership between [[rockseeker-manufactory|Rockseeker Corporation]] and the University of Silverymoon that built it.

### The Thunderdome

The main hangar is a massive structure built into the mountain face, with three-storey-high doors, multiple maintenance bays, Rockseeker Corporation workshop areas, magical lift systems, and observation galleries. It was here that the [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]] first laid eyes on the R-14 Thunderchasers — revealed behind rising blast shutters during [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s initial [[project-starfall|STARFALL]] briefing.

### The Silver Flagon

The unofficial pilot bar, built into the upper mountain level with an observation deck overlooking the valley. A trophy wall displays unit memorabilia, and the establishment is known for experimental cocktails. It was here that [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] and [[gustavo|Gustavo]] led the entire canteen in a rendition of "You've Lost That Lovin' Feelin'" — cementing their status as the base's resident troublemakers.

### Training Airspace

Secured airspace extends over the [[moonwood|Moonwood]] and the River Rauvin, with emergency landing fields, weather-controlled training zones, and combat practice areas. A control tower extends from the mountain face, housing magical communication arrays, flight path monitoring, and the emergency response centre from which [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] watched the training sorties unfold.
