## Background

S'Olaa is Cirrus al-Kayan's brother and the head of the Al'Kayan clan. Unlike Cirrus, S'Olaa has aligned himself with Amnian interests, working as their agent in operations against Silverymoon.

## Role in the Campaign

S'Olaa's betrayal of familial bonds creates a personal conflict for Cirrus during the Amnian invasion of Silverymoon. His involvement with the enemy forces adds emotional stakes to the battle for the city.

## Notable Traits

- Head of the Al'Kayan clan
- Amnian agent working against Silverymoon
- Source of personal conflict for his brother Cirrus
