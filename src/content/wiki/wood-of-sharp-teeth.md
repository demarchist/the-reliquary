The Wood of Sharp Teeth is a dark forest south of [[baldurs-gate|Baldur's Gate]], skirting the Chionthar River route toward [[scornubel|Scornubel]]. Goblin tribes inhabit its borders, and the forest grows increasingly twisted and corrupted toward its interior.

The Gorgon's Maw — a cavern deep within the wood, hidden behind enchantments — served as the lair of a Hag coven comprising two Night Hags, an Annis Hag, and a Feral Green Hag, along with a Marilith. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] had originally intended to lure the group there to be killed during his time serving [[ceregorach|Ceregorach]]. When the group eventually returned to cleanse it, they destroyed the coven, freed imprisoned children, and collapsed the cavern.
