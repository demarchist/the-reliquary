Sera Von Bumidere is the matriarch of the Bumidere family and mother to Horatio, Ernst, Kurt, and Emma. A woman in her late fifties, she presents the perfect image of Neverwinter nobility — rolling curls piled high, gowns of silk decorated with gemstones and pearls. The image is partly literal: Sera uses illusion magic to conceal leggings, boots, and a short sword strapped to one thigh beneath the illusory folds of her dress.

In her youth, Sera adventured with the Harpers out of Waterdeep, where she met her husband Esimir. She retains her edge — greeting her long-lost son with joy, but noting his missing arm with a dry "Really?" before being delighted by his prosthetic. She runs the estate with warmth and authority, personally lighting the warded fireplaces with a casual gesture and hosting guests with equal parts grace and efficiency.
