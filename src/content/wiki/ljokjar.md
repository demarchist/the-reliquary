Ljokjar is a huge Frost Giant distinguished by his light blue hair and handlebar moustache. A member of Meljir's family group, he was travelling to Shatterpeak for his own wedding — a feast celebrating his marriage to the Skuldir Tribe's new matriarch.

The party first encountered Ljokjar during the gnoll ambush on the frozen lake, where he fought alongside his kin. At the wedding feast, he was seen drinking with the aggressive giant Suttung and his entourage, enjoying his own celebration while the small folk competed in the giants' challenges.
