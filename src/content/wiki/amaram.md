## Background

Amaram is the local archmage of Baldur's Gate, a scholar and practitioner of considerable magical power who maintains connections with the Mage's Guild.

## Role in the Campaign

When the party arrives in Baldur's Gate during the undead mist crisis, Amaram provides crucial research into the nature of the necromantic mists and their connection to Orcus. His findings help the party understand the broader scope of the threat they face.

## Notable Traits

- Accomplished researcher with expertise in necromantic phenomena
- Well-connected within Baldur's Gate's magical community
- Provides vital intelligence about the mist curse and its origins
