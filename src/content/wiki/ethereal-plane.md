The Ethereal Plane is a misty, fog-filled dimension that exists as a border between the Material Plane and the Inner Planes. Creatures on the Ethereal Plane can see into the Material Plane as ghostly, translucent images, and vice versa under certain magical conditions. The plane serves as a conduit for travel between dimensions and is home to ghosts, phase spiders, and other creatures that exist between states of reality.

## Tactical Use

[[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] made the Ethereal Plane a regular part of the party's tactical repertoire through his Nightmare mount [[steden|Steden]], whose planar-shifting ability allowed the group to move between the Material and Ethereal Planes at will. During the [[battle-of-the-tar-pits|Battle of the Tar Pits]], JmJm led the ambush by galloping from the Ethereal astride Steden with [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]], catching the halfling cultists completely off guard.

## The Battle Against Hohonu

The Ethereal Plane played its most dramatic role during the battle against Hohonu in [[al-askar|Al Askar]]. JmJm and [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] witnessed the ancient dragon's prison erupting in the Ethereal as the Great Wyrm attempted to pull itself free. Tark bravely entered the Ethereal to grab the soul of the fallen [[camea|Camea]], storing it in a waterskin while swimming through a cloud of specters near the Death Wyrm. The plane's overlap with reality allowed Hohonu to project his power through the weakened veil, striking at the party even from within his prison. Cirrus rode Steden into the vortex of the prison to pull Tark from the Death Wyrm's clutches.
