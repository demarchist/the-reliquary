## Description

Narhan is the god of Knowledge in Al Askar's pantheon of Ancients. He appeared to Feanon in the Ethereal visions of the Temple of Karthus, taking the form of Theodemir, Feanon's old teacher.

## Nature

To Narhan, all knowledge holds precisely equal value. He is as likely to expound on the nutritional content of grilled squid as he is to reveal the secrets of the Calamity. This makes extracting useful information from him an exercise in patience.

## Revelations

Despite his eccentricities, Narhan revealed a critical truth: Zalaam, the god of death for this world, is an imposter — not the true deity of death but something else wearing that mantle. He also directed Feanon to seek the Isle of the Elves in the northwest to continue his search for knowledge and aid in healing the world.
