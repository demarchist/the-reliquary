Emma Von Bumidere is Horatio's youngest sister — no more than fourteen years old, with orange hair, cream skin, and freckles. She was starstruck upon meeting Patches, breathlessly asking "are you her?" and gasping in delight when the rogue briefly dropped her moon-elf disguise to reveal her true Drow features. Emma was seen during Val Tengu's sparring lessons with a bow across her lap, suggesting she is being trained in arms alongside the other children of the estate.
