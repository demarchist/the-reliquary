The Establishment is a bustling underworld commercial complex located at a crossroads within the Barony of Thunder Bluff. It is owned and operated by [[Aliyah Faen Tlabbar|Patches]], who uses it both as a business venture and as a way to cement her network of connections among the shady underbelly of the region.

## Exterior

A nondescript building at a crossroads, its sign reading "The Establishment" in faded, peeling paint. Nothing about the exterior invites scrutiny. The entrance is a heavy oak door.

## Layout

The Establishment is a conglomeration of inn, brothel, and casino. Inside, dim lighting and thick curtains create an atmosphere of seedy luxury. The main floor is a bustling space where Aliyah slides through the crowd nodding at patrons and employees as if greeting an extended family. Secluded booths line the walls, each shielded from view by heavy curtains and lit dimly enough to allow for genuine privacy — the kind of privacy where serious conversations happen.

A basement runs beneath the main floor, accessible to regulars and used for purposes that are not advertised. A staircase of rickety steps at the back of the room leads to upper floors of private rooms, denied to most patrons. Aliyah maintains her own room on these upper floors, which serves as both quarters and office.

## Culture

The Establishment is a popular gathering place for members of the criminal underworld — a place where deals are made, secrets are traded, and alliances are formed. The smell of pipe smoke, strong liquor, and perfume is omnipresent. Laughter and soft music fill the air during busy hours. Aliyah enforces a strict code of conduct and does not tolerate unauthorized violence within its walls, which gives the place a peculiar reputation: deeply dangerous by association, but a safer venue for difficult conversations than almost anywhere else in the Barony.

The [[Faerûn Defense Force]] uses The Establishment regularly as a base of operations for sensitive missions.
