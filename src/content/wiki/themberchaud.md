Themberchaud the Grand is an Ancient Red Dragon and counterpart to Protanther. Together they were tasked with planeshifting the Dragons of Shou Lung to their new lairs via the Great Tree of Bahamut in Bloodstone City.

When the Dragonflight arrived, Themberchaud was nowhere to be seen — and Protanther's attempt to scry on him failed. His Duergar attendant Gartokkar Brutt noted with alarm that the volcano to the north was likely his master's lair, and that a Red Dragon of Themberchaud's power would only allow his volcanic lair to erupt in the most dire of circumstances.

The Dragonflight flew north to investigate, finding the entrance to Themberchaud's lair guarded by two giant Oni standing over a blood-soaked sack.
