Khelon is a retired operator of the 3rd Special Actions Detachment, the Silverymoon military unit known as the Mindslayers. The tallest of the three veterans — lean and angular, with sharp cheekbones, pale eyes that track movement with predatory precision, and a thin scar from his left ear to his jaw. His bearing is the most obviously military, his posture parade-ground straight even while playing cards.

Khelon served as the unit's strategist and intelligence specialist. He briefed the party on Illithid tactics, biology, and weaknesses, and recognised that Fëanon's psychic link to Nostalgia for Infinity could be used as camouflage — a technique the Mindslayers had theorised about but never had anyone capable of executing. He worked with Fëanon on the psychic camouflage technique through hours of meditation and concentration exercises.

He told Patches she would have made an excellent Mindslayer. She told him she did not work well with authority. He said that was also a Mindslayer trait.

During the descent, Khelon served as marksman, putting crossbow bolts into every drone that moved with mechanical precision. He settled in Gashaus after the mission with Garm, Halar, and Alura.
