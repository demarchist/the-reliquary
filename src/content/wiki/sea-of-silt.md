The Sea of Silt is a vast expanse of gleaming sand dunes on the far southern coast of Al Askar, thousands of miles southwest of Iyum. Beneath its surface, the Obsidian Order maintains a hidden encampment — a network of artificial caverns grown from twisting, rootlike basalt, accessed by burrowing Khonsfa beetles that vibrate through the sand to carry passengers underground.

The encampment serves as the Order's forward base in their war against Vecna, positioned near the subterranean passages that lead down through Saurian ruins and ancient mines to the Disphotic Forest and ultimately to the Font of Fawden. The party reached the Sea of Silt via Doc's Wind Walk spell, covering the enormous distance in a few hours.
