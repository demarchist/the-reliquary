The Shattered Spire is one of [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]]'s most notable establishments — a multi-story feast hall, drinking house, and fighting pit connected to the city by a massive wooden rope bridge wide enough for Stone Giants to cross. It sits apart from the main body of the city, its scale and noise a contrast to the dour efficiency that characterizes Duergar industry.

The interior is built for all sizes. The first two floors are scaled up for Stone Giants, with furnishings and a dumbwaiter system designed to service creatures many times the height of a Duergar. The upper three floors return to regular scale, connected by the same elaborate system of lifts and scroll-carrying mechanisms that handles food and drink with surprising efficiency. A pair of massive wooden double doors on the main floor opens into the fighting pits, from which the constant roar of the crowd can always be heard.

## The Party's History Here

The Shattered Spire was where the party first met [[chadle|Chadle]], [[hemeth|Hemeth]], and [[veygar|Veygar]] — the intelligence dinner of session 16 that gave them most of what they needed to navigate [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]]'s politics. It was also where they first encountered the Drow scout Xalith, who had been paid by [[ilvara-mizzrym|Ilvara]] to report their location and who subsequently offered to work for them instead.

Morgran runs the bar on the main floor — a Duergar barkeep with enough professional warmth to take a 90-gold order and mean it when he promises them a good evening. The establishment stocks Darklake lager, which Tark drank in implausible quantities while theatrically pretending to be drunk.

[[snorbul|Snorbul]] came here alone in session 21, looking for a way to cheer up Tark, wandered into the fighting pits, misread a betting slip as a fight signup, stripped off his pants for freedom of movement, and leaped into the arena against the Drow noble Bolekk Barrison Del'Armgo. He lost, was healed by a Duergar cleric, and received a small bonus from the impressed Drow.
