Bohd's Band is a mercenary company contracted by Silverymoon to strengthen the defences of the Barony of Thunder Bluff. The company includes a small unit of Dwarven Sappers and is led by Bohd, a half-orc mounted on a blood-drinker wyvern named Kim. Its warpriest is Miranda, a Cleric of Sune.

The group assessed Bohd in the training arena and found him a competent fighter — a solid addition to the Faerûn Defense Force, though outmatched by any of the Heralds.
