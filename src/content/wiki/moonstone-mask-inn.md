## Description

The Moonstone Mask Inn is a renowned establishment in Neverwinter that caters to adventurers, mercenaries, and travelers. It serves as a hub for quest-seekers and a gathering place for information about the region's dangers and opportunities.

## Significance

The Moonstone Mask serves as the party's base of operations during their time in Neverwinter, where they accept contracts, gather intelligence, and make contacts with various factions operating in the city.
