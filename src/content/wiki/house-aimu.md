House Aimu is a noble family of [[helioglabulus|Helios]], with their manor situated in the upper districts of the city. [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]], a Paladin of [[lathander|Lathander]] and the house's most prominent member, broke into the Cathedral of Lathander and stole the [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]] — a celestial greatsword the group had recovered from [[barovia|Barovia]], catalogued by the Church as a [[sunsword|sunsword]].

Despite Lord Aimu's actions, the house retains its social standing. The Queen's hospitality was extended through House Aimu when [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] was recognised as a visiting nobleman — a townhouse and concierge were offered, situated opposite the Aimu Manor itself.
