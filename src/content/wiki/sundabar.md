Sundabar is a dwarven citadel in the Nether Mountains, sacked by orcs, dragons, and [[drow-faction|Drow]] two years before the party's arrival — and rebuilt into something far greater. A fifteen-metre seamless stone wall encircles the city, a fifty-foot moat at its base, flame-spitter cannons lining the ramparts, and fortified bridges serving as fortresses in their own right. The city is now linked to the dwarven capital of Gauntlgrym by a magical gate through which trade and reinforcements flow freely.

## The Uppercity

The surface remains scarred — streets cleared of debris but buildings left as the orcs left them, shanty towns springing up in old neighbourhoods. The derelict Temple Plaza holds four shattered cathedrals open to the sky. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] found the seediest quarter through Thieves' Cant markers, reaching the Sloppy Sahuagin to reconnect with her Xentarim contacts.

## The Undercity and the Everfire

Accessed via massive cargo elevators flanked by fortified ramps, the Undercity is scanned by a Cleric of Moradin at the entrance. The Military Training Ward — the Beardless Brawl Hall — stretches through a vast chamber of columns, barracks, and practice fields. The Hopping Hair tavern, with its bouncing-beard sign and seven firepits, serves as the military's social hub. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] dominated its fighting ring, knocking out challengers to thunderous applause and flowing coin.

At the heart of the Undercity lies the Everfire — a shallow pit two hundred feet across filled with bubbling lava, dozens of forges ringing it, dwarves in golem-like armour scraping precious metals from its surface with long skimmers. [[megno-slatesunder|Megno]] Slatesunder maintains his workshop near the Everfire, where he, [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]], and [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] spent a month crafting [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]]'s prosthetic arm. The Northern Clan Vaults are managed by Vaultkeeper Darrak Goldhold, accessed through glittering golden doors.

## Significance

The party spent a full month in Sundabar, using it as a staging ground before heading to [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]]. Key events include the Reaver cleansing ritual atop the ruined Temple of Helm that birthed [[eclipse|Eclipse]], Gundren's brothers establishing a permanent workshop, and the discovery of a Zhengyi Ziggurat beneath the city — grown from the life force of Megno's apprentice Molly and destroyed by [[mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]] and the Delzoun Emperor.
