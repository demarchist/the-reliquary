## Description

Thueba Swamp is a vast tropical swamp in Al Askar, a fetid expanse of gnarled trees, dense vegetation, and rising mist under the glare of twin suns. The humidity is oppressive and relentless, with biting insects swarming the eyes and mouths of travellers. Channels of murky water hundreds of feet wide separate islands of tangled vegetation, and unseen things lurk beneath the surface — vines that drag the unwary under and creatures that pull even the undead to watery graves.

## Dangers

The swamp is extraordinarily hostile. Gargantuan crocodiles — some twenty feet long, others pushing forty — prowl the waterways, many riddled with Zalaam's Corruption. Their bites carry infection that can blind and cripple within hours. Legions of undead patrol the eastern reaches near Sharza, including skeletal giants that hurl trees and boulders and regenerating ghost giants that wield spectral greatswords and blasts of freezing wind. Corrupted halfling tribes allied with the entity Hohonu ambush travellers with blighted arrows and exploding skull-bombs.

The swamp also contains ancient Saurian ruins haunted by the frozen ghosts of a thousand-year-old war against the Phaerimm. These sites are cursed — supernaturally cold, hostile to fire, and inhabited by undead sheathed in icy energy.

## Inhabitants

The halfling people of 'Āina Pulu inhabit the western reaches of the swamp. Their community is split between followers of the old ways, led by the shaman Camea, and the Abyss Walkers — a faction that worships the imprisoned ancient dragon Hohonu Hāmau and performs blood sacrifices to weaken its prison.

## Significance

The party traversed Thueba Swamp to reach two critical sites: the Machina Factory, an ancient Saurian manufacturing facility containing a database of Ringed Veil addresses crucial for their journey home, and the Temple of Fawden, which served as the site of a divine ritual and an escape route through the Underdark back to Faerûn.
