Fen is the youngest member of Meljir's family group — a beardless Frost Giant who carried a large chest bound with iron chains on his back during the journey to Shatterpeak. He was present during the gnoll ambush on the frozen lake and at the subsequent wedding feast, though he kept a lower profile than his older kin.
