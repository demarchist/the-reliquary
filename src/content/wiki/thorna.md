Thorna is an Air Genasi Cleric and a member of the Faerûn Defense Force. She serves alongside the other members of the council that governs the Barony of Thunder Bluff.

Little is recorded of Thorna's personal history or motivations, though her presence among the ranks of the Faerûn Defense Force speaks to her considerable power and trustworthiness.
