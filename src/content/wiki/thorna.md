Thorna is an Air Genasi Cleric and a member of the Faerûn Defense Force, serving alongside the other members of the council that governs the Barony of Thunder Bluff. She wields an icy hammer capable of channelling frost magic.

Thorna's personal history is largely unknown, even to herself. When the party encountered the ancient shaman Umu at the Obsidian Order's encampment near the Sea of Silt, Umu conducted a spirit-walking ritual to divine Thorna's past and returned with a cryptic answer: her destiny lay in the frigid north of Faerûn, and she was not alone — she was one of two.

In Al Askar, Thorna's magic proved unreliable. The wild magic storms that plagued the surface caused her spells to surge unpredictably — at the Obsidian Order's camp, a simple casting triggered a wild magic eruption that an elven spellcaster had to counterspell, earning her sharp admonishment and dark looks. Despite this instability, Thorna played a critical role during the ritual at the Font of Fawden, using her Ray of Frost to cool a red-hot Sapphire Circuit that threatened to destroy the Ringed Veil mid-ritual, allowing Gundren to bypass the sabotaged component.
