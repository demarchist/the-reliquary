Vaketh is the god of Power in the world of [[al-askar|Al Askar]], worshipped at a domed temple perched atop a stone pillar jutting from a magma lake within the caldera of Mt. Saqar. He had been imprisoned by [[vecna|Vecna]] for an eternity before the party's intervention freed him. He now travels as a celestial passenger bonded to [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]]'s soul, empowering the monk's prosthetic arm and lending divine intervention in critical moments.

## The Temple

The Temple of Vaketh was originally noted by [[jareem|Jareem]] as being undefended by the Shadowfell creatures that guarded the other temples on Al Askar — though by the time the party arrived, it had been overrun by Howlers, Banshees, the Lonely, and ultimately a Saurian Death Lord with four Boneclaws who teleported directly from the Army of the Dead in Sharza. The party was drawn there by a prophecy from the Temple of Carthus promising Snorbul great power at Vaketh's shrine. The temple was accessed by two narrow bridges from the mainland across the magma lake. After defeating the Death Lord, Jareem led the cleansing trials that sanctified the temple once more.

## The Bonding

At the Font of Fawden, defending against Vecna's legions in the final confrontation on Al Askar, Tark's hand was severed by a Death Lord. Vaketh spoke to him for the first time — a deep, resonant voice asking to help. Tark accepted. With a thunderclap, the limb regrew out of crimson energy, and Tark rushed back to the fray with his god-hand, shattering the Death Lord's Boneclaws one by one and breaking his invulnerability.

## The Prosthetic

The divine intervention at the Font was temporary. Back in [[faerun|Faerûn]], Tark felt Vaketh's continued presence — hitchhiking on the monk's soul, as per their agreement. The god wanted only to come along for the ride, having spent an eternity imprisoned by Vecna. With Vaketh's power and the focus of Bahamut, Tark reshaped [[moot|Moot]]'s white-wood staff around his wounded arm, weaving threads of reddish metal through the grain until a wooden hand grew to fit the wound. The prosthetic glows deep red in combat, does not tire, and grants Tark the ability to fly.

## Ongoing Role

Vaketh continues to empower Tark as the group pursues [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] in the [[bloodstone-lands|Bloodstone Lands]]. When Tark resolved to destroy the [[flint-dagger-of-li-xian|Flint Dagger of Li Xian]], Vaketh stirred and offered to help. Tark channeled his Ki through the prosthetic arm — crimson light tracing every line and joint of the white wood — and obliterated the cursed weapon in an explosion of scarlet light that blew out the back wall of a barn. When [[cassian|Cassian]] cast *Detect Good and Evil* moments later, the prosthetic blazed with celestial power.
