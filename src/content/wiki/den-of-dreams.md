The Den of Dreams is a magical sanctum housed in the basement of the Fane of Selune, open to members of the public for a fee.

## Atmosphere

The Den is dark and smoky, filled with pillars and pillows, hushed whispers, and the occasional sound of quiet sobbing. It is an otherworldly and unsettling place that exists at the boundary between waking and dreaming.
