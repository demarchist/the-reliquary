## Description

Moondancer Village is a Moon Elf settlement nestled within the Moonwood forest. The village maintains traditional elven ways and serves as home to elves including Lelria, Jindree, and Fesk.

## Significance

The village connects to the broader narrative of the Moonwood and the threats facing its elven inhabitants, including Drow raids that have endangered the forest communities.
