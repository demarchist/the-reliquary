Vakuun the Dreadblade is an Ancient Red Dragon from Shou Lung and a member of the Dragonflight. His humanoid form is that of a portly human man.

He has a marked tendency toward the collection of worship. Upon descending on a Tabaxi village early in the journey, Vakuun cowed the locals and anointed a chosen for his cult before departing — a move quickly undone by Aurelian. That same night, while the Dragonflight rested at an extinct volcano, a mysterious humanoid figure descended to the caldera and met with the sleeping Vakuun. Roars of rage were heard through the dark. By morning, the extinct volcano was active once more.
