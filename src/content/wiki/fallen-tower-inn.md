The Fallen Tower Inn is a large establishment located near the border of the Rift and Protector's districts in Neverwinter, across from a guard post. Outwardly a bustling tavern with a stage for performers and dozens of patrons, it also serves as one of the headquarters of the Dead Rats — Neverwinter's thieves' guild, run by the Rat King.

The inn features hollow walls with crawl spaces and passages used by guild enforcers for surveillance and quick movement between rooms. Scratches in Thieves' Cant mark various surfaces for those who know how to read them.

The party used the Fallen Tower as the staging ground for their trap against the Red Grin Killer — the warlock Strigu who was stalking a bard named Jake. The resulting battle involved a summoned fire elemental, Meenlocks attacking through the wall passages, and the collapse of a section of the second floor. The Dead Rats demonstrated impressive organisation during the crisis, distributing scrolls of water-jet to contain the flames with practiced efficiency. The spy Galen Marek operates from the establishment, maintaining his own network within the guild's territory.
