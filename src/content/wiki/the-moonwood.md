## Description

The Moonwood is a vast magical forest that lies north of Kossenberg, beyond the Barony of Thunder Bluff. Its remote depths harbour strange creatures and planar anomalies — the Shadowfell bleeds close to the material world here, and its southern reaches border the Evermoors.

## The Ziggurat

During the group's absence in Svartalfheim, a massive crystalline Ziggurat of black crystal grew in a clearing within the southern Moonwood — spawned by the Zhengyi Tome of Creation and the cursed chair-person trapped in Cirrus's demiplane. The Doomstar Legion and their goblinoid allies established a perimeter around the structure, and the Shadowfell's influence intensified — a black orb visible in the Ethereal Plane with an undulating umbilical descending into the forest.

Animals fled the southern Moonwood in the days before the assault, describing "the bad rock that grows" to Doctor Breakfast's animal companions. The forest fell completely silent — not a single creature stirring — as the Ziggurat neared completion.
