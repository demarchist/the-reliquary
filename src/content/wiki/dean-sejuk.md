## Background

Dean Sejuk is the head of Silverymoon's prestigious College, a center of arcane learning and scholarship. He is an old friend of Fëanon, and the two share a long history of academic collaboration.

## Role in the Campaign

Sejuk hosts the party at his manor in Silverymoon, providing them with resources, information, and connections during the Amnian crisis. His position at the College gives the party access to valuable research and magical expertise.

## Notable Traits

- Respected academic and arcane scholar
- Long-standing friendship with Fëanon
- Well-connected within Silverymoon's political and magical circles
