## Background

Von Issinger holds the position of Master of Coin in Silverymoon, managing the city's financial affairs and treasury. Behind his official role, he secretly collaborates with Amnian forces.

## Role in the Campaign

Von Issinger's treachery is uncovered during the Amnian invasion of Silverymoon. His betrayal from within the city's own government represents one of the most dangerous aspects of the coup attempt, as he provides the enemy with critical intelligence and financial resources.

## Notable Traits

- Traitor within Silverymoon's government
- Master of Coin with access to sensitive financial information
- Father of Chaunce, a young informant
