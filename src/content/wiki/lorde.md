## Background

Lorde first appears as JmJm's rival bard, accompanied by her hulking barbarian backup singer Gronk. Beneath her performer's persona, she is actually a Harper agent operating under deep cover.

## Role in the Campaign

Initially presented as a comedic rival, Lorde's true purpose is revealed when she is found trapped in Sigil, having been sent by the Harpers to stop the Goblin King from consuming JmJm's mind and using his body to access the prime material plane.

## Notable Traits

- Talented bard who rivals JmJm in performance
- Harper agent operating under deep cover
- Accompanied by Gronk, her barbarian backup singer
