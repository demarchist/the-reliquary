Jeckeyll is a Halfling female Archmage in the service of Lord Dagult Neverember. She researches the magical barrier sealing the Chasm beneath Neverwinter, the Thayan threat, and incidents of undeath plaguing the region. Her expertise provides the party with critical intelligence for their expedition into the Chasm.
