The Mines of Und are a network of tunnels and caverns beneath the village of [[und|Und]] in the Galena Mountains. Originally a dwarven mining operation with pick-scarred walls and timber shoring, the mines have been fundamentally altered by the [[elder-orb|Elder Orb]] that took up residence in their depths. The deeper passages have been reshaped into something uncanny — walls threaded with veins of glowing crystal, stone too smooth and too symmetrical to be natural, the air humming faintly with power.

## Structure

The mines open from the hillside above the village into the upper tunnels — still recognisably dwarven in their construction. Further in, the worked stone gives way to the Elder Orb's influence. Tunnels branch in multiple directions: passages to the left draw air downward toward the deeper chambers where the creature lairs.

The upper levels contain a large cavern with deep pits and crevasses — home to a nest of Ropers, their stalactite-like forms indistinguishable from natural rock to anyone unfamiliar with the [[underdark|Underdark]]. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' Drow heritage and years beneath the earth allowed her to spot the creatures where others would have walked straight into their tendrils.

Further in, a breakroom carved into the tunnel wall held the body of a miner — weeks old, every bone in his body pulverised while the flesh remained intact. The cause of death was never determined.

## The Eyes

Throughout the tunnels, eyes are embedded in the crystal-veined walls — closed but alive, their orbs shifting restlessly beneath the lids. These are the Elder Orb's dreaming sentinels, extensions of its psychic influence that project its will outward even in dormancy. When the group entered the mines, one of these eyes snapped open and fired a beam of necrotic energy at [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] before being destroyed.

## Reality Warping

The Elder Orb's influence causes the stone itself to become unstable. The tunnels periodically warp without warning — floors and walls twisting and melting like candle wax, dragging anyone standing on them down into the softening rock. Fëanon identified the effect as the creature's dreams leaking into physical reality, reshaping matter on a subconscious whim. The group encountered this phenomenon twice during their initial descent, pulling themselves free before the stone resolidified.

## The Ethereal Signature

From the [[ethereal-plane|Ethereal Plane]], the mines blaze with power — lines of light pulsing through the rock like a heartbeat, visible even from the border plane. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] observed this signature from atop [[steden|Steden]] after the battle in the village, confirming that something far more powerful than an ordinary Beholder resided below.

## The Crystal Chamber

The outer chambers of the mine are home to lesser aberrations drawn to the Elder Orb's power. A Spectator and a Gauth were found gathering iridescent crystals from the walls — the Gauth clutching them in its tentacles like a miser hoarding coins. The group destroyed both creatures, clearing the outer chambers before pressing deeper toward the Elder Orb's true lair.

Bioluminescent fungi line the walls of the deeper caverns, their soft glow catching the scattered crystals and casting the mine interior in a shifting kaleidoscope of colour.
