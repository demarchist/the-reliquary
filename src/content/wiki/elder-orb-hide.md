A long strip of hide, harvested from the underside of the [[elder-orb|Elder Orb]] of [[und|Und]] after the group slew the aberration in its [[mines-of-und|crystalline lair]]. The material is unlike conventional leather — thick, rubbery, faintly translucent, and visibly enchanted. The piece came free in a single broad cut across the creature's underside as the group worked through the corpse for salvage, and went into a [[bag-of-holding|Bag of Holding]] for transport back to the Barony.

The hide of an Elder Orb retains a residual fraction of the creature's antimagic property — spells that target items crafted from it are sloughed off the way the living creature shrugged off Banish and Disintegrate. The applications are obvious: armour, cloak linings, scroll cases, a hood for a wizard's hat that will keep a casting alive against counterspells. The material will need careful work by a skilled crafter to render it usable, but the potential is significant.

It joins the eight harvested eyestalks as the most valuable haul from the lair — the central eye, ruined by the antimagic punch [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] used to finish the creature, was lost.
