## Description

The Cathedral of Twilight is built into a floating island of stone suspended above a vast nebula-filled sky. Its great spire descends and twists, warped by a black hole floating in the Astral Sea far beneath it. Reached by a corkscrewing stone arch that performs a full revolution — with gravity shifting to match the walker — the cathedral's exterior belies an interior of impossible vastness.

## Interior

Inside, pairs of robed clerics attend the cathedral — one wearing a serene mask of silver (for Selûné), the other a solemn mask of onyx (for Shar). These are the Clerics of the Twinborn Goddesses.

At the cathedral's centre, a great hole descends to Yggward and the Shadowfell. Twelve Mirrors of Loss — mirrors of perfect darkness — float by the precipice. Petitioners may touch a mirror to enter the dream of a slumbering ancestor and commune with the ancient dead. The dreamer must consent to be observed.

## Significance

The group visited the Cathedral to seek the blessing of Patches' ancestors. Through the mirrors, they entered the dream of Kallista Alamreth Undonwe and changed the outcome of her eternal last stand. Gundren used a separate mirror to study the techniques of Opheli Lu, the ancient bowcrafter. The ceremony naming Patches as Matron Mother of House Alamreth Undonwe was conducted within the cathedral's halls.
