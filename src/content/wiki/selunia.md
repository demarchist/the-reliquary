Selunia is the settlement at the heart of the Barony of [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]], named in honour of the goddess [[selune|Selûne]]. Built around Lake Selunia and anchored by [[feanons-tower|Fëanon's Tower]] and the [[keep-of-thunder-bluff|Keep of Thunder Bluff]], the town has grown from a frontier outpost into a thriving community under the stewardship of the [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]]. Lord [[neverember|Neverember]] designated Selunia as a base of operations, sending a garrison and resources to support its development.

## Landmarks

The town sits where sunlight gleams off Lake Selunia and the rolling landscape stretches to the [[moonwood|Moonwood]] and the [[evermoors|Evermoors]]. Key landmarks include the [[keep-of-thunder-bluff|Keep of Thunder Bluff]] with its red-hued stone, the [[rockseeker-manufactory|Rockseeker Manufactory]] where [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]]'s dwarves produce firearms and extradimensional latrines, [[feanons-tower|Fëanon's Tower]] with its teleportation chamber, the Fane of [[selune|Selûne]] on Sérindë Isle where [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] tends to the moon goddess, and Over the Moon — the tavern where residents and adventurers gather. [[mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]] himself arrived to complete the town's defensive walls during the party's Underdark campaigns.

## A Growing Community

Freed slaves from across the North have made their way to Selunia, drawn by the promise of a fresh start under the party's protection. The first caravan of thirty-two freed slaves arrived from [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]], and newcomers are told to seek out Li Tan upon arrival. The Barony received 150 parcels of new land from [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]], and a mutual protection pact with [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]] yields three thousand gold in cut gemstones and five hundred gold in salt per month. Local life includes festivals honouring Lathander, the presence of Beryan the Sage at Fëanon's library, and the cultural mixing of former enemies — Drow, humans, dwarves, and halflings — living side by side.

## Under Threat

Despite its walls and garrison, Selunia remains vulnerable. The dragon [[ancalagon|Ancalagon]] retaliated against the party by dropping abducted civilians onto the town's streets — multiple innocents killed on impact. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]], returning from the [[shadowfell|Shadowfell]], stood at the Fane and detected absolute silence in the nearby [[moonwood|Moonwood]] — an ominous sign of unknown danger pressing against the Barony's southern border.
