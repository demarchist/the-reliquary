Willow Aldencroft attended the debutante ball at the [[keep-of-thunder-bluff|Keep of Thunder Bluff]] as [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi's]] potential betrothed in an arranged marriage. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] surveilled the woman and found her suspiciously competent — well-armed and prepared for danger, with something beneath the surface that defied easy identification. [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] was almost relieved at the possibility that his betrothed might be an assassin, considering it a far simpler problem to resolve than a relationship.
