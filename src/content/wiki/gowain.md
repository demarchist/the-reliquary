Gowain, known as the Shadow Abbot, is a tall, leather-clad servant of Strahd Von Zarovich driven to madness. Possessing teleportation abilities, he relentlessly hunts the party throughout Barovia, appearing when least expected. He murders the priest Lucien of Lathander at the Feast of St. Andral, demonstrating his willingness to commit atrocities in his master's name.
