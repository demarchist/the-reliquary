Ares is a gargantuan Ancient Red Wyrm encountered ransacking [[Themberchaud]]'s volcanic lair in the Bloodstone Lands. He wears a black exoskeleton of magical armor with a large gemstone embedded in the chestplate — the source of a charm effect that pulses with dark energy drawn from the north.

He was accompanied by a cadre of sickly, ashen White Dragons (an adult and four younglings) and an Efreeti Lord who departed at his command.

## Disposition

Ares claims no fealty to Tiamat or any god, seeking only his own power and salvation. He spoke of a *new order* and the possibility of a place within it for those who would follow him. When [[Vakuun the Dreadblade]] pressed him on Tiamat's call to unite against the coming darkness, Ares laughed it off.

[[Vakuun the Dreadblade|Vakuun]]'s casting of Protection from Good and Evil revealed a charm upon Ares — momentarily dispelled, then immediately restored by power channeling from the north through the gemstone in his armor.

## Encounter

Rather than allow a battle that could prove fatal to both sides, the [[Dragons of Shou Lung]] withdrew from the lair. As they retreated, Ares struck — detonating an explosion around [[Fellasin Stormwing|Fellasin]] and breathing magically enhanced fire at [[Vakuun the Dreadblade|Vakuun]], fire that burned even his red kin. Ares then fled north with his entourage toward the great storm on the horizon.

## Connection to Zhengyi

Ares's armor and the charm channeled through it appear to originate from [[Zhengyi]]'s power base to the north. Whether Ares is a willing servant, an unwitting thrall, or something more complicated remains unclear.
