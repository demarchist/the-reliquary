The Saurian Deathlord's Greatsword is a blazing titanium sourcework weapon — unique, otherworldly, and wielded by the last great general of a long-dead interplanetary empire. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] claimed the weapon after defeating the Deathlord at the Font of Fawden, the same battle in which the ancient Saurian's burning blade took his left hand.

The group traded the greatsword to [[dima|Dima]] at [[izzys-stuff|Izzy's Stuff]] in [[helioglabulus|Helios]] as part of the price for a jar of [[elder-brain-ichor|Elder Brain Ichor]] — the final component for Tark's [[windrunner-armor|Windrunner Armor]]. Dima fawned over the blade, recognising its value immediately.
