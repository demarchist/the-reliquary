## Description

Mt. Saqar is a massive active volcano in Al Askar, featuring a Fiery Gorge and the Temple of Vaketh on its slopes. Deep within its magma-heated interior lies the legendary Titanic Forge, an ancient forge capable of working titanium and other rare metals.
