## Description

Mt. Saqar is a massive active volcano in Al Askar, visible for many miles across the badlands, belching a continual cloud of gases and ash. The Fiery Gorge — a great chasm on the mountainside — provides access to the caldera, which contains a vast lake of lava fed by a thousand-foot lava-fall produced by a gargantuan worm creature whose maw disgorges an endless stream of molten stone. The ruins of an ancient Titanic city rise from the lava lake, and packs of bony fiends prowl the volcanic landscape. The Temple of Vaketh sits on the slopes nearby.

## The Caldera

The approach to Mt. Saqar transforms the terrain from sandy badlands to black volcanic grit punctuated by smoking sulphur pits and hidden lava pools beneath thin crusts. Charred humanoid remains litter the area. Inside the gorge, the sky is obscured by ash and gas, with the only illumination coming from the red glow of magma. The heat actively burns exposed skin and the air is barely breathable.

Deep within, a massive arched bridge of Titanic design spans the lava lake, ending beneath the lava-falls. Beyond the falls — accessible only by cutting through the cascading molten stone — lies a great tunnel leading to the Titanic Forge. The Cult of the Black Skull established a stronghold here, chaining a Ruby Crystal Dragon at the forge and using alghoul hordes as guardians. The caldera was also home to the Psi'Shara — giant psionic insectoids displaced by the cult's activities.

## Significance

The party ventures into Mt. Saqar to access the Titanic Forge, where they craft powerful titanium weapons and armor for the challenges ahead. The volcano's slopes also contain a trial temple where the party faces a Death Lord — an undead Saurian lord.
