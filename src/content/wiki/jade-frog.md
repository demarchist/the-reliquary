The Jade Frog is a golem of animated jade that sprang to life when the [[spellforge|Spellforge]] in [[wave-echo-cave|Wave Echo Cave]] discharged its energy into a small jade frog statue in the party's packs. The vortex flared, snuffed out, and the statue became a living construct containing the Spellforge's power and an [[artifact-shard|artifact shard]] within its body.

## Early Behaviour

The golem displayed a mischievous streak from the start. While the party was in [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]], the Jade Frog snatched an earring from a passing man's ear — the victim looking around in confused pain, unable to figure out what had happened.

## Slippy the Scout

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] nicknamed the Jade Frog "Slippy" and used it extensively for reconnaissance. During the party's search for a [[bedine|Bedine]] monolith in [[anauroch|Anauroch]], Slippy scouted ahead and fled back to Patches when it detected a massive sandworm coiled near the base of the stone.
