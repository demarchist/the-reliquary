The Great Tree of Bahamut stands at the highest terrace of [[bloodstone-city|Bloodstone City]] — a massive white tree set in a plaza of gleaming marble gardens, linked through its roots to Yggdrasil itself and thence to the planes beyond. The Tree is the planar pathway by which the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]] were to ascend to their assigned lairs across the cosmos.

## The Gatherers

Two ancient dragons were assigned to operate the Tree as its dragon-side stewards: [[protanther|Protanther]], the abdicated king of the Gold Dragons, and [[themberchaud|Themberchaud the Grand]], the Ancient Red of [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]]. Between them they were to receive each member of the Dragonflight, walk them through the planeshift to the lair prepared for them by their majordomos, and so usher the entire flight into ancienthood. The white tree's marble plaza, ringed by two dozen yellow-robed monks, served as the gathering place for the rite.

The plan did not survive contact with the [[bloodstone-lands|Bloodstone Lands]].

Themberchaud did not arrive. Volcanic activity erupted to the north and Protanther's attempt to scry on his counterpart failed. The Ancient Gold sent the chromatic members of the Dragonflight north to investigate, and several others of the flight followed at need — a divergence that scattered the dragons across the region in the worst possible week.

## The Fall

When [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] and several of her flight broke off in search of a missing Red Dragon emissary, they found instead [[vulkan|Vulkan]] and [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] stationed above the ruin of [[palischuk|Palischuk]] on a great [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]]. They watched at least one of their kin fall to a [[golden-orb|Golden Orb]] in the pursuit. [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] — answering [[bahamut|Bahamut]]'s direct call to the Dragonflight's defence — reached the Great Tree in time to fight beside Protanther.

She watched Protanther fall before Vulkan.

What has become of the Tree itself in the wake of its abdicated-king's death has not been confirmed. The plaza, the monks, the marble gardens at the highest terrace of Bloodstone City — these remain. The dragon who held the rite has not.
