Steden is a Nightmare — a fiendish, fire-wreathed horse capable of traversing the Ethereal Plane. The mount is primarily ridden by Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews, who uses Steden's planar-shifting ability to move between the Material and Ethereal Planes with devastating tactical effect.

During the battle at Hohonu's tar pits, JmJm led the ambush by galloping from the Ethereal Plane astride Steden with Cirrus and Gundren, catching the halfling cultists completely off guard. Steden also served as the party's means of reconnaissance and rescue within the Ethereal Plane — Cirrus rode the Nightmare into the vortex of Hohonu's prison to pull Tark from the Death Wyrm's clutches.
