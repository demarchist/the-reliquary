Steden is a Nightmare — a fiendish, fire-wreathed horse capable of traversing the [[ethereal-plane|Ethereal Plane]]. The mount is primarily ridden by [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews]], who uses Steden's planar-shifting ability to move between the Material and Ethereal Planes with devastating tactical effect.

During the [[battle-of-the-tar-pits|Battle of the Tar Pits]], JmJm led the ambush by galloping from the Ethereal Plane astride Steden with [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]], catching the halfling cultists completely off guard. Steden also served as the party's means of reconnaissance and rescue within the Ethereal Plane — Cirrus rode the Nightmare into the vortex of Hohonu's prison to pull [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] from the Death Wyrm's clutches.
