Aegir is the ancient patriarch of the frost giant settlement on Shatterpeak. Distinguished by a beard that reaches the floor, a mammoth-tusk walking stick, and a wrapped robe of tartan secured at one shoulder by a massive silver brooch, he is often found dozing in his great chair at the back of the ice hall.

Aegir presided over the final event of the competition between the giants and the party — "The Cheese Sniff." Eighteen wheels of cheese were presented in sets of three, one good and two rotten, and the contestant had to identify which to eat by smell alone. Gundren Rockseeker proved the superior judge of foul odours, besting the old patriarch. Rather than take offence, Aegir struck up a friendship with the dwarf, and the two retired to the hall to discuss cheese at length.
