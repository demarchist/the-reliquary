The Circle — also referred to as the Circle of Mystra — is a group of powerful arcanists who meet at a location called The Rock on the [[astral-plane|Astral Plane]]. [[mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]] sent [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] a formal invitation to meet The Circle, delivered in a purple velvet envelope sealed with black wax flecked with gold. The letter whispered the archmage's name when touched and let out a small, satisfied whimper when opened.

The invitation noted that time was malleable on the Astral Plane and that Fëanon should contact Mordenkainen when he had some time to spare. Fëanon pocketed the invitation and considered his archwizard's staff — still in the planning stages — which Mordenkainen had previously encouraged him to craft as a prerequisite for joining.
