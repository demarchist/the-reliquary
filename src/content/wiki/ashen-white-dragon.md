Ashen White Dragons are a rare and poorly understood type of dragon — dull grey in colour, sickly in appearance, and associated in old stories with servitude to a greater wyrm. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] recalled a tale of a Red Dragon that kept them as ashen, sickly pet hounds.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] spotted eight of these creatures through her spyglass, circling above one of the true mountains of the Galena range during the group's journey along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]]. No one in the group had heard of grey dragons before. The eight dove in unison behind the closer peaks and were not seen again — though a distorted roar echoed across the mountain vales in the early hours of the following morning, likely theirs.

## Over Frostmoore Vale

The same eight Ashen Whites — and a ninth, larger sickly-grey adult that arrived later in a thunderclap of displaced air — descended on the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]] at sundown. The largest of the eight carried a [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizard of Thay]] on its back as a mount, complete with a saddle and an arcane focus, confirming that the formation answers to a controlling power rather than acting as wild creatures. The flight worked in coordination with the [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Vampire Knight of Frostmoore]] and his own larger Ashen mount, all of them apparently sent to interdict the recovery of the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] from Lord Aimu.

When [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] emerged from the chapel with the Sunsword burning in his hand, the two nearest Ashen Whites in mid-stoop *flinched* at the brightness — confirming, as expected of dragons in the white family, that the sunlight radiance of a true Sunsword burns them as it burns vampires.

## The Slaughter

The flight did not survive the night. The smaller wyrmlings stooped on [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] in her Copper form and were torn apart in seconds — three of them killed in close combat and the fourth slowed out of the air by her Slow Breath, then finished by [[cassian|Cassian]]'s arrows, [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' bolts, and the household guard's thundercannons. The two surviving adults peeled off under the Vampire Knight's call to retreat, breathed a last desperate jet of ice at the slope below them, and climbed for the ridge — both of them already wounded and slowed by a second column of Izmakella's breath.

The Vampire Knight's own larger Ashen mount, carrying him invisible up the ridge, took Cassian's last three magical arrows in close succession, followed by Patches' poisoned bolts and the archers' final volley as it laboured past their range. It bellowed in pain, folded its wings, and crashed amongst the distant trees of the mountainside. Its body would be recoverable. The two adults that limped clear of the valley were last seen disappearing into the darkening sky toward the high notch of the pass.

## Significance

The original Ashen Whites encountered by the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]] at [[themberchaud|Themberchaud]]'s lair, in service to the enthralled Ancient Red [[ares|Ares]], suggested that the breed is the controlled retinue of a greater Red. The Knight of Frostmoore's flight — eight wyrmlings under the command of two adults, mounted by a Thayan and a Vampire — confirms that the same breed is now operating much further south, in service to [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]]'s wider apparatus. The Reds at the top of the chain — [[ares|Ares]] in the north, [[vulkan|Vulkan]] above [[palischuk|Palischuk]] — appear to be the source of the breed itself.
