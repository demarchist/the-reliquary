## Overview

The Ironarm Mercenary Company is a professional military organization contracted by Amnian forces during their attempted invasion of Silverymoon. They served as soldiers-for-hire, conducting patrols and combat operations throughout the city.

## Role in the Invasion

During the Amnian coup attempt, Ironarm personnel were stationed throughout Silverymoon to enforce occupational control and suppress resistance. They fought against the Knights in Silver and the city's defenders.

## Aftermath

Following the failed invasion, the Ironarm mercenaries were defeated alongside their Amnian employers, ending their occupation of Silverymoon.
