## Background

Gerwan is a half-orc bard who found himself trapped in Barovia, spending about a week living among the Vistani before encountering the party.

## Role in the Campaign

Gerwan joins the party as a fellow traveler desperate to escape the cursed domain of Barovia. His bardic talents complement the group's capabilities as they navigate the dangers of Strahd's realm.

## Notable Traits

- Half-orc bard, an unusual combination
- Familiar with Vistani customs from his time among them
- Motivated primarily by the desire to escape Barovia
