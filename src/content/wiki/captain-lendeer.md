Captain Lendeer is a human city guard captain who arrived at the wharf after the party captured The Silent Virtue. He coordinated the perimeter around the Sallow Manatee and facilitated the arrest of Petey Greene. He informed the group that by city law, the captured ship could not be claimed outright but would be remanded to the City Watch, with the party offered first right of refusal to purchase it at a discount.
