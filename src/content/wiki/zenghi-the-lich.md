Zenghi the Lich is an ancient lich originating from the Bloodstone Lands, specifically the Desecrated Barrows in Vaasa. A powerful necromancer, he has a history of annihilating communities and raising undead armies.

## Threat

Zenghi aims to expand his dominion by destabilizing regions like Thunder Bluff through necromantic afflictions and chaos. He commands advanced necromantic magic, including possession, undead creation, and widespread curses.
