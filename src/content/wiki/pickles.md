Mr. Pickles is a blue-skinned Astral Goblin who serves as [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' fiercely loyal servant, bodyguard, and personal companion. He is one of several goblins transformed by the [[tavern-of-doors|Tavern of Doors]]' extradimensional nature, and despite his small stature and limited combat ability, he has accompanied the party through [[barovia|Barovia]], the [[underdark|Underdark]], and beyond — proving himself one of the Spellforged's most enduring members.

## Introduction

Mr. Pickles' debut set the tone for his entire character. He walked into a room carrying drinks, startling [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]], who killed the goblin with a flurry of blows and a snapped neck. [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]], feeling bad, healed him with lay on hands — and Mr. Pickles got right back to work as if nothing had happened.

## Devotion to Patches

Mr. Pickles' loyalty to Patches goes beyond service into genuine protectiveness. During an Oni ambush at a farmhouse, he was frozen solid by a Cone of Cold and nearly killed. Once Buumi revived him, seeing his mistress bleeding, he unfurled his whip and drew a cut across the Oni's eyebrow. When half-ogres pressed the attack, Pickles stabbed one in the back repeatedly, screaming *"GET AWAY FROM THE MISTRESS!"* — the single most emotional outburst in his otherwise stoic career.

He brews tea from hot spring water during rests, stands guard with a crossbow across his lap while the party sleeps, and was specifically requested by Patches for the Underdark expedition — she considered him essential.

## In the Field

Pickles is trusted with responsibilities that belie his stature. During the desperate escape from [[castle-ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]], he drove the wagon through rain and ballista fire alongside [[ezmerelda|Ezmerelda]] and Chekhov, then fired crossbow bolts at the pursuing vampire horde. He has served as messenger, escort leader, and sentinel — guarding treasure, leading dwarven miners back to the [[selunia|Barony]], and delivering word to the party of arriving guests.
