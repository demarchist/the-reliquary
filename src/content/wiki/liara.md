## Background

Liara is a priestess who serves as a prominent member of the Usurpers of Rudjek in the world of Al Askar. She worships Zalaam, the god of death, but distinguishes between legitimate worship and the corrupted practices of the Cult of Zalaam (Black Skull).

## Role in the Campaign

Liara serves as an ally to the party during their time in Al Askar, helping them navigate the political and religious complexities of the region. Her knowledge of Zalaam's true teachings proves valuable in understanding and combating the cult's corruption.

## Notable Traits

- Priestess of Zalaam who opposes the Cult of the Black Skull
- Member of the Usurpers of Rudjek revolutionary government
- Distinguishes between true faith and cult corruption
