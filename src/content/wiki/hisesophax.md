Hisesophax was the Balor of Juiblex, a Greater Demon commanding the demonic forces that occupied the Dome in Blingdenstone. An ooze-covered monstrosity capable of showering acid over a sixty-foot cone, teleporting across the battlefield, and lashing out with corrosive pseudopod whips, it was the most dangerous opponent the group faced during the Battle of Blingdenstone.

After a protracted and brutal fight across both the interior and exterior of the Dome, Hisesophax was banished by Cirrus using a planar iron fork — the demon pulled in on itself in a flash of voidish purple light, strengthening the planar walls as it was torn from reality.
