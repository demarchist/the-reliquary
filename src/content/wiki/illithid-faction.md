The Illithid, commonly known as mind flayers, are a race of psionic aberrations that feed on the brains of sentient creatures. With their tentacled faces, vast psionic powers, and utterly alien intelligence, the Illithid are among the most feared creatures in the [[underdark|Underdark]] and beyond. They once ruled a vast empire that spanned the multiverse, enslaving entire races including the ancestors of the [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] and Githyanki.

## Nostalgia for Infinity

The Elder Brain [[nostalgia-for-infinity|Nostalgia for Infinity]] — thirty feet across, a pulsating mass of grey-pink tissue laced with glowing veins — dwelt in a hive beneath Ladypeak, entombed within a bio-organic Nautiloid the size of a castle that had fused with the surrounding stone over three thousand years. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] — the youngest and most reckless of [[theodemir|Theodemir]]'s six acolytes — proposed entrusting the [[eye-of-aren|Eye of Aren]], reasoning that a hive mind with no interest in surface politics was the safest vault imaginable. The other acolytes resisted but relented one by one.

The Elder Brain betrayed them, attempting to enslave all six mages. Five escaped. Fëanon was subjected to ceremorphosis — neural filaments threading through his nervous system, the collective attempting to overwrite his identity — but his magical psyche proved incompatible. Instead of clean assimilation, his mind shattered. The collective discarded him into the flesh recycler as a failed experiment, but a raw magical aspect of his consciousness burned its way free, creating a permanent psychic link to the Elder Brain that plagued him for fifty years.

## The Infiltration

The party later infiltrated the hive using psychic camouflage maintained by Fëanon, navigating organic tunnels with breathing membranes, bioluminescent fluid, and assimilation chambers containing translucent pods. They rescued Alura from a pod, confronted the Elder Brain in its cathedral-sized dome, and reclaimed the Eye. In response, the Elder Brain triggered the Nautiloid's launch sequence — the ancient vessel tore itself free from Ladypeak, devastating the mountain, and rose through the clouds. The collective was finally going home.

## The Darklake

The party encountered a living Illithid Nautiloid on the [[darklake|Darklake]] — a vessel that looked more like a giant squid than a ship, its operator psionically aware of the group. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] boarded the vessel to retrieve a jade memory-egg from the ship's captain, passing through fluid-filled chambers containing suspended brains — the ship's cargo, harvested for the hive. A second Illithid hive, Eliatrope — Vision of the Infinite — was later detected by Fëanon near Ched Nasad, hundreds of miles to the southeast.
