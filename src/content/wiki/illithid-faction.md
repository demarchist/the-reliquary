The Illithid, commonly known as mind flayers, are a race of psionic aberrations that feed on the brains of sentient creatures. With their tentacled faces, vast psionic powers, and utterly alien intelligence, the Illithid are among the most feared creatures in the [[underdark|Underdark]] and beyond. They once ruled a vast empire that spanned the multiverse, enslaving entire races including the ancestors of the [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] and Githyanki.

## Nostalgia for Infinity

The Elder Brain Nostalgia for Infinity dwelt in a hive beneath Ladypeak, where [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] — the youngest and most reckless of [[theodemir|Theodemir]]'s six acolytes — proposed entrusting the Eye of Aren, reasoning that a hive mind with no interest in surface politics was the safest vault imaginable. The other acolytes resisted but relented one by one. They stole the Eye and delivered it to the hive, surrendering their memories of the conspiracy in exchange for the Elder Brain's silence.

The Elder Brain betrayed them. It used the artifact — containing a shard of [[the-last-word|The Last Word]] — to amplify its psionic reach, and Fëanon's mind was shattered in the process. [[megno-slatesunder|Megno]] swung his hammer at the attacking Illithids and was flung across the chamber by a telekinetic blast. Sala made the call to run, and they fled the hive without Fëanon. The party later infiltrated the hive and reclaimed the Eye, freeing Theodemir's spirit and Fëanon from decades of enthralment.

## The Darklake

The party encountered Illithid vessels on the [[darklake|Darklake]] during their journey to [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]], and the "Eye of Theodemir" and the Illithid hive were among the topics discussed at the Council of Helm's Hold as part of the broader threat assessment facing the North.
