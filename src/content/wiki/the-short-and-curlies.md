The Short and Curlies is a halfling inn located in the residential quarter of Sundabar's Undercity. It served as the meeting place for a group of thieves planning to rob the Southern Vault.

Patches tracked the halfling spy to this establishment and observed her meeting with three co-conspirators — a gnome mage named Frumple Binswaddle, a half-elf woman, and a human man. After the heist was foiled at the bank (resulting in Frumple's death), Patches returned to The Short and Curlies, where the three surviving thieves had gathered to discuss the fallout. She poisoned their drinks at the bar and then methodically killed the half-elf and the human in their rooms upstairs, completing her assignment from Lady Faye.
