The Tavern of Doors is a magical extradimensional space gifted to [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] by the goddess Sharess, accessible through a small lacquered box that the party carries with them. It has served as the group's mobile base, escape hatch, and home between adventures since the earliest days of the campaign.

## Origin

At the Stonehill Inn in Phandalin, Sharess — goddess of hedonism and indulgence — materialised in JmJm's room one night. Despite his insolence, or perhaps because of it, she gifted him the Tavern of Doors. The party explored it immediately, discovering a magical harp in one of its rooms and a bottle of Wine of Charming, which they promptly drank half of.

## The Space

The Tavern's interior grows and expands over time, generating rooms, facilities, and resources suited to whoever needs them. It contains a kitchen, stables, a forge, individual rooms for each party member — JmJm's is opulent to the point of self-parody — and a vault where the group stores dangerous artifacts and critical supplies. The forge was used by the Rockseeker brothers to craft [[gwen|Guinevere]], Gundren's clockwork lion. JmJm's room has a back door that opens onto a portal whose destination no one has confirmed.

[[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] theorised at the Neverwinter Refounding Festival that the Tavern was not a pocket plane at all — noting that when the box was placed inside another extradimensional space (a spell emporium tent of liquid mercury), no planar tear resulted. His conclusion: the box is a portal to somewhere else entirely.

## The Astral Goblins

The Tavern is tended by a colony of goblins who transformed after living inside the box for about a week — their skin turning blue, their eyes becoming solid black. They became Astral Goblins, reshaped by the Tavern's extradimensional nature. The most notable is Mr. Pickles, who serves as a general attendant and has survived being accidentally killed by [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] (healed immediately by [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]]'s lay on hands, after which he returned to work as if nothing had happened). Steven, the goblin butler, accompanies JmJm on missions and was once carried on JmJm's back during a desperate flight from an Alhoon. A large goblin cook was torn apart in the stables — an incident no one has fully explained — and the stall was never cleaned.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] sacrificed one of the Tavern's goblins to feed [[the-reaver|the Reaver]]'s hunger during a night in the vault beneath Neverwinter. When JmJm noticed the goblin missing the next morning and demanded a search, no trace was found.

## Tactical Use

The Tavern serves as the party's primary emergency shelter. They have piled into it to escape [[venomfang|Venomfang]]'s poison breath, sheltered in it during watches by climbing trees with the box, hidden inside it during infiltrations (once emerging painted purple, for reasons known only to themselves), and used it as a mobile prison — stashing an unconscious Duergar captive in the never-cleaned stables under the watch of the Astral Goblins.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] and [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] established a tradition of climbing a tree with the box during watches, settling in above the group's camp with the entrance twenty feet off the ground. When Patches mentioned approaching ghouls to Tark during one such watch, the monk charged out of the Tavern in a rage — forgetting he was up a tree. Patches caught him. They both toppled out anyway.
