Claudine is a human woman and former contact of [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] with connections to Bregan D'Aerthe. She was lost to the Muzgardt Duergar clan several years before the party arrived in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]], working as a slave laborer in their brewery and alchemy operation. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] sought her out to find a way into [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]] that was less conspicuous than the obvious routes.

When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] finally located Claudine in the Muzgardt Brewery's alchemy shop, the woman did not initially recognize the Drow. Only when [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] removed her platinum mask did Claudine recognize her old associate, calling her by her true name. Claudine lives with her daughter Marzeline, who works alongside her restocking shelves. Despite being under the watchful eye of their Duergar overseer Seke, Claudine was able to share intelligence about Faen Tlabbar trading operations in the area during their covert meetings.

## The Extraction

As [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] began to unravel under a building demon siege, the group could no longer wait. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] and [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] went to the Muzgardt Brewery directly. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] created a distraction — buying potions from Seke — while [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] slipped into the back and found Claudine and Marzeline doing inventory. She explained: they had to leave now. No warning, no preparation.

Claudine hugged her daughter, smiled nervously, and nodded. They simply walked out.

They gathered as much of their own belongings as they could carry, slipped past Seke while [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] held his attention, and walked casually back to the waiting rickshaw. Seke was none the wiser.

The group escaped [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] through the Whorlstone Caverns and [[bilrigger|Bilrigger]]'s Sanctum, [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] drawing the teleportation circle in chalk while Claudine and Marzeline watched in terrified, uncomprehending silence. When the singularity opened and the group stepped through, the two women were dragged along.

## The Surface

The teleport deposited everyone in [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s wizard tower, still under construction on an island in Selunia. They climbed the staircase to the ground floor and pushed open the double doors.

A soft spring breeze came in. The waxing moon reflected on still water.

Claudine let out a gasp and fell to her knees, sobbing. Marzeline crouched beside her and held her as she wept.

She had not seen moonlight in years.

Claudine has since established her alchemy practice at the Barony, finally working freely.
