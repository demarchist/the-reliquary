'Āina Pulu is the homeland of the halfling people who inhabit the western reaches of Thueba Swamp in Al Askar. The halfling tribes who live here are a deeply traditional people with no metalworking — they were fascinated and terrified by the outlanders' casual use of metal for mundane tasks like cooking.

The community has been torn by an internal schism. The Abyss Walkers — a faction that has existed within the tribe for untold ages — have grown in power as the world has darkened, seizing control and performing human sacrifices to awaken the ancient dragon Hohonu Hāmau from its imprisonment. Those who follow the old ways, led by the shaman Camea, are forced to hide their true allegiances and operate in secret.

'Āina Pulu lies in the direction of the ancient Saurian Machina Factory, making it a waypoint on the party's journey through the swamp.
