## Background

Sebek is the Speaker of the Black Skull, a prominent undead figure in the Cult of Zalaam who serves as the cult's public face in Iyum. Wrapped in linen bandages and cloaked in a black shawl lined with shells and topaz gems, he preaches Zalaam's gospel of ending and rebirth to crowds in the city's plazas.

## Role in the Campaign

Sebek first confronted the party in a public plaza where he was preparing a human sacrifice while preaching to a crowd. Cirrus challenged him publicly, and the Wardens intervened to disperse the gathering. After an assassin in Sebek's retinue stabbed Gundren, the party turned the cult's retreat into an interrogation that revealed the undead nature of the cult's leadership and their headquarters in the Harbour District.

That night, Sebek returned with a thousand mind-controlled civilians chanting "Alhamdu Zalaam," besieging the cantina where the group was drinking with Djoser. The mob was under the influence of Vecna's Corruption — a sickness that would only worsen as Vecna's power grew. The group worked to neutralise the civilians without lethal force, using sleep spells, hypnotic patterns, Gundren's flood of water, and Jareem's psionic cleansing. Sebek deployed assassins and dispelled Gundren's mount, but was ultimately caught in Patches' tar bomb and beaten unconscious by Tark. When his wounds began regenerating, Buumi landed on the rooftop and crushed his skull with his hammer. The Speaker's death dissipated the enchantment over the mob.

## Notable Traits

- Undead cultist with regeneration abilities
- Protected by Mirror Image and capable of Dispel Magic
- Commands assassins and can raise mind-controlled mobs through Vecna's Corruption
- His death released the enchantment controlling the mob
