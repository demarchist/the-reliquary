## Background

Sebek is the Speaker of the Black Skull, a prominent figure in the Cult of Zalaam. As an undead entity, he possesses the ability to regenerate from seemingly fatal wounds, making him a persistent and dangerous foe.

## Role in the Campaign

Sebek preaches the cult's dark gospel in Iyum, spreading corruption and undermining the Usurpers of Rudjek's rule. The party eventually confronts and destroys him in the city.

## Notable Traits

- Undead cultist with regeneration abilities
- Charismatic preacher who spreads the cult's influence
- Speaker and public face of the Black Skull cult
