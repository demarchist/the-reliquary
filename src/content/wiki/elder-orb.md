The Elder Orb is an ancient Beholder inhabiting the deepest reaches of the [[mines-of-und|Mines of Und]] in the Galena Mountains. Unlike the younger aberrations the group destroyed in the village above, the Elder Orb is a creature of immense age and power — capable of warping reality around its lair, projecting psychic influence across an entire settlement, and feeding from sources of magic rather than flesh alone.

## Discovery

After destroying the second Beholder that erupted from a cloaked dwarf in the village of [[und|Und]], [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] lingered in the [[ethereal-plane|Ethereal Plane]] atop [[steden|Steden]] and observed the mines blazing with power — lines of light pulsing through the rock like a heartbeat, visible only from the other side of the veil. The energy was far too strong for an ordinary Beholder.

[[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] identified the source as an Elder Orb — a Beholder that had lived for centuries, its powers deepening with age. Where younger Beholders twist their immediate surroundings, an Elder Orb reshapes reality itself around its lair. The Beholders the group had killed were its children — spawned copies planted within the villagers as agents. The Elder Orb was the true [[the-dreamer|Dreamer]], projecting its will from the depths.

## Feeding

Fëanon explained that while Beholders consume flesh for sustenance, the truly ancient ones can also feed from strong sources of magic — draining enchantments and ley lines. Whatever the Elder Orb found in the mines, it had been feeding from it long enough to thread the surrounding stone with glowing crystal and twist the tunnels into something too perfect to be natural.

## The Lair

The mines surrounding the Elder Orb's lair bear the marks of its influence. The stone is threaded with veins of luminous crystal, the walls unnaturally smooth and symmetrical. Eyes embedded in the rock — closed but dreaming, their orbs moving restlessly beneath the lids — serve as sleeping sentinels, projecting the creature's influence outward. The tunnels are infested with aberrations drawn to its power: Ropers nesting in the upper caverns, a Spectator and a Gauth gathering crystals in the outer chambers, and a twisted spider-creature with a half-humanoid face lurking in the passages.

Most dangerously, the Elder Orb's dreams leak into physical reality. The stone in the tunnels periodically warps and melts without warning, softening like candle wax and dragging creatures down into the rock. The group experienced this twice during their initial descent, barely pulling free before the stone resolidified around them.
