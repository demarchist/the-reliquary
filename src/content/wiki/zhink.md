## Background

Zhink is a Moon Elf warrior who serves as one of Lady Faye's champions. He possesses supernaturally enhanced reflexes that make him a formidable combatant.

## Role in the Campaign

Zhink joins the party during their operations in Baldur's Gate, initially as Lady Faye's representative. He proves to be a capable companion, lending his martial skills to the group's adventures.

## Notable Traits

- Enhanced reflexes beyond normal elven capabilities
- Serves as Lady Faye's champion
- Skilled warrior and reliable ally
