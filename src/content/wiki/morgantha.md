Morgantha, known to locals as Granny, is a hag who operates a pastry cart in Barovia. Her baked goods — made from human flesh — are a horrifying staple of the cursed land. She operates out of the Old Bonegrinder windmill on the road to Vallaki, where the party confronts her and her coven.
