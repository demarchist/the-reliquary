## Background

Vladalma is the Matron Mother of House Faen Tlabbar, one of the noble drow houses of Menzoberranzan. She is the mother of Patches (Aliyah Faen Tlabbar) and exercises her authority with ruthless determination.

## Role in the Campaign

Vladalma operates from the shadows, using agents like Lo'Dan to spy on her daughter Patches and ordering assassination missions that create complications for the party. Her machinations represent the ever-present threat of drow politics reaching into surface affairs.

## Notable Traits

- Matron Mother wielding considerable political power in Menzoberranzan
- Manipulative mother who uses her daughter as a pawn
- Commands a network of spies and assassins
