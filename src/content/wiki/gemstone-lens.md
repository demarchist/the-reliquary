## Description

The Gemstone Lens is a mysterious diamond artifact inscribed with the symbols of various deities. Its precise origins and full capabilities remain subjects of study and speculation.

## Properties

The Lens appears to interact with divine energies, its god-symbols suggesting connections to multiple pantheons. Its potential applications in magical rituals and divine communication make it an artifact of significant interest.
