The Gemstone Lens is a massive diamond — the size of a dinner plate and a good three inches thick — inscribed with old Gnomish script and the symbols of multiple deities: [[selune|Selûne]], [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]], [[mystra|Mystra]], Callarduran, and the Stonefather.

## Discovery

The Lens was presented to the party by [[gurnik|Gurnik]] and the leaders of [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]] after the defeat of [[juiblex|Juiblex]]. [[dorbo|Dorbo]] and his wife [[senni|Senni]] explained that it was ancient and they had no idea what it was. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] and [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] examined it with jewellers' kits, [[snorbul|Snorbul]] noted it was warm to the touch, and [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] — his interest stoked by [[selune|Selûne]]'s symbol — bent to study it intently.

## Properties

The artifact defied easy identification. The best [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] could determine was that it served as a focusing lens for a ritual of considerable power — some manner of spell "scroll" encoded into diamond. Its full capabilities and purpose remain subjects of study.
