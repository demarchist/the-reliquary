The Faerûn Defense Force is a council of powerful adventurers who govern the Barony of Thunder Bluff. Their detractors know them as the Heralds of Woe.

## Members

- **Fëanon Sërindë** — High Elf Wizard
- **Cirrus Al Kayan** — Tiefling Divine Soul Sorcerer
- **Horatio Von Bumidere (Buumi)** — Human Paladin
- **Tarkathee Daru (Tark)** — Halfling Monk
- **Aliyah Faen Tlabbar (Patches)** — Drow Rogue
- **Gundren Rockseeker** — Dwarf Gunslinger
- **Snorbul** — Svirfneblin Barbarian
- **Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews (JmJm)** — Half-Elf Bard
- **Thorna** — Air Genasi Cleric

## Governance

The members of the Faerûn Defense Force live within the Barony of Thunder Bluff and collectively govern it as a council. They commissioned the Keep of Thunder Bluff as a fortress and seat of power, built by skilled dwarven architects and engineers.

## Reputation

The group's dual reputation — heroes to some, heralds of woe to others — reflects the complex and sometimes destructive consequences of their adventuring. Their governance of the Barony is marked by the diverse and often conflicting philosophies of its members.
