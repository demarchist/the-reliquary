## Background

Jadger was a Burrow Warden of Blingdenstone — a member of an elite warrior caste that was entirely wiped out during the Drow invasion a century ago. After death, his ghost lingered in the settlement, one of the few benign spectres among many hostile ones.

## Role in the Campaign

The ghost of the Burrow Warden secretly trained young Deep Gnomes, including Trisk and Jinu, passing on what knowledge he could of the lost Warden traditions. After the group lifted the curse of Blingdenstone by defeating the Stone Priestess and cleansing Ogremoch's Bane, Jadger revealed himself at the tavern celebration — leaping through a table shouting "BOO!" before introducing himself.

He led the group and Trisk through secret tunnels to three hidden armories containing dozens of sets of enchanted weapons, armour, and tomes on Deep Gnome strategy. In the final cache, he pointed out a red-bound book with emeralds in its cover — the lost art of spell-gem tattooing that had once given the Wardens their powers. Trisk swore to learn and refound the order. Jadger smiled, nodded, and transcended.
