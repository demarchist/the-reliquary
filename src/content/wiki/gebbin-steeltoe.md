## Background

Gebbin Steeltoe is a dwarven patrol captain operating in the region around the Rauvin River. He leads a contingent of dwarven soldiers tasked with maintaining security in the area.

## Role in the Campaign

Gebbin's patrol comes to the party's aid at the Rauvin River, providing rescue during a dangerous encounter. His assistance marks the party's entry into the northern dwarven territories.
