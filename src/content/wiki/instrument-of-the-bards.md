An Instrument of the Bards is one of a class of legendary musical instruments crafted by elven loremasters in ancient times, each named for a specific elven house and each capable of channeling significant bardic magic through its strings or pipes. The instruments enhance the spellcasting of any bard who plays them and provide the ability to invoke a number of powerful spells through performance — Charm Person, Suggestion, Mass Suggestion, and various more potent enchantments depending on the specific instrument.

## Recovery

The party found the Instrument in a small ornate chest tucked beneath the balcony of the [[elder-orb|Elder Orb's]] lair, alongside a [[scroll-of-demiplane|Scroll of Demiplane]] and a [[potion-of-supreme-healing|Potion of Supreme Healing]]. The cache was modest by the standards of an aberration that had lived for centuries, which the group attributed to the Elder Orb's earlier displacement from his original Netherese ruin in the north — the bulk of his hoard remained wherever [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] had driven him from, or in the buried ruin itself.

[[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] tucked the Instrument under one arm and walked off with it without breaking eye contact with anyone. No one contested the claim.
