## Background

Megno Slatesunder is a Dwarven Enchanter and explorer affiliated with the Explorers League. His expertise in ancient ruins and magical devices makes him an invaluable guide for dangerous expeditions.

## Role in the Campaign

Megno leads the party's expedition to the Sanctum of Krasus within Shatterpeak mountain, providing knowledge about the ancient Netherese tower and its dangers. He later assists with various construction and enchanting projects for the party.

## Notable Traits

- Member of the Explorers League
- Expert in ancient Netherese architecture and devices
- Skilled enchanter with practical dungeon-delving experience
