The Staff of Defense is a magical staff that was wielded by Iarno, the mage who led the [[redbrand-ruffians|Redbrand Ruffians]] under the alias "Glasstaff" — a name derived from the staff itself. When the party captured Iarno beneath [[tresendar-manor|Tresendar Manor]], they claimed the staff as part of his confiscated belongings.
