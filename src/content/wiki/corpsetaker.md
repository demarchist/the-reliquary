The Corpsetaker is an ancient entity capable of possessing living bodies. It took control of Cercei — Horatio von Bumidere's adopted daughter — and commanded the assault on the Bumidere estate in Neverwinter, floating above the battlefield in red robes with orbs of fire orbiting its head. The voice was unmistakably Cercei's but strangely modulated, cold and calculating.

When confronted by Tarkathee Daru during the attack, Corpsetaker claimed to have consumed Tark's family in order to awaken, suggesting the entity feeds on life force and had been dormant. It demonstrated devastating magical ability — chain lightning, fireball, psychic scream, dispel magic, and feeblemind — far beyond what any child or even most adult mages could wield.

Corpsetaker coordinated with Li Xian the Oni and a team of Amnian strike operatives during the assault. When Aurinax and Anaxtrasa arrived to challenge the ancient red dragon in the harbour, Corpsetaker calculated quickly and ordered a retreat. Cirrus briefly banished the entity, but it broke free almost immediately.

As a parting gift, Corpsetaker struck Cirrus with a feeblemind enchantment, reducing the warlock to a comatose state. The entity escaped with Li Xian and the surviving Amnian agents, and its ability to jump between hosts makes it an elusive and terrifying foe.
