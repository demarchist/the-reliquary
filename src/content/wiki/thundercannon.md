The Thundercannon is [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren Rockseeker]]'s signature weapon — a massive artificed firearm of devastating power and his primary instrument of destruction since the party's earliest adventures. Its distinctive reports have signalled the dwarf's presence in countless battles, from the mines of Wave Echo Cave to the skies over [[al-askar|Al Askar]].

## Design

The weapon fires explosive shells, grapeshot, birdshot, and lightning-imbued rounds from an enormous barrel that Gundren handles with the ease of long practice. Over time he has modified and improved the weapon extensively, adding an underslung grenade launcher and other enhancements through his work at the [[rockseeker-manufactory|Rockseeker Manufactory]]. The weapon drew admiring attention from the dwarven smith [[dogan|Dogan]] at [[gauntlgrym|Gauntlgrym]], who expressed eagerness to see what the Manufactory would produce.

## Notable Kills

The Thundercannon's first dramatic moment came in Wave Echo Cave, where Gundren landed the killing blow on a massive Barlgura demon — a critical shot straight through its chest. When the Black Spider's lieutenant Nezzan was brought to his knees and begged for his life, Gundren — whose brother had died because of the Drow's scheming — told him to suck it, and blew his head off.

The weapon turned an Alhoon's Stone Golem into a greased, helpless wreck; reduced a Banderhobb to a wet pop as the creature clambered from the rubble; blew the zombified head off Kasimir's reanimated corpse after a Finger of Death spell killed him mid-stride; and shredded insect swarms at Berez while Gundren hauled himself from the water, bleeding and swearing.

## Aerial Platform

Gundren's fighting style is built around combining the Thundercannon's long-range firepower with aerial mobility — first astride [[gwen|Guinevere]] and later from the back of his winged owlbear mount [[arkto|Arkto]]. From altitude, he provides devastating fire support, raking enemy positions from above while the rest of the party engages on the ground. He poured lightning-imbued shot into the giant vampire Beerna during the siege of the Wizard of Wines, and conducted fly-overs through the alleys of [[luskan|Luskan]], dropping Grease on pursuing armed groups who panicked and fell flat.
