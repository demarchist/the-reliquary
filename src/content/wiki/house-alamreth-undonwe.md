## History

House Alamreth Undonwe — the House of Starry Twilight — was an ancient Drow house devoted to Eilistraee and Selûné rather than Lolth. Its last Matron Mother, Kallista, sacrificed herself in a divine detonation to save her fleeing people from demonic attack. The resulting blinding of Kallista's Lolth-worshipping daughter triggered an inter-house civil war that destroyed the house almost entirely. A single surviving daughter forged a new house from the remnants — the house that would eventually become House Faen Tlabbar.

## Refounding

In the Cathedral of Twilight at Yggward, the awakened spirit of Kallista blessed Patches — Aliyah — as the new Matron Mother, restoring the ancient house name and severing Patches' ties to House Faen Tlabbar. The ceremony was overseen by Cirrus with divine providence and witnessed by Crisserei the Planetar and the Clerics of the Twinborn Goddesses.

The refounding carries a divine mandate backed by Eilistraee and the Pantheon of the Moon, granting Patches genuine authority as Matron Mother of a house free from Lolth's corruption.
