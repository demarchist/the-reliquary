## Description

Orrah is the goddess of harmony, balance, and time in Al Askar's pantheon of Ancients. She appeared to Buumi in the Ethereal visions of the Temple of Karthus, taking the form of his mother in her younger adventuring days.

## Revelations

Orrah told Buumi he was already on the path to protecting his friends. When he asked how to restore harmony to Al Askar, she directed him to the Black Sea — where the world and the Shadowfell meet — where two of the reagents Feanon needs for his reality-mending ritual could be found.
