## Background

Ingrbotha is the elderly matriarch who leads the frost giant community on Shatterpeak. She commands respect through age, wisdom, and the authority of her position.

## Role in the Campaign

The party encounters Ingrbotha's community during their expedition to Shatterpeak. Navigating frost giant customs and earning the matriarch's trust proves essential for accessing the Sanctum of Krasus within the mountain.

## Notable Traits

- Respected matriarch of the Shatterpeak frost giants
- Oversees community events including the wedding of Ljokjar
- Gatekeeper whose approval is needed for passage through giant territory
