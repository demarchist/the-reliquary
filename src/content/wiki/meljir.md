Meljir is a Frost Giant with bob-cut silver hair and a talent for healing magic. She leads her family group — her father Mimor, her brother or kinsman Ljokjar, and the young Fen — and serves as their spokesperson, casting translation magic to communicate with the party in Common.

When the party intervened to save her family from a gnoll ambush on the frozen lake beneath Shatterpeak, Meljir declared that honour demanded hospitality. She guided the group to the frost giant settlement on the mountain, introduced them to the matriarch Ingrbotha, and took personal responsibility for the small folk during their stay. She shared Eyrak — fermented mammoth milk — with the group, leading to an evening of heavy drinking that loosened even Patches' tongue.

At the wedding feast, Meljir participated in the competitions, stepping forward for the wrestling match. Despite her considerable size advantage, Tark's speed and leverage brought her down, though she conceded gracefully, blaming the drink. She rebuffed Buumi's romantic advances — twice. When the group departed the next morning, they found her passed out under a table. She declined to guide them up the mountain but assured them the giants would keep their sleigh safe.
