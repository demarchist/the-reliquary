## Description

Crisserei is a Planetar in service to Selûné who can manifest in multiple forms. In her Seraphim form, she is a celestial destroyer who fires searing radiant arrows from the sky. In her standard form, she radiates a warm glow — though her shadow is notably dark and cold, hinting at the duality of the Twinborn Goddesses.

## Role

Crisserei serves as Cirrus's primary celestial ally. She presided over the vision in the Cathedral of Twilight, revealing the true history of Kallista's last stand and comforting the ancient Matron Mother. She oversaw Patches' blessing ceremony as Matron Mother of House Alamreth Undonwe.

When she sensed an imminent threat to Cirrus's place of power in the Barony, she provided the group with a Planeshift window to return home. During the subsequent goblin raid, Cirrus summoned her in Seraphim form and she shot bat-riders from the sky with her radiant bow, breaking the aerial assault.
