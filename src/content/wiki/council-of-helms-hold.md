## Overview

The Council of Helm's Hold was a historic assembly of regional faction leaders and power brokers held at the Helm's Hold citadel south of Neverwinter. The council was convened to address the escalating crises threatening Faerûn.

## Participants

Leaders from Silverymoon, Neverwinter, and other major factions gathered to share intelligence and strategize collective action against threats including Zhengyi the Lich, the undead mists, and other supernatural dangers.

## Significance

The council marked a crucial moment of alliance-building among previously fragmented powers, establishing the Faerûn Defense Force and laying the groundwork for coordinated resistance.
