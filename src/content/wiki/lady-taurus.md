## Background

Lady Taurus is the head of House Taurus, a noble family in Ras Alkan that has fallen on harder times. Though she maintains appearances — a walled villa with a courtyard fountain, numerous guards and servants, expensive silks — the house has recently been forced to downsize.

## Role in the Campaign

The party returns Lady Taurus's wayward daughter Hamda after finding the girl's beetle mount wandering the city outskirts. In gratitude, the Lady offers dinner, lodging at the villa during her absence, and payment — in exchange for accepting a job: finding House Taurus's last merchant ship, which has gone missing near the reef. The pirate band known as the Demon Claws is suspected.
