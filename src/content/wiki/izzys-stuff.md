Izzy's Stuff is a magic shop in the merchant ring of [[helioglabulus|Helios]], situated on a quiet cul-de-sac directly across from [[tazmikella|Tazmikella]]'s Odds and Sundries. Where Taz's shop is a converted house full of exquisite oddities priced in centuries, Izzy's is the more impressive establishment of the two — better appointed and stocked with a plethora of magical goods, including extraordinary rarities.

The shop is minded by [[dima|Dima]], a Drow warlock bound to the enigmatic Izzy by a magical anklet. Izzy is described as being away on business, and Dima implies that Izzy is his warlock patron. The true nature of Izzy — mortal, fiend, or something else entirely — remains unknown.

[[tazmikella|Taz]] cited Izzy's competition as the reason for her own shop's going-out-of-business sale, though her mild annoyance on the subject carried the weight of someone who had seen empires rise and fall and found this particular inconvenience merely amusing.

The group conducted a significant trade at the shop, exchanging the [[saurian-deathlord-greatsword|Saurian Deathlord's greatsword]] and other valuables for a jar of [[elder-brain-ichor|Elder Brain Ichor]] — the final ingredient [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] needed for his [[windrunner-armor|Windrunner Armor]].
