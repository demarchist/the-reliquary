Galen Marek is a spy who operates out of the Fallen Tower Inn in Neverwinter, maintaining a network of armed operatives within the establishment. He first approached Tark at the bar, introducing himself as a man looking for help securing the city from evildoers — a claim that barely concealed his true profession.

After the party killed the Red Grin Killer — the warlock Strigu — Galen provided practical support, mobilising his men with crossbows and coordinating the response. As thanks for saving Jake, dealing with the warlock, and agreeing to investigate the slavers connected to the Red Belt, Galen arranged a private suite for the group at The Long House, a luxury spa in the Protector's Enclave. He also provided intelligence about the slaving operation, noting that captives from the Red Belt were being shipped to markets via the docks.

Galen appears well-connected throughout Neverwinter's underworld, operating alongside the Dead Rats thieves' guild while maintaining his own agenda. His willingness to work with the party suggests he views them as valuable assets in the power dynamics of the city.
