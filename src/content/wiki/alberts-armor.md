Albert's Armor is an armour shop in the Ward of Artifice in Sundabar, run by Albert Bubblegump. Albert is the spitting image of his brother Gilbert, though considerably taller and sporting a fancy mithril waistcoat.

Cirrus Al Kayan visited the shop on Gilbert's recommendation after the group discovered — to their collective astonishment — that the warlock had been adventuring without any armour. Cirrus found an excellent suit of enchanted leather and negotiated a significant discount by trading away plate armour and a few grenades.
