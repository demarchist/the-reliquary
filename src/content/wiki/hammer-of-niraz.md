The Hammer of Niraz is a legendary artifact weapon of [[al-askar|Al Askar]], wielded by [[djoser|Djoser]], Grand Warden of [[iyum|Iyum]]. It glittered across his back as a symbol of the [[usurpers-of-rudjek|Usurpers']] authority after the revolution against the vampire lord Rudjek.

## Acquisition

The party learned of the Hammer from [[jareem|Jareem]], who identified it as a relic powerful enough to serve as the Icon of Power in [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s Ritual of Harmony. After a sweeping negotiation with Sultan Bashir in [[iyum|Iyum]], the party exchanged food, medicine, equipment, and promises to breach the Golden Tower's wards and cleanse the Dark Spire in return for the Hammer and other concessions. Djoser handed it to [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]], who passed it to [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] — who inspected it and declared it unfinished. It could be completed at the [[titanic-forge|Titanic Forge]], but would need more titanium.

## Notable Use

When [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] became lost in the [[tome-of-al-azif|Tome of Al Azif]]'s corruption, Buumi rushed over with the Hammer of Niraz and brought it down on the dark tome in a devastating smite, cracking one of its covers with a burst of screams and necrotic energy.
