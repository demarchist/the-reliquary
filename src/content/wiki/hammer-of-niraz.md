## Description

The Hammer of Niraz is a legendary artifact weapon wielded by Djoser, Grand Warden of Iyum. It carries significant magical power and historical importance to the people of Al Askar.

## Properties

The Hammer serves as both a weapon of war and a ritual implement. Its magical properties make it instrumental in Fëanon's rituals, suggesting it possesses power beyond simple combat applications.

## History

The Hammer of Niraz was wielded by Djoser during the revolution against the vampire lord Rudjek and continues to serve as a symbol of the Usurpers' authority in Iyum.
