The Silent Virtue was a three-masted galleon moored in Neverwinter harbour, ostensibly a trading vessel representing Luskan and Ship Rethnor. In reality it served as Captain Vaas Redblade's base of operations for a slaving ring that trafficked people from Neverwinter's slums.

The ship featured a masterfully concealed compartment beneath the hold — a hidden space no more than three feet tall running the length of the vessel, accessed through a disguised metal hatch beneath the floorboards. Thirty-two prisoners were found chained within when the party boarded.

The group assaulted the ship in a night raid, with Cirrus disguising himself as Vaas to sow confusion among the crew while the others approached by boat. The battle left the ship damaged — icicles hanging from every mid-deck window after Cirrus's cone of cold — but intact. It was remanded into the custody of the Neverwinter City Watch, with the group offered first right of refusal to purchase it at a discount.
