## Description

Magistrate Li is the chief civil administrator of the Barony of Thunder Bluff, handling legal matters, governance, and liaison with Silverymoon. She operates from the Keep of Thunder Bluff and has served the Barony through its rapid growth from frontier outpost to thriving community.

## Role

Li coordinates civilian affairs including land grants, trade agreements, and formal treaties with external powers such as Silverymoon. She manages the day-to-day governance of the Barony while the Faerûn Defense Force handles military and adventuring matters. During the goblin raid on the Barony, she coordinated the defence from the security of the Keep.
