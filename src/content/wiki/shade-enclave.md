Shade Enclave is a [[netherese|Netherese]] floating city that survived the catastrophic fall of Netheril by retreating into the [[shadowfell|Shadowfell]], where it endured for centuries while the empire's other enclaves crashed to earth and the land below became the Anauroch desert. Once the pinnacle of Netherese civilisation — a meritocracy of archwizards and scholars — the Enclave devolved during its long exile in the Plane of Shadow into an aristocracy, its ruling class more concerned with bloodlines than brilliance. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] was disappointed to learn of this decline when he spoke with [[lady-avelyere|Lady Avelyere]] at the Council of Helm's Hold.

## Operations

The Enclave maintains a quiet but deliberate presence on the surface. [[lady-avelyere|Lady Avelyere]] serves as its diplomatic envoy, arriving at the Barony of [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]] in an ornate black carriage flanked by Shadowmonk bodyguards. The Enclave's Coven — its inner circle of arcanists — personally acquires Netherese artefacts wherever they surface, and Lady Avelyere proposed a standing contract with [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]: all Netherese artefacts in his possession, including the [[iron-tome|Iron Tome]] and his robes, to be returned upon his natural death. Fëanon said he would consider it.

Near Myth Drannor, the Enclave operates the Way of the Pointed Step — a monastic tradition led by Master Jorell, a vampire who supposedly never needs to feed. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] defeated one of Lady Avelyere's Shadowmonk bodyguards in a sparring match and was invited to visit the monastery and train there.

## Interests

The Enclave's overriding concern is the recovery of Netherese cultural heritage — [[mythallar|Mythallars]], robes, medallions, scrolls, and other artefacts scattered across [[faerun|Faerûn]] since the fall. Lady Avelyere carefully avoided discussing Mythallars during her visit to the Barony, and Fëanon was equally careful not to reveal that he possessed one in an unfinished state. The Enclave agreed to send nobles to patronise [[selunia|Selunia]]'s establishment and auction house — commerce as a vector for artifact recovery, wrapped in the courtesies of diplomacy.
