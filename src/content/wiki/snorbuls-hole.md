Snorbul's Hole is a labyrinthine complex of underground tunnels and chambers located within the Barony of Thunder Bluff. It is owned, operated, and championed by Snorbul himself.

## Surface

At surface level, Snorbul's Hole is an unassuming cavity surrounded by piles of gravel and loose stones — giving no hint of the bustling complex below. It is, by design, hard to find if you don't already know where to look.

## The Descent

The entrance opens onto a steep, winding tunnel that leads downward into the darkness. As the descent continues, the air grows thicker and more humid. What little torchlight exists casts deep, flickering shadows across damp, rough-hewn walls, giving the impression of entering the very heart of the earth. The pungent scents of sweat and stone thicken with each step. Before the arena comes into view, it announces itself — the sounds of cheering crowds, metal clashing on metal, grunts of effort, and the occasional triumphant roar echoing up through the tunnels.

## The Arena

The tunnels open into a cavernous chamber filled with rowdy spectators surrounding a fighting pit. The atmosphere is raw and primal — a pressure in the air that distinguishes it from any surface venue. Martial competitions run continuously, some governed by Snorbul's strictly enforced rules, others no-holds-barred. Gambling is strongly encouraged. The crowd's energy is genuine and unfiltered; people here respond to the fight itself, not to ceremony or presentation.

As a "Down-wizard" style complex (though built by a Barbarian rather than a wizard), Snorbul's Hole reflects its creator's preference for underground spaces and his Svirfneblin heritage.
