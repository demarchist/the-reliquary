The Deck of Illusions is a magical item consisting of a set of painted cards, each depicting a different creature. When a card is drawn and thrown to the ground, it creates a lifelike illusion of the creature depicted, complete with appropriate sounds and movements. The illusions cannot physically interact with the environment or deal damage, but are convincing enough to fool enemies in combat. Each card can only be used once, making the deck a finite resource.

## Patches' Use

The Deck became one of [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' signature tools, used to sow chaos and buy time in combat. During a battle with soldiers, she spun a card into the oncoming ranks and a roaring hill giant materialised in their midst, scattering the formation. In the [[underdark|Underdark]], she threw a card into a mass of charging cultists, conjuring a hulking human druid with deer antlers and thick leather armor that drew fire away from the party. She also deployed cards during covert operations in [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]], using the illusions as distractions while skulking through hostile territory.
