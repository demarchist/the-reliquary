Kasimir is a dusk elf mage living in Barovia who is consumed by the desire to resurrect his sister Patrina. Patrina was once a suitor of Strahd Von Zarovich, but was turned to stone by her own people to prevent the union. Her remains are imprisoned in the crypts of Castle Ravenloft. Kasimir's desperate quest provides both aid and complications for the party.
