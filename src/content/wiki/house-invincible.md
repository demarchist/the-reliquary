## Description

The House Invincible is a fortress cathedral dedicated to the god Helm, located in Silverymoon. It combines the grandeur of a cathedral with the defensive capabilities of a military fortification, fronted by the Plaza of the Watchers.

## Significance

During the Battle of Silverymoon, the House Invincible and its paladins serve as a crucial defensive position against the Amnian invaders. The Plaza of the Watchers before it becomes the site of the climactic battle that repels the coup attempt.
