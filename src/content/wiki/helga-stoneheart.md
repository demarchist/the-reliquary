Helga Stoneheart is a dwarven mason who arrived at Megno Slatesunder's workshop in Sundabar alongside Forgefather Halfhand, tasked with overseeing the construction of the party's castle at the Barony.

A practical professional, Helga was mildly annoyed upon learning she would need to work alongside orcs at the construction site, but accepted the arrangement without serious protest. She presented the group with a ledger for signature to formalise the contract, then departed with a team of dwarven miners and scouts — led by the blue goblin Pickles — to begin work at the Barony.
