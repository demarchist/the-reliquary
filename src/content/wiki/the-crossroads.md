## Description

The Crossroads is a hub location at the convergence of multiple planes — where Svartalfheim, Nidavellir, the Shadowfell, and the Astral Sea meet. It sits on a vast floating island of stone amid shattered rock and drifting crystal, with the Astral Sea glimmering through the gaps.

Two paths diverge from the Crossroads: one loops down into the increasingly chaotic floating islands of Nidavellir, and the other leads across a corkscrewing stone arch to the Cathedral of Twilight — the gateway to Yggward, the Drow necropolis.

## Ringed Veils Network

The Crossroads also features a gate platform, a barn, and an inn, serving as a waypoint in the Ringed Veils (Fargate) network — the ancient Saurian teleportation system that connects worlds and planes.

## Significance

The party first uses The Crossroads as a staging point during their planar travels through Al Askar. They return later to escort a treasure caravan from Dokkar and visit the Cathedral of Twilight, where Patches receives the blessing of her ancestor Kallista and is named Matron Mother of House Alamreth Undonwe.
