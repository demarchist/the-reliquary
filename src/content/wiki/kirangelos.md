Kirangelos is an ancient Behir dwelling in the subterranean caverns of [[svartalfheim|Svartalfheim]] on the plane of Ysgard. The many-legged beast encountered [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] during the warlock's extraplanar exploration through the [[well-of-many-worlds|Well of Many Worlds]]. Curious rather than hostile, Kirangelos coaxed a pearl necklace from [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and in exchange revealed his location — [[svartalfheim|Svartalfheim]], the subterranean realm of the plane of Ysgard, near the [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]]-devoted [[drow-faction|Drow]] city of [[dokkar|Dokkar]].
