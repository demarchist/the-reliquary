## Background

Shaahir is a powerful Amnian Archmage who serves as the primary magical force behind the Amnian invasion of Silverymoon. He is identified by his distinctive solid gold staff.

## Role in the Campaign

Shaahir provides the arcane firepower for the Amnian coup attempt, using his considerable magical abilities to challenge Silverymoon's own magical defenses and the Spellguard.

## Notable Traits

- Powerful archmage in Amnian service
- Carries a distinctive solid gold staff
- Key magical asset in the Battle of Silverymoon
