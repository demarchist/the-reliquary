The Efreeti Bottle is a magical container found in the upper chambers of the Golden Tower in [[iyum|Iyum]], disguised as a crystal slab among a collection of powerful spell scrolls, an Ioun Stone, and fourteen extremely valuable gems. It serves as a prison for an Ifrit known as the Insufferable One.

The party discovered the bottle after fighting a tentacled abomination embedded in the ceiling of the chamber. When they identified the crystal slab's true nature, negotiations with the Insufferable One began — but the Ifrit's difficult personality quickly convinced the group to let the creature stew and move on to more pressing matters.
