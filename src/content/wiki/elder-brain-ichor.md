Elder Brain Ichor is a rare alchemical substance derived from an Illithid Elder Brain. [[dima|Dima]], the shopkeeper at [[izzys-stuff|Izzy's Stuff]] in [[helioglabulus|Helios]], described it as the only specimen he had ever seen — remarking that Elder Brains do not normally part with their ichor.

The ichor is the final ingredient required to forge the [[windrunner-armor|Windrunner Armor]], a set of legendary schematics [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] has been assembling components for across years of travel. The group acquired a single large purple jar of the substance in trade, exchanging the [[saurian-deathlord-greatsword|Saurian Deathlord's greatsword]], the Signet of [[house-faen-tlabbar|House Faen Tlabbar]], a copy of the armor schematics, and other goods.
