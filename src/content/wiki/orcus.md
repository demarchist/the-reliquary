Orcus is the Demon Lord known as the Prince of Undeath and the primary cosmic antagonist of the campaign. From his throne of skulls in the Abyss, the goat-skulled, bat-winged monstrosity drives the overarching conflict — commanding the necromantic mists, dispatching his Children to seize the fragments of [[the-last-word|The Last Word]], and working to bring [[atropos|Atropos]], the Planet Born Dead, into the Prime Material Plane.

## The Artifact Shards

Orcus seeks to collect the fragments of The Last Word — an ancient artifact of Creation that was used to defeat him in ages past. Ancient vault murals beneath [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]]'s Chasm depict him wielding a radiant artifact and skull staff before being overthrown by celestials, who split the artifact into pieces and scattered them across the multiverse. His servants race to recover them before the party can.

## The Children of Orcus

Orcus commands a hierarchy of powerful undead servants known as his Children:

- **[[kangaxx|Kangaxx]]** — the Golden Lich, who infiltrated the party and manipulated them into carrying him through the vault's wards to seize the [[artifact-shard|artifact shard]]. Destroyed when the [[atropos|Atropal]] detonated above [[baldurs-gate|Baldur's Gate]].
- **[[zhengyi|Zhengyi]]** — the Witch-King, a former Red Wizard of Thay who became an enormously powerful lich with Orcus's patronage. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] saw, through the artifact's vision, Orcus pushing Zhengyi back into the world through sheer divine might.
- **[[strahd-von-zarovich|Strahd Von Zarovich]]** — the vampire lord of [[barovia|Barovia]], confirmed by [[mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]] as an ally of Orcus.

## The Necromantic Mists

The necromantic mists that plague the world — raising the dead, destabilizing entire regions — radiate from Atropos as the elder evil draws nearer to the Prime Material Plane. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] concluded that the spontaneous reanimation, the Thayan attacks, and the mists all stemmed from the same source. Orcus commands these forces as instruments of terror, softening the world for what is to come.

## Servants and Agents

Beyond the Children, Orcus fields an army of fiends and undead. His Manes of Thanatos — silent, swarming fiends — poured through a collapsing barrier in the Sanctum of Krasus. A Hierophant of Annihilation, one of Orcus's own barons, was encountered in the [[underdark|Underdark]] — fifteen feet tall even hunched, wearing a circlet of gold with blade-like bars masking a void where its face should be. [[snorbul|Snorbul]] recognised it from childhood tales.

## The Prophecy

The Balgura Prophecy foretells his return: *"The Planet Born Dead waxes... The Goat Prince commands and his children are reborn; one of Gold, one of Adamantite and one of Obsidian."* The Spectators of [[wave-echo-cave|Wave Echo Cave]] first speculated that the "Goat Prince" referred to Orcus — a suspicion confirmed many times over as the campaign unfolded.

## The Cosmic Threat

Mordenkainen warned that Orcus and his servants had made a concentrated effort to neutralise anyone powerful enough to oppose them — the archmage's own imprisonment in Barovia was part of the greater plan. Even [[vecna|Vecna]], operating independently on [[al-askar|Al Askar]], feared becoming a slave to Orcus. As Lemerlais laid it out plainly: Atropos and Orcus would not stop with one planet — they would hunt anyone powerful enough to oppose them.
