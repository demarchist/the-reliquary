## Background

Lemerlais is a High Elf woman who serves as the House Wizard for Lady Faye. She is a skilled practitioner of magic who manages the arcane needs of Lady Faye's household.

## Role in the Campaign

Lemerlais assists the party through her connection to Lady Faye, providing magical support and resources when needed. Her presence reflects Lady Faye's investment in maintaining powerful magical allies.
