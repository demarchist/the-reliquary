Lemerlais is a high elf woman with long blood-red hair, light caramel skin, and faintly glowing green eyes. She serves as Lady Faye's house wizard, replacing whatever arrangement preceded her. She was introduced to the party aboard The Bearded Lass II in Neverwinter's harbour, wearing a crimson gown trimmed in gold.

Fëanon Serinde recognised the name — Lemerlais was the name of an elvish pirate queen from before the Spellplague, over a century earlier. Why someone bearing that name would now serve Lady Faye remains an open question. She greeted Cirrus as "Lord" Cirrus, already aware of his title, suggesting access to intelligence well beyond casual acquaintance.

During the briefing aboard the ship, Lemerlais provided critical strategic analysis. She reported that dark messengers from Zhengyi had been intercepted — promises of lichdom and undeath offered to leaders around the world, sowing distrust among potential allies. When Fëanon argued the group could simply flee rather than fight, Lemerlais laid out the truth: Atropos and Orcus would hunt anyone powerful enough to oppose them. Flight would be temporary at best.
