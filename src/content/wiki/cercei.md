Cercei is a young girl residing at the Bumidere estate under the care of [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]]'s family. A black dragon hatchling who hatched while the party was in [[barovia|Barovia]], she was placed under the tutelage of Mr. Shon — actually the Oni [[li-xian|Li Xian]] in disguise. Despite starting as quite a handful, Cercei improved significantly under Shon's guidance. She is often seen wearing flowing red dresses with elaborate bows, and has a headstrong personality, once spotted berating her tutor while he hung his head in apparent shame.

## The Simulacrum

[[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s wild-magic-gifted truesight revealed that both Cercei and the Amnian wizard Leigham were Simulacra — clay puppets, not the real individuals. Li Xian had stolen [[val-tengu|Val Tengu]]'s truesight lantern and placed a Geas on the security chief to prevent him from questioning the gaps. The false Cercei wielded modify memory magic far beyond what a copy of a child should possess. When [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] blasted the simulacrum, it exploded into clay. The real children were evacuated to [[sundabar|Sundabar]].

## True Nature

Buumi harbours a desire to "save" Cercei from becoming evil like other chromatic dragons. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] meditated over the real Cercei, asking [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]] to help the dragon overcome the worst of what had been done to her. The Corpsetaker later possessed the real Cercei, adding another layer of tragedy to Buumi's protective bond with the young dragon.
