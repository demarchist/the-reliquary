The Grey Ghosts are a thieves' guild operating in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]], the Duergar city of the deep [[underdark|Underdark]]. They specialize in brokering stolen goods, sensitive information, and high-value items that cannot move through legitimate channels in the tightly controlled city. The Grey Ghosts maintain connections across multiple factions — including the Derro Savants — making them useful intermediaries for those who need things acquired or made to disappear.

The group learned that the Grey Ghosts had come into possession of a stolen Red Dragon Egg, likely acquired through or on behalf of the Council of Derro Savants. The egg's true value to whoever commissioned the theft was unclear, but the ownership dispute between the thieves' guild and the Flameguard added another volatile element to [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]]'s already fraught politics.
