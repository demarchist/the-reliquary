The Duergar, or grey dwarves, are a subterranean race of dwarves dwelling deep in the [[underdark|Underdark]]. Enslaved long ago by the [[illithid-faction|Mind Flayers]], they eventually won their freedom but were forever changed by the experience — grim, humourless, and cruel. They possess innate psionic abilities, including the power to turn invisible and enlarge themselves, and their society is built around industry, mining, and slave labour.

## Gracklstugh

The party encountered the Duergar extensively in [[gracksuluth|Gracklstugh]], the Dark Iron City. From the merchant Hemeth who provided river transport across the [[darklake|Darklake]] to the [[stoneguard|Stoneguard]] military forces, the Duergar represented both allies of convenience and potential threats. The city's complex political landscape — rival factions, corrupt officials, and a paranoid ruling council — forced the party to navigate carefully while pursuing their goals.

## The Chardalyn Mines

Duergar miners operated extensively in ancient Netherese [[chardalyn|Chardalyn]] mines, extracting the magic-absorbing crystal for their own purposes. The party clashed with Duergar mining operations in the Underdark, discovering letters confirming additional mines far to the north past the Spine of the World. Clan Sunblight's operations in Icewind Dale represented a northern extension of these mining ambitions.
