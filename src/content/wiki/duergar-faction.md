The Duergar, or grey dwarves, are a subterranean race of dwarves dwelling deep in the [[underdark|Underdark]]. Enslaved long ago by the [[illithid-faction|Mind Flayers]], they eventually won their freedom but were forever changed by the experience — grim, humourless, and cruel. They possess innate psionic abilities, including the power to turn invisible and enlarge themselves, and their society is built around industry, mining, and slave labour.

## Gracklstugh

The party encountered the Duergar extensively in [[gracksuluth|Gracklstugh]], the Dark Iron City. The merchant Hemeth provided river transport across the [[darklake|Darklake]] on his somewhat decrepit Duergar steamship, and the city's complex political landscape — rival factions, corrupt officials, and a paranoid ruling council — forced the party to navigate carefully. [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] was recognised as kin by the grey dwarves, which opened some doors and slammed others.

## Sloobludop

At Sloobludop, a Deep Father cult had captured a Duergar merchant and caged him near a sacrificial altar. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] stepped into [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]]'s line of fire to prevent bloodshed and asked for a chance to free the prisoner peacefully. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] slipped through the city on her spider-silk bracers to reach the cage from behind. The freed Duergar vanished instantly — a small bat appeared in the cage, flapped twice, and flew toward the north dock and his barge.

## The Chardalyn Mines

Duergar miners operated extensively in ancient Netherese [[chardalyn|Chardalyn]] mines, extracting the magic-absorbing crystal for their own purposes. The party clashed with Duergar mining operations in the Underdark, discovering letters confirming additional mines far to the north past the Spine of the World. Clan Sunblight's operations in Icewind Dale represented a northern extension of these mining ambitions.

## Duergar Raiding

A Duergar band was discovered operating a portal network through which they intercepted supply convoys between [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]] and Gracklstugh. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] investigated the portal chamber beneath a surface estate where the Duergar had impersonated a human lord, uncovering the extent of their raiding operations. Their craftsmanship — including the scale-work on Displacer Beast armour spotted in the tunnels approaching Menzoberranzan — was immediately recognisable to the party's more experienced members.
