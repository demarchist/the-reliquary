## Background

Hazzim is a low-level cultist from Al Askar who was once a member of the Cult of Zalaam (Black Skull). Disillusioned with the cult's corruption, he defects and seeks a new path.

## Role in the Campaign

After defecting from the cult, Hazzim provides the party with valuable intelligence about the Cult of Zalaam's operations and structure. He later appears as a friend and ally during the party's extended adventures in Al Askar.

## Notable Traits

- Former Cult of Zalaam member turned defector
- Provides insider knowledge about cult operations
- Shows courage in breaking from a dangerous organization
