## Background

Hazzim is a survivor from Al Askar who was drawn into the Cult of Zalaam (Black Skull) out of desperation. He is a friend of Raveel, another reluctant cultist. Disillusioned with the cult's corruption, he defected and was discovered by the party in the Saurian mines.

## Role in the Campaign

Hazzim provides the party with valuable intelligence about the Cult of Zalaam's operations, including lore about Zalaam as a god of death and darkness whose balance was shattered by the Saurians a thousand years ago. He identifies the Alghoul — a legendary creature that spawns lesser ghouls — when it stalks the party through the mines.

During the assault on the cult's fortified Ringed Veil chamber, Hazzim sheltered inside the Delzoun Instant Fortress. Cirrus secretly placed him in a demiplane for safekeeping during the chaotic escape through the Veil to Al Askar.

## Notable Traits

- Former Cult of Zalaam member turned defector
- Keeper of Al Askar's oral traditions and legends
- Friend of Raveel, another reluctant cultist
- Shows courage in breaking from a dangerous organization
