## Description

The Bog is a vast, otherworldly swamp that exists beneath the Tavern of Doors. This strange landscape is populated by sentient mushrooms, bizarre creatures, and scattered ruins from forgotten ages. Goblin Town sits within the Bog, along with the constantly rearranging Labyrinth.
