The Phaerimm are an ancient and terrible aberration race — conical creatures with gaping maws and four arms — considered enemies of all creation. Wherever they appear, all other races must band together to fight them or face annihilation. They had not been seen in millennia by the time the party encountered their remains.

The Phaerimm possess the terrifying ability to reroute and consume magic, making them formidable opponents even for powerful spellcasters. Their ghostly remnants retained this ability, devouring spells directed at them during battle.

The party discovered evidence of the Phaerimm's ancient war against the Saurians in a frozen fortress within Thueba Swamp. The ruins contained the mummified corpses of Saurians, elves, and Phaerimm alike — remnants of a desperate battle that ended in catastrophe when an elven mage's failed summoning unleashed a shadow spirit that imprisoned everyone within. The Saurians had encountered the Phaerimm even in Faerûn, suggesting a conflict that spanned multiple worlds.
