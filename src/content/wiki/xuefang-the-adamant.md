Xuefang the Adamant is an Ancient White Dragon from Shou Lung and a member of the Dragonflight. Their humanoid form is that of a White Dragonborn.

Xuefang is fiercely independent, consistently hunting or scouting alone rather than resting with the group. They refused to be contained in the abandoned Blue Dragon lair in the desert, instead hunting and claiming a monster's canyon lair for the night. Later, they hunted alone on a glacier in the Bloodstone highlands. When the Dragonflight landed at Bloodstone City, Xuefang flew north to scope the tundra — and was the first to notice the ominous clouds and volcanic eruption that signaled something had gone wrong with Themberchaud's lair.
