Urmas is one of the three Stone Giant archivists who maintain [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]], the legendary demi-plane library. She serves as the Archivist of the Present, residing in a massive ruby sphere where she reclines on pillows, sometimes smoking a giant-sized pipe while viewing visions of the current state of the multiverse. Though the archivists maintain a policy of non-interference, Urmas broke protocol to share that the vision of the deep pit of corruption pointed to the Planet Gaia — a world so distant that few pathways still connected it to Faerûn.
