Aurelian, also called Althul the Protector, is an Ancient Gold Dragon from Shou Lung and a member of the Dragonflight. He is the group's moral counterweight — where others seek worship or plunder, Aurelian seeks to minimize harm.

When Vakuun anointed a Tabaxi villager for his cult, Aurelian quietly turned around and incinerated the chosen before rejoining the group. When the Rock Gnome archmage used a Ring of Wishes to expel the Dragonflight, Aurelian resisted the magic and remained behind to tend to refugees — a gesture Fellasin noted before choosing to spare the city itself, channeling his fury against only the archmage. Aurelian rejoined the group alongside Fellasin, continuing west.
