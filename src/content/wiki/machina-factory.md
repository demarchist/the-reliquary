The Machina Factory is an ancient Saurian manufacturing facility deep within Thueba Swamp — a grand edifice slowly sinking into the mire. Originally built for the mass production of Machina constructs, it was later commandeered by the Lady in White, a powerful artificer serving Vecna, who repurposed it for her own horrific experiments.

## Layout

The Factory is a multi-level underground complex accessed through a fortified main entrance. The upper level contains a cafeteria, a prison, a chemical warehouse, an arcanist's study (the Lady's quarters), metal storage lockers, and a ferrofluid-guarded passage to the lower levels. A Firelance turret emplacement overlooks the main foyer.

The lower levels house barracks, a surgery room, an incinerator disguised as a crystal-coated chamber, and a massive automated foundry equipped with conveyors, smelters, and crucibles. Beneath the foundry lies a vast hangar containing obsidian Machina databases and a disassembled Ringed Veil.

An ancient Saurian transport tunnel connects the Factory to a broader underground network, with the buried remains of a brass transport train partially accessible from the swamp above.

## The Lady's Experiments

Under the Lady in White's direction, the Factory produced Machina-biological hybrids — Alghouls fused with Machina components capable of concussive blasts or Beholder-like eyebeams, cloned subjects implanted with volatile Sourcework Cores, and other abominations. Secret cloning vats were hidden within the Lady's study, and a surgery room was used for implanting Machina into living subjects.

## The Party's Infiltration

The party reached the Factory through the underground Saurian tunnels after fighting through a Kraken and a Machina-infused Mimic. They found that the Lady in White had recently withdrawn her forces, leaving behind Mezzoloth mercenaries, experimental subjects, and at least three massive hidden bombs. After battling through the Factory's defenders and traps, the group located the Ringed Veil in the deepest hangar and recovered the address to Faerûn from the obsidian database.

Vecna confronted Feanon directly in a Time Stop as the doors to the hangar melted from an outside assault. The group escaped via a Contingency teleport to Iyum moments before the Lady in White detonated her bombs, destroying the entire facility.
