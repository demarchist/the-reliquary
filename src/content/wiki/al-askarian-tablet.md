The Al'Askarian Tablet is a communication device originating from the world of [[al-askar|Al Askar]], carried by [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] in his [[bag-of-holding|Bag of Holding]]. The tablet can receive and send messages and alerts its user to waiting communications through physical contact.

## The Message

During the group's journey along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]], Fëanon brushed against the tablet while reaching into his bag and discovered a waiting message: *"You have a wonderful home."*

The message sent Fëanon into a panic. He teleported immediately back to [[feanons-tower|his Tower]] to check for intrusions, called an emergency meeting of all residents, and with the help of Grymedire, duplicated the message onto a larger display for the group to analyze. No breach was found, and Maya suggested the message might simply be a friendly overture — a "Tome and Chill?" — rather than a threat.

Fëanon responded with *"Perhaps we could collab?"* and resolved to monitor the tablet and the tower more closely.
