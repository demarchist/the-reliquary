The Harpers are a secretive organization operating across Faerûn. They train and deploy skilled agents — proficient in combat, espionage, and subterfuge — to carry out missions in service of their goals.

## Known Agents

Ella is a notable Harper agent encountered during the campaign. Raised and trained by the Harpers from a young age, she exemplifies the organization's approach of molding operatives from childhood into formidable and versatile agents. Her eventual crisis of faith and personal transformation speaks to the moral complexities inherent in the Harpers' methods.
