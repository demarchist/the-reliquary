The Lady in White is a powerful artificer-wizard who commanded the Machina Factory deep in Thueba Swamp. Draped in white robes with a white veil and a wreath of white roses, she oversaw a programme of horrific experimentation — fusing Saurian Machina technology with humanoid subjects and Shadowfell creatures to create weaponised abominations.

Her facility produced Alghoul-Machina hybrids, Beholder-Alghoul fusions, cloned subjects implanted with Sourcework Cores, and other volatile creations. A secret laboratory within the Factory contained exquisitely crafted cloning vats, one holding what appeared to be a clone of the Lady herself.

When the party infiltrated the Factory, the Lady had already withdrawn her forces — leaving behind Mezzoloth mercenaries, her experimental subjects, and at least three massive bombs hidden throughout the lower levels. Feanon scried on her using flesh cut from her clone and saw her floating high above the Factory, hand raised mid-snap, waiting to detonate the charges.

The group escaped via a Contingency teleport moments before Vecna confronted them directly. The resulting explosion destroyed the entire Factory. Whether the Lady in White survived the blast or perished with her creations remains unknown.
