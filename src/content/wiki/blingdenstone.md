Blingdenstone is a [[svirfneblin-faction|Svirfneblin]] (Deep Gnome) city in the [[underdark|Underdark]], accessed through a grand staircase a hundred and fifty feet wide ascending into warm, crystal-lit stone. The settlement's stonework is covered in subtle whorls and patterns mimicking natural forms, buildings carved into cavern walls with rope bridges spanning between them. Pools of coloured light fall from crystal formations, fire-beetle lanterns hang in darker areas, and the air carries a faint smell of minerals, water, and cinnamon.

## Leadership

The city is led by Daco [[diggermattock|Diggermattock]] from the Diggermattock manor, a tiered structure with garnet veins running through its walls. High Priest [[gurnik|Gurnik]] Tapfingers, Priest of Callarduran Smoothhands, commands the city's Earth Elementals and spiritual defenses. [[snorbul|Snorbul]]'s uncle [[gruttag|Gruttag]] lives in the city — his reports were grim: Blingdenstone was tested every day, people lost daily, and some were acting strangely, distant and quiet, as if something were working on them.

## The Haunting

The city is full of ghosts — remnants of the Drow invasion that destroyed the Warden warrior caste. Some are benign, pointing the living to hidden valuables. Others are dangerous — the Screamer haunts a building that could house dozens, refusing to let anyone inside. When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] and Snorbul staged a fistfight at the Foaming Mug, hostile ghosts attacked. Snorbul's drunken mind resisted possession, feeling the ghost's grief and hatred of Drow. Patches let a sorrowful ghost possess her willingly, showing it [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]]'s devotion running through her — nothing the ghost had expected from a Drow.

## Rockblight and the Stone Priestess

Rockblight is a sealed section of cavern beyond the barricades, plagued by Ogremoch's Bane — a corruption that drives Earth Elementals into mindless rage, preventing Gurnik from sending his most powerful servants inside. The Stone Priestess — a Medusa Drow wreathed in red lightning, surrounded by ranks of petrified Drow and guarded by a massive armoured basilisk — ruled the Rockblight from a delusional court. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] learned through communion with Eilistraee that the settlement was cursed by [[lolth|Lolth]], tied to the Stone Priestess who had once served the Spider Queen.

The party earth-glided into Rockblight inside Earth Elementals, circumventing the defenses over an hour of swimming through solid stone. They destroyed the Stone Priestess, lifting the curse — gargoyles crumbled, ghosts began speaking words of kindness and pointing to hidden valuables before passing on. Gurnik and Diggermattock began planning a push into the reclaimed caverns.

## The Battle of Blingdenstone

The party's arrival coincided with escalating ooze incursions from the north-east, connected to [[juiblex|Juiblex]], Demon Lord of Oozes. Gelatinous Cubes appeared inside the settlement itself — the party annihilated one that had engulfed a Deep Gnome on the Diggermattock steps. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] scried on the second half of the crystal key to [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]] and discovered it floating inside the Demon Lord's body.

After the cleansing of Rockblight, Snorbul enforced dignity for the [[boulda|wererat]] newcomers, hurling a Deep Gnome who spat on them ten feet across the room and declaring that anyone who showed them disrespect would answer to the new Wardens.
