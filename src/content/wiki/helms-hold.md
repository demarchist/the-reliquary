## Description

Helm's Hold is a fortified citadel located approximately 30 miles south of Neverwinter. Named for the god Helm, it serves as a neutral meeting ground and strategic military position.

## Significance

Helm's Hold is the site of a pivotal council meeting where faction leaders from across Faerûn gather to coordinate their response to the escalating threats facing the realm. The Council of Helm's Hold establishes the groundwork for alliance-building among previously fragmented powers.
