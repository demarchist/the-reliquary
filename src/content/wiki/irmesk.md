## Background

Irmesk is the young Burgomaster of Barovia village, having inherited the position after his father was killed during Strahd's assaults on the town. He has an adopted sister, Ireena, who bears a striking resemblance to Strahd's long-dead bride Tatyana.

## Role in the Campaign

The party meets Irmesk at the Blood on the Vine tavern in Barovia village. He provides critical information about the current state of the cursed land and the threats facing his people, helping the party understand the scope of Strahd's tyranny.

## Notable Traits

- Young leader thrust into a position of responsibility by tragedy
- Protective of his adopted sister Ireena
- Struggles to maintain hope for his people under Strahd's shadow
