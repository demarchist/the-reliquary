## Overview

The Refounding Festival is a grand celebration held in Neverwinter to commemorate the city's liberation from Thayan and Netherese rule. It marks a turning point in Neverwinter's history, symbolizing renewed independence and hope.

## Events

The festival features elaborate ceremonies, performances, markets, and tournaments across the city's grounds. Notable festival fixtures include Sha'Alazzi's Magic Emporium, the Dragonchess tournament, and various pavilions for different guilds and factions.

## Significance

The party's attendance at the Refounding Festival leads to the founding of the Spellforged Adventuring Company, encounters with Kangaxx, and the beginning of the Neverwinter chapter of their adventures. A coordinated cult attack during the festival foreshadows the dangers ahead.
