Ront is an Orc who was imprisoned at [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]] alongside the party during their early days in the [[underdark|Underdark]]. Sent by Gruumsh on a quest to find an object of power beneath the surface, he shared a cell with the group, and his abrasive personality made an immediate impression: when he learned [[patches|Patches]] had deserted the Drow, he spat at her feet. Yet when JmJm yelled at the Drow guards and took a crossbow bolt for it, Ront walked over and pulled him up. He liked the group's attitude — arguing and fighting rather than cowering — and that counted for something.

## The Prisoner

In the cage at Velkynvelve, Ront presented himself as exactly what he appeared to be: a proud, hot-tempered warrior with a war god's mandate. He survived the escape from the outpost by leaping from a great height and cratering into the pool below, protected by a shell of ice armor. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] healed him; Ront glared at the Drow wizard he blamed for "dropping him." When the group showed Ront the fragment of [[the-last-word|The Last Word]], he stared at it in genuine awe and said it might be what Gruumsh had sent him to find. He proposed to prove his worth by killing [[tark|Tark]], whom he identified as clearly the weakest member of the group. Cirrus agreed with obvious amusement. The proposal was never enacted.

## Corruption

At [[sloobludop|Sloobludop]], during the Kuo-Toa civil war, Ront's downfall began. A small Kuo-Toa approached him and the two began talking in Orcish — the creature's eyes meeting his with unsettling focus, Ront's own gaze glazing over. [[shuushar|Shuushar]] saw it for what it was: the figure was an Incubus demon wearing a flesh mask, and it had been working Ront for some time. Under its command, Ront blasted [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] with eldritch force. When the Incubus fled into the ruined city, Ront followed.

The corruption deepened over the subsequent months in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]]. The group encountered him twice in the city — first when the Incubus (calling itself Leemok) introduced the creature attacking [[fey|Fey'Anon]] in an alleyway as Ront: a hulking figure in a heavy black cloak, tentacles erupting from beneath the fabric to tear at the wizard. The Orc's body had been changed, a second mouth forming somewhere in the mass of him, his arm replaced by a writhing tentacle studded with tusked growths. He carried warlock abilities — Eldritch Blast, Hellish Rebuke, and Dimension Door — and attacked with a two-mouthed scream that was no longer quite the sound a living creature should make.

## The Brand and the Blood Ritual

By the time the demons launched their final assault on Gracksuluth in session 30, Ront had become something the group barely recognized. He charged at Fey'Anon with murderous purpose during the [[derro-faction|Derro]] riot, his disfigured form drenched in the blood that had begun to fall around him like rain — drawn from the wounds of the fallen in a pattern the group gradually understood. [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] called out that killing Ront might trigger something terrible. The warning was too late, or perhaps irrelevant: Ront was already the ritual's consummation.

## The Vessel

Surrounded and beaten down on the bridge over Ladueger's Furrow, Ront turned the malformed tendrils of his own arm inward. The tusks of the tentacle bit into his own flesh. He tore himself apart. The flayed pieces scattered across the bridge and the surrounding stone, and from the carnage something unfolded — growing, gaining scale and muscle and fur and mass, rising to thirty feet of height with two heads screaming in unison.

[[demogorgon|Demogorgon]], Prince of Demons, had been summoned. The blood ritual that had been building since [[sloobludop|Sloobludop]] was complete at last — Ront its final price. The Orc who had leapt from Velkynvelve's ceiling and crawled from an icy pool was gone. What remained on the bridge was a god of the Abyss, and it immediately asked where the artifact was.
