## Description

Euphoria — whose full name is *The Euphoria One Feels When Besting an Inferior* — is an Illithid Remembrancer encountered at the inn *Her Gift* in Dokkar. Dressed in flowing purple robes, Euphoria feeds on the psychic resonance of emotions rather than brains, savouring the feelings generated by memory-laced drinks and the intense experiences of adventurers.

## Purpose

The Mind Flayer is collecting memories and data for the colony's Long Sleep. The Illithid believe that Atropos and Orcus cannot be stopped, and their kind intends to flee to the farthest reaches of the cosmological background to hibernate until a new universe forms. For that hibernation, they need as much information as possible.

## Association with the Group

Euphoria asked to travel with the group to collect richer memories and emotional data. Despite Tark's knowledge that a Githzerai monk was hunting this very Illithid, the group extracted a binding promise of good behaviour and allowed Euphoria to accompany them. The Mind Flayer provided supporting fire during the vision-battle against Kallista's demons and was deposited in one of Feanon's containment tanks upon the group's return to the Barony.
