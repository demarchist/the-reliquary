The Whorlstone Caverns are an extensive network of tunnels and chambers carved into the rock beneath [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]], accessible from the oldest sections beneath the Hall of Sacred Scrolls. They are older than the Duergar city above and were not made by Duergar hands — the craftsmanship is crude, improvised, distinctly non-Duergar, the product of centuries of Derro habitation.

The caverns are saturated with [[faerzress|Faerzress]], the crystallized wild magic of the [[underdark|Underdark]]. Fey'Anon felt the tingle of magical energies the moment the party descended past the transition point, and the ambient power of the place makes standard magical precautions unreliable. The Faerzress crystals throughout the tunnels throw a pale, shifting light across the phosphorescent fungi that grow in the wetter chambers, giving the place an eerie luminescence.

## What Lives Here

The [[warlocks-of-ceregorach|Warlocks of Ceregorach]] maintain their council chambers in the Whorlstone, meeting in a large amphitheater carved into the rock beneath the Hall of Sacred Scrolls. The Council of Derro Savants — warlocks with patrons ranging from Archfey to Demons and Devils — convenes here regularly, adjudicating their disputes and scheming against one another with the Beholder [[o-alla|O'Alla]] as mediator.

[[bilrigger|Bilrigger]]'s private sanctum is located deeper in the caverns: a set of rooms behind a door cut to look like natural stone, with nearly-invisible metal hinges sliced impossibly clean. The sanctum itself was opulent by Derro standards before [[milgre|Milgre]]'s vampire spawn tore through it in a prior confrontation, leaving smashed furniture and pooled blood.

The caverns also contain a large water-filled chamber with a central fungal island, where the party encountered dancing Myconids and found the small sprout Rumpadump. Chasme demons were spotted using the tunnels as an infiltration route toward the city above.

## The Party's Exploration

The party followed [[droki|Droki]] into the Whorlstone in sessions 17 through 19, descending past the Hall of Sacred Scrolls, through the Savants' chambers, and deeper into the tunnel network in pursuit of [[bilrigger|Bilrigger]] and then [[milgre|Milgre]]. The tunnels took the better part of an hour to navigate each way, requiring navigation past spiderwebs, fungal hazards, and Derro political geography.
