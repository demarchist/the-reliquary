The Stonespeaker Crystal is a magical crystal from Gravenhollow that serves as a key to the legendary stone giant library in the Underdark. The group sought the crystal across multiple sessions in Blingdenstone, eventually recovering both halves and reuniting them at the altar of Entemoch's Boon after the Battle of Blingdenstone.

When Snorbul placed the cracked ends together, they sealed with a soft glow and the crystal shimmered with inner energy. Feanon identified it as a Stonespeaker Crystal from Gravenhollow.
