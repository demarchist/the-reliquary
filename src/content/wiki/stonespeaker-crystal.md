The Stonespeaker Crystal is a magical crystal from [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]] that serves as a key to the legendary stone giant library in the [[underdark|Underdark]]. The group sought the crystal across multiple sessions in [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]], eventually recovering both halves.

## Recovery

After the [[battle-of-blingdenstone|Battle of Blingdenstone]] and the banishment of [[juiblex|Juiblex]], [[gurnik|Gurnik]] led the party down to Entemoch's Boon — a natural chamber of rough stone shimmering with mineral deposits, its stepped sides forming a natural amphitheatre. Two items rested on a stone altar grown from the floor. The first was a half of the crystal the group had been seeking.

[[snorbul|Snorbul]] picked it up and placed the cracked ends of both halves together. They sealed with a soft glow and the crystal shimmered with inner energy. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] identified it as a Stonespeaker Crystal from Gravenhollow.

## The Stonespeaker Ring

The party later recovered a smaller version of the crystal — a Stonespeaker Ring — from [[corpsetaker|Corpsetaker]]'s camp. The ring granted Augury and the ability to summon the Library of Gravenhollow. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] held onto it, though the group agreed it must be kept secret and safe.
