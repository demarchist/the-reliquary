Ahmahd is an elven ranger of the Obsidian Order, assigned by Umu to guide the party from their underground encampment near the Sea of Silt down through the Saurian tunnels toward the Font of Fawden. A young woman in reconnaissance gear, she materialised from the shadows at Umu's command — quiet, professional, and visibly unimpressed by the outlanders she had been tasked with escorting.

Her opinion improved somewhat during the journey through the Nasnas-infested Saurian facility, where the party's overwhelming firepower — fifty Nasnas dead and a rope bridge ambush foiled — forced her to admit she had been wrong about their capabilities. She guided them as far as the Disphotic Forest before taking her leave, unwilling to descend further and risk being trapped below the surface with no way out.
