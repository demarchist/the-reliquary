Kurt Von Bumidere was one of Horatio's brothers, a member of the Bumidere noble family of Neverwinter. He was in a relationship with a halfling named Thessalt — later suspected of being the spy known as "Holst" — who was a frequent guest at the family estate.

Kurt was killed during the coordinated assault on the Bumidere estate. After the initial fireball explosions shattered the dining hall windows, a Cloudkill spell filled the room. Already burned and prone from the blast, Kurt was unable to escape the caustic gas and died where he lay.
