Talon is a +1 longsword found during the clearing of the Redbrand hideout beneath [[tresendar-manor|Tresendar Manor]]. [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] asked to wield it after returning from his solo rescue mission, and the party obliged. It served as his primary melee weapon in the early chapters of the campaign before he acquired more powerful armaments.
