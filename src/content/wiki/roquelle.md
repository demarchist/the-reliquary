Roquelle is a High-Elf woman with a silver eye and silver fingertips, brought into existence by [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus Al Kayan]] through the spell True Polymorph. Cirrus prayed to [[selune|Selûne]] over a particularly fine stone he had plucked from the Galena mountainside, then shaped it into a living person — a new priest of Selûne, born from rock and devotion.

She was created inside [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s [[magnificent-mansion|Magnificent Mansion]] during the group's journey along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]].
