Protanther was an Ancient Gold Wyrm and the abdicated king of the Gold Dragons. He awaited the Dragonflight of Shou Lung beneath the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree of Bahamut]] in [[bloodstone-city|Bloodstone City]], flanked by two dozen yellow-robed monks.

He and his counterpart [[themberchaud|Themberchaud the Grand]] were tasked with helping each of the ascending dragons planeshift to their new lairs via the Great Tree — a pathway linked to Yggdrasil and the planes beyond. He had assembled a cadre of Dragon Blessed attendants, one for each member of the Dragonflight, to serve as majordooms in their new lairs.

Protanther's left forearm was missing from the elbow — an old wound that festered with the dark energies of the chromatic goddess, preventing healing. The chromatic members of the Dragonflight noted this with some satisfaction.

His attempt to scry on the absent Themberchaud failed. When volcanic activity erupted to the north, Protanther asked the chromatic dragons to fly and investigate their kin — a request that scattered the Dragonflight across the region at the worst possible moment.

## The Fall

Protanther did not survive the week. [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] later confirmed his death from her own account: she had been called to the Dragonflight's defence by [[bahamut|Bahamut]] in the same hours that [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] and the Red Greatwyrm [[vulkan|Vulkan]] descended on the Tree from their [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] over [[palischuk|Palischuk]]. She had reached the Great Tree in time to fight beside Protanther.

She watched him fall before [[vulkan|Vulkan]].

The abdicated king of the Gold Dragons — keeper of the Great Tree, gatherer of the ascending Dragonflight, anchor of Bahamut's western plan — was killed by a Red Dragon in service to the Witch-King. The rite he had been preparing has not yet been resumed. Whether the Tree itself remains operable in the absence of a Gold dragon-side steward is a question the surviving Dragonflight has inherited along with the rest of the disaster.
