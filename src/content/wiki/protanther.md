Protanther is an Ancient Gold Wyrm and the abdicated king of the Gold Dragons. He awaits the Dragonflight of Shou Lung beneath the Great Tree of Bahamut in Bloodstone City, flanked by two dozen yellow-robed monks.

He and his counterpart Themberchaud the Grand were tasked with helping each of the ascending dragons planeshift to their new lairs via the Great Tree — a pathway linked to Yggdrasil and the planes beyond. He has assembled a cadre of Dragon Blessed attendants, one for each member of the Dragonflight, to serve as majordooms in their new lairs.

Protanther's left forearm is missing from the elbow — an old wound that festers with the dark energies of the chromatic goddess, preventing healing. The chromatic members of the Dragonflight note this with some satisfaction.

His attempt to scry on the absent Themberchaud failed. When volcanic activity erupted to the north, Protanther asked the chromatic dragons to fly and investigate their kin.
