The Warlocks of Ceregorach are a cadre of Derro Savants who derive their power from [[ceregorach|Ceregorach]], the Laughing God — a trickster archfey deity whose domain encompasses cunning, deception, and the cruelty of a jest that cuts too deep. They operate out of the [[whorlstone-caverns|Whorlstone Caverns]] beneath [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]], maintaining a presence in the city's politics through bribery, blackmail, and the manipulation of information.

[[bilrigger|Bilrigger the Savant]] is their most prominent member — or at least the face that the warlocks show to outsiders. His private chambers in the Whorlstone were furnished with conspicuous wealth, and he was known to move through [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]]'s political landscape with unusual confidence for a Derro. The group discovered that the Bilrigger who met them in his dining room was a Simulacrum — a constructed double — suggesting the real Bilrigger operated at a careful remove from direct encounters.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus al-Kayan]] had a history with Ceregorach before Selûne claimed him: the suffering he endured under the Laughing God's influence was real, and Bilrigger's attempts to rekindle that relationship were ultimately unsuccessful. The theological argument between warlock and former warlock ended in violence, the simulacrum shattering into its component teeth on the dining room floor.

Droki, a lesser Derro serving as Bilrigger's courier and errand runner, served as the group's reluctant guide through the Whorlstone Caverns in their search for the real Bilrigger — and for the Vampire Savant [[milgre|Milgre]], whose vampire spawn the Council of Savants had grown tired of cleaning up.
