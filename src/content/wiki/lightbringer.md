Lightbringer is a mace sacred to the god Lathander. According to a book written in Dwarvish recovered from an alchemy chamber in [[tresendar-manor|Tresendar Manor]], the mace was lost within [[wave-echo-cave|Wave Echo Cave]] along with the [[spellforge|Spellforge]]. Its recovery was one of the objectives driving the party's initial search for the cave, though the mace was never located during their expedition.
