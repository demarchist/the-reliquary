Halar is a retired operator of the 3rd Special Actions Detachment, the Silverymoon military unit known as the Mindslayers. Broader across the shoulders than even Garm, with a thick neck and arms that strain his shirt, he bears the permanent squint of someone who spent decades staring into darkness. His blond hair is pulled back in a warrior's knot.

Halar served as the unit's heavy weapons specialist and carried a massive axe that he polished until it reflected the morning light before the mission. He was fascinated by Gundren's artificing work — particularly Gwen — having never met an artificer before, and taught the dwarf how to use the anti-psionic equipment effectively in combat.

During the descent, Halar's axe never stopped moving. He took an acid spray across his armour from the nautiloid's biological defences and fought through it without complaint. He settled in Gashaus after the mission.
