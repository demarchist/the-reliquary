The Rising Sun is a multistory establishment in the second-to-last ring of [[helioglabulus|Helios]], occupying one of the largest buildings in the district. It serves as both an exclusive nightclub for the city's nobility and the operational centre of [[mister-luko|Mister Luko]]'s criminal organisation.

## Exterior

The building is constructed of dark hardwood and richly painted stucco over a foundation of deep Bloodstone, with teardrop-roofed turrets capped in copper still bright enough to catch the evening sky. Stained glass windows glow warmly from within, revealing nothing but movement. The front doors are brass, covered in panelled relief depicting workers, farmers, and miners building the city — posed with the deliberate dignity of people who knew they were being commemorated. The building says money, and sees no reason to be modest about it.

## The Foyer

The entrance is a study in controlled impression: dark hardwood panelling, red silk upholstery, small tables bearing objects placed purely to confirm that cost is not a consideration. The coat-check runs behind a row of golden bars like a particularly elegant bank. Guests surrender their weapons and dress accordingly to enter the establishment proper. Magical lockboxes receive gear, and slate-and-gold keycards are issued in return. Hospitality is guaranteed — and the guarantee runs both ways.

Luko's men staff the foyer in matching attire: long coats of rich dark silk trimmed in fur, fastened with vivid sashes, with boots of curling-toed dark leather. All share the same haircut — shaved clean save for a tufty bounce of curls at the crown — and moustaches of magnificent size.

## Interior

The gambling floor is hazy with sweet smoke, populated by silk-wrapped servers carrying silver trays. The noise is managed and deliberate — the clink of glassware, the movement of chips, polite laughter held just below a certain volume. Above the main floor, two mezzanine levels hold dining booths and curtained halls. Above those, a third-floor dining room glows warmly behind large windows, where Luko holds court over a long table that seats perhaps thirty.
