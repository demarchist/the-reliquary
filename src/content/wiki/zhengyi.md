Zhengyi, the Witch-King, is a powerful lich who once ruled the desolate land of Vaasa with an iron fist, commanding armies of undead and monstrous servants. A former Red Wizard of Thay, he became one of [[orcus|Orcus]]' most dangerous Children — the Balgura Prophecy names three: one of Gold ([[kangaxx|Kangaxx]]), one of Adamantite (Zhengyi), and one of Obsidian. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] saw, through the artifact's vision, Orcus pushing Zhengyi back into the world through sheer divine might.

## The Imprisonment

[[theodemir|Theodemir]] sacrificed his life to create the Eye of Aren — a crystalline psionic artifact built around a shard of [[the-last-word|The Last Word]] — as the keystone of the binding spell imprisoning the Witch-King. The casting demanded more than magic: it required the caster's life force. The spell held for decades, but a greater power worked to unravel the enchantment. Theodemir's spirit, preserved within [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s Ring of Mind Shielding, warned that the spell had been failing for a year, giving them only nineteen hours before it collapsed entirely. The [[conclave-of-silverymoon|Conclave's]] observations confirmed that Zhengyi's mind was on the brink of reaching critical mass — a re-awakened Witch-King represented an existential threat to the realm.

## Enmity with Tark

Zhengyi's forces destroyed Tark's homeland and murdered his family. The Oni [[li-xian|Li Xian]] killed Tark's parents in Zhengyi's service — a truth that crystallised through Tark's dreams, when he saw the necromancer's corpse rise and transform into an Oni, speaking with the other Oni who had attacked the party at [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]]. Through dream spells, Tark contacted his enslaved master [[gaddock|Gabbock]] — all skin and bone, milky blind eyes — languishing in a prison run by Zhengyi's followers until the monk [[kenshi|Kenshi]] and his disciples stormed the complex and freed everyone.

## The Ziggurats

Zhengyi's influence extends through dark Ziggurats grown from life force. One beneath [[sundabar|Sundabar]] was grown from the life force of [[megno-slatesunder|Megno]]'s apprentice Molly, killing her. [[mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]] and the Delzoun Emperor destroyed it. Another was discovered in the [[thunder-bluff|Barony]] — its broken phylactery made of black Sourcestone and mythically rare Prismeum.

## The Cosmic Web

Zhengyi does not act alone. The cult leader at the [[titanic-forge|Titanic Forge]] on [[al-askar|Al Askar]] claimed that Zalaam ([[vecna|Vecna]]) was brother to Zhengyi and kin to Orcus — a web of undead powers working toward the same catastrophic end. Every thread the party pulled revealed the same pattern: the Children of Orcus, backed by [[atropos|Atropos]], were converging.
