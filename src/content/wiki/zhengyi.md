Zhengyi, the Witch-King, is a powerful lich who once ruled the desolate land of Vaasa with an iron fist, commanding armies of undead and monstrous servants. His original reign of terror was ended by a coalition of heroes who destroyed him — but a greater power has since been working to resurrect him. The wizard Theodemir sacrificed his very life force to cast a spell holding that resurrection at bay, but for years the enchantment has been slowly unraveling.

The threat of Zhengyi's resurrection has been a driving force in the campaign. Theodemir's spirit, preserved within Fey's Ring of Mind Shielding, warned that the spell was failing, giving the party only hours before it collapsed entirely. The Witch-King's return represents one of the most dangerous threats facing the North, and the party's efforts to stop or contain him have shaped much of their journey.

Zhengyi commands a flight of enthralled dragons through the use of Hollow Armor — Vecna-cursed metal exoskeletons embedded with controlling gemstones. The Red Greatwyrm **Vulkan** serves as his dragon overlord, commanding the other armored wyrms in his name. Confirmed servants include the Ancient Red Dragon **Ares** and **Ancalagon the Void**, both clad in Hollow Armor and operating under Vulkan's authority.
