## Overview

The 3rd Special Actions Detachment — known informally as the "Mindslayers" — was a Silverymoon military unit specifically trained for anti-Illithid operations. The unit developed specialised weapons, tactics, and mental discipline techniques for combating Mind Flayers and their collective intelligence networks. It was disbanded decades ago when the Illithid threat was deemed to have receded, though several veterans continued to meet at a hall in the military quarter.

## Equipment

The Mindslayers developed and maintained an arsenal of anti-psionic equipment:

- **Psychic disruption grenades** — crystal spheres packed with enchanted minerals that detonate in a burst of psionic static, overloading Illithid telepathy within a thirty-foot radius
- **Psionic-cancelling bubble devices** — mechanical constructs projecting a field of psychic silence that blocks telepathic communication
- **Crystal-powered helmets** — headgear lined with resonant crystal that dampens incoming psionic attacks by half
- **Analgesic bombs** — alchemical devices flooding an area with numbing agent, disrupting the pain-based control mechanisms Illithids use on thralls

## Veterans

Three retired operators — Garm, Halar, and Khelon — came out of retirement when the party needed to infiltrate Nostalgia for Infinity's hive beneath Ladypeak. Despite fifteen years of inactivity, the three fell back into formation with the unconscious precision of soldiers who had never truly stopped being soldiers. They provided weapons, tactical briefings, mental discipline training, and direct combat support during the descent.

## The Last Mission

The Mindslayers accompanied the party into the hive and fought through the nautiloid's defences during its catastrophic launch. All three survived. After the mission, Garm, Halar, and Khelon settled in the village of Gashaus with Alura, helping the local community while the group continued to Silverymoon.
