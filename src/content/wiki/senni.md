Senni is the wife of Chief [[dorbo|Dorbo]] Diggermattock and co-leader of [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]]. She was present during the trade negotiations with the Heralds following the liberation of the city from [[juiblex|Juiblex]], helping to present the [[gemstone-lens|Gemstone Lens]] artefact that had been recovered from Entemoch's Boon.
