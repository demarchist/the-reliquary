Silverymoon is a major northern city on the continent of Faerûn, known as the Gem of the North. Ringed in silver walls with metallic towers, columned buildings and majestic trees, the city blends Elven and human architecture seamlessly — flying buttresses of pale marble, colonnaded walkways suspended between towers, and everywhere the soft hum of the Mythal like a song just beneath hearing.

## Institutions

The city is home to the Lady's College — a sprawling campus of pale towers, cloistered courtyards and enchanted gardens — which houses the Conclave of Silverymoon, the city's premier association of practising wizards. The Spellguard serves as the city's magical enforcement agency, protecting arcane infrastructure and investigating magical crimes. The House Invincible, fortress cathedral of Helm, anchors the city's martial defences. The Vault of Sages serves as Silverymoon's great library and archive.

## Political Situation

During the campaign's events, Silverymoon faced growing pressure from Amnian commercial interests. Amnian merchants established trade offices and infrastructure funding programmes throughout the city under the direction of Rama Al Kayan, with Amnian flags appearing on public buildings. Beneath this veneer of commerce, the Amnians were positioning soldiers disguised as merchants and travellers for a coup attempt during the Winter Solstice — an invasion that the party helped expose and ultimately repel during the Battle of Silverymoon.

## The Mythal

The city is protected by the Mythal — an ancient magical barrier that regulates spellcasting within the city limits and can be activated to disrupt teleportation in and out of the city. The Mythal's activation during the Amnian crisis forced the party to fly rather than teleport back to the city.

## Significance

The party visits Silverymoon multiple times throughout the campaign, dealing with the Conclave conspiracy, the Amnian invasion, diplomatic negotiations, and the growing threat of Zhengyi's return.
