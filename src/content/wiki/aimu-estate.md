The Aimu Estate sits at the back of [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]], a wooded mountain valley in the Galena foothills a day's ride north of [[ironfang-keep|Ironfang Keep]]. It is the late wife's old estate of [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] — a modest noble manor by the standards of [[helioglabulus|Helios]]'s upper rings, comprising a two-storey house of fine stone and slate, terraced gardens of vines and vegetables, a small orchard near the lake, and a glittering lake fed by a cascade of small waterfalls running down from the high peaks.

The most prominent structure on the grounds is not the house itself but a separate, ornate chapel of [[lathander|Lathander]] — set apart from the manor, with a slate roof bearing a Morninglord sunburst and tall narrow windows. The chapel houses the altar on which Lord Aimu placed the stolen [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] when he arrived at the estate in flight from the Cathedral.

## Defences

The estate is held by Knights of the [[crimson-dawn-knights|Order of the Crimson Dawn]] sworn to Lord Aimu's household, led by a plate-clad veteran named [[ishin|Ishin]]. When the party arrived at sundown — bluffing their way past a peephole-guarded front door under [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s pretence of being a troupe of travelling performers — six fully armoured knights opened the door in formation, ornate bows already strung and enchanted blades drawn at the sight of an incoming dragon.

## The Battle

On the evening of the party's arrival, a flight of eight [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen White Dragons]] crested the valley ridge, carrying a [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizard of Thay]] on the largest of them and answered shortly after by a Vampire Knight on a sickly adult white dragon mount wielding an adapted [[saurian-lance|Saurian lance-weapon]]. The estate's east wing was torn open by a lightning bolt fired from the lance; trees and vineyards were scorched; the lake bay below was filled with the wing-beats of a copper dragon in adult form. The battle was joined as the sun dipped below the rim of the mountains.

The chapel itself was untouched.

The battle ended at twilight with the [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Vampire Knight]] driven from his own globe of darkness, the [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizard]] dead, the wyrmlings torn apart by [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] and finished by the archers, and the two surviving adult Ashen Whites limping for the ridge. Lord Aimu wielded the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] in the close — the radiance making the Ashen Whites flinch at his approach — and the household archers shot the Vampire's own great mount out of the sky at the edge of their range.

## After the Battle

The conversation that followed unfolded in two halves. In the chapel, the [[gnome-of-the-vale|bitten Gnome]] was killed and resurrected by knife and diamond to break a vampire's curse — and revealed in death as the Adult Copper Dragon [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]]. Afterwards, [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] departed with her for [[bytopia|Bytopia]].

In the manor's undamaged front room, Lord Aimu laid the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] across the table between him and the remaining party, listened to [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] recount their work against [[orcus|Orcus]] and [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]], and then told his own tale of the Queen, the [[red-wizards-of-thay|Thayan delegation]], and the Cathedral audience that had prompted the theft. The blade remained on the table when he retired to meditate over what to do with it next.

## The Night Attack and the Earthquake

Lord Aimu's answer came by morning's-eve in the form of inaction: he left the [[sunsword|Sunsword]] on the table for the party to take up, and [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] spent the quiet hours of the night attuning to it while [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] drained the household's gems to feed [[eclipse|Eclipse]] and [[cassian|Cassian]] tended his wounded winged unicorn [[uni|Uni]] in the stable yard. It was here that [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] revealed that the white-wood of Tark's prosthetic arm had been cut from the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Tree of Bahamut]].

The peace did not survive the night. The [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizards of Thay]] returned in force for a [[the-thayans-attack|second assault]], opening with a chanted earthquake that tore the manor apart — walls folding, the slate roof cascading off its rafters, [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] and several [[crimson-dawn-knights|paladins]] buried in the rubble. What the lightning bolt of the dusk battle had begun, the earthquake finished: the fine stone house at the back of [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]] was reduced to ruin, and the grounds became a battlefield of ghouls, vampires, and Thayan spellcasters before the night was through.
