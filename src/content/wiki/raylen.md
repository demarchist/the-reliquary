## Background

Raylen is a charming moon elf merchant operating under the cover of an exotic goods store at the Neverwinter Festival. In truth, he is an agent of Bregan D'aerthe, the drow mercenary organization.

## Role in the Campaign

Raylen initially befriends the party through commerce, but later reveals his true allegiance by poisoning the group with a non-lethal toxin as part of Bregan D'aerthe's schemes in Neverwinter.

## Notable Traits

- Charming and charismatic merchant persona
- Bregan D'aerthe operative working under deep cover
- Skilled poisoner who uses non-lethal methods
