Moot is an elderly Monk of great power, always accompanied by a flock of seven golden canaries. Despite his advanced age, his martial abilities are formidable — he defeats Lord Penterghast in the semi-finals of the Neverwinter martial tournament.

Moot befriends Tarkathee Daru and gifts him his tremendously enchanted staff before fighting alongside the group during the attack on the Neverwinter Festival. One of his canaries, Ysera, takes a particular liking to Tark and decides to follow him, granting him the Alert feat and communicating with him telepathically.

## The Staff and the Tree

The true origin of Moot's staff went unremarked for chapters until the party reached the [[bloodstone-lands|Bloodstone Lands]]. At the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]], the Adult Copper Dragon [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] watched [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] meditate over his white-wood prosthetic arm — reshaped from Moot's staff — and asked whether the old monk had kept a number of golden canaries in his company. When Tark confirmed it, she named the wood's source: the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree of Bahamut]], the holy white tree at the highest terrace of [[bloodstone-city|Bloodstone City]], linked through its roots to Yggdrasil itself. The staff Moot gifted Tark had been cut, somewhere in its history, from the living wood of [[bahamut|Bahamut]]'s own tree — and the golden canaries that have always accompanied Moot appear to be a sign of that same connection to the Platinum Dragon, marking the elderly monk as a figure tied to the holiest tree in the Bloodstone Lands.
