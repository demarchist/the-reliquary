Garm is a retired operator of the 3rd Special Actions Detachment, the Silverymoon military unit known as the Mindslayers. Enormous — six and a half feet of scarred, weathered muscle — with a jaw like a shovel, hands that could crush a helmet, and a nose broken so many times it no longer points in a consistent direction. His grey hair is cropped to stubble and his voice sounds like gravel being poured into a bucket.

Garm was the first to recognise Fëanon's interrupted enthrallment, noting he had seen it once before — and that man had died screaming. When the party explained their plan to infiltrate the Illithid hive, Garm told them he had been sitting in the veterans' bar for fifteen years waiting for someone to say those words.

During the descent into Nostalgia for Infinity's hive, Garm served as the point breaker — holding collapsing corridors apart with his bare hands, hurling psychic disruption grenades with devastating accuracy, and physically throwing Illithid drones through walls. He settled in Gashaus after the mission.
