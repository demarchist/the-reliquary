Umu is the eldest and most revered member of the Obsidian Order's encampment near the Sea of Silt — the oldest elf any of the party had ever encountered. She is a Shaman, not a Druid, but was trained under the last Druid of Al Askar before he was murdered over nine hundred years ago. The sight of Doctor Breakfast — the first Druid she had seen since her mentor's death — moved her to tears.

When the party arrived at the Obsidian Order's underground camp, Umu examined Patches with particular interest. She recognised the drow's elven kinship but recoiled upon sensing the ancient curse in her blood — the demonic taint inflicted during the Crown War. Umu suggested the curse might be cleansed through a magical tattoo, altering Patches's existing mark of betrothal with the old magic of the Al Askarian elves.

Through a spirit-walking ritual, Umu divined that Thorna's destiny lay in the frigid north of Faerûn, and that she was one of two. When Cirrus convinced her of the urgency of reaching the Font of Fawden, Umu pledged the Obsidian Order's support — offering the group magical tattoos as a reward if they could deprive Vecna of Fawden's power. She assigned the ranger Ahmahd to guide the party into the depths.
