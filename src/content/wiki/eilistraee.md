Eilistraee, the Dark Maiden, is the [[drow-faction|Drow]] goddess of beauty, song, dance, swordwork, hunting, moonlight, and the redemption of the drow. She is the daughter of Corellon Larethian and [[lolth|Lolth]], and chose exile alongside the drow out of love for her people, hoping to one day lead them back to the surface and the light.

## Patron of Patches

Eilistraee is [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' divine patron, bound to her through [[eclipse|Eclipse]] — the sentient obsidian blade born from the cleansing ritual that destroyed [[the-reaver|the Reaver]]. When Patches smashed the last hag's tooth upon the cursed dagger atop the altar in the ruined Temple of Helm in [[sundabar|Sundabar]], the fragments melted into liquid mercury. Patches reached in and drew out an obsidian blade — her hair lengthened, her form became more lithe, and she floated gently to the ground bound to the Dark Maiden.

Patches communes with Eilistraee through a Crystal Geode and [[chardalyn|Chardalyn]] focus stone, receiving guidance — though the goddess does not always answer. In [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]], Eilistraee revealed that the settlement was cursed by Lolth. When the party defeated the Stone Priestess, Patches initially wanted to revive and convert the Medusa by Eilistraee's edict. After communing, she stood and decapitated the statue with Eclipse instead — Eilistraee had wanted commitment and dedication, not needless suffering. The act empowered Eclipse further.

When a grief-stricken ghost possessed Patches in Blingdenstone, it found Eilistraee's devotion running through every part of her — a divine weapon tethered to her soul, nothing the ghost had expected from a Drow.

## Moonfire

Eilistraee's divine gift of Moonfire — shared with [[selune|Selûne]] — proved essential for the ritual involving the Crystal relic, connecting the goddess to the broader cosmic conflict between light and darkness.
