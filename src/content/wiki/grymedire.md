Grymedire — Grym to those who know him — is a resident of [[feanons-tower|Fëanon's Tower]] in the Barony of Selunia. He is one of the tower's students and occasionally works the front desk.

When [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] called an emergency meeting after receiving a mysterious message on the [[al-askarian-tablet|Al'Askarian Tablet]], Grymedire helped duplicate the message onto a larger display for the group to analyze. He also revealed that he had received a piece of mail for Fëanon days earlier while staffing the front desk — a purple velvet envelope sealed with black wax flecked with gold, which turned out to be [[mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]]'s invitation to the Astral Plane.
