Aurinax is an adult gold dragon who allies with the party and assists in safeguarding powerful artifacts. He breathes upon a platinum-gold mixture to create Aurum, a magical alloy, and helps ferry the celestial artifact shards to secure locations. His draconic power and ancient wisdom make him a formidable ally in the escalating cosmic conflict.
