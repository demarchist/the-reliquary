Grein Osstyl was a young [[drow-faction|Drow]] wizard serving as apprentice to the archmage [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion DeVir]]. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] recognised his house name as belonging to a minor family of no significance. He was sent by his master to ferry the Heralds to [[the-labyrinth|the Labyrinth]] using a spell-gem of teleportation, as [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion]] was occupied with the ritual to banish the Demon Lords.

Grein was later spotted distributing enchanted clay urns throughout [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]] on [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion's]] behalf. When the group captured him and placed a Geas preventing him from speaking about them, the humiliation drove him to seek revenge. He confronted them during the slave break, wielding devastating magic through a black sourcestone harness — but was ultimately killed when [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] shot him with his [[thundercannon|thundercannon]].

In death, [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion's]] curse on the harness activated. Grein's desperate attempt to ascend into lichdom was twisted, transforming him instead into a Boneclaw — an undead servant bound to his master's will. The group hunted and destroyed the creature, though [[feanon-serinde|Feanon]] felt its essence being pulled back toward [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion]].
