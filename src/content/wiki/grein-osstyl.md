Grein Osstyl is a young [[drow-faction|Drow]] wizard serving as apprentice to the archmage [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion DeVir]]. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] recognised his house name as belonging to a minor family of no significance. He was sent by his master to ferry the Heralds to [[the-labyrinth|the Labyrinth]] using a spell-gem of teleportation, as [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion]] was occupied with the ritual to banish the Demon Lords.
