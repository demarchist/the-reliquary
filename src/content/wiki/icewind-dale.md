Icewind Dale is a frigid, windswept region in the far north of Faerûn, beyond the Spine of the World mountains. Ten frontier towns are clustered around several large lakes that remain frozen for much of the year, with a single lonely mountain rising from the surrounding tundra.

[[thorna|Thorna]] discovered through visions in [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]] that she had spent her early childhood in this region — taken through the streets of one of the towns as a little girl, with locals showing fear and anxiety at the sight of her and her mother. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] recognised the description and identified it as the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale, accessible from the [[selunia|Barony]] by travelling west to [[luskan|Luskan]] and then north around the Spine of the World.
