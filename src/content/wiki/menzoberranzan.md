Menzoberranzan is the great [[drow-faction|Drow]] capital city of the [[underdark|Underdark]] — a vast metropolis of black stone with intricate Drow architecture, dominated by the competing noble houses. The great magical clock tower of Narbondellyn glows red-hot and is visible across the entire cavern. [[lolth|Lolth]]'s matriarchal priesthood dominates all — worship of any other entity is banned on pain of death.

## Noble Houses

The city's power structure is a hierarchy of scheming noble houses locked in constant intrigue. [[house-baenre|House Baenre]] sits at the apex as the ruling house — its archmage Gromph was responsible for summoning the Demon Lords into the Underdark. [[house-faen-tlabbar|House Faen Tlabbar]] is connected to [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' family and held her brother [[finn|Finn]] captive, dressed in noble attire with his hair cut to denote his station. House Mizzrym (the fourth house) and House Vandree (the seventh) controlled the prison outpost at [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]] where the party was first held. House Duskryn (the ninth) was tied to operations the group encountered in the tunnels. [[bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'Aerthe]], led by [[jarlaxle|Jarlaxle]], operates in the shadows outside the official power structure.

## The Rescue of Finn

The party's primary objective in Menzoberranzan was to rescue Finn from House Faen Tlabbar. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] devised the plan: summon the Demon Lords to the Qu'Ellarz'Orl — the noble plateau, specifically the Kyorbblivvin mushroom forest — creating a devastating distraction while keeping most of the city's population out of the line of fire. During the chaos, they would mount the rescue. [[lodan|Lo'Dan]] arranged a meeting with Jarlaxle at the ruins of the old Do'Urden compound on the city's northern wall to secure Bregan D'Aerthe's cooperation.

## The Aftermath

The consequences of the group's actions were severe. [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion DeVir]] used the chaos to attack the city directly — distributing enchanted clay urns that dispensed sickening fog through the streets, siphoning enormous energy through his ritual in the Clawrift, and fleeing with an army of Slaad under his control. Menzoberranzan was left in turmoil. The slaves the party had freed were led to safety by Bregan D'Aerthe scouts, eventually pouring through a rift into the [[selunia|Barony]] with Red Slaad snapping at their heels.

Beneath the city's stench-streets, the group fought Grein Osstyl — Vizerion's apprentice, who wielded devastating magic through a black sourcestone harness. In death, Vizerion's curse twisted Grein into a Boneclaw, which the group hunted and destroyed through the slave pens before teleporting home to discover Finn waiting in the Barony.
