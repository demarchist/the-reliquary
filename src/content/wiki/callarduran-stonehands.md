Callarduran Stonehands is a gnomish god of stone, mining, and the deep places of the earth — particularly associated with the Svirfneblin and the Underdark, but counted among the gnomish pantheon at large. He is one of several gnomish powers whose presence on [[bytopia|Bytopia]], the Twin Paradises, makes the plane the customary afterlife of his people.

## At the Lair

When [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] and [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] brought [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] to Bytopia for her ascension, the Deva who came to receive the Copper Dragon was a Deva of Callarduran Stonehands — gold-skinned, robed in the colour of broken geode, drifting forward over the meadow grass at the unhurried gait of a being who had been awaiting them. He greeted them in flawless Draconic and explained that Azoo's lair had been prepared in the nearby hills: a great worked cave-system in the side of an extinct volcano, fitted for an Ancient Copper Wyrm and waiting for her to arrive.

That Callarduran Stonehands — a stone and Underdark deity — was the steward assigned to a Copper Dragon's planar lair fits the metallic dragon's affinity for caverns, tunnels, and the carved geometries of the deep earth. The arrangement appears to have been settled long before Azoo crossed the [[ringed-veils|Veils]] west, and it stood undisturbed even after her flight from [[vulkan|Vulkan]] and the [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] above [[palischuk|Palischuk]].
