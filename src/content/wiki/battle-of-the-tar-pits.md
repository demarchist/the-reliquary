## Background

The corrupted halflings of 'Āina Pulu had been performing blood sacrifices at a bubbling tar pit deep in Thueba Swamp — the prison of the ancient Death Wyrm Hohonu. The ritual was three sacrifices away from shattering the prison and releasing the draconic demigod. The party, guided by the halfling shaman Camea, infiltrated the sacrificial site disguised as prisoners.

## The Assault

The attack was launched on two fronts. JmJm, Cirrus, and Gundren burst from the Ethereal Plane astride Steden the Nightmare, while Feanon and Buumi reconstituted from Gaseous Form at the prisoners' side. The Grand Shaman and his followers were caught completely off guard.

A dwarven prisoner of Al Askar broke free during the chaos, seizing a Saurian Firelance from a pile of confiscated belongings and turning it on the Shaman. Despite the combined assault, the Grand Shaman proved nearly unkillable — sustained by a power conduit linking him to the Altar of Hohonu. He threw Camea into the pit and killed her with a Circle of Death.

## The Turning Point

Tark identified the conduit between the Shaman and the Altar from the Ethereal Plane. While JmJm rescued Tark and Camea's body, Feanon and Gundren focused their attacks on the Altar itself. When it shattered and tumbled into the tar, the Grand Shaman dropped dead instantly — his unlife severed.

## Aftermath

With the ritual fouled, the party revived Camea using a diamond and her soul, which Tark had rescued from Hohonu's vortex in the Ethereal Plane. Feanon then cast a permanent Antipathy spell on the site, reinforced by the powerful leyline beneath the tar pits, to prevent anyone from approaching the prison again. The party departed Thueba Swamp, turning southwest toward the Machina Factory.
