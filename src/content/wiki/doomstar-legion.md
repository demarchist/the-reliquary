## Description

The Doomstar Legion is a mercenary army from the Second Layer of Hell, commanded by the Archdevil Mathias Doomstar, Lord of Dis. Their field commander for the Barony operation is the Narzugon Beucephelus.

## Composition

The legion fields sculpted-armour legionnaires wielding tower shields and thundering poleaxes — javelins that strike with thunderclaps and magically return to the thrower's hand. They fight in choreographed formations and can teleport into dazzling group poses that force enemies to avert their eyes. Their battlemages employ Sickening Radiance and Eldritch Blasts from the cover of Tiny Hut shelters.

Each legionnaire can flex their muscular form to manifest sculpted metal armour, rendering conventional projectiles ineffective.

## Operations

The Doomstar Legion was deployed to guard a crystalline Ziggurat in the Moonwood — a structure spawned by a Zhengyi Tome of Creation through a cursed victim. Working alongside Ancalagon the Black Dragon and a goblinoid army, they orchestrated a diversionary raid on the Barony to keep defenders focused south while the Ziggurat neared completion.
