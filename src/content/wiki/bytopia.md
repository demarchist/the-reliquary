Bytopia is an Outer Plane consisting of two parallel landmasses with reversed gravity, connected by great columns of ice at the mountain peaks. The lower land is warm, amber and red as if in perpetual autumn; the upper is verdant, wet, and stormy. The landscape is one of alpine meadows, rolling hills, and meandering rivers populated by great elk, marmots, and golden eagles.

Cirrus discovered Bytopia through the Well of Many Worlds. He found a well-travelled cobblestone trail carved into a mountainside, leading up to Stronghaven — the domain of the Gnomish god Gaedral Ironhand. He encountered a Rock Gnome caravan and traded a planar tuning fork, acquiring the means to return.

## The Lair of Azoo

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] returned to Bytopia in escort of [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] — the Adult Copper Dragon resurrected in [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]]'s chapel — for the planar leg of her ascension. They stepped through onto soft grassland under the warm, low-angled gold of a midsummer afternoon, the inverted under-sky of the upper land hanging above them, and were met by a Deva of [[callarduran-stonehands|Callarduran Stonehands]], gold-skinned and robed in the colour of broken geode, who had been waiting for them.

The Deva greeted them in flawless Draconic and led Azoo away to her assigned lair in the nearby hills — a great worked cave-system in the side of an extinct volcano, fitted for an Ancient Copper Wyrm and waiting for her to arrive. Below the slope where the dragon departed, a small gnomish settlement of round-roofed cottages and a bustling open-air market drew Cirrus and JmJm down into the candid, friendly curiosity of a people who had not in living memory been afraid of anyone.
