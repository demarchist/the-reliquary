Cassian is a traveler between worlds — a wanderer from the distant material plane of [[oerth|Oerth]], moving through the planes in pursuit of two things at once: the source of a prophetic darkness that had disturbed his sleep across many worlds, and a longer, quieter search for his missing parents that he keeps largely to himself. He arrived in [[faerun|Faerûn]] with no formal introduction, descending out of the night sky over a Damaran farmstead in response to a flash of crimson light, and accepted the invitation to ride with the group over a campfire before the embers had cooled.

## Appearance

His equipment is the accumulation of a life spent in motion: worn boots and practical leathers layered beneath unmistakably fine gear, a beautiful filigreed blade worn at his hip with the ease of long habit, a bow used with quiet and unhurried precision. He carries a cloak that renders him invisible at need — he had shed it before introducing himself in the farmyard but had arrived in it, which said something about his instincts. He dresses for the occasion when occasion demands it; he arrived at the Rising Sun in tails and a top hat.

## The Visions

Cassian had been following a thread of dark prophecy across the planes before the farmstead explosion drew him to ground. The visions were specific: a great evil moving through a northern land, the tolling of terrible bells, the dead stirring, and — most usefully — the certainty of powerful allies waiting to be found. He dove from the sky toward the flash and the frost and found what the visions had promised.

Over the campfire he heard about [[gaddock|Gaddock]], and about the waking version of the nightmares he had been receiving in his sleep. He asked to travel with the group a while, to trace the source of his dark visions. The group agreed without deliberation.

## Uni

Cassian's mount is a magnificent creature — a silver-coated Pegasus with a single spiraling horn at its brow, rendering her simultaneously winged, celestial, and singular. [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] coined the name Uni on arrival, the pun landing faster than a formal introduction could, and it stuck. Uni is serene to an unusual degree: she touched down on a chaotic farmstead in the middle of the night and stood perfectly composed throughout the encounter that followed.

She does not extend this serenity to [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s devil-horse, which she regards with undisguised celestial distaste. The feeling is mutual. On the road to [[helioglabulus|Helios]], the two mounts maintained maximum possible separation for the entirety of the journey, and the glaring did not stop.

## The Missing Parents

He mentioned it once, over the campfire on the first night: beyond the visions, beyond the dark purpose that had brought him here, he was traveling the planes in search of his missing parents. He did not elaborate, and no one pressed.
