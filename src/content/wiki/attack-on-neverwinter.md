The Attack on Neverwinter was a coordinated strike carried out during the evening of the Council of Helm's Hold. Multiple targets were hit simultaneously: the Bumidere estate, the Keep of Thunder Bluff, the Sundabar vault, and the city at large.

## The Assault

Fourteen fireballs were detonated across the city in populated areas as a distraction. The Bumidere estate was the primary target — struck by fireballs that overwhelmed its protective wards, followed by a ground assault from Amnian Shadowthief assassins equipped with Thayan Drop Box gear, the Oni Li Xian, the Amnian strike team (Farrim, Salama, Dellig, Xor, and Oana), and the possessed Cercei operating under the name Corpsetaker.

An ancient red dragon — larger than Rahadin — attacked the harbour, strafing ships and the docks with fire. At the Keep of Thunder Bluff, an iron golem was dropped through the ceiling followed by four Amnian wizards and a marble anti-magic golem that neutralised Delly Griplim's defences, killing her.

In Sundabar, eight Oni with bags of holding full of undead attacked the vault containing the Last Word fragments. The Oni were killed but tore a rift into the Astral Plane as a final act.

## Casualties

The attack devastated the Bumidere family. Esimir Von Bumidere was killed instantly by the initial fireball. Kurt Von Bumidere died to a Cloudkill spell. Sera Von Bumidere was killed by Li Xian's Circle of Death. Ernst Von Bumidere was killed but revived by Buumi. The Amnian delegate Gneiss was killed by poisoned crossbow bolts. Archmage Delly Griplim was killed at the keep. Abdul Al Selemchant was killed by the fireballs. Numerous city guards, servants, and civilians perished across the city.

## Aftermath

The attack marked the opening of a broader war. Thay invaded its neighbours. Amn — under Shaahir's coup — marched north. The Moonwood burned under a Drow assault. Waterdeep, Baldur's Gate, and other cities were simultaneously attacked. Tethyr, Narfell, and the Efreeti of Calimshan formally declared war on the North.
