## Background

General Omar is an Amnian military leader who commands forces during the Amnian campaign in the north. Despite serving the Amnian cause, he is regarded as a fundamentally decent man caught up in politics beyond his control.

## Role in the Campaign

Omar leads Amnian military operations in the region around Silverymoon. Unlike many of his political masters, he conducts himself with honor, creating a complex antagonist who is more sympathetic than the Amnian cause he represents.

## Notable Traits

- Professional soldier with a strong sense of honor
- Respected even by his enemies for his decency
- Caught between duty and conscience
