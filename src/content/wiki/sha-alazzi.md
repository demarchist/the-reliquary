## Background

Sha'Alazzi is a renowned merchant and enchanter whose grand magical shop is one of the highlights of the Neverwinter Refounding Festival. Her emporium offers incredible magical merchandise that draws adventurers from across the Sword Coast.

## Role in the Campaign

Sha'Alazzi hires the Spellforged Adventuring Company to clear a magical tower, providing the group with one of their early contracts in Neverwinter. Her connections and resources make her an influential figure in the city's mercantile circles.

## Notable Traits

- Operates one of the most impressive magical shops in the region
- Commands significant wealth and magical knowledge
- Serves as a quest-giver and merchant contact for the party
