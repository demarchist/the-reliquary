Lord Brusso Muskov is the head of [[house-muskov|House Muskov]] in [[helioglabulus|Helios]]. When the group infiltrated his home to steal the family Seal, [[cassian|Cassian]] discovered the Lord in a deeply disturbing state on the second floor.

## Appearance

He is sixty years old, thin, wiry, and obviously tall, with wispy silver hair. When Cassian found him, he was hunched over a meal tray, wrapped in fur blankets before a roaring fire in a room stripped of its windows. His complexion was pallid and sickly.

## The Symptoms

Lord Brusso was raving — hurling trays of food and screaming for the meat to be rarer, rocking back and forth complaining about the cold and a pounding headache. His servants waited on him in the adjoining room with the rigid posture of people who had been shouted at recently and expected to be again. The room had no windows, a roaring fire despite the summer heat, and trays of raw meat.

Cassian reported his findings to the group, suggesting that Lord Brusso might be one of the creatures they were hunting. The symptoms — aversion to light, craving for raw meat, complaints of cold, violent deterioration — bore scrutiny.

## Collapse

When the group convinced the second-floor guards to let them in under the pretence of healing the ailing Lord, they arrived to find a serving girl dropping a tray of raw meat and screaming. Lord Brusso lay sprawled on his arched back, arms and hands locked into an agonised rictus, his face frozen in a pained scream, his body utterly still.
