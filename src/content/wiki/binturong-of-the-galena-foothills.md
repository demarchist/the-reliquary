The Binturong is an enormous furry creature — a bearcat — that slumbers beneath the boughs of a massive sacred tree at the centre of a small village in the foothills of the Galena Mountains, along the road toward [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]].

The villagers bring it regular offerings and regard it as a patron of the land. Whether the creature is truly supernatural or simply ancient and venerated is unclear, but its size is far beyond any natural bearcat and its presence carries a weight that the villagers treat with quiet reverence.

The group stopped at the village for directions on their way to [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]] and paid their respects, offering food. The Binturong chuffed and purred in appreciation — a gentle giant whose most remarkable quality, beyond its stature, was the unmistakable smell of buttered popcorn that hung in the air beneath the tree.
