Lothli is a Wood Elf Ranger who joins the group with her tamed wolves. She trekked to the ruins of Thundertree alone, her wolves tracking a scent to Reidoth's old house, and single-handedly rigged a rope to pull the buried [[tavern-of-doors|Tavern of Doors]] free — reuniting the party after the green dragon Venomfang destroyed the settlement.

## Scouting and Combat

Lothli guided the party through Neverwinter Wood and served as lead scout during the infiltration of [[cragmaw-castle|Cragmaw Castle]], where she and [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] killed two goblins with synchronised sneak attacks. In [[wave-echo-cave|Wave Echo Cave]], she sliced an Ochre Jelly apart while [[doc|Doc]] blasted it off [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] with a gust of wind. She anchored ropes for Tark's descent across crevasses and kept the group moving through hostile terrain.

## The Refounding Festival

At the [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]] Refounding Festival, Lothli won the archery tournament after a rival half-elf bard was disqualified. In the martial tournament, she was defeated by [[hendrell|Hendrell]], the Champion of the Moonwood — a loss that sparked an unexpected infatuation with the elven champion. She left the tournament to spend time with Hendrell, while [[doc|Doc]] mimicked the cry of Hendrell's eagle companion from above during his bout with [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]], momentarily distracting him and costing him a hit.
