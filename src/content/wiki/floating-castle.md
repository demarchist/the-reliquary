The Floating Castle is a fortress in the sky above the ruined half-orc city of [[palischuk|Palischuk]], the operational seat from which [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] and his Red Greatwyrm [[vulkan|Vulkan]] direct the dark ritual their Oni army is performing below.

## Glimpses

The Castle first surfaced as *the Castle* — the destination to which [[themberchaud|Themberchaud]] was taken by Vulkan on Zhengyi's orders, named under Speak with Dead by an Oni at the doorway of the Ancient Red's looted lair. A later scrying showed Themberchaud lying in the desolation of a ruined humanoid settlement, with an iceberg of dark ice several hundred feet across hanging in the air two hundred feet above him — red-robed humanoids drifting beneath it, pale and bald, with wands in their hands and long trains of scarlet cloth flowing in unnatural wind.

[[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] confirmed the rest. She and several of the Dragonflight encountered the Castle in person above Palischuk and watched its occupants — Zhengyi and Vulkan, working in concert — direct an Oni army through a ritual the Dragons did not have time to identify before Vulkan and his hunting pack drove them off. A great [[golden-orb|Golden Orb]] drifted alongside the chase, its gaze unmaking dragons in flight.

## Implications

A flying fortress, an Archlich, a Red Greatwyrm, an army of Oni, a Golden Orb, and a half-orc city ground to bone for a ritual whose purpose is not yet known — assembled openly in the Vaasan north, within striking distance of [[damara|Damara]] and the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]]. Whatever Zhengyi is preparing to do from the Castle, he is not bothering to hide that he is preparing to do it.
