Velka is [[veygar|Veygar's]] cousin and a skilled [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] scout. She was part of the advance party sent to explore the ancient [[saurians|Saurian]] mines accessed through the [[ringed-veils|Ringed Veil]] network. When the [[cult-of-zalaam|Cult of Zalaam]] overran the mines, she survived by hiding in the ceiling catwalks and ventilation system. She dropped down to greet the group upon their arrival and warned them that something else was hunting in the tunnels alongside the cultists.
