## Background

Ruthir is a young mage who serves as Fëanon Sërindë's apprentice, studying the arcane arts under the High Elf wizard's tutelage at Fëanon's Tower.

## Role in the Campaign

Ruthir assists with maintaining the tower and supporting Fëanon's research efforts. As the party's operations grow in scale, Ruthir helps manage the growing infrastructure of their base of operations.

## Notable Traits

- Dedicated student of the arcane arts
- Loyal apprentice to Fëanon
- Helps maintain the party's tower headquarters
