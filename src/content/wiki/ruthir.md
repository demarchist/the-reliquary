## Background

Ruthir K'Ree is a young High Elf wizard who graduated from the Silverymoon mages' academy. After a few years of adventuring, he sought out a master and presented himself at Fëanon's Tower, offering a broken Ring of Spell Turning from Myth Drannor as tuition. Despite lacking a letter of recommendation, Fëanon accepted him as his first apprentice, thrilled at the prospect though careful not to show it.

## Role in the Campaign

Ruthir was given chambers one level below the great Library hall and got to work immediately for his new master. He assists with maintaining the tower and supporting Fëanon's research efforts as the party's operations grow in scale.

## Notable Traits

- Noble-born, identifiable by his pendant and class ring
- Eager and hard-working student of the arcane arts
- Arrived wearing school robes in notable disrepair from his adventuring years
