Amn is a powerful mercantile nation located south of the Sword Coast, governed by the Council of Six, a shadowy ruling body whose members' identities are officially secret. The nation's wealth is built on trade, with Amnian merchants and caravans reaching every corner of Faerûn. Amn's capital, Athkatla, is a city of immense prosperity, but the nation's pursuit of profit often puts it at odds with its neighbors.

Amn has been a significant antagonist in the campaign, launching a coup attempt in [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]] — positioning soldiers disguised as merchants throughout the city under the direction of Rama Al Kayan — and sending diplomatic delegations to Helm's Hold with mixed intentions. The Amnian retainers [[gneiss|Gneiss]] and [[chani|Chani]] initially fought against the party during the Silverymoon invasion before defecting after the assassination attempt at the [[horatio-von-bumidere|Bumidere]] estate. The backing of the Church of the Night Mother by [[shaahir|Shaahir]] and other Amnian wizards suggested deeper connections to [[orcus|Orcus]] and [[atropos|Atropos]].
