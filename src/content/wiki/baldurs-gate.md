Baldur's Gate is a large coastal city on the Sword Coast where the party arrives aboard a damaged ship during the third chapter. The city is gripped by fear — the necromantic mists have reached its streets, the dead are rising nightly, and mercenaries augment the city guard as civilians refuse to venture out after dark. The captain who brought them described it as "the end times."

## The Mist

The undead fog crept through Baldur's Gate each night, bringing wraiths, zombies, and spirits that killed anyone caught outside. When [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] held the [[artifact-shard|artifact shard]] out of their inn window into the mist, it was driven back immediately — a discovery that led to the shard being worked into [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]]'s shield. That same open window later admitted wraiths that killed seven people on their floor before the party destroyed the undead.

## Lady Faye and the Vault Job

[[lady-faye|Lady Faye]] hired the Spellforged to infiltrate Lord Fendrel's vault as a test of its security, presenting the mission through her associate Lord Hufflebram. The job was accessed through a masquerade party at the lord's manor. However, the entire operation turned out to be a [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] trap — the deep dwarf [[veygar|Veygar]] had been impersonating Lord Faendral, and both Lady Faye and [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] were captured and held as bait in the deepest chamber of ancient ruins beneath the estate. The party fought through the ruins, battled Veygar's forces, and rescued both.

## The Brotherhood and the Catacombs

The fanatical Brotherhood cult operated within the city, and the party confronted them in the catacombs beneath Baldur's Gate. An Alhoon — an Illithid lich — emerged during the fighting in the ancient tunnels, adding psionic horror to an already desperate encounter.

## The Atropal Detonation

Above Baldur's Gate, the party's confrontation with [[kangaxx|Kangaxx]] reached its climax. The golden lich had manipulated them into carrying him through the celestial vault's wards to seize the artifact shard, and when the party destroyed the Atropal Knight in a meteor crater outside the city — hitting it with three simultaneous fireballs — Kangaxx rocketed down from the sky too late. The Atropal turned to black stone and exploded, and Kangaxx detonated with it mere dozens of feet above the crater. The group was engulfed in light and awoke at a crossroads in a dark forest with a sign reading "[[barovia|Barovia]], 15 miles."
