The Bloodstone Lands is the common name for the cold northeastern frontier of [[faerun|Faerûn]], encompassing the kingdoms of [[damara|Damara]] and [[vaasa|Vaasa]], the Galena Mountains that divide them, and the settlements built from the region's distinctive scarlet ore. It is a land of harsh winters, brief golden summers, resilient people, and a long history of conflict with the undead armies of [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]], the Witch-King.

## Geography

The region stretches from the frozen tundra of Vaasa in the north to the farmlands and steppe of Damara in the south, divided by the Galena Mountains. [[bloodstone-city|Bloodstone City]] sits in the mountain pass between the two kingdoms, its walls threaded with the scarlet ore that gives the region its name. The capital [[helioglabulus|Helioglabulus]] sprawls across flat grasslands at the foot of the Damaran Steppe, ringed by concentric walls of dark stone shot through with bloodstone. The half-orc city of [[palischuk|Palischuk]] stands in Vaasa, its yurt-like structures a contrast to the fortress architecture further south.

Ironwood trees grow in the Vaasan lowlands — it was among them that [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]]'s parents made their home before their murder at the hands of [[li-xian|Li Xian]].

## Politics

King Yarin Frostmantle of Damara and Queen Concetinna of Vaasa rule jointly from [[helioglabulus|Helios]]. The Warlock Knights of Vaasa — who once sacked Bloodstone City — have merged with the Paladins of [[lathander|Lathander]] to form the [[crimson-dawn-knights|Order of the Crimson Dawn]], governing under the banner *For the Greater Good*. The [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizards of Thay]] maintain an embassy in the capital and trade openly, and the region sits within an axis of nations led by Amn that includes Thay, Vaasa, and Damara. King Frostmantle has signed non-aggression treaties with Zhengyi's Thayan allies, though rumblings of war with Impiltur darken recent days.

## The Shadow of Zhengyi

The spell cast by [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s master Theodemir that sealed Zhengyi has been failing, and Vaasa stands as the most likely staging ground for the Witch-King's return. Nobles and wizards across the Bloodstone Lands — anyone standing against Zhengyi — have been slaughtered in the night. A sickness spreads through the region, and the party's intelligence suggests that [[gaddock|Gaddock]], Tark's master turned vampire, now operates within [[helioglabulus|Helios]] itself.

## Connection to the Party

Tark looked upon his homeland for the first time in almost seven years when the group Planeshifted to the outlying farms of Helioglabulus. The [[flint-dagger-of-li-xian|Flint Dagger of Li Xian]] — the cursed blade that killed his parents — had been pulling him north toward the region for weeks before he destroyed it in a spectacular explosion on a Damaran farmstead. The group's current objective in the Bloodstone Lands is to confront Zhengyi's network, find and free Gaddock, and recover the [[sunsword|Sunsword]] stolen from the Cathedral of Lathander.
