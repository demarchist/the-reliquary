The Spellforge was an ancient magical artifact located deep within [[wave-echo-cave|Wave Echo Cave]], manifesting as a green glowing energy vortex pulsing atop a pedestal in an ornate chamber. It was the reason the [[rockseeker-brothers|Rockseeker brothers]] originally sought the cave, and the object of [[black-spider|the Black Spider]]'s obsessive search.

## Discovery

The party found the Spellforge in a vast cavern guarded by two Spectators — Igi and Gladi — who had kept vigil over the chamber for centuries. The guardians confirmed that the "Goat Prince" referenced in the Barlgura's prophecy could refer to [[orcus|Orcus]], Prince of Undeath.

## Absorption

As the group passed the Spellforge on their way deeper into the complex, the energy vortex flared violently — then snuffed out entirely. The [[jade-frog|Jade Frog]] statue in their packs sprang to life, becoming a living golem of animated stone. The Spellforge had been absorbed into the frog, along with the [[artifact-shard|artifact shard]] hidden within. The discovery set the party on the path that would define the rest of the campaign — the search for the fragments of a greater celestial artifact scattered across the multiverse.
