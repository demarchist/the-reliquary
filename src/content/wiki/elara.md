Elara is a High Elf scholar and member of the Conclave of Silverymoon. Though ancient, her elven heritage grants her perpetual youth. She is intelligent, curious, and compassionate — a skilled investigator with a keen eye for detail.

## Appearance

Elara is tall and slender, with long silver hair and piercing blue eyes. She is extremely beautiful and appears perpetually young. Her dress is always elegant and tasteful, and she carries herself with an air of dignity and grace.

## Relationship with Fëanon

Elara is a loyal friend and valuable asset to Fëanon Sërindë. She is able to see things from a different perspective than he can and is always willing to challenge his assumptions. She serves as a source of emotional support for Fëanon, helping him stay focused and motivated. Her intelligence, curiosity, and compassion complement Fëanon's own considerable talents.
