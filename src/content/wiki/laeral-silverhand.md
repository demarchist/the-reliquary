Laeral Silverhand is the Open Lord of Waterdeep — tall, slender, and striking in her middle years, with stark white hair, pale eyes, and white robes trimmed in silver. She visited the Keep of Thunder Bluff for a briefing from Mordenkainen on the growing threat of Orcus before teleporting back to Waterdeep that same night.
