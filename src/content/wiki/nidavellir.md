Nidavellir lies at the bottom of the Endless Stair in Dokkar, where the depths of Svartalfheim border a confluence of planes — Pandemonium, the Shadowfell, the Astral Sea, Ysgard, and others for those who look hard enough. It is tended primarily by dwarves and gnomes, who at the time of the party's visit were preparing a lair and hoard for a Blue Dragon coming to ascend — in friendly competition with Dokkar's citizens, who were furnishing a lair for a Silver Dragon.

On the border of Nidavellir and Svartalfheim sits the great Twilight Cathedral at the gates of Yggward, the necropolis where ancient Drow souls go to slumber.
