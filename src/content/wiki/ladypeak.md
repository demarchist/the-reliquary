Ladypeak is a jagged peak in the western Nether Mountains, notable for the ruins of the Lady's Hand Monastery on a shelf of rock two-thirds up its face. The monastery was abandoned two centuries ago, and local shepherds avoid the area due to reports of unusual seismic activity.

Beneath the mountain lay the buried hive of Nostalgia for Infinity — an ancient Illithid nautiloid fused into the mountain's roots for three thousand years. The nautiloid's organic tunnels extended deep beneath the peak, accessible through a concealed fissure on the northern face.

When the party reclaimed the Eye of Aren from the Elder Brain, Nostalgia for Infinity triggered the nautiloid's long-prepared launch. The bio-ship tore itself free from Ladypeak, devastating the mountain and leaving a massive crater of shattered stone and displaced earth. The party escaped the rising vessel and regrouped at the bottom of the crater.
