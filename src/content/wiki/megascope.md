The Megascope is a holographic communication device gifted to [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] by the [[harpell-family|Harpell family]], who wanted regular updates on the [[tavern-of-doors|Tavern of Doors]]. It consists of three brass tripods mounted with crystal-bearing armatures, lenses, and etched brass gears, set around a marble slab engraved with a focusing glyph. It was far too large to carry on the road.

The device can contact other Megascopes for long-distance communication and also functions as an astral meditation focus. It served as the party's primary means of staying in contact with allies across great distances throughout the campaign.
