## Description

Ishta is a Drow warrior of House Faen Tlabbar and Patches' sister. She wears full adamantine plate armour covered in intricate web filigree and spider motifs, carries a writhing snake-headed whip, and fights with the zeal of a true Lolth devotee. Her silver hair cascades in long braids, her head is backlit by a dark halo, and her eyes blaze with purple fire.

## The Luskan Ambush

Ishta appeared aboard the ship *The Moniker* in Luskan harbour alongside Jarlaxle and a Drow wizard, leading a squad of House Faen Tlabbar warriors. She confronted Patches directly, calling her an apostate and demanding her death. The ambush was complicated by six barrels of smokepowder beneath the feast table — a failsafe that Morik the Rogue detonated, destroying the ship entirely. Ishta's fate in the explosion is unknown.
