Faerûn is the primary continent of the world of Toril, stretching from the frozen reaches of Icewind Dale in the north to the jungles of Chult in the south. It serves as the stage for the Rise of Atropos campaign, with the party's adventures spanning from the frontier town of Phandalin on the Sword Coast to the subterranean depths of the [[underdark|Underdark]], the cursed demiplane of [[barovia|Barovia]], the alien world of [[al-askar|Al Askar]], and the blood-soaked tundra of the [[damara|Bloodstone Lands]].

## The Continental War

By the latter stages of the campaign, Faerûn is embroiled in a continent-spanning conflict reminiscent of a world war. On one side, an Axis of nations led by [[amn|Amn]] and including [[red-wizards-of-thay|Thay]], [[vaasa|Vaasa]], and [[damara|Damara]]. On the other, an Alliance led by Waterdeep and including [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]], [[baldurs-gate|Baldur's Gate]], and [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]]. The Barony of [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]] is a vassal of Silverymoon and thus on the side of the Alliance.

The war escalated dramatically when Amn deployed the [[vk-31-frostbite|VK-31 Frostbite]] — revolutionary aircraft that devastated Alliance military infrastructure. The Alliance responded with [[project-starfall|Project STARFALL]], developing the [[r-14-thunderchaser|R-14 Thunderchaser]] to counter the aerial threat. The mercantile nation of Sembia, allied with the Alliance, sits on a key supply line between the northeastern Axis nations and their Amnian allies — making its cities, particularly [[selgaunt|Selgaunt]], frequent targets of enemy raids.

## The Barony of Thunder Bluff

The [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]] was awarded the Barony of Thunder Bluff by High Marshal Arasume of [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]] for their service to the realm. The Barony sits in the Silver Marches, centred around [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]] — a cliff-top settlement overlooking Lake [[selunia|Seluna]]. It has become a significant power in the region, housing [[feanons-tower|Fëanon's Tower]], the [[fane-of-selune|Fane of Selûne]], the [[rockseeker-manufactory|Rockseeker Manufactory]], the [[jmjm-theater|JMJM Theater]], and [[the-establishment|The Establishment]].

## Existential Threats

The campaign's central conflicts involve three world-spanning threats operating in concert. [[Zhengyi]], the Witch King, commands armies of undead, Oni, and dominated dragons from a castle of black ice in the Bloodstone Lands, hunting for Fragments of [[the-last-word|The Last Word]]. [[orcus|Orcus]], the Demon Prince, seeks to reassemble the same artifact to achieve ultimate power. And [[vecna|Vecna]], the Whispered One, has devoured the gods of [[al-askar|Al Askar]] and threatens to extend his dominion to Toril itself. Behind them all looms [[atropos|Atropos]], the cosmic embodiment of entropy whose awakening would unmake reality.
