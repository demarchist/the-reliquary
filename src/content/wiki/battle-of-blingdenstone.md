## Background

After the group lifted the curse of Ogremoch's Bane and defeated the Stone Priestess, the Deep Gnomes of Blingdenstone rallied their forces — supplemented by newly forged alliances with the wererat warren and the Heralds of the Barony — to reclaim the northern sections of their city from the demonic forces of Juiblex.

## The Battle

The assault was executed as a three-pronged attack:

- **Northern strike:** Tark, Snorbul, and Thorna attacked from the north with cave badgers and Trisk's young warriors, cutting off the enemy's retreat.
- **Southern firing line:** Gundren, Cirrus, Feanon, and the Gnomish priests and druids formed a ranged line to draw the enemy south, supported by Chardalyn bells and devastating arcane firepower.
- **Central push:** Chief Diggermattock led the main Deep Gnome army — backed by Earth Elementals and the wererat fighters — through the central gates to flank.

Patches entered the Ethereal Plane to scout ahead, discovering the Dome's interior and confronting Juiblex itself. The Demon Lord offered an alliance against its rival Zuggtmoy; Patches declined and returned to fight.

## Outcome

The Balor Hisesophax was banished by Cirrus using a planar tuning fork. The ooze-impostors (Oblex creatures) were destroyed. Juiblex's shadow manifested to curse the victors before retreating north with its remaining forces. Casualties among the Gnomes were low — few dead but many wounded, healed by the restored divine conduit.

The battle ended the demonic occupation of Blingdenstone. In the aftermath, the Stonespeaker Crystal — key to Gravenhollow — was recovered from Entemoch's Boon.
