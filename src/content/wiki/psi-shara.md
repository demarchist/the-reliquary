## Description

The Psi'Shara are massive, armoured insectoids with powerful psionic abilities. They communicate exclusively through telepathy and can psionically dominate humanoid minds, compelling victims to march willingly toward their nests as food and slave labour.

## Role in the Campaign

The Psi'Shara were driven from their home inside the volcano of Mt. Saqar — likely displaced by the Cult of the Black Skull's activities there — and emerged onto the surface looking for prey. They dominated Liara, Thirsty Fingers, and half the surviving crew, compelling them to march south toward the volcano. The party tracked the caravan, discovered the domination, and fought the insectoids after Feanon detected their telepathic communication. Djoser and his Medjay arrived near the end of the battle to help finish them off.

## Notable Traits

- Giant armoured insectoids with potent psionic domination abilities
- Survive a disintegrate spell — extremely durable
- Communicate exclusively through telepathy
- Native to the volcanic interior of Mt. Saqar
- Displaced by the Cult of the Black Skull's presence in the volcano
