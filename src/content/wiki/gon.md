Gon is a mercenary serving under the barbarian Coho in a company operating out of [[iyum|Iyum]]'s dockside district. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] found Gon to be the most intriguing member of the group during their initial meeting. Under the terms of their hire, Gon was tasked with leading a squad into the dungeon beneath the Pyramid of Rudjek to establish a beachhead, while Coho reported to the Golden Tower for supplies.
