Queen Concetinna Frostmantle is the Queen of [[vaasa|Vaasa]] and co-ruler, with her husband King Yarin Frostmantle of [[damara|Damara]], of the joint kingdom administered from [[helioglabulus|Helios]]. The merger of the Warlock Knights of Vaasa and the Paladins of Lathander into the [[crimson-dawn-knights|Order of the Crimson Dawn]] is, in political terms, the Queen's institution — Vaasan warlock tradition wedded to Damaran paladin discipline beneath her hand.

## The Sequestration

By [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]]'s account, the Queen had grown unusually reclusive in the weeks before he stole the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]]. She appeared in public only at the social gatherings she hosted in honour of the [[red-wizards-of-thay|Thayan delegation]]; otherwise she was sequestered in the keep. The King had been with her throughout. The Order's discreet methods of checking the wellness of the monarch they were sworn to defend turned up no fault — but the Order's methods, Lord Aimu noted, were unobtrusive and by no means infallible.

## The Cathedral

When the Thayan delegation requested an audience with the newly-acquired Holy Relic of Lathander, Lord Aimu advised the Queen to refuse them. She overrode his counsel and granted the audience. She escorted the Wizards into the Cathedral personally, took the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] in her own hands, turned it once, and set it back on its plinth before the delegation withdrew to their quarters.

Lord Aimu watched the entire exchange. Something in the way she handled the blade — something in the cool quality of her gaze on the radiance — climbed into the back of his throat as a paladin's instinct of *wrong*. The paladins had no proof. He stole the sword anyway.

Whether the Queen is herself, herself-but-compromised, or no longer herself at all is a question the group has inherited along with the blade.
