Nalkara is a titan-like being who confronted [[thorna|Thorna]] at the Great Glacier blocking the way into [[icewind-dale|Icewind Dale]]. She claimed to be [[thorna|Thorna's]] divine sister and attempted to forcibly take her hammer. [[thorna|Thorna]] escaped but was badly beaten and was saved by the Halfling [[merrigold|Merrigold]]. The nature of Nalkara's claim and her true identity remain unclear.
