The Sapphire Circuits are components within the Ringed Veil that were discovered during the party's inspection of the gate at the Golden Tower. Original circuits — metal, crystal, and arcane housings — had been transmuted into pure sapphire through what Feanon identified as a mechanism resembling Instant Summons, with the components being swapped remotely and continuously.

The sabotage was subtle. The replaced circuits appeared functional but were subtly different from the originals, their purpose unclear even to Hasrala, the ancient Saurian spirit who helped diagnose the problem. Gundren and Feanon spent hours stripping every piece of Machina Factory salvage and replacing corrupted components while Feanon Counterspelled each new transformation as it occurred.

Despite their thorough debugging, at least one Sapphire Circuit was missed. During the ritual at the Font of Fawden, a panel on one of the Veil's outer radii began to overheat dangerously. Thorna cooled it with a Ray of Frost, and Gundren bypassed the circuit using a braided cord of precious metals recovered from the Machina Factory, allowing the ritual to continue.
