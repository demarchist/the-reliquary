The Well of Many Worlds is a legendary magical item that appears as a fine black cloth about six feet in diameter. When placed on a solid surface, it creates a two-way portal to another plane of existence, chosen either randomly or through the will of the user.

## Recovery

The Well was found among the remains spilled from the belly of a Changed creature — an ancient Devoured of the Pallid Prince — in the [[underdark|Underdark]]. It had been swallowed along with several elves and their possessions long ago.

## Cirrus and the Search for Selûne

The Well became [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]]'s obsession. Driven by his bond with [[selune|Selûne]], he spent night after night casting the cloth against walls and stepping through the portals alone, searching for the plane where his goddess resided.

One portal opened to an underground lake in [[svartalfheim|Svartalfheim]] on the plane of Ysgard — a cavern fed by a roaring waterfall, teeming with life and suffused with silvery light. There Cirrus met [[kirangelos|Kirangelos]], an ancient Behir, and encountered followers of [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]] — warm, white-clad [[drow-faction|Drow]] who offered to escort him to the petitioner city of [[dokkar|Dokkar]], where he could acquire a planar fork.

Cirrus continued opening portal after portal with the Well, desperate to find Selûne's domain. The cloth became his most valued possession — a doorway to anywhere, used in service of a single question.
