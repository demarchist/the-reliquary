Petey Greene is a fat, balding human man who ran the Sallow Manatee inn on the Neverwinter docks. Behind the legitimate business, he served as Captain Vaas Redblade's shore contact, laundering profits from the slave trade by melting down gold, silver, and pirate coin into ingots through a goldsmith named Blek Blekson. He was arrested when the party raided the Sallow Manatee, having been knocked unconscious before he could use the blunderbuss he had aimed at them.
