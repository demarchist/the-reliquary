Javen Tarkimos is a huge man in gold-trimmed plate armour with dark skin, amber eyes, short curling hair, and a pointed goatee. He leads the Watchers of Helm, the paladin order that guards and administers Helm's Hold.

Stern and serious in his mid-thirties, Javen initially questioned Buumi's motives for calling the Trials of the Crusader — wondering if the rarely-performed ceremony was merely a bid for celebrity. Satisfied by the sincerity of Buumi's response, Javen wished him luck and later arrived to watch the Trials in person, fighting alongside his paladins when the summoning went catastrophically wrong and devils breached the arena's containment.
