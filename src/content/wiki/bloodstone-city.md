Bloodstone City is a fortress city nestled in the Galena Mountain pass between Vaasa and Damara. Its walls are built of dark stone threaded through with gleaming scarlet bloodstone ore.

At the highest terrace of the city lies a plaza of gleaming marble gardens surrounding the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree of Bahamut]] — a massive white tree that serves as a pathway to the planes, linked to Yggdrasil itself. It was here that [[protanther|Protanther]], an Ancient Gold Wyrm, awaited the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragons of Shou Lung]] to begin their ascension to ancienthood. Two dozen yellow-robed monks attended the ancient wyrm beneath the tree's boughs.

The city's culture has a familiar thread to the dragons of Shou Lung, though they regard it as clearly inferior to their homeland.

## The Fall of Protanther

[[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] later confirmed that Protanther did not survive the disaster that struck the Dragonflight's gathering. The Red Greatwyrm [[vulkan|Vulkan]] and the Archlich [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] descended on the rite from their [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] over ruined [[palischuk|Palischuk]], and the abdicated king of the Gold Dragons fell to Vulkan in the engagement at the Tree. What has become of the plaza, the monks, and the Tree itself in the absence of its dragon-side steward has not yet been confirmed.
