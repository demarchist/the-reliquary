Bloodstone City is a fortress city nestled in the Galena Mountain pass between Vaasa and Damara. Its walls are built of dark stone threaded through with gleaming scarlet bloodstone ore.

At the highest terrace of the city lies a plaza of gleaming marble gardens surrounding the Great Tree of Bahamut — a massive white tree that serves as a pathway to the planes, linked to Yggdrasil itself. It was here that Protanther, an Ancient Gold Wyrm, awaited the Dragons of Shou Lung to begin their ascension to ancienthood. Two dozen yellow-robed monks attended the ancient wyrm beneath the tree's boughs.

The city's culture has a familiar thread to the dragons of Shou Lung, though they regard it as clearly inferior to their homeland.
