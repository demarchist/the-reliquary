Yarshud is an ancient elf — possibly half-elf — connected to the Convent of [[selune|Selune]] in [[amn|Amn]]. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] first saw him in visions from [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]], leading the ceremony in which his mother took her religious vows. The elf had wispy white hair and near-white eyes, and smiled at the young woman with clear pride. An earlier vision showed Yarshud comforting [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus']] mother as a lost child during a festival in [[athkatla|Athkatla]] — their first meeting, and the beginning of her connection to the Moon Goddess's faith.
