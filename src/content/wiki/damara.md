Damara is a kingdom in the cold northeast of Faerûn, bordered by the desolate land of Vaasa to the north. It is a hardy, frontier nation whose people have long lived under the shadow of threats from their northern neighbor, most notably the Witch-King Zhengyi and his armies of undead. Damara's warriors are renowned for their resilience and martial prowess, forged by generations of conflict with the forces of evil.

Damara's fate is closely tied to the sealing of Zhengyi, as the spell cast by Theodemir kept the lich's forces from pouring south through Vaasa into Damara and beyond. With the spell failing, the threat of Zhengyi's return looms large over both Damara and the wider North, making the party's efforts to contain the Witch-King critical to the kingdom's survival.

## Current Affairs

When the group arrived in the Bloodstone Lands, they found Damara much changed. King Yarin Frostmantle and Queen Concetinna of Vaasa rule jointly from the capital Helioglabulus. The Warlock Knights of Vaasa — who once sacked Bloodstone City — have made peace and merged with the Paladins of Lathander to form the Order of the Crimson Dawn, governing under the banner *For the Greater Good*. The Red Wizards of Thay maintain an embassy in the capital and trade openly. Rumblings of war with Impiltur and Thay in the west darken recent days.
