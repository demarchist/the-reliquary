Talley was one of two survivors the party discovered near the Machina Factory in Thueba Swamp. Exceedingly tall and athletic, wearing tribal furs, she possessed the ability to turn invisible — a result of the Machina implants threaded throughout her body. She had no memories before waking in the Factory a few days prior, when a storm shattered her stasis vat and she fled into nearby ruins.

After her companion — a silver-skinned man — exploded from a Sourcework Core embedded in his chest, the group attempted to save Talley through surgery. They found Machina implants throughout her body, all connected to a Sourcework Core fused to her heart. Despite Gundren's careful work to separate the core, the device detonated the moment it was extracted, killing Talley instantly.
