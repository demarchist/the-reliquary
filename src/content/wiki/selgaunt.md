Selgaunt is a sun-baked port city on the shores of the Sea of Fallen Stars in the mercantile nation of Sembia. A Mediterranean-style trade hub, it sits on a critical supply line between the northeastern Axis nations of [[Vaasa]] and [[Damara]] and their [[amn|Amnian]] allies to the southwest — making it a frequent target of enemy raids during the continental war.

## The Night Raid

The [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]] arrived in Selgaunt pursuing intelligence on [[Zhengyi]]. Under cover of darkness, unknown aircraft struck from the skies — their approach heralded only by an ominous humming. The assault was devastating and unprecedented: port facilities reduced to rubble, merchant ships transformed into floating infernos, the city's defenders obliterated within minutes. It was the first recorded use of the Amnian [[vk-31-frostbite|VK-31 "Frostbite"]] against a major Alliance target.

[[Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews|JmJm]]'s attempt to extinguish the resulting fires with summoned rain evolved into a tempest that sent mudslides cascading through the flood-prone coastal city, turning its streets into raging rivers. The fires were quenched, but the locals made it clear the group had overstayed their welcome.

It was here that [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] and [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] exchanged a knowing look and teleported away without explanation — the first indication that they had been keeping a secret for eighteen months.
