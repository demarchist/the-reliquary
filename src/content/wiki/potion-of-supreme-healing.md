A Potion of Supreme Healing is the most potent healing draught produced by conventional alchemy, restoring a vast quantity of vitality to whoever drinks it — sufficient to rouse a downed combatant from the brink of death and return them to fighting condition. The potion is prized among adventurers for the simple reason that it is rarely available when it is most needed, and its presence in a party's pack frequently makes the difference between a hard-fought victory and a wipe.

## Recovery

The potion was recovered from a small ornate chest in the lair of the [[elder-orb|Elder Orb]] beneath the [[mines-of-und|Mines of Und]], alongside a [[scroll-of-demiplane|Scroll of Demiplane]] and an [[instrument-of-the-bards|Instrument of the Bards]]. It joined the group's stock of emergency medicine — held in reserve for the inevitable next bad encounter.
