Shal Bartok commanded a tribe of Efreeti operating along the front lines near [[scornubel|Scornubel]] during the war. He had converted a temple of Chauntea into his war camp, surrounded by human tribesmen and living elemental fire, with prisoners caged in the burned-out gardens and a planar gate connecting to the [[city-of-brass|City of Brass]].

When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] infiltrated the spire and stole the legendary Efreeti Chain armour, Shal Bartok flew into a rage — summoning fire elementals and unleashing an enslaved Red Dragon to hunt the thief. He failed to catch her. Disgraced by the loss of the armour, he was recalled to the [[city-of-brass|City of Brass]] in shame, and his vassals withdrew from the front lines — giving a tremendous boost to the Allied war effort.
