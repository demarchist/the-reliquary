The Saurian Firelance is an ancient ranged weapon created by the Saurian civilisation before the Calamity. Despite being a thousand years old, its technology remains functional and devastating — projecting searing energy at range that far surpasses anything in contemporary weapon design.

Firelances have been found across the ruins and battlefields of Al Askar. A dwarven prisoner at Hohonu's tar pits recovered one from a pile of confiscated sacrificial belongings and used it to devastating effect against the Grand Shaman. The weapons are also prized by those who have occupied Saurian sites — in the Machina Factory, a gatling variant was mounted on a statue overlooking the main foyer and manned by a Mezzoloth fiend as an automated defence turret.

Gundren Rockseeker took detailed schematics of the Factory's turret emplacement, and the group recovered additional Firelances from the facility's storage lockers.

## The Lance Variant

In [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]], the party encountered the same Saurian sourcework technology adapted into an entirely different form: a ten-foot cavalry lance carried by the [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Vampire Knight of Frostmoore]], whose split tip channels arcane charge into a Lightning Bolt fired along the line of aim — the [[saurian-lance|Saurian Lance]]. It is the first appearance of [[saurians|Saurian]] weaponry in hostile Faerûnian hands on this side of the [[ringed-veils|Ringed Veil]] and raises the question of how such weapons have made their way back from [[al-askar|Al Askar]].
