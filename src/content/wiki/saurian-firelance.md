## Description

The Saurian Firelance is an ancient ranged weapon created by the Saurian civilization before The Calamity. Despite being a thousand years old, its technology remains functional and formidable.

## Properties

The Firelance projects devastating energy at range, representing Saurian technology that far surpasses contemporary weapon design. Its power makes it a prized find among the ancient ruins.
