The Drow, or dark elves, are a subterranean elven race that dwells primarily in the [[underdark|Underdark]]. Cast out from the surface world millennia ago in the Crown Wars, the Drow built a civilisation of terrible beauty and cruelty beneath the earth, dominated by the worship of [[lolth|Lolth]] the Spider Queen. Drow society is matriarchal and ruthlessly competitive, with noble houses constantly scheming against one another for power and the favour of their dark goddess.

## Role in the Campaign

The Drow have been a persistent presence throughout the campaign. The party was imprisoned by them at [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]] under the priestess Ilvara Mizzrym, navigated the political intrigues of [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]], and encountered both the cruelty of [[house-baenre|House Baenre]] and the mercenary pragmatism of [[bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'Aerthe]]. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] (Aliyah Faen Tlabbar) is herself a Drow of [[house-faen-tlabbar|House Faen Tlabbar]], and her complex relationship with her heritage drives much of her personal story — from the surface-world suspicion she constantly endures to her devastating weaponisation of Drow priestess disguises.

## Not All Serve Lolth

Not all Drow worship the Spider Queen. The followers of [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]], the Dark Maiden, seek redemption and a return to the light. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] discovered their petitioner city of [[dokkar|Dokkar]] in [[svartalfheim|Svartalfheim]] through the [[well-of-many-worlds|Well of Many Worlds]] — warm, white-clad Drow covered in mithril jewellery who offered him escort and aid. Patches' ancestor Kallista served as a priestess of both Eilistraee and [[selune|Selûne]], and Patches herself became a champion of Eilistraee through the forging of [[eclipse|Eclipse]].

## Drow Equipment

Drow craftsmanship is renowned for stealth-oriented items. [[piwafwi|Piwafwi]] cloaks — dark cloaks of elvenkind — are standard equipment for Drow warriors, granting near-invisibility in shadow. [[vizerion|Vizerion]] gifted the party a chest of piwafwi as a show of good faith.
