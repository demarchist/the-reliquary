The Amnians are the citizens and agents of the mercantile nation of [[amn|Amn]], known throughout [[faerun|Faerûn]] for their commercial acumen, political cunning, and willingness to use military force to protect their trade interests. Amnian merchants dominate trade routes across the continent, and their nation's wealth funds a sophisticated network of diplomats, spies, and soldiers. The Amnian approach to international relations is fundamentally transactional, viewing every interaction through the lens of profit and advantage. Their leadership includes the shadowy Council of Six, the Cowled Wizards under [[shaahir|Shaahir]] Al Selemchant, and military commanders like General [[omar|Omar]].

## The Battle of Silverymoon

The party's first major encounter with the Amnians was a coordinated invasion of [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]] during the Winter Solstice festival. Over a thousand soldiers — disguised as merchants during the preceding weeks — struck simultaneously with mages, warrior-priests, and the Ironarm Mercenary Company, who had infiltrated the city as plain-clothes agitators. Arcane bombs were placed along the city wall every six metres, and [[shaahir|Shaahir]] personally led a magical assault on the Palace. The coup failed when High Marshal Arasume and the Mythal Golems repelled the assault, and General [[omar|Omar]] was stabbed in the back by Abdul Al Selemchant during the retreat. The captured soldiers were ransomed back to [[amn|Amn]].

## Diplomacy and Betrayal

The subsequent diplomatic delegation to Helm's Hold included S'Olaa Al Kayan, Abdul Al Selemchant, Leigham Al Selemchant, [[gneiss|Gneiss]], and [[chani|Chani]]. The negotiations fractured when a coordinated assassination strike hit the [[horatio-von-bumidere|Bumidere]] estate — Amnian agents, Thayan assassins, and the Oni Li Xian attacked together, killing multiple members of the Von Bumidere family. The attack revealed deep divisions within the Amnian ranks: Gneiss and Chani proved honourable allies, while figures like the assassin Xor served darker agendas. Gneiss was killed defending the estate and later resurrected by the party.

## Military Capabilities

The Amnians have escalated the continental war through technological innovation. Their [[vk-31-frostbite|VK-31 Frostbite]] aircraft — manufactured at a fortified arcane foundry in the mountains — gave them devastating aerial superiority, with at least seven confirmed precision strikes on Alliance infrastructure before [[project-starfall|Project STARFALL]] fielded the [[r-14-thunderchaser|R-14 Thunderchaser]] in response. The Shadowthieves of [[amn|Amn]] serve as elite assassins with arcane tattoos enabling gaseous form and misty step. Amnian operations have included collaboration with Efreeti raiders in contested lands and supplying the Doomstar Legion with equipment for attacks on [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]].
