## Background

Jareem is a Ruby Crystal Dragon who takes the form of a fire genasi when in humanoid shape. He originates from the world of Oerth and came to Al Askar for reasons tied to his draconic nature.

## Role in the Campaign

Jareem's tragic story centers on his mate Ajak, who was killed and transformed into a Hollow Dragon that now guards the dead city of Sharza. The party encounters Jareem while navigating the complex politics and dangers of Al Askar.

## Notable Traits

- Ruby Crystal Dragon with a humanoid fire genasi form
- Hails from the world of Oerth
- Driven by grief over his corrupted mate Ajak
