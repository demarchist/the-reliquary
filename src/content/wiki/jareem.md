## Background

Jareem is a Ruby Crystal Dragon who takes the form of a large male fire genasi — pitch-black scleras, glittering ruby irises — when in humanoid shape. He originates from the world of Oerth, the same world as Mordenkainen, though the two have never met. He came to Al Askar centuries ago with his mate Ajak, sensing a perversion of reality that compelled them as Crystal Dragons — beings driven to preserve and stabilise reality — to intervene. The situation was far worse than they realised.

## The Tragedy of Ajak

Ajak was tricked into wearing cursed armour by the Lady in White, a powerful servant of Zalaam. The armour killed her and raised her corpse, transforming it into a Hollow Dragon that now guards the City of the Dead — Sharza. Jareem has been trying to save his mate and this world ever since, working through a network of elven spies and agents and sitting on the council of the Obsidian Order.

## Role in the Campaign

The party freed Jareem from the Cult of the Black Skull at the Titanic Forge, where he had been chained and tortured after being captured while trying to cleanse the Temple of Vaketh. Using his powerful psionics, he read each hero's soul — identifying Tark's Crystal Dragon-touched ki, Gundren's dual Modron consciousness, Thorna as a shard of Auril, and Patches as a cursed Dawn-Elf. His intelligence was instrumental in Feanon's deduction that Zalaam was the ascended archlich Vecna.

Jareem flew the party across Al Askar, assisted in the cleansing of the Temple of Vaketh (where they fought a Saurian Death Lord and Boneclaws), and revealed that he and the Obsidian Order had secretly freed the goddess Horrah — who was feigning continued imprisonment to avoid alerting Vecna. He accompanied the group to Iyum and helped unravel the mystery of Doc's 9th-level Dream spell, theorising it was Vecna's targeted attempt to eliminate a druid.

## Notable Traits

- Ruby Crystal Dragon with potent psionic abilities — can read souls, communicate telepathically, and detect magical auras
- Hails from Oerth; knows of Mordenkainen but has never met him
- Fire-immune, able to walk through lava and the Titanic Forge
- Member of the Obsidian Order's council
- Recognised techniques of a Ruby Dragon in Tark's ki, possibly linking to the Monastery of the Yellow Rose in Vaasa
- His dragon form is massive enough to carry the entire party in flight, though shapeshifting is taxing — a source of friction with Tark, who punched him for not shifting during the Death Lord battle
