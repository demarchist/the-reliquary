Peade is [[tark|Tark]]'s Carrion Crawler — a massive, centipede-like predator of the deep tunnels, capable of traversing any surface including walls and ceilings, whose paralytic mucus-tentacles make it one of the more dangerous ambush predators of [[underdark|the Underdark]]. In Tark's hands, it became a mount.

## The Taming

The rescue party encountered Peade in the tunnels south of [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]], following the scent of a familiar smell: Carrion Crawlers. The creature appeared in a large tunnel, already saddled — someone had been riding it before the party arrived. When Tark shadowstepped into the shadows to investigate, the creature panicked and bolted. Rather than fleeing, the monk strapped himself into the leg-mounted saddle, designed for inverted riding along the ceiling, and attempted to calm the spooked animal. A single Eldritch blast from [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] — aimed not to wound but to interrupt the panic — was enough. Between the jolt and Tark's steady hands on the reins, the crawler settled.

The monk named the creature Peade on the spot, noting with obvious admiration how naturally the Crawler moved along the ceiling. The saddlebags yielded a map of the area, a letter directing someone named Blurg toward [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]], and a fancy bottle of red liquor.

## Role in the Underdark

Peade served as Tark's primary mount through much of the chapter's Underdark travel. The creature's ability to move across any surface — ceiling included — gave Tark a tactical advantage in the cramped tunnel environments, and the Carrion Crawler's natural menace was a deterrent to opportunistic predators. Tark took to the creature with characteristic directness: he bonded with it through patience, darkness, and proximity, the same qualities the deep tunnels demand of anyone who survives them.

A Carrion Crawler is not a creature that inspires affection in most people. Peade is evidence of the distinction between most people and Tark.
