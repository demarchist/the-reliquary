## Background

Harold Ophrella is the ghost of High-Hold's former commander, killed when the castle was destroyed two years prior. His spirit remains bound to the ruins, unable or unwilling to move on.

## Role in the Campaign

The party encounters Harold's ghost within the ruins of High-Hold, where he shares the history of the castle's destruction and provides valuable information about the region's past and current dangers.

## Notable Traits

- Spectral guardian of High-Hold's legacy
- Possesses knowledge of the castle's history and the events that destroyed it
- Lingering spirit bound to the place of his death
