Mister Luko is the guild boss of [[helioglabulus|Helios]]'s criminal upper echelon, operating from the third-floor dining room of the [[rising-sun|Rising Sun]] with the unhurried composure of a man who has made a great many assessments and rarely been wrong about any of them.

## Appearance

He is older — late fifties, perhaps — with salt-and-pepper hair worn neatly, a silver goatee trimmed to a point, and the pale complexion of a man who conducts his best business after dark. He wears a bright red silk longcoat with white ermine trim, a black sash, and shoes of dark elegant leather with a curling toe. His hands carry more silver rings than are strictly necessary. His men share a uniform look: shaved clean to the scalp save for a tufty bounce of curls at the crown, with moustaches of magnificent size and considerable curvature.

## The Rising Sun

Luko runs the [[rising-sun|Rising Sun]] — an upscale gambling establishment and nightclub in the upper rings of Helios. The operation is multi-layered: a foyer with golden-barred coat-check windows and magical lockboxes, a grand gambling floor hazy with sweet smoke, two mezzanine dining levels, and a third-floor dining room where Luko holds court. The no-weapons policy is enforced without exception, and the guarantee of hospitality runs both ways.

## The Muskov Job

When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] was passed upward through the thieves' guild hierarchy to Luko's attention, he wasted no time. He had sent a team to steal [[house-muskov|House Muskov]]'s family Seal at the request of [[dimitri-muskov|Lord Dimitri Muskov]]. The team vanished without a trace — no arrests, no commotion, no word. The job remained unfinished and his reputation with it.

He offered the group a deal: complete the heist and account for his missing people, and he would share everything he knew about [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]], the [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]], and the city's current situation — plus a powerful magic item. Patches shook his hand across the dining table before the rest of the group had finished deliberating.
