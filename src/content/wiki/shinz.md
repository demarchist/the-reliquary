Shinz is a half-[[drow-faction|Drow]] girl — probably a slave — who works as a tout for Narbondellyn's Shadow in the Eastmyr district of [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]]. She approached the Heralds in the city streets and handed them flyers for the inn, describing it as the nicest place for non-[[drow-faction|Drow]] to stay.
