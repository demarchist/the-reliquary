The Long House is an upscale spa and lodging establishment located in the Protector's Enclave of Neverwinter. From the outside, its marble facade could be mistaken for a temple or private residence — the only indication of its nature is a green door with a golden lion knocker.

Inside, guests are greeted with a marble foyer, plush seating around a rose-petal fountain, and warm air flowing from the interior. The establishment is managed by a woman named Rei who insists on shoe removal upon entry. Amenities include mineral pools, treatment areas, saunas, and private suites. The VIP suite features a large bubbling mineral pool, four adjunct baths, recessed cushions, a fireplace, and two king-sized bedrooms. A crystal communication device allows guests to summon staff for refreshments, companionship, or various recreational substances.

The party stayed at The Long House courtesy of Galen Marek, who provided a private suite as thanks for killing the Red Grin Killer. It was here that Cirrus received a divine visitation from Selûne, who restored some of his physical deterioration.
