The Crystal Tuning Fork is a planar travel device recovered from the Sanctum of Krasus within Shatterpeak mountain. Crafted from crystal, it serves as the material component for the spell Plane Shift — but unlike a standard tuning fork keyed to a single plane, its Netherese origin suggests capabilities beyond ordinary planar forks.

## Attunement

When [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] learned Plane Shift on the same night that an Astral rift was torn open in [[sundabar|Sundabar]], he recognised [[selune|Selûne]]'s hand in the coincidence. He raced to the vault where eight Oni had combined their bags of holding, ripping a hole in the universe that showed the Astral Sea — purple nebulae and infinite stars. Cirrus dropped to his knees beneath the rift and held up the crystal fork.

The attunement took twenty-four hours. Everyone rested save Cirrus, prostrate before the tear while grim-faced dwarven veterans stood guard around him. When the rift finally collapsed, the fork glowed softly in his hand, humming with resonance — fully attuned and ready to carry him between the planes.

## Significance

The fork gave [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] the ability to reach other planes of existence, a capability that became central to the party's later campaigns — including their journeys through the [[underdark|Underdark]] and beyond.
