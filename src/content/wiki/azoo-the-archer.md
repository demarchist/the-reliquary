Azoo the Archer is an Ancient Copper Dragon from Shou Lung and a member of the Dragonflight. Their humanoid form is that of a Gnome. Azoo was among those who snuck into a local Green Dragon's lair alongside Asha and The Black Arrow to steal treasure and eggs while the other dragons kept the host occupied.
