Azoo the Archer is an Adult Copper Dragon from [[shou-lung|Shou Lung]] and a member of the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]]. Her humanoid form is that of a small, foreign-looking Gnome. Azoo was among those who snuck into a local Green Dragon's lair alongside Asha and The Black Arrow to steal treasure and eggs while the other dragons kept the host occupied.

## The Bitten Ward

Azoo was the small, foreign-looking [[gnome-of-the-vale|Gnome]] that [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] carried down the slopes above the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in a torn evening gown — bitten in the neck by the [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Vampire Knight of Frostmoore]]'s flight after days of harrying through the Galena foothills, the curse in its final hours. Her connection to Izmakella was that of a Shou Lung sister-dragon to a Copper resident in the west: when [[bahamut|Bahamut]] called Izmakella to the Dragonflight's defence and Azoo's route back to the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree]] was cut off, Izmakella took the bitten gnome under her arm and ran for the only hallowed ground she could reach.

## The Resurrection

[[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]]'s Lay on Hands closed every wound save the bite. The party concluded that the curse could only be broken by killing Azoo cleanly on the chapel's hallowed stone and resurrecting her into a body the bite had never touched. Izmakella refused at first; [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] turned her with a gentle account of the [[anduriel|Planetar]] whose body had given the [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]] its grace.

[[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] slid a knife into the gnome's heart. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] held a diamond above her and called on [[selune|Selûne]]. The small body grew in death — into the brilliant Adult Copper Dragon she truly was, larger than Izmakella in this form by half a length — and breathed as the silver light poured into her chest and the diamond disintegrated.

## The Tale

Azoo confirmed and extended what Izmakella had begun. The Dragonflight had been counted at ten, not nine. Several of them had broken off from the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree]] to the north in search of a missing Red Dragon emissary. They had found [[vulkan|Vulkan]] and [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] above ruined [[palischuk|Palischuk]] instead — stationed on a great [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]], directing an Oni ritual in the bones of the city below. The dragons had fled. Vulkan and a hunting pack had pursued, accompanied by a great [[golden-orb|Golden Orb]] whose gaze unmade dragons in flight. One of their kin had fallen to it. She did not know what had become of the others. She believed most were scattered alive.

## To Bytopia

[[bahamut|Bahamut]]'s compulsion to ascend had not faded with the bite or the flight. Azoo still had to reach her assigned lair on [[bytopia|Bytopia]] and become an ancient. Lord Aimu could not assist with the planar travel. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] could.

They stepped through with her onto the warm grassland of the Twin Paradises, were met by a Deva of [[callarduran-stonehands|Callarduran Stonehands]] whose god had been assigned her stewardship, and watched her go up over the rolling shoulders of the hills to the worked cave-system fitted for an Ancient Copper Wyrm. Her thanks were brief and dragon-formal. They would do.
