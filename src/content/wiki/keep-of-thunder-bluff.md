The Keep of Thunder Bluff is a formidable fortress standing atop the thousand-foot rocky prominence of [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]]. Its seamless walls of red-hued stone reflect moonlight across the valley. Built by skilled dwarven architects, engineers, and labourers, it was commissioned by the [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]] after High Marshal Arasume awarded them the Barony. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] proposed naming it Herald's Keep at the Council of Helm's Hold, and everyone agreed.

## Institutions

Within its walls stand two notable institutions:

- **The Temple of Helm** — a functioning cathedral with clerics and trainee paladins. Part of its copper roof was converted into a makeshift aviary where Bohd's crimson wyvern basked in the morning sun. When the barony came under attack, [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] leaped the wall to rouse the Temple's warriors.
- **The [[rockseeker-manufactory|Rockseeker Manufactory]]** — [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]]'s workshop, producing two firearms a month, extradimensional latrines for the settlement, and experimental weapons. Dogan of the Delzoun Empire toured it and looked forward to seeing what it would produce.

## The Siege

The Keep's most dramatic moment came during the siege by [[ancalagon|Ancalagon]] the Void and his goblinoid army. The black dragon flew over the [[evermoors|Evermoors]] flanked by a cloud of goblin bat-riders, bearing down on the Keep while Gundren sent word for civilians to shelter within its walls. The fortress that the dwarves had built proved its worth.
