The VK-31, codename "Frostbite," is [[amn|Amn]]'s revolutionary military aircraft — the weapon that changed the face of aerial warfare across Faerûn. Its capabilities are largely unknown to Alliance intelligence; every attempt to investigate has been met with overlapping circles of security.

## Capabilities

The Frostbite demonstrates capabilities that should be impossible — manoeuvres that defy conventional engineering, with no traditional propulsion signatures and no recognisable spell matrices. Survivor accounts and trace magical signatures are the only intelligence available. At least seven confirmed strikes on Alliance military infrastructure have been attributed to VK-31 formations, each executed with devastating precision against supply depots, communication posts, and defensive positions.

## The Selgaunt Raid

The most dramatic demonstration of the VK-31's capabilities came during the night raid on [[Selgaunt]], witnessed firsthand by the [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]]. The attackers struck under cover of darkness with an ominous humming as their only herald, obliterating the city's port facilities and defenders within minutes before vanishing. The raid represented a significant escalation in the Amnian aerial campaign and was the immediate catalyst for the [[faerun-defense-force|FDF]]'s recruitment into [[Project STARFALL]].

## Countermeasures

The [[r-14-thunderchaser|R-14 Thunderchaser]] was developed specifically to counter the Frostbite threat, with [[mcas-mythalmar|MCAS Mythalmar]]'s instructor squadron flying smaller, more manoeuvrable aircraft designed to simulate the VK-31's flight characteristics during training exercises.
