## Background

Izek serves as the chief enforcer for Burgomaster Vargas Vallakovich of Vallaki. He possesses apparent supernatural strength that makes him a fearsome presence in the fortified town.

## Role in the Campaign

Izek storms into the plaza demanding the party stop freeing prisoners, establishing himself as a dangerous obstacle. He serves as Vargas's muscle, enforcing the Burgomaster's increasingly unhinged edicts through intimidation and violence.

## Notable Traits

- Possesses supernatural strength beyond normal human limits
- Fanatically loyal to Burgomaster Vargas
- Feared by the citizens of Vallaki
