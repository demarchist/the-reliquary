The Lodestone is a powerful magnetic artifact of Saurian origin, recovered from a titanium puzzle box within the Machina Factory. It was found alongside a Scroll of Cloning, both locked away by Mezzoloth fiends who had been attempting to crack the box when the party captured them.

The device proved essential to navigating the Factory. Feanon used it to repel the ferrofluid traps that guarded several chambers, clearing safe passage through otherwise impassable corridors. When inserted into a hexagonal receptacle, the Lodestone activated ancient Saurian mechanisms — extending solid floors over ferrofluid pools and unlocking sealed doors.

In the Factory's foundry, the Lodestone was drawn into a ferrofluid-Sourcework amalgam, where it reignited the power core of an ancient Saurian Colossus. Feanon retrieved the device after the amalgam was destroyed.
