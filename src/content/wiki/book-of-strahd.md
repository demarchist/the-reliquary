## Description

The Book of Strahd is the personal journal of Count Strahd von Zarovich, detailing his history, motivations, and the events that led to his transformation into a vampire lord. It was found in a Vistani cart at Madame Eva's camp.

## Properties

The journal provides invaluable insight into Strahd's psychology, his obsession with his lost bride Tatyana, and potential weaknesses that might be exploited. A Vistani family safeguarded the book for generations, recognizing its importance.

## History

The party's discovery of the Book of Strahd provides critical intelligence for their efforts to either destroy Strahd or find another means of escaping Barovia.
