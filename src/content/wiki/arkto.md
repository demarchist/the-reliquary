Arkto is a figurine of power belonging to [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren Rockseeker]] that, when activated, summons a great winged owlbear. Gundren first used the figurine during the party's escape from [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]] in the [[underdark|Underdark]], where Arkto carried him, [[snorbul|Snorbul]], and the Svirfneblin twins safely to the cavern floor below. Since then, the winged owlbear has become Gundren's primary aerial mount, providing him with a stable platform from which to fire his [[thundercannon|Thundercannon]] during battles.

Arkto has been instrumental in countless engagements, from air support during the defense of the Barony to dramatic rescues such as plucking [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] from danger in mid-dive. The owlbear has also served as general transportation for the party, carrying children to safety, ferrying groups through [[ringed-veils|Ringed Veils]], and enabling Gundren's signature fighting style of raining fire from above.
