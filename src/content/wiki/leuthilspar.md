Leuthilspar is the capital city of [[Evermeet]], a place of soaring crystal spires and ancient libraries whose collections span millennia of elven scholarship. The city sits at the heart of the island's civilization, a testament to the heights of elven art and arcane mastery. It is the birthplace of [[Fëanon Sërindë]], who spent his formative centuries among its towers before departing for the academies of Faerûn.
