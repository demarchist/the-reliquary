Eclipse is [[patches|Patches]]' signature weapon — a sentient blade of glittering obsidian formed from liquid mercury during the cleansing ritual that destroyed the Reaver. Over the course of the campaign, the weapon evolves alongside its wielder, a reflection of Patches' journey from paranoid survivalist to champion of [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]].

## Origin

When Patches shattered the last hag's tooth upon the cursed Reaver atop the altar in the ruined Temple of Helm in Sundabar, the dagger's fragments melted into a pool of mercury. The liquid metal shaped itself into a levitating disk that bathed Patches in light as she was drawn to it. Her hair lengthened, her form became more lithe, and she reached into the liquid metal up to her shoulder to draw out an obsidian blade that solidified in her grip. Her skin and eyes shone with a rich lustre as she floated gently back to the ground — now bound to Eilistraee, the Dark Maiden, through the weapon.

## Appearance and Abilities

Eclipse responds to Patches' will, manifesting as a rush of liquid mercury when summoned — a momentary, shimmering cascade that resolves into gleaming black glass. The blade is heavily enchanted, capable of cutting through materials that defeat ordinary weapons. During a gem heist in Silverymoon, Patches used Eclipse to sever a glassteel display case that had stymied conventional tools entirely.

The weapon's sentient nature links it to both Patches' Drow heritage and her bond with Eilistraee. It is not mere equipment but a manifestation of identity.

## In the Battle Against Demogorgon

Eclipse's most significant moment came in the climactic battle against [[demogorgon|Demogorgon]] at the bridges of Ladueger's Furrow in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]]. As the Prince of Demons bore down on [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] — drawn by a bluff about the artifact — Patches levitated to a balcony height, leaped the width of the bridge, and landed on Demogorgon's back. She climbed him strike by strike. Finding the point where the two spines of the twin-headed demon fused into one, she plunged Eclipse into the Demon Lord's flesh. The obsidian blade that had begun as a pool of silver in a ruined temple met the oldest evil in the [[underdark|Underdark]] at the top of his spine.
