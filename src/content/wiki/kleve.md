Kleve is a Death Slaad who serves as bodyguard and envoy to the [[drow-faction|Drow]] archmage [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion DeVir]]. The Heralds first observed the creature in its true form while visiting [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]], where it appeared as a giant frog-like demon following [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion]].

Kleve later appeared at [[feanons-tower|Feanon's tower]] in disguise — a short, grey-skinned human with a frog-like face, beady dark eyes, lank hair, and uniformly grey clothing, a greatsword across its back. The disguise was unsettling in its near-humanness, and when it smiled to reveal pointed fangs, any pretence of normalcy evaporated. It delivered a dinner invitation from its master, then turned invisible at [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches']] request while the group deliberated.
