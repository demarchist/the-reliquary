## Background

Kenshi is a powerful monk hailing from the distant land of Kara-Tur. He is an old friend of Gaddock, Tark's master, and possesses formidable martial skills.

## Role in the Campaign

Kenshi rescues Gaddock from the forces of Zhengyi the Lich, bringing him back from captivity. His intervention preserves Tark's master, though Gaddock's condition after his rescue raises troubling questions about what was done to him during captivity.

## Notable Traits

- Monk of exceptional skill from Kara-Tur
- Old friend and ally of Gaddock
- Brave enough to infiltrate Zhengyi's forces on a rescue mission
