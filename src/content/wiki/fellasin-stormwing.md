Fellasin Stormwing is an Ancient Blue Dragon from Shou Lung and a member of the Dragonflight. Of the nine, Fellasin is perhaps the most volatile.

When the Rock Gnome archmage of a trade city used a Ring of Wishes to expel the Dragonflight, Fellasin battled the divine compulsion pulling him west and turned back to exact vengeance. He was deterred only by the sight of Aurelian already tending to refugees — unwilling to cause harm in front of his companion, he instead found the archmage and bit off his hand, taking the Ring of Wishes as recompense before finally submitting to the call west. He flew on with Aurelian in tow.
