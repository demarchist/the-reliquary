The Temple of Helm is a grand temple located within the walls of the Keep of Thunder Bluff. It is dedicated to Helm, the god of guardians, protectors, and watchmen.

## Architecture

The temple is a large, imposing structure with high walls and a tall, domed roof. The interior is spacious and open, with columns supporting the ceiling and a large central chapel. It is a plain and functional place, designed to serve the needs of the vulnerable, poor, and needy in the community.

## Purpose

The temple is run by a group of devout followers of Helm who work tirelessly to provide aid and assistance to those in need. It serves as a beacon of hope and a place of refuge, open to all who seek help or guidance. The Temple of Helm is well-respected by the local population and is an important part of the community of Thunder Bluff.
