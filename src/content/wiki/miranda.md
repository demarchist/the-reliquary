Miranda is the Warpriest of Bohd's Band, a human woman in her twenties with sharp, thin features, a dark complexion, shockingly bright eyes, and face-paint depicting the sigils of Sune. She wears elegant gowns with a large jewelled dagger on one hip and a prayer book on the other.

In the field she performs vital healing magic for the mercenary company. Outside of combat, she encourages passion in all forms — including operating escort services for the wealthy, the proceeds of which are funnelled to less fortunate artists, craftsmen, and anyone with a passion to be nurtured. She took up residence in the Establishment, paying five hundred gold per week in rent.
