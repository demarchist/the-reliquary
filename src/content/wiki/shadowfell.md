The Shadowfell is a dark, twisted reflection of the Material Plane, a realm of shadow, despair, and undeath. Colors are muted, light is dim, and an oppressive sense of melancholy pervades everything. It is the domain of the goddess [[shar|Shar]] and serves as a waypoint for souls of the dead travelling to their final resting places.

## Barovia

[[barovia|Barovia]] exists as a demiplane connected to the Shadowfell's dark energies — a cursed pocket dimension where [[strahd-von-zarovich|Strahd Von Zarovich]] was imprisoned. The Shadowfell's oppressive atmosphere suffused the valley, and the mists that enclosed Barovia drew on the same shadow-planar energies that fuel undeath across the multiverse.

## Shade Enclave

The [[netherese|Netherese]] floating city of Shade Enclave survived for centuries within the Shadowfell after the fall of Netheril. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] spoke with Lady Avelyere of Netheril at the Council of Helm's Hold, who recognised his robes as Netherese. He was disappointed to learn that the enclave had devolved from meritocracy to aristocracy during its long exile in the Plane of Shadow.

## The Underdark Bleed

In the [[underdark|Underdark]], the boundary between the Material Plane and the Shadowfell grew dangerously thin. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] jaunted to the [[ethereal-plane|Ethereal Plane]] and found pools of dark shadow and weakened planar barriers — the Shadowfell bleeding through. Denizens of the Plane of Shadow had begun to cross over, and the party discovered that their sleeping companions were being drawn into the Shadowfell through tears in the Ethereal boundary. Atop the high plateaus near the Spine of the World, the bleed was even worse.

## Yggward and the Twilight Cathedral

At the borders of [[svartalfheim|Svartalfheim]] and Nidavellir, where multiple planes converge — Pandemonium, the Shadowfell, the [[astral-plane|Astral Plane]], Ysgard — stands the Twilight Cathedral at the gates of Yggward. This vast necropolis houses the slumbering ancient [[drow-faction|Drow]] who followed [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]], their souls resting among elven mausoleums. It was here that [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' ancestor Kallista emerged — a gleaming soul walking out of Yggward, darting and teleporting closer between graceful strides — to bestow her blessing at the ceremony that named Patches Matron Mother.

## Campaign Presence

In [[al-askar|Al Askar]], [[vecna|Vecna]] commanded an army of Shadowfell monstrosities in the Black Sea, and Shadowfell creatures guarded the temples of the gods. The plane's necrotic energies empowered the undead forces the party faced across both worlds — a constant reminder that the darkness between planes was not merely metaphorical.
