Jacopo is the House Wizard of House Faen Tlabbar in [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]], tasked with training [[finn|Finn]] — [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches']] captive son — in the arcane arts. He clearly resents the duty and takes every opportunity to beat the young elf. [[finn|Finn]] reported the abuse to his mother during a Dream spell, noting that whatever veneer of civility had once existed in the household had dropped entirely.
