Borborygmos — known to the Bedouin people of the Anauroch as the Bone Hunter — is a gargantuan Cyclops who has lived on the High Plateau of the northern Anauroch desert since time immemorial. He is easily the largest giant the party has encountered, clad in expertly crafted armour of tanned Wyvern hide and carved bone, all made by his own enormous hands, and wielding a massive flail of lashed Wyvern-tail stingers.

Despite his primitive materials, Borborygmos demonstrates considerable culture and intelligence. He speaks ancient Netherese — reacting with disgust to Draconic — and possesses tremorsense, pressing his fingers into the stone to detect vibrations. He was found in the Wyvern Graveyard in a standoff with an ancient dying Wyvern, each waiting for the other to expire.

Cirrus negotiated in Netherese for the group to kill the Wyvern, but Borborygmos made clear he would contest the spoils. When the group slew the beast and fled with a severed leg, the Cyclops raised his hands and foot and smashed the ground, sending a tidal wave of stone and sand after them. They escaped through a teleportation circle with seconds to spare. Cirrus later sent the titan a gift and a letter.
