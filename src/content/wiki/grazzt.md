Graz'zt is a Demon Lord of Pleasure who was among the fiends summoned to the [[underdark|Underdark]] during the Demon Lord incursion. He took up residence atop the largest pleasure house in [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]], sitting upon a bizarre throne of sculpted muscle and flesh, presiding over an orgy of excess — gorging, drowning, dancing, and coupling unto death. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] scaled the building to investigate and was nearly pulled in by the Demon Lord's psychic assault before centring himself and leaping away. He refused to describe what he had seen.
