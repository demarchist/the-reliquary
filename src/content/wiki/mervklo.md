Mervklo is a Berbalang — a creature resembling an oversized imp with gangly arms, folded wings, and large eyes on an elongated head. The party discovered him inside the Sanctum of Krasus, hunched over a book at a lectern, where he had spent two weeks trying to decipher a Netherese tome without success.

Patches resolved the encounter with characteristic directness: a flash bomb to the eyes and tripwire hogtying before Mervklo could react. Furious and restrained, the creature nonetheless provided useful intelligence during its ramblings — mentioning a "Dream Orb" (a Beholder responsible for dreaming smaller beholder-kin into existence) and "the others" (the ghosts haunting the Sanctum). He had crossed from the Astral Plane seeking secrets.

The group was annoyed to discover they could read the Netherese text that had eluded Mervklo, which irritated him so thoroughly that he stopped talking entirely. Not wanting to kill the creature but unwilling to leave a potential enemy at their backs, they kept him hogtied on a Tenser's floating disk — until the trapped Beholder's antimagic gaze fell upon both Mervklo and the disk, dispelling both constructs simultaneously and ending the Berbalang's captivity in an unexpected fashion.
