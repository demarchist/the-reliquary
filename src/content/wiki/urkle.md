Urkle is a dwarven man who lives on the outskirts of the village of [[und|Und]] in the Galena Mountains, where he tends livestock. When the other villagers fell under the influence of a [[the-dreamer|Beholder]] hiding in the nearby mines, Urkle noticed the change — the dreamlike stupor, the unnatural contentment — and came into town asking questions. The villagers, controlled by the aberration they called The Dreamer, put him in enchanted ankle stocks on a silenced platform where he could not speak or protest.

The group freed him and he provided crucial information: the villagers had only started acting strangely a few weeks prior, and something in the mines was responsible.
