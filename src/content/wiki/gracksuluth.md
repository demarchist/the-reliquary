Gracksuluth is the major [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] capital city of the [[underdark|Underdark]], also known as the Dark Iron City. The party arrived aboard [[hemeth|Hemeth]]'s paddle steamer, passing Chain Towers covered in construct carvings before the stench hit — sulphur, oil, coal, and the harsh tang of metal and smog, a chemical sheen coating the water's surface. The city declared itself immediately: vast, brutal, and indifferent to outsiders.

## Layout

Gracksuluth is divided by Ladueger's Furrow — a massive chasm running through its centre, descended via a complex system of Duergar elevators that consumed the better part of two hours to navigate. All smaller rock formations have been cleared, leaving massive stone support pillars reinforced by Duergar engineering, with industrial complexes sprawling around each one.

Key districts include the Blade Bazaar — the largest market in the city, a crowded press of Deep Gnomes with Earth Elemental bodyguards, [[drow-faction|Drow]] in [[piwafwi|piwafwi]] cloaks, and even Aboleths in floating glass tanks — the Darklake District for foreign traders, the West Cleft derro ghetto at the bottom of the Furrow, and the Gholbrorn's Lair complex where foreigners are housed. The massive smithing complexes of Clan Anvilthew and Clan Steelshadow dominate the industrial quarter.

## Slave Economy

The sheer amount of slave labour was immediately apparent. Unlike the Drow, who took sadistic pleasure in tormenting their slaves, the Duergar approach was coldly practical — slaves looked dirty but well fed, kept in reasonable condition to ensure efficiency. Most were Orcs, Goblins, and other Underdark denizens, though surface dwellers appeared occasionally. The city's vagrant population was entirely Derro — mad, mutant Dwarf-kin.

## The Party's Time

The party navigated Gracksuluth's politics with forged work papers purchased from Hemeth's fence Chadle, with [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] passed off as a foreign Duergar and [[snorbul|Snorbul]] registered as a slave to prevent his capture. They dealt with the Grey Ghosts thieves' guild, the Council of Derro Savants, the dragon [[themberchaud|Themberchaud]], and the Aboleth Panoptes. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] politely stated that he hated Gracksuluth more than almost anywhere he had ever been. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] stormed from his room demanding to leave, unable to stand the city's dour mood any longer.

The smog thickened over time, seeping into buildings and making people cough. [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] built breathing masks using assassin gas masks from [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]] as a model. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s Magnificent Mansion provided the only real respite — clean air, proper food, and actual comfort within the extradimensional space.

## The Battle at the Bridges

Gracksuluth's most dramatic moment came during the climactic battle against [[demogorgon|Demogorgon]] at the bridges spanning Ladueger's Furrow. The Prince of Demons bore down on the party as [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] leaped onto his back and drove [[eclipse|Eclipse]] into his spine, and [[snorbul|Snorbul]] climbed the Demon Lord axe-stroke by axe-stroke.
