## Description

Raveel is a young man who joined the Cult of Zalaam out of desperation rather than devotion. He was the first of his group to discover the cult's presence in the Saurian mines, drawn by the promise of steady food and shelter in the harsh world of Al Askar. Finding conditions tolerable, he sent word for his friends — including Hazzim — to join him.

Within days, the cult's true nature revealed itself: the corruption, the dark rituals, the ghouls. By then, leaving seemed more dangerous than staying. When the party breached his quarters, Raveel surrendered immediately and renounced the cult's evil, offering to guide them in exchange for protection.

## Role

Raveel proved a valuable, if untrusted, guide through the deeper sections of the Saurian mines. He led the party to the Mural of the Word and revealed that a fragment of the Last Word lay within the Black Pyramid in the dead city of Sharza. He also warned them of the cult's defences around the main Ringed Veil, intelligence that proved critical to their assault strategy.

He carried tablets of communication used by the cult to coordinate between cells, which the party confiscated.
