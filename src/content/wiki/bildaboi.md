Bildaboi is the leader of a small Rock Gnome caravan encountered by Cirrus on Bytopia, the Twin Paradises and afterlife of the Gnomes. He inspected the warlock, deemed him trustworthy, and agreed to trade a solid gold planar tuning fork for Bytopia in exchange for Cirrus's zinc fork for the Plane of Water — plus a future favour valued at ten thousand gold. His caravan was heading to the town of Rumblecuppse in the valley below Stronghaven.
