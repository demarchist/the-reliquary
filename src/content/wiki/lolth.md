Lolth, the Spider Queen, is the chief deity of the [[drow-faction|Drow]] and the demon queen of spiders, dwelling in the Demonweb Pits of the Abyss. She demands absolute loyalty: worship of any entity other than Lolth is banned on pain of death. Refusing an order from a priestess results in punishment up to and including death. If there were no witnesses to a crime, no crime was committed. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] recounted these rules plainly as the party prepared to enter [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]].

## Menzoberranzan

Lolth's influence dominates the great Drow capital, where her priestesses command the noble houses and wage constant political warfare. The Shrine of Lolth in [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]] served as the outpost's spiritual centre, and the Drow priestess [[ilvara-mizzrym|Ilvara]] pursued the party through the [[underdark|Underdark]] in her goddess's name.

## The Curse of Blingdenstone

Lolth cursed [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]] through the Stone Priestess — a Medusa Drow wreathed in red lightning who had once served the Spider Queen before her transformation. [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]] confirmed the curse's nature when Patches communed at Gurnik's shrine. The party destroyed the Stone Priestess and lifted the curse, Patches decapitating the petrified priestess on Eilistraee's command — the Dark Maiden's daughter undoing the Spider Queen's work.

## The Demon Lords

When [[demogorgon|Demogorgon]] was cast from [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] into the Abyss, the Demon Lord's last coherent thought raged at Lolth: *How DARE she cast him from his own domain. But no matter — it must have cost her dearly. Perhaps even the fragment of the Weave she stole so long ago.* The Spider Queen's machinations extended beyond her drow — she manipulated demon lords and cosmic forces alike.
