The Warlocks of Kelemvor are an order of warlocks sworn to [[kelemvor|Kelemvor]], the God of the Dead and judge of the natural cycle of life and death. They are the Vaasan half of the merger that produced the [[crimson-dawn-knights|Order of the Crimson Dawn]] — the Warlock Knights of [[vaasa|Vaasa]] who once sacked [[bloodstone-city|Bloodstone City]] in the previous era, now bound under the banner *For the Greater Good* to the Paladins of [[lathander|Lathander]] from [[damara|Damara]].

## Doctrine

Where the Paladins of Lathander serve the rising sun and the moral renewal of each day, the Warlocks of Kelemvor stand for *balance* — the right and proper passage of the soul through death into judgement. They are not necromancers and they do not raise the dead. They oppose, with particular vehemence, the undead corruption that has spread north from [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]]'s domain and east from the cults of [[orcus|Orcus]] and [[vecna|Vecna]]. To the Warlocks, vampirism, lichdom, and the Witch-King's grown ziggurats are not merely evil but *categorically wrong* — violations of the office their god holds.

## In the Order

The order's command is jointly held by [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] of the Paladin half and [[lady-timea|Lady Timea]] of the Warlock half — the political and theological partnership that holds the merger together. Warlocks of Kelemvor accompany Crimson Dawn knights on investigations into suspected necromancy, as when the order arrived at [[house-muskov|House Muskov]] following the death of [[brusso-muskov|Lord Brusso]] and confirmed a lingering necromantic foulness in the corpse. They serve, in practice, as the order's living detectors of the dark.
