Elion Loberos was a young elf of striking beauty with dark hair and pale features, one of Theodemir's five acolytes who participated in the conspiracy to hide the Eye of Aren. He was the quietest of the group — watchful and reserved, rarely offering opinions during debates but observing everything with an intensity that unsettled his peers.

During the debate over the Eye's fate, Elion said nothing, simply inclining his head when the decision was made. When the Elder Brain betrayed the acolytes inside the hive, Elion moved with a speed and martial grace that did not match his mild scholarly manner, cutting down two Illithids with blades that materialised from nowhere and helping to extract the shattered Fëanon from the chamber.

He was experienced through the memory simulation by Tark, who noted that something about the memory felt concealed — as though it was deliberately holding back.
