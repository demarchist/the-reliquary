## Description

The Golden Tower — also called the Magi Tower — is a white marble tower topped with multiple spires, connected to the main palace of Iyum by a graceful arching bridge. It sits atop its own pillar of stone, originally serving as the seat of power for Rudjek's Grand Vizier, where the vampire lord and his Vizier created dark magical items and conducted experiments.

## Structure

The tower contains multiple levels: a basement built into the aquifer beneath the city, a throne room with a crystal control chair and golem guardians, trapped guest rooms designed by Rudjek as torture chambers, and two penthouse suites. The first penthouse — Kumuzu Isa's chambers — housed three small Ringed Veils (portals to other locations), an Efreeti Bottle containing the Insufferable One, and a tentacled horror embedded in the ceiling. The upper penthouse was Rudjek's personal chambers, filled with mimics, Antipathy spells, animate carpets, a Clay Golem, illusionary balconies, and a beautiful but enchanted planetarium.

## The Cleansing

The tower was protected by outer wards that summoned elementals, sealed doors warded against intrusion, and bronze statues that discharged lightning. The party brute-forced the wards, dispelled the doors, and claimed the crystal throne — Buumi breaking its security enchantments and Tark attuning as the tower's controller. In the basement, they discovered the source of Iyum's plague: hundreds of cages containing corrupt flesh that oozed disease into the city's water supply. They destroyed it and purged every level of traps and horrors.

Sultan Bashir granted Tark the title of Pasha so he could legally retain the tower, and Jareem remained as its guardian while the party departed for Thueba Swamp. The tower's Ringed Veils provided a portal network that the group used to reach the mountains above the swamp.
