## Description

Naima is the second mate aboard The Revenger, serving under Captain Drizzly Dawg. She is a Water Genasi with short-cropped green hair, pale blue skin, and a thin scar across her jawline. She takes command when the captain is incapacitated — which, given Drizzly's fondness for drink, is not infrequent.
