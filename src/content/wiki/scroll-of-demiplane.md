A spell scroll inscribed with the 8th-level conjuration *Demiplane* — when read, the scroll opens a doorway to a small, self-contained extradimensional space of the caster's design. The interior is a featureless chamber roughly thirty feet on a side, but successive uses of the spell can return to the same demiplane and elaborate it over time, allowing a wizard or a sorcerer of sufficient power to carve out a private pocket of reality for storage, sanctuary, or experiment.

## Recovery

The Scroll was found in a small ornate chest in the lair of the [[elder-orb|Elder Orb]] beneath the [[mines-of-und|Mines of Und]], alongside an [[instrument-of-the-bards|Instrument of the Bards]] and a [[potion-of-supreme-healing|Potion of Supreme Healing]]. The cache was unusually small for an aberration of the Elder Orb's age and stature, which the group attributed to the creature's earlier exile from a Netherese ruin in the frozen north — the bulk of his accumulated wealth remained in [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]]'s possession or buried beneath the glaciers that still concealed the original lair.
