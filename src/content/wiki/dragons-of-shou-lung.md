The Dragons of Shou Lung — also called the Dragonflight — are a group of ancient dragons from the hidden Empire of [[shou-lung|Shou Lung]], drawn west for the first time in thousands of years by a divine compulsion to ascend to ancienthood in new lairs across Faerûn. The flight was originally counted at nine; [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo]] later corrected the count to *ten*, suggesting an additional dragon whose identity has not yet surfaced.

The flight comprises chromatic and metallic dragons:

**Chromatic:** Asha Bellana (Green), Fellasin Stormwing (Blue), Vakuun the Dreadblade (Red), The Black Arrow (Black), and Xuefang the Adamant (White).

**Metallic:** Gzunteit the Bold (Brass), Aurelian (Gold), [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo the Archer]] (Copper), and Kalphena the Wise (Silver).

The dragons exude tremendous magic even as they travel — their passage reshapes the land, raising monoliths from the earth and summoning oases from the sand in their wake. They teeter on the edge of ascension to ancienthood. Their destination was the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree of Bahamut]] in [[bloodstone-city|Bloodstone City]], where [[protanther|Protanther]] awaited to help planeshift each of them to their new lairs.

## The Disaster at the Tree

The rite did not proceed cleanly. Before the flight could complete their planar transitions, several of the Dragonflight broke off to the north in search of a missing Red Dragon emissary the Pass had warned them was overdue. They did not find him. They found instead the Red Greatwyrm [[vulkan|Vulkan]] and the Archlich [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] stationed on the back of a great [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] hovering above the ruined husk of [[palischuk|Palischuk]], directing an army of Oni through a slow, deliberate dark ritual.

The Dragonflight tried to escape. Vulkan and a hunting pack of Oni gave chase, accompanied by a great floating [[golden-orb|Golden Orb]] the colour of fresh metal whose gaze unmade dragons in mid-air. At least one of their kin fell to the Orb. Meanwhile [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] — a Copper Dragon resident in [[helioglabulus|Helios]] — was called to the Tree's defence by [[bahamut|Bahamut]] and fought beside [[protanther|Protanther]] in the ensuing engagement. She watched Protanther fall before Vulkan.

[[azoo-the-archer|Azoo]] herself was harried south through the foothills for days by the [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Vampire Knight of Frostmoore]]'s flight of [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen Whites]] — cut off from her route back to the Tree — and finally reached the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]] as a bitten gnome under Izmakella's arm. She was killed and resurrected in the chapel to break the vampire's curse, then escorted to her assigned lair on [[bytopia|Bytopia]] by [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]].

The status of the rest of the flight is unclear. Most are believed to have scattered alive. Several are confirmed lost.
