The Dragons of Shou Lung — also called the Dragonflight — are nine ancient dragons from the hidden Empire of Shou Lung, drawn west for the first time in thousands of years by a divine compulsion to ascend to ancienthood in new lairs across Faerûn.

The group comprises five chromatic dragons and four metallic dragons:

**Chromatic:** Asha Bellana (Green), Fellasin Stormwing (Blue), Vakuun the Dreadblade (Red), The Black Arrow (Black), and Xuefang the Adamant (White).

**Metallic:** Gzunteit the Bold (Brass), Aurelian (Gold), Azoo the Archer (Copper), and Kalphena the Wise (Silver).

The dragons exude tremendous magic even as they travel — their passage reshapes the land, raising monoliths from the earth and summoning oases from the sand in their wake. They teeter on the edge of ascension to ancienthood. Their destination is the Great Tree of Bahamut in Bloodstone City, where Protanther awaits to help planeshift each of them to their new lairs.
