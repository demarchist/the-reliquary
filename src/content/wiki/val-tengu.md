Val Tengu serves as the bodyguard for the Bumidere family at their estate in Neverwinter's Bluelake District. A quiet, observant man, he was first seen smoking a pipe by the open door of a small tower turret on the estate's roof.

Val is extremely perceptive — he detected Patches moving behind him despite her considerable stealth, remarking "Drow, eh?" without looking back. He told her he had read Buumi's letters and was fairly confident she was one of the good ones, but warned her plainly not to hurt Buumi. He conducts regular security sweeps of the estate, checking every window, and was among those alerted when the group suspected Thessalt of being involved in an assassination plot.
