## Background

Ventari is a wood elf treasure hunter who encountered the party's camp near Mt. Saqar while searching for something valuable to bring back to his clan. He arrived astride a Tujalri mount and was initially cowed by the group's display of magical power — summoned constructs, fiery drake steeds, and flying brooms — but accepted their invitation to breakfast.

## Role in the Campaign

Ventari confirmed the legend of the Titanic Forge within Mt. Saqar's Fiery Gorge and guided the party into the volcano. He was adamant that the Temples of the Gods and especially Sharza were complete death traps, warning the group against venturing there. Despite his reservations about their broader ambitions, he was willing to join the expedition into the volcano.

## Notable Traits

- Wood elf treasure hunter seeking the Titanic Forge
- Knowledgeable about Mt. Saqar and local legends
- Saved Buumi from falling into a hidden lava pool during the approach
- Considers the group's plans regarding Sharza to be suicidal
