## Background

Ventari is a wood elf treasure hunter driven by the promise of the legendary Titanic Forge hidden within the depths of Mt. Saqar.

## Role in the Campaign

Ventari's quest for the Titanic Forge aligns with the party's own interests in Al Askar, leading to a partnership as they navigate the volcano's dangers together.

## Notable Traits

- Experienced treasure hunter and explorer
- Knowledgeable about the legends of Mt. Saqar
- Driven by the pursuit of the Titanic Forge
