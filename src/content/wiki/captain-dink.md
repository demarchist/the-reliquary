## Background

Captain Dink commands The Bearded Lass, a three-masted sailing vessel. He is a seasoned mariner available for charter work.

## Role in the Campaign

Captain Dink provides the party with naval transport aboard The Bearded Lass during their adventures, ferrying them between coastal destinations.
