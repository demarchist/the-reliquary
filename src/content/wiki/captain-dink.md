Captain Dink Duwafu is a dwarf sea captain distinguished by his shaved head, tattoos, fiery red beard, and black half-plate armour with a battleaxe strapped to his back. He commands The Bearded Lass II, a three-masted caravel painted blue.

Dink first appeared at the Fallen Tower Inn in Neverwinter alongside his companion Tara, a half-orc druid. They had been sent by Lady Faye to locate the party and invite them aboard. Dink surrendered his captain's cabin to Lady Faye during her stay in port, a mark of either loyalty or pragmatism — likely both. He continues to provide naval transport for Lady Faye's operations.
