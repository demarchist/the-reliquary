## Background

Chekov is a young Barovian child who was captured by Morgantha the night hag, destined to be used as an ingredient in her cursed pastries made from human flesh.

## Role in the Campaign

After being rescued from Morgantha by the party, Chekov attaches himself to the group as a squire. Despite the horrors he has witnessed, he shows resilience and determination to help his rescuers however he can.

## Notable Traits

- Survivor of hag captivity
- Loyal squire to the party
- Represents the innocent victims of Barovia's dark forces
