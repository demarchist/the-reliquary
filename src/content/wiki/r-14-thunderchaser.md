The R-14 Thunderchaser is a revolutionary magical aircraft developed jointly by the University of [[Silverymoon]] and [[rockseeker-manufactory|Rockseeker Corporation]] as part of [[Project STARFALL]]. Built from principles reverse-engineered from Spelljammer wreckage discovered in the [[moonwood|Moonwood]], it represents the pinnacle of combined arcane engineering and dwarven metallurgy — and the Alliance's answer to the Amnian [[vk-31-frostbite|VK-31 "Frostbite"]].

## Design

The R-14 is a sleek, single-seat fighter with a crystalline matrix at its core that pulses with arcane energy. [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]'s innovations in crystalline matrix manipulation stabilise the modified Spelljammer helm crystals that provide primary power, while the University's advances in thaumaturgical field dynamics enable the control systems.

The Thunder Drive system harnesses raw magical potential in ways the original Spelljammer architects never imagined — sophisticated, powerful, and temperamental. The craft demands perfect harmony between pilot and machine. In Gundren's words: "A thoroughbred — beautiful, powerful, and occasionally wild."

An early two-seater prototype — slower but more heavily armed — was also present at [[mcas-mythalmar|MCAS Mythalmar]]. [[Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews|JmJm]] claimed it on sight and flew it with [[Gustavo]] as his back-seater. Standard R-14 pilots were paired with Modron back-seaters — small clockwork creatures that handled targeting and systems monitoring.

## Combat Performance

In their first combat exercise against Viper's instructor squadron — flying smaller, more manoeuvrable aircraft designed to simulate the Amnian threat — the R-14s proved devastatingly effective. The [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]] eliminated the entire opposing force without a single loss, with JmJm and Gustavo's two-seater downing an instructor within seconds of engagement.

The aircraft's superiority came with risks. On the return flight from that same exercise, JmJm crossed [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]]'s jetwash, stalling an engine and entering an uncontrollable flat spin that sent the two-seater into the sea — a near-fatal reminder that the R-14's power does not forgive carelessness.

## Equipment

Pilots are issued flight suits, jackets, and helmets fitted with magical communication stones — "Rocky-talkies" — that enable real-time voice communication between aircraft. Each pilot receives a thick flight manual detailing the R-14's systems, reminiscent of a military NATOPS manual.
