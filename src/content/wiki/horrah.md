Horrah is the goddess of Time in the world of Al Askar. During the climactic battle at the Font of Fawden — with the group overwhelmed by Vecna's undead legions from Sharza, a Lich advancing, undead Storm Giants approaching, and more Death Knights diving from above — Thorna raised her hammer and called upon the gods to intervene.

Horrah answered. A golden light pierced the storm clouds above Vecna's pyramid, and time itself froze — everything and everyone except the party. The divine Timestop gave the group a reprieve to heal, regroup, and prepare for a final stand, while Cirrus used the frozen moment to push the ritual to its completion and destroy Fawden.
