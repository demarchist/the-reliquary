Fawden is the goddess of Chaos in the world of Al Askar, one of the last remaining deities being slowly devoured by Vecna (operating through Zalaam). Her consumption has been the cause of the increasing wild magic and High Storms plaguing the world. As Vecna consumed more of her essence, the moon dipped lower and magical instability worsened across the land.

Fawden communicated with Cirrus through a dream vision: he saw her as a gargantuan creature being consumed by Vecna in eel form, wailing in agony as her essence was devoured. She showed him the Temple of Fawden — overrun by corruption, a purple singularity floating in the ceiling vaults, the Saurian Death Lord and his retinue lying in wait. She also revealed a secret entrance through a cracked pool that fed water into the Underdark, bypassing the Death Lord entirely.

Fawden acknowledged she was dying but refused to let Vecna win. She proposed one last great act of chaos: during a ritual at the Font, with the Last Word floating above it, a portal would open and Selune would enter this world. The majority of Fawden's divine energy would flow to Selune, with only fragments going to Vecna and other gods. A small piece of Fawden would survive as an aspect of the new Moon goddess. Cirrus accepted the plan.

The party later executed this plan, and Fawden was destroyed in a flash of light — her sacrifice ensuring Vecna could not claim her full power.
