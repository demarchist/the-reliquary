The Bloodstone Pass is the main route through the Galena Mountains, running from [[helioglabulus|Helios]] into the mountain range. [[ironfang-keep|Ironfang Keep]] lies along the pass, marking the turnoff point for [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]] to the north — the location where [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] fled with the stolen [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]].

The pass climbs from grassy, tree-speckled hills through rocky foothills of sparse conifers, with the true peaks of the Galena Mountains rising steeply in the distance. Along the route, the group encountered ranger cabins stocked for travellers, herds of [[razorhog|Razorhogs]] hunted by local woodsmen using fireworks, and the small mining village of [[und|Und]] — a settlement in the foothills that had fallen under the psychic control of a [[the-dreamer|Beholder]] in its mines. Eight [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen White Dragons]] were spotted circling a distant peak before diving out of sight.
