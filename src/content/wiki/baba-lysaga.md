## Background

Baba Lysaga is an immensely powerful witch who resides in an annihilated town within Barovia. She serves as the master of Morgantha and the other night hags operating in the cursed land.

## Role in the Campaign

As the power behind Morgantha's hag coven, Baba Lysaga represents one of the greater threats within Barovia. Her influence extends through her hag servants who prey upon the desperate populace with cursed pastries made from human flesh.

## Notable Traits

- Commands other hags as their superior
- Possesses formidable magical power
- Resides in the ruins of a destroyed settlement
