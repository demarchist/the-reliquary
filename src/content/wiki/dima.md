Dima is a Drow warlock who tends the magic shop [[izzys-stuff|Izzy's Stuff]] in [[helioglabulus|Helios]], the establishment sitting directly across the cul-de-sac from [[tazmikella|Tazmikella]]'s Odds and Sundries.

## Appearance

He is a strikingly beautiful Drow man with cascading curls of silver hair, warm blue eyes, plum-coloured skin, and a smile of perfect teeth. He wears spectacles and carries himself with a relaxed, bookish charm — the group found him reading behind the counter with a half-full wine glass at his elbow.

## The Anklet

A magical anklet and chain adorns his leg, the delicate rings fuzzing to insubstantiality a few inches from his limb. The sight alarmed the group, who suspected enslavement. [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] nearly swore to destroy Izzy on the spot before Dima intervened — he had entered into his pact willingly. The nature of the arrangement and Izzy's true identity remain unclear, though Dima confirmed that Izzy is likely his warlock patron.

## The Elder Brain Ichor

Dima proved to be a remarkably well-stocked shopkeeper. When [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] asked about [[elder-brain-ichor|Elder Brain Ichor]] — the final ingredient for his [[windrunner-armor|Windrunner Armor]] — Dima produced a large purple jar of the substance from the back room. He noted it was the only specimen he had ever seen, remarking that Elder Brains do not normally part with their ichor. The trade that followed cost Tark the [[saurian-deathlord-greatsword|Saurian Deathlord's greatsword]], the Signet of [[house-faen-tlabbar|House Faen Tlabbar]], and a copy of the armor schematics.
