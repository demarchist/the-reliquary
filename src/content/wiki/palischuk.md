Palischuk was a Half-Orc city in [[vaasa|Vaasa]], in the Bloodstone Lands of northeastern Faerûn. Its structures were yurt-like, built primarily of canvas, hide, and wood, with stone used only for foundations and city walls. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] recognised the city in visions from [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]] while searching for his home village — a Halfling settlement hidden somewhere nearby in the forests and snowcapped mountains of the region.

## The Ruin

The city has fallen. [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] flew over it during the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]]'s northward sweep in search of the missing [[themberchaud|Themberchaud]] and found Palischuk ground to its bones beneath the [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] of [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] and [[vulkan|Vulkan]]. An army of Oni stood in the ruin, performing a slow and deliberate dark ritual whose purpose the Dragonflight did not have time to identify before Vulkan and a hunting pack drove them off. A great [[golden-orb|Golden Orb]] drifted alongside the chase.

[[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] identified the city by name the moment Azoo described it. Half-orc cities of that yurt-and-bloodstone profile are rare enough in the Bloodstone Lands that the description left little doubt. The destruction of Palischuk in the service of an Oni ritual under a Lich's flying castle is among the heaviest single blows the [[bloodstone-lands|Bloodstone Lands]] have absorbed in the present campaign.
