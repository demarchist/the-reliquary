Palischuk is a Half-Orc city in [[vaasa|Vaasa]], in the Bloodstone Lands of northeastern Faerûn. Its structures are yurt-like, built primarily of canvas, hide, and wood, with stone used only for foundations and city walls. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] recognised the city in visions from [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]] while searching for his home village — a Halfling settlement hidden somewhere nearby in the forests and snowcapped mountains of the region.
