The Windrunner Armor is a set of legendary equipment that [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] has been working toward for years, following schematics he acquired during the group's travels. The armor's construction requires a number of extraordinarily rare components, the last of which — [[elder-brain-ichor|Elder Brain Ichor]] — was obtained through a trade at [[izzys-stuff|Izzy's Stuff]] in [[helioglabulus|Helios]].

With all ingredients finally in hand, Tark began working with [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] to schedule the armor's creation at the [[rockseeker-manufactory|Rockseeker Manufactory]]. The full properties of the completed armor remain to be seen.
