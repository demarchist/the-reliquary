The Cloak of Wings is a magical mantle worn by [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] that, when activated, unfurls great feathered wings from the cloak's shoulders and grants the Paladin sustained flight.

He first deployed it openly in the battle above the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] — launching into the air to support [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] when the floating monk was knocked down a mountainside by a Red Wizard's arcane strike, and remaining airborne through the rest of the engagement to draw a bow on the [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Vampire Knight]] as he retrieved his paralysed ally. The cloak gave the Watcher of Helm the kind of mobility his plate armour and greatsword normally precluded — closing on flying targets, holding altitude over a chaotic battlefield, and putting a shot into a black-armoured vampire mid-flight before being driven back by a sphere of cold darkness.
