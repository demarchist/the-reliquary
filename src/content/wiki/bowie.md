Bowie is a planar traveler and the apparent father of Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews (JmJm). He has been exploring the Ringed Veil network of ancient portals, and his arrival brings both reunion and new complications. He aids the party against cultists within the portal network and travels with the blue-skinned native Maloo.
