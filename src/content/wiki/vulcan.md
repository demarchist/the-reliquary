Vulcan is a name surfaced through Speak with Dead — the entity responsible for removing [[Themberchaud]] from his volcanic lair and taking him to *the Castle*, acting on the bidding of [[Zhengyi]]. Nothing else is known about Vulcan's nature, appearance, or allegiances.
