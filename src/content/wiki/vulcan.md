Vulcan is the early-surfaced spelling of [[vulkan|Vulkan]] — the Red Greatwyrm in service to [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] who removed [[themberchaud|Themberchaud]] from his volcanic lair and took him to the [[floating-castle|Castle]]. The name was first heard through Speak with Dead at the entrance to Themberchaud's looted lair, before later confirmation by [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] and [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] re-spelt it as the more standard *Vulkan*. The full account of the Greatwyrm — his role at the [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] above [[palischuk|Palischuk]], his pursuit of the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]], and his felling of [[protanther|Protanther]] at the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree of Bahamut]] — is recorded under [[vulkan|Vulkan]].
