The Flint Dagger of [[li-xian|Li Xian]] is a cursed Anathame — a dark weapon of necromantic ritual, tethered to a soul or power — wielded by the Oni lord who murdered [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]]'s parents in [[vaasa|Vaasa]]. It served as a link between [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]]'s network and the party for months before Tark obliterated it in the [[bloodstone-lands|Bloodstone Lands]].

## Origin

The dagger first appeared in Tark's prophetic dreams while the group fled the Sanctum of Krasus. In the dream, he relived his family's murder and watched Li Xian transform into his true Oni form, pick up a wicked flint knife, and place it on his belt. The weapon was subsequently used in combat against [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] during the attack on the Bumidere estate, where Li Xian flew across the room wreathed in purple flame with the flint knife slashing. During the second engagement, Cirrus was saved from the blade only by a tomb of diamond-hard ice that formed around him, stopping the blow — the weapon chipped the ice but left him unharmed within.

## Recovery

When the group ambushed and killed Li Xian in the Underdark, the flint dagger was left resting on the cold stone floor among the Oni's remains. Tark picked it up and felt a thunderous heartbeat that forced him to drop it. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] grabbed it but bled from the nose on contact. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] identified it as an Anathame tethered to a dark power — a vision revealed a twisted skeleton with a blackened heart in a dark crypt. He broke the scrying connection, and the group placed the weapon within Cirrus's Ioun Stone's non-detection radius to prevent it from serving as a link to Zhengyi.

## The Pull North

The dagger did not stay quiet. Weeks later, Tark felt a pull at his hip — a faint heartbeat coming from within his [[bag-of-holding|Bag of Holding]]. Fëanon picked it up and felt transplanar power thrumming within. Patches recognised the blade as something akin to her old cursed weapon, Stabby. After the Corpsetaker's essence fled east, the knife fell silent — but Tark could feel the direction of its owner, the phylactery pulling him toward the Bloodstone Lands. By the time the group arrived in [[damara|Damara]], the dagger thrummed constantly, pulling him north.

## Destruction

On a Damaran farmstead outside [[helioglabulus|Helios]], Tark withdrew the dagger in the small hours and meditated before it. [[vaketh|Vaketh]] — the celestial passenger bonded in Al Askar — stirred and offered to help destroy the cursed weapon. Tark gathered his Ki and channeled it down through his prosthetic arm. Crimson light traced every line and joint of the white wood until it blazed. He struck.

The blow obliterated the Flint Dagger in an explosion of scarlet light that tore out the entire back wall of the barn and sent shattered timber raining across the moonlit fields. A spray of frost and ice from the detonation caught the silver light as the cursed blade was reduced to nothing. The explosion drew [[cassian|Cassian]] and his celestial Pegasus out of the night sky — the flash visible from miles above.
