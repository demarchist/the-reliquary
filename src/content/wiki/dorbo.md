## Background

Dorbo is the chief of Blingdenstone, the Deep Gnome city in the Underdark. Together with his wife Senni, he governs the gnomish settlement and manages relations with other Underdark communities.

## Role in the Campaign

Dorbo provides the party with hospitality and assistance during their passage through the Underdark. Blingdenstone serves as a safe haven in the dangerous subterranean world.

## Notable Traits

- Respected leader of the Deep Gnome community
- Governs alongside his wife Senni
- Maintains Blingdenstone as a beacon of civilization in the Underdark
