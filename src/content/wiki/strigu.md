Strigu was a warlock in service to Ceregorach, the Tooth Fairy, and the serial killer known throughout Neverwinter as the Red Grin Killer. His modus operandi was distinctive and horrifying: he used Meenlocks — fae servants of his patron — to terrorise victims with their worst fears through night terrors for days before striking, ripping out their teeth and removing their lips. At least forty-three victims were found this way.

In his true form, Strigu was barely recognisable as a halfling — pallid grey skin, stringy hair, emaciated limbs, jaundiced eyes too large for his skull, and teeth filed to fangs set in cracked lips. He could disguise himself convincingly, appearing as a dwarf or a human street urchin.

Cirrus Al Kayan tracked Strigu as part of his divine quest from Selûne to hunt down the remaining warlocks of Ceregorach. When confronted, Strigu was arrogant and dismissive, claiming Ceregorach was not dead and still had plans. He demonstrated considerable magical power, including the far step spell.

The final confrontation occurred at the Fallen Tower Inn, where Strigu attacked with a summoned fire elemental and Meenlocks to reach his latest target, a bard named Jake. Cirrus, bathed in Selûne's moonlight, appealed to the creature's capacity for redemption. Strigu accepted Cirrus's embrace — then sank his fangs into the warlock's neck. Gundren shot off his ear, and Patches severed his spine with Eclipse. Cirrus extracted the jade tooth of Ceregorach from the corpse and offered it to Selûne, where it crumbled to dust.
