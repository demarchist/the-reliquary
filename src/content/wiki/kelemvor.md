Kelemvor is the God of the Dead and judge of the afterlife in the Faerûnian pantheon. Unlike the demon lords and dark powers the group has encountered — [[orcus|Orcus]], [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]], and the vampiric forces spreading through the [[bloodstone-lands|Bloodstone Lands]] — Kelemvor embodies the natural cycle of life and death. Balance, not corruption.

## Warlocks in Helios

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] identified the Warlocks accompanying [[boris-muskov|Boris Muskov]] as servants of Kelemvor when they arrived at [[house-muskov|House Muskov]] following the death of [[brusso-muskov|Lord Brusso]]. The Warlocks worked with [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] and [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] to examine the corpse, confirming the presence of necromancy — a faint, lingering foulness — though they could extract nothing more specific. They later assisted in transporting Lord Brusso's body to the Cathedral on holy ground.

These warlocks belong to the [[warlocks-of-kelemvor|Warlocks of Kelemvor]] — the Vaasan order sworn to the God of the Dead, merged with the Paladins of Lathander into the [[crimson-dawn-knights|Order of the Crimson Dawn]] and co-commanded by [[lady-timea|Lady Timea]] beside [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]].
