Georg von Strapp is a retired captain of the Silverymoon Northern Fleet and the brother-in-law of Buumi, having married Buumi's sister Seska. After Seska's death, Georg raised their seven children alone in a villa outside Silverymoon, maintaining the household with military precision tempered by genuine warmth.

## Character

Georg is a tall, broad-shouldered man in his forties with close-cropped grey hair, a straight back, and the bearing of someone who has commanded ships. He is principled to the point of stubbornness — a patriot of Silverymoon who refused to fly Amnian flags on his estate despite mounting pressure from the Amnian trade mission. He proved to be a surprisingly accomplished cook and pianist, products of years as a single father.

## Maria and the Children

Georg fell in love with Maria, a young cleric of Helm sent from the House Invincible as governess for his children. Their quiet courtship was complicated by Baroness Elsa — Georg's intended — who noticed the growing attachment and confronted Maria, causing her to temporarily flee to the House Invincible. Maria returned, Elsa departed gracefully, and Georg and Maria married with Buumi as witness.

## Flight from Silverymoon

When Rama Al Kayan issued a veiled conscription notice demanding Georg join the Amnian Civic Militia, Georg accepted Buumi's help to escape. The group orchestrated an escape during the Midwinter Folk Festival — Georg and his family performing in the ensemble competition before slipping through the western gate under cover of Cirrus's diversionary illusions. Georg took his family and fled into the countryside.

## Fate

Georg and his family were later zombified by the Oni necromancer Li Xian — a devastating fate that made the family's escape from Silverymoon a bitter irony rather than a triumph.
