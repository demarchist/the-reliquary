Lake Seluna is a deep, dark body of water within the Barony of Thunder Bluff, nestled at the base of the Red Hills cliffs where the [[the-moonwood|Moonwood]] plateau meets the Thunder Bluff shelf. It is constantly replenished by the great river cascading off those cliffs in roaring waterfalls, and by glacial outflow from the mountains to the north, keeping its surface perpetually shrouded in mist. In its center lies the rocky, forested island of Sërindë Isle — home to Fëanon's tower. On the mainland shore stands the Fane of Selune, Cirrus Al Kayan's towering white marble spire.

The lake serves as a natural boundary between these two rival landmarks, with Fëanon's tower rising from the island and Cirrus' Fane looming on the shore.
