Archmage Carrowill was a renowned arcanist who once lived in Blingdenstone. Feanon summoned his spirit through Gurnik's ancestral communion and learned that a powerful ritual was hidden somewhere in the city — the ritual to create Prismeum, a crystal powder used in enchanting and to replicate spell components.
