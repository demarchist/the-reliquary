Razorhogs are massive wild boars found in the valleys and foothills of the Galena Mountains along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]]. They are hunted by local rangers who use fireworks to startle and herd the great beasts.

The group encountered a pair of rangers pursuing a herd of Razorhogs across a mountain vale. The animals were enormous and moved in a panicked mass, squealing in fright as the fireworks cracked behind them. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] harvested one of the Razorback piglets before the group returned to the road.
