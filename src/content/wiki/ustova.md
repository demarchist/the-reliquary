Ustova is one of the three Stone Giant archivists who maintain [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]], the legendary demi-plane library. He serves as the Archivist of the Future, residing in a great natural cavern beneath a magnificent amethyst chandelier that sheds crystal shards like falling stars. Easily twenty feet tall and clad in elegant purple robes with gold bangles, Ustova greeted [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] by name when the monk arrived to peer into possible futures, warning him that the experience would be hard on both body and mind.
