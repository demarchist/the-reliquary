Castle Ravenloft is the massive dark gothic castle of Strahd Von Zarovich, perched atop a twelve-hundred-foot column of rock — the Pillarstone — above the village of Barovia. It serves as the seat of his power over the cursed demiplane and houses his vampire court, crypts, dark sanctums, and an army of hibernating vampire spawn numbering in the hundreds.

## Notable Locations

The castle is a labyrinth of trapped corridors, haunted chambers, and architectural grandeur gone to rot. Key locations include: the dining hall where [[strahd|Strahd]] hosted the party for a tense political dinner; the Hall of Bones, a cathedral of death decorated entirely with human remains where the dragon skull of [[argynvost|Argynvost]] was mounted as a trophy; [[rahadin|Rahadin]]'s meticulously kept office with its crystal communication orb; Strahd's private library and study overlooking a portrait of Tatyana; the crystal heart high in the towers, a massive pulsing organ that shakes the walls with each beat and sends marble statues contorting in anguish with each pulse; the catacombs with dozens of stone crypts and a teleportation brazier coded to coloured gemstones; and Sergei's pristine white marble tomb where his perfectly preserved body, journal, the Armour of the Ravenlord, and platinum sword hilt were found.

## Infiltration

The group's approach to the castle was as dramatic as the battles within it. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] free-climbed the twelve-hundred-foot Pillarstone while [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]], in giant raven form, carried others through a lightning curtain that guarded the approach. Entry came through a bat cavern, a spider-infested chasm, and eventually the catacombs — where they discovered Van Richten turned into a vampire and mercifully destroyed him, and found hundreds of hibernating vampire spawn beneath the crypts. In Strahd's library, Patches appealed to the vampire lord's humanity before the group fled deeper into the castle.

## The Final Battle

The assault on Ravenloft became a three-way battle in the grand hall. Young red and white dragons stationed as statue guardians came to life. Lady Wachter's necromantic army of skeletons pressed from one side while the party fought from the other. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] killed the young red dragon with a psychic assault so devastating tears streamed from its eyes as it died. When [[rahadin|Rahadin]]'s form shattered, it revealed an adult silver dragon wreathed in shadowy power — protected by an Atropal entity — and the battle escalated to its final, desperate stage.

Meanwhile, [[strahd|Strahd]] channelled the Fragment of [[the-last-word|the Last Word]] in the desecrated chapel to tear the castle free of [[barovia|Barovia]]'s demiplane. The golden rectangle floated above a white marble altar, glowing white-hot, and energy pulses burned flesh to bone — though Strahd's vampiric regeneration restored him each time. The structure cracked and fractured under the immense magical forces — chunks of wall and floor broke free and floated in the strange energies, magenta light seeping through widening fissures.

## Anauroch

The castle materialised in the Anauroch desert with much of its structure floating in pieces — a state [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] believed might be permanent. The blue dragon [[anaxtrasa|Anaxtrasa]] claimed it as her domain, and the Rockseeker brothers harvested what they could from Rahadin's corpse before the three-hour deadline expired. Strahd's study remained intact — cold ash in the fireplace, grand windows open to desert air — and the catacombs still held crypts and caches to loot.
