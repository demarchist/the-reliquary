Dream Crystals are the strange, faintly glowing minerals that thread the [[mines-of-und|Mines of Und]] in lattices and veins, becoming denser the closer one approaches the [[elder-orb|Elder Orb's]] lair. Their structure is iridescent, their hue a shifting prismatic colour that depends on the light striking them, and their growth pattern is unnatural — too symmetrical, too geometric, too eager. They are not native stone. They are something that has been grown.

## Properties

The crystals are *cold* in a way that has nothing to do with temperature. Direct contact produces a leaching sensation that pulls warmth from flesh the way a wound pulls blood, and the surface of the crystal is coated in a microscopic film that bonds instantly to skin. [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] lost a thin disc of skin from a bare fingertip in the Elder Orb's outer chamber — the wound bloodless, cauterised by cold so deep that the flesh was preserved against the crystal's facet rather than torn from it.

Worse, the crystals *grow*. Filaments extend from the parent shard toward any flesh that has touched it, slowly enveloping it. Whether this is a feeding response, a propagation method, or simply the unconscious reflex of a substance saturated with the Elder Orb's reality-warping influence has not been determined.

## Architectural Behaviour

Throughout the lair, the crystals do not merely grow — they *build*. The deeper passages of the mine descend through a vast crystal shaft whose pillars, niches, lintels, and balconies repeat the geometries of the [[netherese|Netherese]] empire. At the bottom of the shaft, the crystals form a full crystal city: archways, facades, balconies, doorways carved into faces of crystal in the architectural style of an enclave two thousand years dead. Whatever Netherese ruin the Elder Orb originally inhabited in the frozen north, the creature carried the *shape* of that ruin with it, and the crystals beneath Und reproduced that shape from memory.

## Collection

[[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] retrieved several intact shards using Mage Hand and stored them in a [[bag-of-holding|Bag of Holding]] for later study, on the reasoning that whatever the things were, they were going somewhere they could not be touched until someone with relevant expertise could examine them safely.

Their connection to the Elder Orb appears to be substantial — when the creature was slain, the crystals throughout the lair stopped growing, the bioluminescence in their cores dimmed, and the architectural reproduction of the Netherese ruin ceased to extend further into the surrounding stone.
