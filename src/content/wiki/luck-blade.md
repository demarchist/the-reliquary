The Luck Blade is a legendary magical rapier that grants its wielder extraordinary fortune and the ability to alter the course of events through stored wishes. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] claimed it from a treasure hoard in [[barovia|Barovia]], her eyes going wide as she scooped up the small ornate blade.

## In Combat

The Luck Blade became part of Patches' signature dual-wielding style. She drove it through the eye of a Genoskwa during the assault on [[baba-lysaga|Baba Lysaga]]'s swamp lair, killing the regenerating beast before finishing it with [[the-reaver|the Reaver]]. During the moonlit cleansing ritual in [[sundabar|Sundabar]] that destroyed the Reaver, Patches wielded the Luck Blade alongside Strahd's rapier against the Drider manifestation of her greatest fear — rogue and abomination exchanging blow after blow in a blinding display of swordsmanship until Patches drove the blade through her doppelgänger's heart.
