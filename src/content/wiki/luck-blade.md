## Description

The Luck Blade is a legendary magical rapier renowned for its ability to influence fate. It is one of the most coveted weapons in the realms.

## Properties

As a Luck Blade, this rapier grants its wielder extraordinary fortune in combat and the ability to alter the course of events through stored wishes. Its magical properties make it one of the most powerful weapons the party acquires.
