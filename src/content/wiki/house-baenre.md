## Overview

House Baenre is the First House of Menzoberranzan, holding the dominant position in the drow city's complex hierarchy. Under the rule of Matron Mother Baenre, they maintain control through martial prowess, political maneuvering, and devotion to Lolth.

## Influence

House Baenre's dominance extends across the Underdark through control of trade routes, command of elite military forces, and their close relationship with the Spider Queen. They settle disputes between lesser houses and determine the city's grand strategies.
