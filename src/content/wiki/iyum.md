## Description

Iyum is a major city in the world of Al Askar, formerly ruled by the vampire lord Rudjek. After a successful revolution by the Usurpers of Rudjek, the city is now governed by Sultan Bashir. It contains the Golden Tower (Rudjek's former residence) and the Magi Tower.

## Significance

Iyum serves as the party's primary base during their adventures in Al Askar. The city faces ongoing threats from the Cult of Zalaam and undead forces. Grand Warden Djoser leads the Wardens who protect the city, while the Medjay enforce magical regulations.
