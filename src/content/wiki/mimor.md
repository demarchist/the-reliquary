Mimor is an elderly Frost Giant with a massive beard and dark, watchful eyes. He is the patriarch of his small family group, which includes Meljir, Ljokjar, and the young Fen.

When gnolls ambushed his family on the frozen lake beneath Shatterpeak, Mimor was the trailing member of the group. He and his mammoth companion bore the brunt of the attack, taking spears and arrows in their backs before turning to fight. The party's intervention turned the tide, and afterward Mimor regarded the small folk with stoic silence — his expression unreadable even as Cirrus and Buumi healed his wounds.

He accompanied his family to the frost giant settlement and spoke with the matriarch Ingrbotha about the newcomers, helping to secure the group's welcome at the wedding feast.
