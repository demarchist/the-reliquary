The Gnome of the Vale is a small, foreign-looking figure that [[lady-isabel|Lady Isabel]] — running barefoot in a torn evening gown — carried draped limp from one hand down the slopes above the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]]. She bore two oozing, black-edged puncture wounds on her neck, immediately identifiable to [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] as a vampire bite.

Isabel handed her to [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] at the estate's edge with instructions to take her to the chapel — *hallowed ground*. JmJm carried her into the Lathanderian chapel and laid her at the feet of [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]], who knelt and used Lay on Hands to heal the bruises, the cuts, and the body's broader trauma. Every wound closed save the puncture marks on her neck, which remained black-edged and untreated.

## Not a Gnome

When he sat back from the healing, Lord Aimu said quietly that the strength of channelled grace required to mend the small body had been *unusual*. It had not, in his estimation, been the strength one would summon to heal a Gnome.

He did not elaborate.

That an Adult Copper Dragon was carrying her down a mountain by hand, in a torn gown, through a hostile dragon flight, at speed, to consecrated ground — and that the divine effort to heal her exceeded what her apparent species would warrant — suggests strongly that she is something else under the small foreign face. What, exactly, has not yet been revealed. Her condition remains stable on the chapel floor, the bite held at bay by the hallowed stone, and the question deferred.
