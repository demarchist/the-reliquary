The *Gnome of the Vale* was the humanoid disguise of [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] — the Adult Copper Dragon whom [[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] carried, unconscious and bitten, down the slopes above the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]]. The small body bore two oozing, black-edged puncture wounds on her neck, identifiable on sight to [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] as a vampire bite.

Isabel handed her to [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] at the estate's edge with instructions to take her to the chapel — *hallowed ground*. JmJm carried her into the Lathanderian chapel and laid her at the feet of [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]], who knelt and used Lay on Hands to heal the bruises, the cuts, and the body's broader trauma. Every wound closed save the puncture marks on her neck, which remained black-edged and untreated.

## Not a Gnome

When he sat back from the healing, Lord Aimu said quietly that the strength of channelled grace required to mend the small body had been *unusual*. It had not, in his estimation, been the strength one would summon to heal a Gnome.

He did not elaborate.

## Revelation and Resurrection

With the curse in its final hours and Lord Aimu unable to lift it, the party proposed killing her cleanly on hallowed stone and resurrecting her into a body the bite had never touched. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] put a knife in her chest; [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] held a diamond above her and called on [[selune|Selûne]]. The small gnome body grew in death — into the brilliant adult form of [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]], a Copper Dragon of the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]] — and breathed life again as the diamond disintegrated and the silver light poured into her chest.

The Gnome had been the dragon's careful disguise the entire time. The strength Lord Aimu had needed was the strength to heal a Copper Dragon. The full account is recorded under [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo the Archer]].
