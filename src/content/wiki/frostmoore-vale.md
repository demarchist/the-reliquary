Frostmoore Vale lies in the foothills of the Galena Mountains, a day's travel north from [[ironfang-keep|Ironfang Keep]] on the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]]. According to [[mister-luko|Mister Luko]]'s intelligence, it is the location of [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]]'s wife's old estate — and the place where Aimu actually fled after stealing the [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]], having doubled back in secret rather than continuing east to his own lands on the steppe border as the Crown believes.

## The Vale Itself

The party reached the ridge above the vale in late afternoon and looked down into a wooded valley nestled between two mountain shoulders — a glittering lake at the back of it fed by a cascade of small waterfalls running down from the high peaks, terraced vineyards on the southern slopes, vegetable gardens and an orchard near the water. The [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] sat at the back of the valley, modest by capital standards, with a separate and notably ornate chapel of [[lathander|Lathander]] set apart from the manor house. The road switchbacked down off the ridge to the manor's front porch.

## The Battle

The vale was the site of the battle that joined the party's arrival at the estate. Eight [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen White Dragons]] crested the ridge at sundown carrying a [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizard of Thay]] on the largest; the [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Vampire Knight of Frostmoore]] teleported in moments later on a sickly adult white dragon mount wielding an adapted [[saurian-lance|Saurian lance-weapon]]; and on the slopes above the estate [[lady-isabel|Lady Isabel]] of [[helioglabulus|Helios]] revealed herself as an Adult Copper Dragon and joined the fight at the lake's edge. The estate's east wing was torn open by a lightning bolt; trees and vineyards were scorched; and a Red Wizard of Thay died in the saddle of his mount above the chapel.
