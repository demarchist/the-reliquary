## Description

Karthus is a deity of Al Askar who embodies protection, guardianship, and the defence of those who cannot defend themselves — tenets identical to those of Helm on Toril. The Temple of Karthus near Ras Alkan is one of the pilgrimage sites of the Ancients.

## Significance

When the party arrived on Al Askar and discovered their divine connections to Toril's gods were severed, Buumi theorized that gods were transcendent embodiments of universal truths rather than geographic entities. He asked Liara which god on Al Askar defended the defenceless, and she named Karthus. By continuing to live by Helm's code — guardianship, protection, justice — Buumi was able to sustain his divine connection through Karthus, confirming that the gods of Toril existed on Al Askar under different names.

At the Battle of the Font of Fawden, Karthus intervened directly when Tark called for Jareem's aid via the ruby scale. The Crystal Dragon erupted from an orange portal of light, his entire body encased in a swirling carapace of divine armour that made him nigh invulnerable. Clad in Karthus's protection, Jareem engaged the Hollow Dragon — the desecrated corpse of his own mate — and fought the undead abomination to a standstill.
