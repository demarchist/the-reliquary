Lady Avelyere is a [[netherese|Netherese]] noblewoman and arcanist from the [[shade-enclave|Shade Enclave]] — the floating city that survived the fall of Netheril by retreating into the [[shadowfell|Shadowfell]] for centuries. She serves as the Enclave's diplomatic envoy to the surface world and represents the Netherese drive to reclaim their scattered cultural heritage.

## The Council of Helm's Hold

[[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] first encountered Lady Avelyere at the Council of Helm's Hold, where she appeared in a blue gown among the assembled delegates. She recognised his robes as Netherese and was visibly displeased to see an outsider wearing them. Their conversation revealed that the [[shade-enclave|Shade Enclave]] had devolved from the meritocracy it once was into an aristocracy during its long exile in the Plane of Shadow — a disappointment to Fëanon, who admired the original empire's ideals.

## Visit to the Barony

Fëanon invited Lady Avelyere to [[selunia|the Barony]], and she accepted — arriving in an ornate black carriage flanked by two Shadowmonk bodyguards. She surveyed his looted Netherese artefacts with displeasure before touring [[feanons-tower|Fëanon's Tower]], where she was genuinely impressed by his operation. Over the course of their discussions, she expressed strong interest in acquiring the [[iron-tome|Iron Tome]] and Fëanon's robes, proposing a contract: the artefacts would be returned to the Enclave upon his natural death. He said he would consider it.

They reached several agreements: Netherese nobles would patronise the Barony's establishment and auction house, and her Coven would personally acquire any other Netherese artefacts the group obtained. Fëanon carefully steered the conversation toward [[mythallar|Mythallars]], probing her knowledge while taking care not to reveal that he possessed one in an unfinished state. She steered just as carefully away from the subject.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] had drinks with Lady Avelyere and rubbed it in Fëanon's face. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] sparred with one of her Shadowmonk bodyguards — defeating the man easily — and was invited to visit the [[shade-enclave|Shade Enclave]] near Myth Drannor to see the Way of the Pointed Step and meet Master Jorell, a vampire monk who supposedly never needs to feed.
