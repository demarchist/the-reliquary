## Overview

House Faen Tlabbar is a drow noble house within Menzoberranzan's hierarchy. It is the family of party member Patches (Aliyah Faen Tlabbar), led by her mother, the cunning Matron Vladalma.

## Politics

The house maintains its position through careful alliances and economic interests. Vladalma uses agents like Lo'Dan to monitor and manipulate her daughter Patches on the surface, reflecting the ever-present reach of drow politics.

## Significance

House Faen Tlabbar's influence creates ongoing personal complications for Patches, whose surface life is constantly threatened by her mother's schemes and the expectations of drow society.
