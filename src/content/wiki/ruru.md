Ruru is Camea's brother and the ruling high priest of 'Āina Pulu, presiding over a crude court with a massive corrupted Sacrosu — a monstrous crocodilian beast — at his feet. Under Hohonu's influence, Ruru and the Abyss Walker faction seized control of the halfling village, performing blood sacrifices at the tar pits to weaken the Death Wyrm's prison.

When the party infiltrated Aina Pulu disguised as prisoners, Ruru tested Camea's loyalty by threatening to mutilate the captives. He was convinced not to "spoil" the sacrifices and allowed them to be escorted to the ritual site — a decision that proved his undoing.

After the party destroyed the Altar of Hohonu and killed the Grand Shaman, Ruru led a retaliatory horde in pursuit. As the group fled skyward, Ruru chanted in agony atop his Sacrosu, which began to twist and grow — consuming nearby warriors and Ruru himself. What emerged was a grotesque draconic Avatar of Hohonu, an amalgam of flesh and corruption that pursued the party through the swamp before losing their trail.
