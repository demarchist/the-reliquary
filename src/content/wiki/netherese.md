The Netherese were the people of Netheril, the greatest magical empire the world has ever known. At their peak, the Netherese created floating cities called enclaves, powered by [[mythallar|mythallars]] that drew upon the raw magic of the Weave. Their archwizards developed spells of incredible power, pushing the boundaries of what mortal magic could achieve. The empire fell when Karsus attempted to steal Mystra's power, causing the floating cities to crash and the land to be transformed into the desert of Anauroch.

## Campaign Legacy

The Netherese featured prominently at the [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]] Refounding Festival, which celebrated the city's independence from their oppression alongside the Thayans. Netherese medallions found in the ruins of the incomplete Mythallar chamber bypassed the dampening field, and a two-thousand-year time bubble released trapped Netherese arcanists when the party popped it.

[[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] wore Netherese robes — recognised at the Council of Helm's Hold by Lady Avelyere of Netheril, who was displeased to see an outsider wearing them. The resurgent Shade Enclave, which had survived for centuries in the [[shadowfell|Shadowfell]], had devolved from meritocracy to aristocracy — a disappointment to the wizard.

The Netherese also maintained [[chardalyn|Chardalyn]] mines in the [[underdark|Underdark]], the spell-infusable crystal that [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] used for her altar to [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]].
