The Netherese were the people of Netheril, the greatest magical empire the world has ever known. At their peak, the Netherese created floating cities called enclaves, powered by [[mythallar|mythallars]] that drew upon the raw magic of the Weave. Their archwizards developed spells of incredible power, pushing the boundaries of what mortal magic could achieve. The empire fell when Karsus attempted to steal Mystra's power, causing the floating cities to crash and the land to be transformed into the desert of Anauroch.

## The Sanctum of Krasus

The party explored a buried Netherese complex in the Bloodstone Lands containing an incomplete [[mythallar|Mythallar]] — a latticework of successive polyhedra with a Prismeum core, the device that once powered the floating cities. The Sanctum's dampening field suppressed all spellcasting above 2nd level, but four school medallions marked with the pentagram of Netheril bypassed it. In a chamber above the Mythallar, a two-thousand-year time bubble had frozen an experiment at the moment of detonation — four Netherese arcanists caught dying and repeatedly disintegrating in the same instant for millennia. The group recovered golden scrolls containing High-Magic rituals in ancient Netherese, including instructions for the Weave Mythal that protects [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]].

## Shade Enclave

The Netherese floating city of Shade Enclave survived for centuries within the [[shadowfell|Shadowfell]] after the fall of Netheril. [[lady-avelyere|Lady Avelyere]] of Netheril — who arrived at the Barony in an ornate black carriage flanked by Shadowmonk bodyguards — toured [[feanons-tower|Fëanon's Tower]] and proposed a contract: Netherese artefacts would be returned upon [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s natural death, and the Enclave's Coven would personally acquire any others the party obtained. She carefully steered conversation away from [[mythallar|Mythallars]]. The Enclave also maintains the Way of the Pointed Step, a monastery near Myth Drannor led by Master Jorell — a vampire monk who supposedly never needs to feed — where [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] was invited to train. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] was disappointed to learn the Enclave had devolved from meritocracy to aristocracy during its long exile in the Plane of Shadow.

## Campaign Legacy

The Netherese featured prominently at the [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]] Refounding Festival, which celebrated the city's independence from their oppression alongside the Thayans. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] wore Netherese robes — recognised at the Council of Helm's Hold by [[lady-avelyere|Lady Avelyere]], who was displeased to see an outsider wearing them. The Netherese also maintained [[chardalyn|Chardalyn]] mines in the [[underdark|Underdark]], the spell-infusable crystal that [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] used for her altar to [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]].
