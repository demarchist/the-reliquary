Gustavo is a Bard and an old friend of [[Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews|JmJm]] from his days as the feared mercenary known as The Dark Whistler. He worked at [[MCAS Mythalmar]] before being recruited as JmJm's back-seater in the two-seater [[r-14-thunderchaser|R-14]] prototype during [[Project STARFALL]].

## Arrival

Gustavo made his entrance during a volleyball match at Mythalmar — diving shirtless into the court at the last second to defend what would have been the winning point, setting JmJm up for a commanding spike that clinched the game. The reunion between the two old comrades was immediate and warm, and JmJm insisted on the spot that Gustavo would fly with him.

That night in the Silver Flagon, Gustavo declared that a woman at the bar had "lost that loving feeling." The two bards led the entire canteen in a raucous singalong — the first of what would become a reputation for turning any gathering into a performance.

## The Crash

During a combat exercise against Viper's instructor squadron, JmJm crossed [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]]'s jetwash on the return flight. The two-seater entered an uncontrollable flat spin. Both attempted to eject — Gustavo made it out clean, but JmJm struck the canopy and was severely injured before the aircraft hit the water.

In the sea, Gustavo found his friend and cradled the wounded bard. He channelled what bardic magic he had into stabilising JmJm — just enough healing to keep him alive until rescue craft arrived. The act was more than competence; it was the instinct of a man who had already decided, without being asked, that his friend was not going to die today.
