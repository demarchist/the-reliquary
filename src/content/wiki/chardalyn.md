Chardalyn is a rare magical crystal found in the deep places of the world, particularly in ancient Netherese mines and the frozen north around Icewind Dale. The crystal has the remarkable property of absorbing magical energy — when Thorna threw her hammer at a deposit, the oxidized layer exploded off and lightning was absorbed into the larger crystal in a shimmering display.

## Properties

Pure chardalyn can be fashioned into magical items of great power, but the material requires careful handling. Deposits exposed to the air for millennia oxidize beyond use, and unoxidized veins must be identified and carefully extracted. Corrupted chardalyn — tainted by exposure to fiendish or other evil energies — can twist the minds of those who handle it. A malicious Earth Elemental was found dormant within one deposit, radiating hunger from inside the crystal.

## The Netherese Mine

The party encountered significant chardalyn deposits in an ancient Netherese mine in the [[underdark|Underdark]], where [[duergar|Duergar]] miners had been extracting the material. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] recovered a fifteen-pound shard and additional chips, along with a chardalyn ring from a Duergar lockbox. A letter found on site confirmed the Duergar's intent to mine chardalyn and mentioned another mine far to the north, past the Spine of the World near the Lost City of Snow.

Patches arranged the large shard and smaller chips on her altar in the sanctum beneath the [[fane-of-selune|Fane]], asking [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]] if she liked it. She received no response.

## Strategic Value

[[beryan|Beryan]] the Sage investigated chardalyn's properties as part of her ongoing research for the Spellforged, and Lady Faye's intelligence network identified lost chardalyn mines on the Plain of Standing Stones as a promising lead for [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]]'s artificing ambitions.
