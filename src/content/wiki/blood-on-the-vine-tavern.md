## Description

The Blood on the Vine Tavern is the primary drinking establishment in Barovia village. Despite its grim name, it serves as one of the few gathering places for the village's beleaguered inhabitants.

## Significance

The party meets Burgomaster Irmesk at the tavern upon their arrival in Barovia village, learning about the current state of affairs under Strahd's curse and the death of the previous Burgomaster.
