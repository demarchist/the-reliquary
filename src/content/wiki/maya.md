Maya — also known as Sinya Maya — is a resident of [[feanons-tower|Fëanon's Tower]] in the Barony of Selunia, where she serves as the front desk attendant, having succeeded the previous attendant Giuseppe.

When [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] called an emergency meeting over a mysterious message on the [[al-askarian-tablet|Al'Askarian Tablet]] — *"You have a wonderful home"* — Maya was the one who defused the tension, suggesting it was probably just a friendly overture. A "Tome and Chill?" as she put it.
