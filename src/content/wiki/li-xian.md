Li Xian is a powerful Oni lord and necromancer who serves the Corpsetaker and [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]], the Witch-King. A blue-skinned shapeshifter, he murdered [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]]'s parents in the barrows of [[vaasa|Vaasa]] — a truth revealed through Tark's dreams, where he watched the necromancer's corpse rise, transform into an Oni, and speak with another Oni who had attacked the party at [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]]. Tark woke shaking and sweating, told the group everything, and retreated under a blanket.

## The Infiltration

Li Xian infiltrated the [[thunder-bluff|Bumidere]] estate disguised as Mr. Shon, a sage and dragon expert hired to tutor [[cercei|Cercei]]. Under this cover, he replaced both Cercei and the Amnian wizard Leigham with Simulacra — clay puppets so convincing that no one noticed until [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s wild-magic-gifted truesight revealed the truth. He stole [[val-tengu|Val Tengu]]'s truesight lantern and placed a Geas on the estate's security chief to prevent him from questioning the gaps in his memory.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] and [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] found a long blue hair in Mr. Shon's fold-out cot. The pieces clicked. The children were evacuated to [[sundabar|Sundabar]] via teleportation circle while the false Cercei simulacrum tried to see [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s sigil. When [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] blasted the simulacrum, it exploded into clay — but the modify memory spell it had cast on JmJm demonstrated that the construct wielded power far beyond what a copy of a child should possess.

## Connection to Tark

Li Xian's murders drove the most personal conflict in the campaign. Tark dreamed of it repeatedly — nightmares where Li Xian grabbed him by the throat and the world erupted in fire. His father appeared with a cut throat, pointing behind him. The enmity fuelled Tark's drive to destroy Zhengyi and everything connected to the Witch-King's network of Oni servants.

## The Flint Dagger

Li Xian's [[flint-dagger-of-li-xian|flint dagger]] — the weapon used to murder Tark's parents — was recovered from the Oni's corpse after the group killed him in the Underdark. The blade was identified as an Anathame, a dark weapon of necromantic ritual tethered to [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]]'s power. It pulsed with a faint heartbeat and pulled Tark toward the [[bloodstone-lands|Bloodstone Lands]] for weeks. On a Damaran farmstead outside [[helioglabulus|Helios]], Tark channeled his Ki and [[vaketh|Vaketh]]'s celestial power through his prosthetic arm and obliterated the cursed blade in an explosion of scarlet light.
