The West Cleft is where [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]]'s Derro population is confined: a dense, claustrophobic district of iron bars, narrow passages, and dirty scrap-filled caverns accessible through a large set of guarded checkpoint doors near Ladueger's Furrow. The Duergar guards at the checkpoint described the Derro as agitated and prone to violence — a standing condition, by the sounds of it, that had grown worse recently.

The district has the feeling of an old prison repurposed as permanent housing. Cramped hovels press against one another in the carved rock, the atmosphere thick with the particular tension of a community that mistrusts everyone, including itself. Patches noted, scanning from above, that murder was common here even in normal times. The recent spike in deaths — with teeth removed from the victims — had become conspicuous even by West Cleft standards.

The guards confirmed the pattern of missing teeth when asked, though seemed largely indifferent to what was happening inside the checkpoint as long as the violence stayed contained.

## The Party's Entry

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] entered the West Cleft alone, naked and covered in blood and teeth, screaming to be taken to the master. The Derro guards' initial response was "GET HIM BOYS" until Cirrus destroyed enough of them that the remaining ones began to believe the performance. The intimidation worked: a messenger was sent for [[droki|Droki]], who arrived shortly and arranged the meeting with [[bilrigger|Bilrigger]] that had been the group's objective.

[[tark|Tark]] used the same entry point independently to locate Rilka, a Derro warlock in service to [[milgre|Milgre]], and found her hovel guarded by three vampire Derro who attacked without much preamble. He killed all three and returned to the group bloody and pale, having begun to piece together the connection to [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] that would be confirmed later that day.
