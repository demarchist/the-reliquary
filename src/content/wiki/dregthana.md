Dregthana is an old orcish woman who appeared at the frost giant settlement on Shatterpeak during the wedding feast of Ljokjar. Both Patches and Tark sensed her presence watching from the darkness before Tark shadowstepped out to meet her.

She had come to deliver Rudgrub — an orcish smoking preparation — as a gift for the festivities. After being escorted back to the hall, she prepared pipes for any who wished to partake, quickly drawing several giants around her. Her presence suggests ongoing relations between the frost giant community and the orc-barbarian settlement near the hot springs lake below Shatterpeak.
