Barovia is a cursed demiplane trapped in eternal mist, ruled by the vampire lord [[strahd-von-zarovich|Strahd Von Zarovich]]. The land is perpetually gloomy, its people downtrodden and fearful, and escape is nearly impossible without confronting Strahd himself. When [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] rode directly into the mist to test the boundary, he returned having wandered a featureless void for what felt like a hundred years — though only seconds had passed for everyone else.

## Notable Locations

- **[[castle-ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]]** — Strahd's massive dark gothic castle looming above the valley
- **[[vallaki|Vallaki]]** — A fortified town wracked by internal strife under the paranoid Burgomaster Vargas, whom the party deposed
- **[[argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]]** — A ruined manor housing the remains of the silver dragon Argynvost, where the [[holy-symbol-of-ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]] was unearthed
- **[[amber-temple|The Amber Temple]]** — An ancient temple on Mt. Ghakis imprisoning shards of [[atropos|Atropos]], where Fëanon accepted and trapped a dark entity's offering
- **[[old-bonegrinder|Old Bonegrinder]]** — A decrepit windmill housing a coven of hags
- **[[krezk|Krezk]]** — Site of the Abbey where the Planetar [[anduriel|Anduriel]] sacrificed himself to reforge the [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]]
- **[[tser-pool|Tser Pool]]** — A pool at the base of a waterfall where [[mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]] was recovered

## Key Allies

The party found allies among Barovia's beleaguered inhabitants: the [[keepers-of-the-feather|Keepers of the Feather]] (an order of wereravens), the vampire hunter Van Richten (disguised as the bard Rictavio), his apprentice [[ezmerelda|Ezmerelda]], and the dusk elf mage Kasimir.

## The Confrontation

[[madame-eva|Madame Eva]]'s tarokka reading set the party on a quest for three artifacts — the [[holy-symbol-of-ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]], the [[tome-of-strahd|Tome of Strahd]], and the [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]]. The [[tome-of-strahd|Tome]]'s revelations, combined with Sergei's journal, proved that [[rahadin|Rahadin]] had been manipulating events between the brothers for centuries. In the final confrontation, [[ireena|Ireena]] used these revelations to convince Strahd of his chamberlain's treachery, while [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] wielded the Ashbringer against Rahadin's undead forces. Strahd wished the [[artifact-shard|artifact shard]] away before the party could claim it — an act whose interacting Wish spells caused time distortions throughout the demiplane.
