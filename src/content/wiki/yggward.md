Yggward is an ancient necropolis deep beneath Svartalfheim, accessible via the Endless Stair in Dokkar. It sits where the shadow lies deepest — on the border of Nidavellir and Svartalfheim, where the depths transition to the land of shadow. The great Twilight Cathedral stands at its gates, where petitioners may speak to the slumbering souls within.

The eldest Drow of Dokkar descend the Endless Stair to Yggward when they grow weary of the world. Some sleep for short spans and return. Some never come back. The woman the party observed at the top of the stair was over ten thousand years old, taking her second sleep — watched by a crowd who had come to mark her passing.

The bowcrafter Opheli Lu, who made the legendary bone bow Lok'Delar during the last Crown War using Sandpiper Wyvern bone, slumbers in Yggward. Gundren sought her knowledge to guide the forging of the Bloodrinker crossbow.
