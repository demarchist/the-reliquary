The Evermoors is a vast marsh that stretches to the south of Thunder Bluff. This expansive wetland borders the Barony of Thunder Bluff and represents one of the natural boundaries of the region.

The Evermoors occupy the lowest tier of the Thunder Bluff region, lying below the River Rauvin canyon and receiving its combined outflow. Slow-moving black waters thread through dense, tangled vegetation; the ground rarely rises more than a few feet above the waterline, and mist clings to the surface year-round. The wetland appears to stretch indefinitely southward, with no clear boundary between swamp, marsh, and open water. Its brooding, primordial atmosphere makes it a natural barrier — few roads cross it, and those who enter without a guide rarely find their way back out.
