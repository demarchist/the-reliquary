Stason Jatham introduced himself as the new janitor of [[feanons-tower|Feanon's Tower]], arriving unannounced at the wizard's laboratory door. [[feanon-serinde|Feanon]] recognised him almost immediately for what he was — the Last Mindslayer — placed there by [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] to keep an eye on the wizard and handle any necessary cleaning, both mundane and otherwise. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] and Stason passed one another without acknowledgement, their arrangement understood without words.
