## Background

Gruttag is Snorbul's uncle and the head of their clan within Blingdenstone. He suffered the tragic loss of his son Beltran during a period of upheaval in the Underdark.

## Role in the Campaign

Gruttag provides Snorbul with a personal connection to Blingdenstone and offers the party support through familial ties. His grief over Beltran's death underscores the human cost of the conflicts raging through the Underdark.

## Notable Traits

- Clan head within Blingdenstone's Deep Gnome community
- Uncle to party member Snorbul
- Mourns the loss of his son Beltran
