The Svirfneblin, or deep gnomes, are a reclusive race who dwell in the [[underdark|Underdark]]. Unlike their surface cousins, the Svirfneblin are somber and cautious, shaped by the constant dangers of subterranean life. They are skilled miners, gem-cutters, and stone-workers, and their settlements are often hidden from the more aggressive races of the deep. Despite their wariness, the Svirfneblin are fundamentally good-natured and can become loyal allies to those who earn their trust.

## Blingdenstone

The party forged a strong alliance with the Svirfneblin of [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]], where [[snorbul|Snorbul]]'s reputation from the fighting pits of [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] preceded him — watching him fight was everything they had hoped for. The city's residents directed stern, businesslike looks at outsiders by default, most cracking into brief smiles before returning to neutral — except when they landed on [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]], where they went dark before flattening to wary neutrality.

The party cleared the Rockblight, destroyed the Stone Priestess's [[lolth|Lolth]] curse, and earned the gratitude of Chief Diggermattock and High Priest [[gurnik|Gurnik]]. After the cleansing, Snorbul enforced dignity for the [[boulda|wererat]] newcomers, declaring that anyone who showed them disrespect would answer to the new Wardens. Snorbul's uncle [[gruttag|Gruttag]] and his cousin Yuri lived in the city, giving the gnome barbarian a family connection that made the Blingdenstone arc deeply personal.

## Alliance with the Barony

The Svirfneblin expressed gratitude through promises of trade negotiations with [[selunia|Selunia]] and a formal alliance with the Barony of [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]]. The Svirfneblin twins who travelled with [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] were among the earliest deep gnome allies the party gained.
