The Svirfneblin, or deep gnomes, are a reclusive race who dwell in the [[underdark|Underdark]]. Unlike their surface cousins, the Svirfneblin are somber and cautious, shaped by the constant dangers of subterranean life. They are skilled miners, gem-cutters, and stone-workers, and their settlements are often hidden from the more aggressive races of the deep. Despite their wariness, the Svirfneblin are fundamentally good-natured and can become loyal allies to those who earn their trust.

## Blingdenstone

The party forged a strong alliance with the Svirfneblin of [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]], where [[snorbul|Snorbul]]'s reputation from the fighting pits of [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] preceded him — watching him fight was everything they had hoped for. The city's residents directed stern, businesslike looks at outsiders by default, most cracking into brief smiles before returning to neutral — except when they landed on [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]], where they went dark before flattening to wary neutrality. [[snorbul|Snorbul]]'s uncle [[gruttag|Gruttag]] and cousin Yuri lived in the city, giving the gnome barbarian a family connection that made the Blingdenstone arc deeply personal.

## The Rockblight

The Rockblight — a cursed quarter of [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]] under [[lolth|Lolth]]'s influence — was cleansed in a daring operation. High Priest [[gurnik|Gurnik]] provided eight Earth Elementals to carry the party through solid stone, bypassing the Rockblight's defences entirely. They found the Stone Priestess — a Medusa [[drow-faction|Drow]] wreathed in red lightning — seated on her throne, attended by gargoyles and guarded by a massive basilisk curled around boulder-sized eggs. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] decapitated the petrified priestess on [[eilistraee|Eilistraee]]'s command, lifting the curse. The gargoyles crumbled, the ghosts of Blingdenstone began to fade with words of kindness, and Chief Diggermattock immediately recalled troops for a push into the cleared northern caverns. After the cleansing, Snorbul enforced dignity for the [[boulda|wererat]] newcomers, declaring that anyone who showed them disrespect would answer to the new Wardens.

## Alliance with the Barony

The Svirfneblin formalised their gratitude through a mutual protection pact with the Barony of [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]] — yielding approximately three thousand gold in cut gemstones and five hundred gold worth of salt per month, cheaper than overland shipping. The Svirfneblin twins who travelled with [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] were among the earliest deep gnome allies the party gained, and trade between [[selunia|Selunia]] and Blingdenstone became a foundation of the Barony's economy.
