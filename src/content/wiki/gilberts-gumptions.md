Gilbert's Gumptions is an alchemy shop located in the Ward of Artifice in Sundabar, owned and operated by the elderly and remarkably short dwarven alchemist Gilbert Bubblegump.

The party visited the shop while preparing for their expedition to the Sanctum of Krasus, purchasing an assortment of potions and haggling the price down. They struck a deal with Gilbert for a permanent twenty-percent discount in exchange for granting him first right of refusal on any rare potion recipes and reagents they discovered in their travels. Gilbert also recommended his brother Albert's armour shop when Cirrus revealed he had been adventuring without any armour at all.
