The Sallow Manatee is a two-storey dockside inn and bar on the Neverwinter wharves. It employs an Orog bouncer and a redheaded woman named Jenny as security, and its clientele skews toward sailors and dockworkers.

Beneath its unremarkable exterior, the Sallow Manatee served as a node in Captain Vaas Redblade's slaving operation. Petey Greene, the proprietor, laundered profits from the slave trade — melting down gold and pirate coin into silver ingots with the help of a goldsmith in the Tower District. A hidden cellar beneath the main cellar housed the laundering operation.

The inn saw considerable violence during the party's time in Neverwinter. Gaffer Gary was shot dead on its doorstep by Gundren, showering the interior — and Lo'Dan's drink — with gore. Later that night, the group raided the establishment, triggering a booby-trapped front door and arresting Petey Greene.
