## Background

Drizzly Dawg is a charming elf pirate captain who commands The Revenger (formerly the Royal Venture). He is accompanied by his green-haired Water Genasi second mate, Naima.

## Role in the Campaign

Drizzly Dawg provides the party with naval transport and assistance during their adventures across the seas of Al Askar. His ship and crew prove invaluable for reaching remote islands and navigating dangerous waters.

## Notable Traits

- Charismatic and dashing pirate captain
- Commands The Revenger with his second mate Naima
- Reliable ally despite his roguish nature
