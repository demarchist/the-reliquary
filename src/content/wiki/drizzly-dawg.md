## Background

Drizzly Dawg is a charming elf pirate captain who commands The Revenger (formerly the Royal Venture). He is accompanied by his green-haired Water Genasi second mate, Naima.

## Role in the Campaign

The party encountered Drizzly at Pirate's Cove after abandoning Lady Taurus's ship-recovery job. Rather than fight over the stolen Royal Venture, they chartered Drizzly to sail them to Iyum. He lost a drinking contest to Tark — who is immune to alcohol — and had his bed stolen by Cirrus before Feanon fabricated him a magnificent replacement, leaving the captain stunned by magic he called legendary.

When Thirsty Fingers attempted a mutiny, Drizzly won the crew vote handily by reminding them he had lifted most of them from serfdom. In gracious victory, he offered Thirsty a ship of his own — the one moored at the Shaa'barid island — proposing the start of a fleet.

## Notable Traits

- Charismatic and dashing elf pirate captain
- Commands The Revenger with his second mate Naima
- Won his crew's loyalty by lifting many from serfdom
- Carries a portable hole and trinkets that grant magical resistance
- Genuinely curious about the party's extraordinary abilities
