The Watchers of Helm are the paladin order stationed at Helm's Hold, the fortress-cathedral on the coast south of Neverwinter. Led by Javen Tarkimos, the order maintains the Hold as both a religious site and a military stronghold, guarding its teleport chamber with armoured paladins, clerics, and three massive golden golems.

The order suffered losses during the Trials of the Crusader when the summoning went catastrophically wrong — the Narzugon Prime shattered the containment wards and called forth a horde of devils that breached the arena. Two paladins fell in the fighting: Prior Athosa, who was revived by Buumi, and Paladin Belefonte, for whom JmJm offered to perform a reincarnation.
