The Underdark is a vast, interconnected network of caverns, tunnels, and subterranean seas stretching beneath the surface of [[faerun|Faerûn]]. It is home to numerous civilisations and creatures, from the [[drow-faction|Drow]] cities of [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]] to the [[svirfneblin-faction|Svirfneblin]] settlement of [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]], the [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] stronghold of [[gracksuluth|Gracklstugh]], and countless dangers lurking in the lightless depths. The Underdark is divided into three layers — the Upperdark, the Middledark, and the Lowerdark — each more dangerous than the last.

## The Party's Journey

The Spellforged were captured and imprisoned in [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]], a Drow outpost above a vast chasm, by the priestess Ilvara Mizzrym. They escaped alongside fellow prisoners including Shuushar, Ront, and [[pickles|Mr. Pickles]], navigating through the [[darklake|Darklake]], Sloobludop, Gracklstugh, and Blingdenstone while being pursued by Ilvara and contending with the demonic incursions tearing the subterranean realm apart.

The journey forced the party to adapt to an entirely different world — one where the [[faerzress|Faerzress]] blocked teleportation and divination, where [[chardalyn|Chardalyn]] deposits held dormant elementals, and where predators like Carrion Crawlers became mounts rather than threats. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] tamed [[peade|Peade]] in the deep tunnels; [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]], unable to bear the darkness, teleported himself to [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]] for pancakes and a hotel room while the rest camped in a grotto.

## The Demonic Incursion

During the party's time below, Demon Lords had been summoned into the Underdark — [[demogorgon|Demogorgon]], [[juiblex|Juiblex]], and others — shattering the already-fragile peace between the subterranean factions. The party witnessed Demogorgon rise from the Darklake at Sloobludop, fought through Juiblex's ooze-infested corruption at Blingdenstone, and ultimately confronted the Prince of Demons himself at the bridges of Ladueger's Furrow in Gracksuluth.
