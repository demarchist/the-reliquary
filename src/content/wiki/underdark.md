The Underdark is a vast, interconnected network of caverns, tunnels, and subterranean seas stretching beneath the surface of [[faerun|Faerûn]]. It is home to numerous civilisations and creatures, from the [[drow-faction|Drow]] cities of [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]] to the [[svirfneblin-faction|Svirfneblin]] settlement of [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]], the [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] stronghold of [[gracksuluth|Gracklstugh]], and countless dangers lurking in the lightless depths. The Underdark is divided into three layers — the Upperdark, the Middledark, and the Lowerdark — each more dangerous than the last.

## The Faerzress

The [[faerzress|Faerzress]] — deposits of crystallised wild magic embedded in cavern walls throughout the Underdark — makes the realm uniquely treacherous for spellcasters. It blocks teleportation and divination, and direct contact with its energies can trigger catastrophic surges: when [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] attempted to draw on Faerzress power, the surrounding rock heated to incandescence and unleashed a lava flow. Even with a stone anchor, his return teleport was thrown wildly off-target by the interference.

## The Party's Journey

The Spellforged were captured and imprisoned in [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]], a [[drow-faction|Drow]] outpost above a vast chasm, by the priestess Ilvara Mizzrym. They escaped alongside fellow prisoners including Shuushar, Ront, and [[pickles|Mr. Pickles]], navigating through the [[darklake|Darklake]], Sloobludop, [[gracksuluth|Gracklstugh]], and [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]] while being pursued by Ilvara and contending with the demonic incursions tearing the subterranean realm apart.

The journey forced the party to adapt to an entirely different world — one where [[chardalyn|Chardalyn]] deposits held dormant elementals, where predators like Carrion Crawlers became mounts rather than threats, and where passages leaked [[shadowfell|Shadowfell]] energy through weakened planar barriers, spawning deformed shadow creatures that drained life force through contact. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] tamed [[peade|Peade]] in the deep tunnels; [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]], unable to bear the darkness, teleported himself to [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]] for pancakes and a hotel room while the rest camped in a grotto.

Key waypoints included the Crossroads — a massive nexus cavern two hundred feet across, well-travelled by Orcs, Giant Spiders, Drow, and [[duergar-faction|Duergar]] — and Sloobludop, a ramshackle Kuo-Toa settlement of lashed wood and bone where a civil war between the Sea Mother priesthood and the Deep Father cult was escalating to human sacrifice. Whatever lurked in the [[darklake|Darklake]] below Sloobludop was actively hunting the sacrificial offerings.

## The Demonic Incursion

During the party's time below, Demon Lords had been summoned into the Underdark by the archwizard Gromph Baenre of [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]] — [[demogorgon|Demogorgon]], [[juiblex|Juiblex]], and others — shattering the already-fragile peace between the subterranean factions. The party witnessed Demogorgon rise from the Darklake at Sloobludop, fought through Juiblex's ooze-infested corruption at [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]], and ultimately confronted the Prince of Demons himself at the bridges of Ladueger's Furrow in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]].
