Project STARFALL is the Alliance's top-secret response to the Axis powers' aerial warfare program. A joint venture between the [[silverymoon|University of Silverymoon]] and [[rockseeker-manufactory|Rockseeker Corporation]], the project developed the [[r-14-thunderchaser|R-14 Thunderchaser]] — a revolutionary magical aircraft built from reverse-engineered Spelljammer technology — to counter the [[amn|Amnian]] [[vk-31-frostbite|VK-31 "Frostbite"]] threat that had devastated Alliance military infrastructure across the continent.

## Origins

The project began when University of Silverymoon researchers discovered wreckage of a Spelljammer vessel in the [[moonwood|Moonwood]]. Analysis of the wreckage's helm crystal technology revealed principles that, combined with [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren Rockseeker]]'s innovations in crystalline matrix manipulation and the University's advances in thaumaturgical field dynamics, could produce an entirely new form of aerial propulsion. The partnership between the University and Rockseeker Corporation was formed with a single objective: achieve aerial superiority by any means necessary.

[[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] and [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] had been secretly involved in the project for eighteen months before revealing it to the rest of the [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]]. Their abrupt departure from [[selgaunt|Selgaunt]] — teleporting away without explanation after the devastating night raid — led the remaining party members to [[mcas-mythalmar|MCAS Mythalmar]], where the full scope of the operation was unveiled.

## The Briefing

In a dimly lit briefing room at Mythalmar, Fëanon delivered the initial intelligence summary while magical holocrystals projected images above the conference table. He outlined the VK-31 threat — at least seven confirmed strikes on Alliance infrastructure, each executed with devastating precision — before Gundren took over to detail the R-14's development. The briefing culminated with Fëanon pressing a button on the lectern: massive blast shutters slid open to reveal a sprawling underground hangar filled with gleaming R-14 Thunderchasers, their crystalline matrices pulsing faintly as crews prepared them for flight.

## Cover Story

The official cover story for MCAS Mythalmar and the STARFALL program is an "advanced griffon breeding program." All personnel are magically screened, and information is compartmentalized on a need-to-know basis. The facility's cover is maintained by a seemingly abandoned watchtower in the mountains near Silverymoon, staffed by silent figures in gray robes.

## Personnel

The project recruited the members of the [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]] as its pilot cadre, chosen for their unique combination of magical aptitude and martial skill. Fëanon serves as tactics instructor — grounded, watching from the control tower — while Gundren acts as chief engineer and lead instructor under the call sign "Viper." [[gustavo|Gustavo]], an old associate of [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s from his Dark Whistler days, serves as ground crew and later as JmJm's back-seater in the two-seater prototype.
