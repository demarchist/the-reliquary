Dalfred Noakes is a Halfling innkeeper who operates Narbondellyn's Shadow, a surprisingly homey establishment in the Eastmyr district of [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]]. With salt-and-pepper hair, a beaming smile, and a significant scar cleaving one ear in half, Dalfred caters to non-[[drow-faction|Drow]] visitors — though his inn also serves as a [[bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'Aerthe]] safe house. He provided the Heralds with identity papers, shielded rooms for sensitive discussions, and arranged their midnight escort to the old Do'Urden compound to meet [[jarlaxle|Jarlaxle]].
