The Tome of Strahd is the personal journal of Count [[strahd-von-zarovich|Strahd Von Zarovich]], detailing the vampire lord's history, his descent into darkness, and the events that led to the creation of the cursed domain of [[barovia|Barovia]]. A Vistani family at [[madame-eva|Madame Eva]]'s camp had safeguarded the book for generations, and the party recovered it from their cart.

The tome is one of the key artifacts that Madame Eva's tarokka reading identified as essential to the party's quest to confront Strahd, alongside the [[holy-symbol-of-ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]]. Its pages contain entries written in Strahd's own hand, chronicling his love for Tatyana, his jealousy of his brother Sergei, and the pact with dark powers that transformed him into a vampire.

## Deciphering

The tome's archaic script proved difficult to read. [[mordenkainen|Mordenkainen]] took the journal and spent days breaking its cipher, eventually relaying its contents to the party. The entries revealed that Strahd had been an initially just king — a conqueror who loved Tatyana and trusted his brother — before something changed him. Crucially, several pages were missing between Strahd's trip to the [[amber-temple|Amber Temple]] and a later diplomatic journey, suggesting deliberate tampering.

## The Revelation

The party's careful study of the Tome, combined with Sergei's own journal, revealed the crucial truth that [[rahadin|Rahadin]] had been manipulating events between the brothers for centuries. These revelations proved instrumental in the final confrontation with Strahd, as [[ireena|Ireena]] used the conflicting journal entries to convince Strahd of his chamberlain's centuries of treachery and deception.
