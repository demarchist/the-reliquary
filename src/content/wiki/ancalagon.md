Ancalagon the Void is an ancient Black Dragon who seeks Mordenkainen for unknown purposes. He attacks the party in the Evermoors before fleeing south, and later reappears allied with the devils of the Doomstar Legion. He subsequently falls under Zhengyi's dominion, enthralled by Hollow Armor — a black metal exoskeleton embedded with controlling gemstones, the same Vecna-cursed artifice worn by Ares and Vulkan. He is spotted descending from the west toward the Eye of the Storm during the Dragonflight's confrontation with the Witch-King over Vaasa, operating under the command of the Red Greatwyrm Vulkan. His immense power and mysterious agenda make him one of the most dangerous individual threats the party encounters.
