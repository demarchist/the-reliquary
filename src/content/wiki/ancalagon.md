## Description

Ancalagon the Void is an ancient Black Dragon of immense power. He lairs in the swamps of the Evermoors, resting in the water like a great crocodile, and commands a goblinoid army of several hundred — including hill giants, wargs, giant bats, and armoured ogres — from a ruined watchtower on the Withered Hill.

## History

Ancalagon first appeared attacking patrols in the Evermoors before fleeing south. He later resurfaced as the local commander for the Doomstar Legion's operation around the Zhengyi Ziggurat in the Moonwood. Working with the Narzugon Beucephelus, Ancalagon orchestrated a diversionary goblin raid on the Barony to keep the defenders focused south while the Ziggurat completed its purpose. He initially sought Mordenkainen for unknown reasons.

## The Siege of Thunder Bluff

After the Ziggurat was destroyed, Ancalagon led a full-scale assault on the Keep of Thunder Bluff, wearing Hollow Armour — the same Vecna-designed framework of metal and dark gemstones that had enslaved Jareem's mate in Al Askar. The armour absorbed the Delzoun fire cannon barrages that would otherwise have shredded the dragon. Despite his power, the defence held — the orcs destroyed his hill giant allies at the river, and the combined firepower of the Keep forced him to retreat.

In retaliation, Ancalagon snatched civilians from frontier roads and dropped them onto the town at high altitude. The group raided his lair beneath the Withered Hill while he was away, finding two adult black dragon mates, wyrmlings, and a massive hoard, and escaped with some thirty thousand gold worth of treasure.

Interrogation of captured goblins confirmed Ancalagon is a vassal of Zhengyi's power structure who was retasked to the Barony by the Amnians. He answers to a dragon above himself — the source of his Hollow Armour.
