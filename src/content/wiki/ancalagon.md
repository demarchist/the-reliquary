## Description

Ancalagon the Void is an ancient Black Dragon of immense power. He lairs in the swamps of the Evermoors, resting in the water like a great crocodile, and commands a goblinoid army of several hundred — including hill giants, wargs, giant bats, and armoured ogres — from a ruined watchtower on the Withered Hill.

## History

Ancalagon first appeared attacking patrols in the Evermoors before fleeing south. He later resurfaced as the local commander for the Doomstar Legion's operation around the Zhengyi Ziggurat in the Moonwood. Working with the Narzugon Beucephelus, Ancalagon orchestrated a diversionary goblin raid on the Barony to keep the defenders focused south while the Ziggurat completed its purpose. He initially sought Mordenkainen for unknown reasons.
