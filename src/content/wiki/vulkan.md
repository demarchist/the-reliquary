Vulkan is a Red Greatwyrm — older and crueller than any Red dragon the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]] has scented — working in open concert with the Archlich [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]]. His name first surfaced through Speak with Dead at the entrance to [[themberchaud|Themberchaud]]'s ruined volcanic lair, where an Oni guard confessed under questioning that *Vulkan* had taken the Ancient Red to *the Castle* on Zhengyi's orders.

## At the Floating Castle

[[azoo-the-archer|Azoo of Shou Lung]] later confirmed Vulkan's role and his stature. She and several of her flight had broken off from the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree of Bahamut]] to search for a missing Red Dragon emissary, and had found Vulkan instead — stationed on the back of a great [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] hovering above an army of Oni who were performing a slow, deliberate dark ritual over the ruined husk of [[palischuk|Palischuk]]. Zhengyi was with him. The two of them oversaw the rite from above.

When the Dragonflight peeled off from the ritual and tried to escape, Vulkan and a pack of hunting Oni gave chase, accompanied by a great floating [[golden-orb|Golden Orb]] whose gaze unmade dragons in mid-air.

## The Fall of Protanther

[[lady-isabel|Izmakella]] — called to the Dragonflight's defence by [[bahamut|Bahamut]] in the same hours — reached the [[great-tree-of-bahamut|Great Tree]] in time to fight beside [[protanther|Protanther]], the Ancient Gold Wyrm who tended the Tree. She watched Protanther fall before Vulkan.

That a Red Greatwyrm in Zhengyi's service has felled the abdicated king of the Gold Dragons — and that he has done so while a Lich, a Golden Orb, and an army of Oni perform a ritual over the bones of a half-orc city — sets the scale of the Witch-King's northern operation at a level the party has not yet had the leisure to absorb. Vulkan is not a single threat in the field. He is one of the pieces with which Zhengyi has begun to clear the board.
