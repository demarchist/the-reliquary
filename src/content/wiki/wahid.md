The Wahid are enormous reptilian humanoid giants — easily twenty feet tall — who inhabit the deepest regions of the Disphotic Forest near the Font of Fawden. They wear primitive leather clothing and carry great bones as tools, using them to smash apart rock formations to feed on the small blue crustaceans that nest within.

Despite their primitive appearance, the Wahid possess a startling supernatural ability: they can phase through solid rock as if it were no more than air. During the party's rest near the Font, Tark observed a Wahid step through a nearby wall, stand motionless for hours watching the area, then phase back into the stone — only to emerge from the floor nearby and resume its surveillance before disappearing again.

The Wahid demonstrated keen senses, detecting the party's concealed presence despite their magical protections. The group chose stealth over confrontation, using Pass without Trace to slip past the giants on their approach to the Font of Fawden.
