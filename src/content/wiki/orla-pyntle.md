Orla Pyntle is a gnome wizard and one of Theodemir's five acolytes who participated in the conspiracy to entrust the Eye of Aren to the Illithid Elder Brain Nostalgia for Infinity. Barely three feet tall with wild copper hair escaping from a dozen pins, she was known for constant fidgeting that belied razor-sharp intelligence and a gift for rapid calculation.

During the acolytes' debate over the Eye's fate, Orla argued against the Vault of Sages — pointing out that too many people knew of its existence and that the Amnians had already begun establishing a presence in the city. She was experienced through the memory simulation by Cyrrus, who inhabited her perspective during Lei Tan's Grell-based reconstruction.
