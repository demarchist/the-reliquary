Chadle is the daughter of the merchant barge captain [[hemeth|Hemeth]], who lives and works aboard his vessel as it navigates the underground waterways of the [[darklake|Darklake]]. When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] found her father caged at the altar of the Deep Father in Sloobludop, Hemeth's first thought after securing his own freedom was to ask the Drow to look after his daughter.

Chadle is a capable crew member in her own right, having grown up on the [[darklake|Darklake]] and knowing its routes and dangers as well as any adult mariner. She is more trusting of surface-dwellers than most Duergar, shaped by years of her father's commerce with various Underdark races.

When [[hemeth|Hemeth]] was later captured by Stoneguard forces in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]], Chadle was left to maintain the barge and await word of her father's fate. She waited.
