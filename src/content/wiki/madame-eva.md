## Background

Madame Eva is an ancient Vistani seer who resides at a camp near Tser Pool in Barovia. She possesses deep knowledge of the curse that binds the land and its inhabitants.

## Role in the Campaign

Madame Eva performs a pivotal tarokka card reading for the party, revealing that their only means of escaping Barovia is to either destroy Count Strahd von Zarovich or somehow lead him to forgive himself. Her prophecy guides much of the party's actions within the cursed domain.

## Notable Traits

- Possesses genuine prophetic abilities through tarokka card readings
- One of the oldest and most knowledgeable inhabitants of Barovia
- Maintains neutrality while subtly guiding those who might end Strahd's curse
