Captain Vaas Redblade commanded The Silent Virtue, a three-masted galleon moored in Neverwinter harbour. Presenting herself as a short, tanned, blond elven woman in a fancy leather overcoat, Vaas was in truth a hobgoblin — a fact she revealed mid-combat when she dropped the disguise to rally her crew.

Vaas operated a slaving ring out of Neverwinter, trafficking people snatched from the slums and ferrying them out to her ship in silence. She maintained contacts on shore through Petey Greene at the Sallow Manatee, who laundered the profits through a goldsmith named Blek Blekson. Her crew carried Mintarn coinage — pirate treasure from the Pirate Isles.

When confronted at the Keep of Thunder Bluff by the party, Vaas demanded fourteen hundred gold for the death of her crewman Gaffer Gary. The demand fell apart under counter-accusations of piracy and slavery. The subsequent assault on The Silent Virtue ended with her defeat. Cornered, she traded her freedom and that of her champion for information — revealing Petey Greene as her contact and the existence of a strange metal box delivered to the Sallow Manatee.
