Kalphena the Wise is an Ancient Silver Dragon from Shou Lung and a member of the Dragonflight. Alongside Asha, she was immediately seized by nesting instincts upon seeing the walled Rock Gnome city — both began selecting towers to mend into lairs and engaging locals in enthusiastic conversation about architecture, contributing to the collective panic that led the archmage to deploy his Ring of Wishes.
