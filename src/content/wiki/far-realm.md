The Far Realm is a dimension beyond the known planes of existence, lying past the Outer Gates of Reality. Beryan the Sage identified it by process of elimination — its utter foreignness matched no known plane in her extensive knowledge of Planar Cosmology.

Cirrus stumbled into the Far Realm through the Well of Many Worlds. He found an endless expanse of white fog and flat crystal ground where water rained upward and great hunched figures stood motionless in the distance. Their reflections in the crystal ground moved independently, forming a ring around him — masses of writhing rubbery tendrils extending a pulsating appendage toward him. He Planeshifted away immediately.

According to legend, the Elven Archmage Abraxim traversed the Outer Gates and returned infected by Halashnar, the Pallid Prince — an Elder Evil from beyond reality.
