Chipgrin is the leader of the wererat community in the northwestern section of Blingdenstone. A very fat Deep Gnome wererat with a great moustache waxed into magnificent whiskers, he presides over roughly seventy cursed Svirfneblin who have maintained a separate existence from the main settlement for generations.

Initially reluctant to ally with Chief Diggermattock — his people were exhausted from constant ooze attacks, running out of food, and their gnomish connection to the earth had weakened to the point where divine and druidic magic was lost to them — Chipgrin was persuaded by Snorbul's personal oath and the group's mediation. He proved a practical leader, noting that his people could sniff out the shapeshifting ooze-impostors infiltrating Blingdenstone. His wererats fought alongside the Deep Gnome army in the Battle of Blingdenstone.
