Shou Lung is a vast and ancient empire in the far east, hidden from the wider world for thousands of years. It is the homeland of the Dragonflight — a group of nine ancient dragons who have lived within its borders for millennia. A divine compulsion has finally called them west, making the Dragonflight the first of their people to leave Shou Lung in living memory.

The empire is described by its draconic inhabitants as culturally sophisticated — a benchmark against which the western lands they encounter on their journey compare poorly, at least by their own estimation.
