Shou Lung is a vast and ancient empire in the far east, hidden from the wider world for thousands of years. It is the homeland of the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]] — a group of ancient dragons who have lived within its borders for millennia. A divine compulsion has finally called them west, making the Dragonflight the first of their people to leave Shou Lung in living memory.

To its draconic inhabitants, Shou Lung is the *Dragon Empire of the East* — a term [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo]] used when explaining to [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] that she and her brethren had been *freed* from the Empire to fly west and assist against the Darkness of [[atropos|Atropos]]. The word *freed* implies a compact between the Empire's institutions and the dragons that hold it: their long residency was a service, and their crossing west is a release granted by their god [[bahamut|Bahamut]] under conditions the wider world does not yet understand.

The empire is described by its draconic inhabitants as culturally sophisticated — a benchmark against which the western lands they encounter on their journey compare poorly, at least by their own estimation.
