The Golden Orb is a great floating eye the colour of fresh metal that drifted on the air alongside [[vulkan|Vulkan]] and his hunting Oni during the pursuit of the [[dragons-of-shou-lung|Dragonflight]] from the [[floating-castle|Floating Castle]] above [[palischuk|Palischuk]]. [[azoo-the-archer|Azoo]] described it sparingly — there had not been time to study it. Her flight had broken in mid-air when the Orb's gaze passed across one of their number. Her kin had not recovered.

## Speculation

The party has previously encountered the [[elder-orb|Elder Orb of Und]] — a Netherese-fed Beholder of equivalent stature, exiled south by [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]] from a frozen ruin in the north. That an aberration of similar power, this time *gold* in colour and serving the Lich directly, is now drifting in formation with Vulkan over the ruin of [[palischuk|Palischuk]] is too pointed to be coincidence. The Elder Orb was driven from the north. The Golden Orb operates *from* the north. Whether the Witch-King is collecting aberrations of this rank as instruments — or whether the Golden Orb is something other than a Beholder altogether — has not yet been established.
