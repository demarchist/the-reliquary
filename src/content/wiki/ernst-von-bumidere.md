Ernst Von Bumidere is one of Horatio's brothers. During the attack on the Bumidere estate, Ernst survived the initial fireball blasts and downed a potion that created force armour around his ruined noble clothes. He fought alongside the group and the Amnian allies Chani and Gneiss, helping to hold the ballroom against the assassins.

Ernst was killed once during the battle — his throat cut by a fleeing assassin when he outpaced his allies in pursuit — but was immediately revived by Buumi's revivify, the paladin praying to Helm not to lose another family member that night.
