Demogorgon is the self-styled Prince of Demons, one of the most powerful demon lords in the Abyss. His most distinctive feature is his twin baboon heads, Aameul and Hethradiah, each possessing its own cunning personality and constantly scheming against the other even as they share a single body. Demogorgon commands legions of demons and his mere presence drives mortals to madness. His influence extends through cults and corrupted agents throughout the Underdark and beyond.

The party encountered Demogorgon's influence during their time in the Underdark, where his agents sowed chaos and corruption. The orc [[ront|Ront]] fell under the sway of Demogorgon's servants, and the demon lord's presence was felt in the destruction wrought upon Sloobludop and the surrounding Darklake region.

## The Bridge of Ladueger's Furrow

Demogorgon's servants in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] had been preparing a blood ritual for weeks, working through a Derro cult and the increasingly mutated [[ront|Ront]] as their instrument. When the group ambushed the prisoner convoy crossing Ladueger's Furrow, they triggered the ritual's final stage: [[ront|Ront]], surrounded on the bridge, wrapped his own mutated tentacle-arm around himself and tore himself apart. The blood that had been rising toward him in droplets throughout the battle swelled into a vortex, and from the remains, Demogorgon unfolded upward — two heads rising on parallel spines, thirty feet of muscle and scale and matted fur, teeth the size of swords.

He assembled on the bridge and oriented immediately to [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]]. One of the heads spoke in Abyssal — it knew him. It was looking for him. The monk normally carried *it*, referring to [[the-last-word|The Last Word]]. The demon lord's presence in the [[underdark|Underdark]] was deliberate; he had come for the artifact, and now it was within reach.

The group fought Demogorgon across the width of [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] as the city shuddered beneath each step. His tentacle-arms carried a necrotic phage that reduced the vigor of anyone he struck and resisted. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] created a Minor Illusion of the artifact and used himself as bait on the open bridge span. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] later claimed more convincingly to hold the real artifact, planting a visible [[bag-of-holding|bag of holding]] in Demogorgon's path and pulling the demon lord away from the group. [[snorbul|Snorbul]] spent most of the fight ascending the demon's body axe-stroke by axe-stroke.

The Narzugon devil [[anduriel|Anduriel]], whose bridge it was, made a charging pass with her Fiery Lance and struck hard — and paid for it with a devastating counterblow that left her galloping away gravely wounded.

[[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] brought Foehammer across one of Demogorgon's snouts, scattering teeth across the bridge stone. The demon staggered. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]], watching from a balcony overhead, read the moment, leapt onto the demon's back, and ran up the spine toward the place where the two heads met. She found it. She drove [[eclipse|Eclipse]] in.
