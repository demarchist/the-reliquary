Dogan of Clan Battlehammer is a Smith and Artificer who arrived at the Barony flanked by bodyguards to deliver royal invitations for a competition at Gauntlgrym. He presented Gundren Rockseeker with an ornate granite box containing a brick of jade carved with the runes of Clan Battlehammer, Gauntlgrym, and the Delzoun Empire.

The competition would see ten invitees present an invention or work of artifice to aid the northern nations in their war against Amn and Thay. The winner would receive unfettered access to Gauntlgrym's forges and the Dawn Titan Maegera. Dogan eyed the Thundercannon and Gwen with visible appreciation before heading downstairs to enjoy the goblin fights.
