Coho is a barbarian who leads a band of mercenaries operating out of the dockside taverns of Iyum. Tark and JmJm recruited him and his company after spotting them in a rough establishment near the harbour. Though initially reluctant — the mercenaries assumed the job would involve entering the Pyramid of Rudjek, which they flatly refused — Tark's persuasion eventually won them over.

Under the arrangement, Coho would report to the Golden Tower for supplies and equipment, while his lieutenant Gon would take a squad to establish a beachhead in the dungeon of Rudjek's pyramid.
