The Thayans Attack was a coordinated night assault on the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]], launched by the [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizards of Thay]] hours after the party had driven off the dusk attack of the [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen White Dragons]]. It came as the second blow of a single long night — and it caught the household with its mansion full of resting guests, its [[crimson-dawn-knights|paladins]] posted, and two of the party still abroad on [[bytopia|Bytopia]].

## The Opening

A rapid series of booms rolled across the moonlit valley like distant thunder out of a clear sky. The [[crimson-dawn-knights|paladins]] came on guard; [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] and [[cassian|Cassian]] crossed to the windows. In the same moment [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] stepped back through a doorway of moonlight from Bytopia and felt the tension in the grounds — the warning the [[callarduran-stonehands|Deva]] had given them on the plane, come true.

Then a single chanting voice resonated up out of the far treeline, and the ground cracked and heaved.

## The Earthquake

A tremendous earthquake tore through the estate. The fine two-storey manor came apart in seconds — walls folding, floors pitching, the slate roof sliding off its rafters — and everyone within threw themselves toward the nearest opening as the building caved. [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] was buried beneath a collapsing wall and cried out; other [[crimson-dawn-knights|paladins]] were pinned in the rubble, while [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] moved among his men laying on healing and blessings.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] dug Buumi free — and a lightning bolt blasted down from a red-robed [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizard]] floating above the treeline, wand levelled at the ruin. Patches dodged clear; the Thayan darted away into the night.

## Gaddock

[[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] shadowstepped in pursuit of the floating wizard and struck, just as [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' poisoned bolt dropped the Thayan unconscious with Drow venom. But a second presence had shadowed in behind the monk: cold, powerful arms seized him from behind, and Tark turned in the grapple to see the face of his lost master — [[gaddock|Gaddock]], pale and weeping, a horn of black crystal driven into his skull and swirling with inner smoke. The vampire bared his fangs, locked eyes with his beloved student, and bit him, beginning to feed.

[[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] flew to help and was intercepted by the returned [[vampire-knight-of-frostmoore|Armored Vampire]]. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] pulled on the [[mysterious-glove|mysterious glove]] from his past, and the Arcane Hand interposed between Tark and Gaddock, prying the feeding vampire back. Tark broke free, turned to strike his master down — and Gaddock dropped away to the forest floor and retreated into the trees, with Tark following after him.

## The Wider Battle

As Tark gave chase, a horde of ghouls poured down the mountainside toward the [[crimson-dawn-knights|paladins]], summoned by more [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizards]] casting from the forest. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] spotted an Oni step out from behind a tree to hurl a spell before slipping back into cover. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] Farstepped to the unconscious Red Wizard, drew out a [[bag-of-devouring|Bag of Devouring]] whose interior was all jaws, and fed the Thayan's hand into the maw — and the wizard was consumed whole.

A peal of cold laughter answered from the trees, and arcane power lashed out of the dark to blast Cirrus from an unseen foe. The valley filled with the shrieking of ghouls and the spellcasting of the Red Wizards of [[red-wizards-of-thay|Thay]]. The battle for the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] had begun in earnest, and the session closed in the thick of it.
