Boulda is a Deep Gnome wererat from the northwestern warren of Blingdenstone. She was petrified alongside Trisk after coming to his aid during his ill-fated solo assault on the Stone Priestess. The group found her statue — distinguished by strangely sharp features, a long nose, and larger ears giving her a rat-like appearance — standing back to back with Trisk among the ruins.

After being restored by Cirrus, she rejected an offer to relocate to the Barony, insisting that Blingdenstone was her home. She later served as the group's guide into the wererat warrens and helped broker the alliance between her father Chipgrin and Chief Diggermattock.
