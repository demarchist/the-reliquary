## Background

Djoser serves as the Grand Warden of Iyum, holding one of the most important military positions in the city. He is a key member of the Usurpers of Rudjek, the revolutionary group that overthrew the vampire ruler.

## Role in the Campaign

Djoser aids the party during their time in Iyum, wielding the powerful Hammer of Niraz in combat. The artifact he carries later proves instrumental in Fëanon's rituals.

When the spelljammer crashed on Al Askar, Djoser went searching for Liara and instead found Thorna and Patches. He accompanied them to investigate the wreck, arriving with his Medjay on mounts. After the party rescued JmJm and Doc from the Cult of the Black Skull and fought the Psi'Shara that had dominated the crew, Djoser planned to return to Iyum and organise a proper excavation of the spelljammer wreckage — the largest piece having been lost to quicksand.

## Notable Traits

- Grand Warden and chief military officer of Iyum
- Wields the legendary Hammer of Niraz
- Loyal revolutionary who helped overthrew the vampire lord Rudjek
- Knowledgeable about Mt. Saqar's dangers and the Temple of Vaketh
