A piwafwi is a dark cloak of elvenkind crafted by the [[drow-faction|Drow]], enchanted to enhance the wearer's ability to hide and move unseen. The magic woven into a piwafwi helps the wearer blend into shadows and move silently, making them nearly invisible in the dimly lit tunnels of the [[underdark|Underdark]].

## Use by the Party

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] acquired her first piwafwi from the Drow agent Tarla and put it to immediate use — creeping invisibly through a doorway flanked by two hobgoblins at [[cragmaw-castle|Cragmaw Castle]]. In [[barovia|Barovia]], her Drow heritage combined with the cloak rendered her invisible even to vampiric senses.

Later, [[vizerion|Vizerion]] gifted the party a chest of piwafwi cloaks as a show of good faith, equipping multiple members of the group with Drow stealth.

## Limitations

Piwafwi cloaks' enchantments are tied to the Underdark's unique magical properties. Some lose their power when exposed to prolonged sunlight, reflecting the Drow's own vulnerability to the surface world.
