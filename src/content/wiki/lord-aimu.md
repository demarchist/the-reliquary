Lord Aimu is a Paladin of [[lathander|Lathander]] and a nobleman of [[helioglabulus|Helios]]. He broke into the Cathedral of Lathander and stole the [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]] — a celestial greatsword the group had recovered from [[barovia|Barovia]] and donated to the church, where it had been catalogued as a [[sunsword|sunsword]].

The theft provoked a lockdown of the Cathedral and a quiet scandal among the clergy. The priests informed [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] that this was an internal matter, but suggested that the [[house-huron|House Huron]] soirée would be the appropriate venue to pursue the issue through the city's nobility.

[[mister-luko|Mister Luko]] claimed to know Lord Aimu's whereabouts and offered the intelligence in exchange for completing the [[house-muskov|House Muskov]] heist.

## Flight

After the group completed the heist, Luko delivered on his promise. The Crown believed Lord Aimu had fled east to his estate on the border of the steppe, but Luko's intelligence told a different story: Aimu had doubled back in secret, swapping horses and fleeing into the foothills of the Galena Mountains — specifically, to his wife's old estate in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]]. The route runs along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]] to [[ironfang-keep|Ironfang Keep]], then north for a day's travel into the vale.
