Lord Aimu is a Paladin of [[lathander|Lathander]] and a nobleman of [[helioglabulus|Helios]]. He broke into the Cathedral of Lathander and stole the [[ashbringer|Ashbringer]] — a celestial greatsword the group had recovered from [[barovia|Barovia]] and donated to the church, where it had been catalogued as a [[sunsword|sunsword]].

The theft provoked a lockdown of the Cathedral and a quiet scandal among the clergy. The priests informed [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] that this was an internal matter, but suggested that the [[house-huron|House Huron]] soirée would be the appropriate venue to pursue the issue through the city's nobility.

[[mister-luko|Mister Luko]] claimed to know Lord Aimu's whereabouts and offered the intelligence in exchange for completing the [[house-muskov|House Muskov]] heist.

## Flight

After the group completed the heist, Luko delivered on his promise. The Crown believed Lord Aimu had fled east to his estate on the border of the steppe, but Luko's intelligence told a different story: Aimu had doubled back in secret, swapping horses and fleeing into the foothills of the Galena Mountains — specifically, to his wife's old estate in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]]. The route runs along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]] to [[ironfang-keep|Ironfang Keep]], then north for a day's travel into the vale.

## In the Chapel

The party reached the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] at sundown and found Lord Aimu in the chapel — a separate, ornate Lathanderian building set apart from the manor — kneeling at the altar on which he had placed the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]]. He wore a warrior's hakama in cream and brown silk. He did not rise when [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] burst through the doors on [[steden|Steden]]'s back with a dying [[gnome-of-the-vale|Gnome]] in his arm; he simply turned, met the bard's eye calmly, and asked after the wound.

The Lord laid both hands on the Gnome and channelled Lay on Hands — radiance pouring from his palms with the steady, sourceless light of a paladin who had spent his life in the work. Every bruise, every cut, every internal injury closed. The two vampire puncture marks on her neck remained.

Aimu sat back on his heels and observed quietly that the *strength* required to mend her had not been the strength he would summon to heal a Gnome. He did not elaborate. [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] did not press.

## Attunement

When JmJm reached for the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] on the altar and lifted it, the radiance of the blade did not change — the weapon had been bound to its wielder, and JmJm was not it. Lord Aimu stood, smoothed the front of his hakama, and extended one hand. JmJm placed the hilt in his palm.

The Sunsword *lit* — the entire chapel filling with sourceless daylight the instant the lord's fingers closed on the grip. Aimu beckoned to his two guards and walked out toward the battle raging in the valley behind the estate, the celestial blade burning steady in his hand. The two nearest [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen White Dragons]] in mid-stoop lifted their heads at the brightness of him and *flinched*.

Whatever scandal had attended the theft of a holy weapon from the Cathedral of [[lathander|Lathander]], the weapon itself had recognised the man who took it.
