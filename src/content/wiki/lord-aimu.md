Lord Aimu is a Paladin of [[lathander|Lathander]] and a nobleman of [[helioglabulus|Helios]]. He broke into the Cathedral of Lathander and stole the [[sunsword|Sunsword]] — a legendary vampire-killing weapon the group had recovered from [[barovia|Barovia]] and donated to the church.

The theft provoked a lockdown of the Cathedral and a quiet scandal among the clergy. The priests informed [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] that this was an internal matter, but suggested that the [[house-huron|House Huron]] soirée would be the appropriate venue to pursue the issue through the city's nobility.

[[mister-luko|Mister Luko]] claimed to know Lord Aimu's whereabouts and offered the intelligence in exchange for completing the [[house-muskov|House Muskov]] heist. The group has not yet collected on this deal.
