The Celestial Artifact Shard is one of five pieces of an ancient radiant artifact with the power to drive back the necromantic mists plaguing the world. [[orcus|Orcus]] seeks to collect all five fragments, and their recovery drives the overarching quest of the campaign. The shards are fragments of [[the-last-word|The Last Word]], an artifact of Creation broken into seven pieces and scattered across the multiverse.

## The Neverwinter Shard

The first shard the party encountered was hidden within a golden obelisk in a celestial vault beneath [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]]'s Chasm, protected by an Androsphinx's moral trials. [[kangaxx|Kangaxx]] — a servant of Orcus who had been manipulating the party — revealed his true allegiance at the vault's heart, shattering the obelisk with an incantation. The obelisk was consumed in a flash of light, leaving a golden chit no larger than an inch hovering fifteen feet in the air. [[lodan|Lo'Dan]] and his [[bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'Aerthe]] Drow intervened with a devastating ambush, and after a running battle against Kangaxx's true golden lich form, [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] claimed the shard.

## Incorporation

The shard was incorporated into [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]]'s shield, where it served as the party's primary defence against the mists and a bargaining chip coveted by every power they encountered.

## In Barovia

[[strahd-von-zarovich|Strahd]] demanded the artifact shard in exchange for the party's freedom from [[barovia|Barovia]]. When negotiations broke down, Strahd used the power of the [[amber-temple|Amber Temple]] to wish the shard away. The loss, combined with the interacting Wish spells, caused time distortions throughout the demiplane and triggered [[ireena|Ireena]]'s seemingly random deaths and resurrections.
