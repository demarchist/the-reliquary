## Description

Kallista Alamreth Undonwe was a Matron Mother, follower of Eilistraee and Selûné, and apostate of Lolth. She had lilac skin, white hair, and piercing orange eyes — the same eyes that Patches inherited across the ages. She wielded a long silvery glaive with a gracefully curving blade and wore flowing robes of shimmering white silk over delicate armour.

## Last Stand

In the ancient past, Kallista led thirty of her people — including children — to a hilltop henge of elven standing stones as Lolth's forces closed in. A Drow arcanist raised a dome of force around the refugees while Kallista stayed outside to face two Drider-Balors and an invisible Marilith alone. Her own daughter, a Lolth-worshipping Matron, watched from the sky.

Overwhelmed by the demons, Kallista turned her dying scream into a prayer. Her body collapsed into an orb of blinding light and detonated, destroying the demons and blinding her treacherous daughter. A single glowing Moonstone and a field of moon lilies were all that remained. Her people escaped safely.

## Awakening

When Patches touched a Mirror of Loss in the Cathedral of Twilight, she and the entire group were pulled into Kallista's dream — her eternally replaying last stand. The group's intervention changed the outcome for the first time, defeating all three demons. Crisserei, the Planetar of Selûné, then showed the true history.

Moved by the encounter, Kallista offered to bless Patches as the new Matron Mother of House Alamreth Undonwe. She then awakened from her slumber in Yggward and walked up from the necropolis to rejoin the living, intending to visit Dokkar and meet her descendant Finn.
