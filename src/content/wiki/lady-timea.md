Lady Timea is the partner of [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] and his counterpart in command of the [[crimson-dawn-knights|Order of the Crimson Dawn]] — a Warlock of [[kelemvor|Kelemvor]] standing as the mirror to his Paladin of [[lathander|Lathander]]. The two of them are the supreme commanders of the unified order, holding together the merger of the Paladins of Lathander and the [[warlocks-of-kelemvor|Warlocks of Kelemvor]] under the banner *For the Greater Good*.

## In the Cathedral

Aimu and Timea together escorted the [[queen-concetinna-frostmantle|Queen]] and the [[red-wizards-of-thay|Thayan delegation]] into the Cathedral of Lathander for the audience that triggered Lord Aimu's flight. They stood at the queen's side during the examination of the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] and the Wizards' careful, attentive inspection of the blade. Whatever Lord Aimu sensed wrong in that exchange, Lady Timea sensed it with him — but the political risk of acting on the suspicion was Lord Aimu's, and it was Aimu who took it.

Her current movements are not known to the party. She remains in [[helioglabulus|Helios]] at the head of the Order while Lord Aimu sits in hiding at the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] with the stolen blade. Whether she has been compelled to disavow him publicly, or whether the Order is quietly closing ranks around its absent commander, is one of the many questions [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] retired to meditate over before deciding what to do with the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] next.
