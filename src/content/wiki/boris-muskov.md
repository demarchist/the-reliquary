Boris Muskov is the son of [[brusso-muskov|Lord Brusso Muskov]] and cousin of [[dimitri-muskov|Dimitri Muskov]]. Following his father's death, he is the new head of [[house-muskov|House Muskov]] in [[helioglabulus|Helios]].

## Appearance

Boris is a vibrant nobleman roughly Dimitri's age, sporting a shaved head with a combover mohawk and a long black handlebar moustache. His longcoat is silk, delicately embroidered and lined with white ermine, and he carries a silver bejewelled sabre at his hip. He moves with the easy authority of someone accustomed to command.

## His Father's Death

Boris arrived at the family manor with a retinue of house guards, [[crimson-dawn-knights|Crimson Dawn Knights]], city guard, and Warlocks of [[kelemvor|Kelemvor]] to find [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] naked, oiled, and straddling his father's corpse in the middle of what had been — for the better part of an hour — an agonisingly intimate Reincarnation ritual. Boris nearly had the bard seized and thrown from the house until one of the Warlocks confirmed, to everyone's disbelief, that the spell was legitimate. The Reincarnation ultimately failed — Lord Brusso's spirit was unable to return. The body was taken to the Cathedral on holy ground.

## The Pale Monk

Boris confirmed that [[gaddock|Gaddock]] had been hired by Lord Brusso on the recommendation of the Thayans and had been living in the house as a personal bodyguard. He noted the monk bore a crystal shard embedded in his head, horn-like, though Gaddock seemed unbothered by it. When the group explained that Gaddock was likely responsible for Lord Brusso's death, Boris was horrified.

## Alliance

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] offered Boris an alliance of friendship, lord to lord. Boris accepted — his lawyers would draft an official arrangement.
