## Description

The Eye of Aren is a fist-sized crystal that pulses with soft amber light, its surface etched with patterns that shift when viewed from different angles. It was created by the wizard Theodemir as the keystone of the binding spell restraining Zhengyi the Witch-King, built upon a shard of the artifact known as The Last Word.

## Creation

Theodemir designed the Eye to amplify and sustain the enormous magical effort required to keep Zhengyi imprisoned. The casting demanded more than magic — it required the caster's life force. Theodemir poured the last of himself into the Eye in the presence of his acolytes, his body dissolving as streams of golden energy drained into the crystal. The sacrifice was permanent: Theodemir died, but his spirit was preserved within the artifact, bound to the shard of The Last Word at its core.

## The Conspiracy

After Theodemir's death, his acolytes debated where to hide the Eye. The young Fëanon Sërindë proposed entrusting it to Nostalgia for Infinity — an Illithid Elder Brain whose psionic field would amplify the Eye's power a hundredfold. Theodemir himself endorsed the plan with his final decision. The acolytes journeyed to the hive beneath Ladypeak and placed the Eye in the Elder Brain's psychic web, where it remained for decades.

## Recovery

The party infiltrated Nostalgia for Infinity's hive and reclaimed the Eye of Aren, with Fëanon freeing Theodemir's spirit — still bound within the artifact. The spirit was later transferred from the Eye into Fëanon's ring of mind shielding, where Theodemir continued to guide his former student even as his essence slowly degraded.
