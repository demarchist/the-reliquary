Ilvara Mizzrym is a High Priestess of House Mizzrym and Lolth who commands the Underdark outpost of [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]]. She is a meticulous, ruthless commander who fights in fine mesh armor layered with a Piwafwi cloak and a full helm shaped into the likeness of a spider — mandibles and all — a single stark-white braid the only visible evidence that there is a Drow woman beneath it. She carries a snake-headed whip, the serpents alive and coiling around her arm when at rest.

## The Crossroads

Ilvara had been given intelligence on the group before the ambush — exactly who was coming and where. She assembled a force of sixteen Drow, a wizard, two priestesses (including herself), and a contingent of Orc auxiliaries at [[the-crossroads|the Crossroads]], a major Underdark junction. When the group came through, the Orcs sprang the trap as a distraction while Ilvara's warriors dropped from the ceiling and the priestesses froze half the party with Hold Person spells.

The capture was methodical. The Drow subdued rather than killed — pommel strikes and punches, taking prisoners. Ilvara walked forward after the fighting ended and tapped a claw against [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]]'s ice tomb. She told him to surrender or die in perfect Common. When he vanished instead, four Drow pursued and lost him. She did not pursue herself.

In the aftermath she cast a flash of healing light over the injured Drow prisoner Sarith — a small and casually demonstrated power — and announced to the captives that they were slaves of [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]]. When [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] muttered an insult as she turned to leave, she spun and called down a pillar of flame over him. Then she walked away, laughing.

## Velkynvelve

The outpost [[ilvara-mizzrym|Ilvara]] commands is a web-bridge compound of stalactites high above a cavern floor, dim with purple faerie fire and loud with a distant waterfall. She administered it with calm authority, running a facility whose other subordinates included her lieutenant Shoor Vandree — a male Drow with extensive burn scarring and a lightning wand — and a wizard she kept close.

When demons assaulted Velkynvelve during the prison break, Ilvara led her barracks troops toward the prison level and summoned Balgura demons to bolster the defenses. A Nalfeshnee arriving through the assault stalled her forces with a guttural exchange between the demon lords' servants and the priestesses — something she had not expected. The group used the chaos to escape.

## The Pursuit

The Wind Walk that carried the group from Velkynvelve outpaced whatever pursuit Ilvara organized. She did not give up. Weeks later, [[doc|Doc]]'s scrying confirmed her pursuit party was still following south — pressing toward [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] by a slower route. She tracked Shoor Vandree's return to Velkynvelve after [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] shot him through the cheek and the domination on him ended, and rebuilt her intelligence network from there.

By the time the group had been in [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] for some weeks, Ilvara had a Drow scout named Xalith watching the city and reporting back. When Xalith realized she had been spotted by [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] from across the street, she decided a negotiation was better than being hunted, and offered her services instead. Through this, the group learned Ilvara knew they were in the city.

The group's response was to hire Xalith for their own use, with a plan to lure Ilvara into the reach of [[themberchaud|Themberchaud]] the Dragon — a threat that could end the pursuit definitively.
