Ilvara Mizzrym is a Drow High Priestess of House Mizzrym who commands the Underdark outpost of Velkynvelve. She leads the capture and imprisonment of the party, setting in motion their harrowing journey through the Underdark. Her authority and cruelty exemplify the brutality of Drow society.
