Merrigold is a Halfling who saved [[thorna|Thorna]] after she was badly beaten by the titan [[nalkara|Nalkara]] near the Great Glacier blocking [[icewind-dale|Icewind Dale]]. She accompanied [[thorna|Thorna]] back to the [[selunia|Barony]] and then into the [[underdark|Underdark]], pursuing the group through the [[ringed-veils|Ringed Veil]] network. The two arrived at [[the-crossroads|the Crossroads]] simultaneously with [[bowie|Bowie]] and his companion Maloo.
