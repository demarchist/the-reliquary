## Description

The Titanic Forge is an ancient forge hidden deep within the magma-heated interior of Mt. Saqar, accessible only by crossing a massive Titanic bridge that passes beneath a thousand-foot lava-fall. A large wrought-alloy staircase descends into an intricate forge complex where channels of bubbling lava wind through a huge mould chamber and steam vents. The forge sits above a pool of lava, supported by massive pipes and adorned in angular patterns — both alien and ancient in design, its craft transcending mortal capability.

## Properties

The forge harnesses the volcano's natural heat to achieve temperatures necessary for working titanium. Items crafted here possess exceptional durability and magical potential.

## History

When the party discovered the Forge, it was occupied by the Cult of the Black Skull, who had chained a Ruby Crystal Dragon at the forge entrance and were torturing it with firelances while trying to sway it to their cause. After the party defeated the cultists and their alghoul horde — with Patches detonating the dragon's locks — the freed dragon directed them to the Forge below.

The party crafted titanium weapons and armor for multiple party members: a spear for Tark, shield for Buumi, armor for Thorna, scimitar for JmJm, tome-box for Fëanon, and greatsword for Snorbul.
