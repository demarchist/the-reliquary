Halrak Sudrin is a hydromancer from the Moonshae Isles, a territory controlled by the [[amnians|Amnians]]. He was captured and held in the slave pens beneath [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]] before being freed during the group's assault on the West-Wall Slave Market. He appeared in the [[keep-of-thunder-bluff|Keep of Thunder Bluff]] shortly after, confused and disoriented, and was identified by Li as the strange man who had materialised in the [[selunia|Barony]]. He chose to stay in town.
