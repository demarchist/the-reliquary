Hyrokken is a female Frost Giant from the settlement on Shatterpeak. She first appeared during the wedding feast competitions, where she ran the foot race against Patches — smiling before the start, only to be outpaced by the Drow's supernatural speed. She accepted the loss gracefully.

When the party fled the self-destructing Sanctum of Krasus and descended to the village, Hyrokken came out to meet them. She reported that the fiendish horde from the Sanctum had passed through the settlement, led by a small creature in red robes with a feminine voice. She warned the group not to enter the village — hospitality had been fulfilled and some of the male giants might be hostile — then hurried back to retrieve their sleigh and inform the elders.
