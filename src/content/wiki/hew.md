Hew is a +1 axe recovered from the green dragon [[venomfang|Venomfang]]'s hoard in the ruined mage's tower of [[thundertree|Thundertree]]. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] looted it along with the rest of the hoard — 200 gold pieces, silver goblets set with moonstone, a scroll of Misty Step, a scroll of Lightning Bolt, and a Vial of Infinite Liquids — while the dragon was distracted after nearly killing the rest of the party.

The axe saw its most memorable moment at [[cragmaw-castle|Cragmaw Castle]], where [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] had been held prisoner. Standing in nothing but his long johns after his rescue, the dwarf requested a weapon and was given Hew.
