## Background

High-Marshal Arasume holds the highest military rank in Silverymoon, commanding the city's armed forces and coordinating its defense strategies.

## Role in the Campaign

During the Amnian invasion, Arasume leads Silverymoon's military response, organizing the defense at the Plaza of the Watchers and coordinating with the party to repel the invading forces. Her leadership proves crucial to the city's survival.

## Notable Traits

- Silverymoon's supreme military commander
- Decisive leader in times of crisis
- Instrumental in the defense against the Amnian coup
