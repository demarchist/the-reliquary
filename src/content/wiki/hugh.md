Hugh is Lord Neverember's spymaster — a fat man with a head shaped like a potato, small beady eyes, and noble clothes that do little to improve his striking lack of looks. He was found sitting on the steps of the throne in the aftermath of the attack on Neverwinter, having survived the assault on the keep.

Hugh led the strategic briefing that revealed the full scope of the coordinated war, laying out intelligence across a map of Faerun that showed simultaneous invasions, naval engagements, and terrorist attacks spanning the entire continent. He noted that every attack made strategic sense except the Drow assault on the Moonwood, which troubled him as it didn't fit the pattern.
