The Black Arrow is an Ancient Black Dragon from Shou Lung and a member of the Dragonflight. Their humanoid form is that of a Goblin.

The Black Arrow joined Asha and Azoo in raiding a local Green Dragon's lair early in the journey. When the group rested in an abandoned Blue Dragon lair in the desert, The Black Arrow's dreams summoned a fetid oasis from the sands — a manifestation of the raw magic the ascending dragons radiate in their sleep, subsequently supercharged by Asha with plant growth. They also assisted Xuefang in carrying the largest prey from the group's hunt over the inland sea.
