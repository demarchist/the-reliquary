The Vampire Knight of Frostmoore is an undead lord, armoured in fluted, asymmetrical black plate, who teleported into the battle above the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] mounted on a sickly grey adult white dragon — larger than the [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen White Dragons]] of the flight by half again — in a thunderclap of displaced air.

## Appearance

His helm has no faceplate, only a deep T-slit through which two red eyes glow softly. Below the slit, pallid lips part in a faint, deliberate smile, revealing delicate vampire fangs. He carries a long tower shield of black steel on one arm and, in the other, a ten-foot lance bearing technology the group recognised immediately: a [[saurian-firelance|Saurian]] [[saurian-lance|lance-weapon]] — the kind that had no business existing on this side of the [[ringed-veils|Ringed Veil]].

He salutes opponents with the lance before engaging. He flies without wings, leaping bodily from his saddle with the casual grace of a man who weighs nothing.

## Combat

His first act on arrival was to fire a Lightning Bolt from the lance toward [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]], who had launched into the air on a borrowed spear and shadowstepped to within striking distance of the [[red-wizards-of-thay|Red Wizard]] riding the lead dragon. Tark dodged. The bolt continued past, ploughed a smouldering rent through three walls of the estate's east wing, and caught [[lady-isabel|Lady Isabel]] full across the shoulder at the end of its arc.

When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] paralysed the Red Wizard with a poisoned crossbow bolt, the Vampire Knight kicked off his own saddle and *flew* across the gap of air to retrieve his ally. He caught the falling, paralysed Thayan bodily, took half a dozen arrows and crossbow bolts from the party as he did — the Wizard's body absorbing every shaft — and made it back to his dragon's saddle holding a corpse. He looked down at the riddled body in his arms, roared in cold fury, and engulfed the entire position in a sphere of cold darkness that swallowed himself, his mount, and the dead Thayan utterly. When the darkness collapsed, there was nothing left.

## Implications

He arrived precisely when his ally needed extraction, with a teleport-grade movement and a dragon mount available on demand. He carried a Saurian lance — a piece of [[al-askar|Al'Askarian]] technology unlike anything the group has seen in Faerûnian hands. He worked in concert with a Red Wizard of [[red-wizards-of-thay|Thay]] and a flight of Ashen Whites — a coalition that ties together the dragons over the Galena Mountains, the Thayan presence in [[helioglabulus|Helios]], and an unknown source of Saurian weaponry on this side of the [[ringed-veils|Ringed Veil]].

His identity remains unknown. He is *not* [[gaddock|Gaddock]] — Tark's vampire master is a bald pale monk with a black crystal staff, very much not an armoured lance-knight. The Knight of Frostmoore is something else, and his sudden appearance over a Damaran noble's hidden estate, in support of a Red Wizard apparently sent to interdict the recovery of the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]], suggests a coordination that runs much deeper than the group has yet uncovered.
