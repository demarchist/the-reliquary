The Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews Theater — commonly known as The JMJMT or simply the JMJM Theater — is an elaborate performance venue within the Barony of Thunder Bluff. It is owned, operated, and headlined by [[Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews|Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews]] himself.

## Exterior

The entrance is announced by a red carpet leading to ornate doors — a statement of arrival that JmJm considers the opening act of any performance.

## Interior

Inside, the theater is defined by its soaring ceiling and ornate decoration, with soft magical lanterns creating an atmosphere of sophistication and glamor. A grand chandelier dominates the hall. Elegantly cushioned seats face the stage, which features a technologically advanced design by [[Gundren Rockseeker]], combining mechanical ingenuity with magical flair — capable of producing smoke, pyrotechnics, dramatic lighting, and synchronized magical effects.

The backstage area is a working environment of scaffolding, pulley systems, sandbags, and rigging that supports the elaborate machinery of JmJm's productions. An orchestra pit sits before the stage. The whole apparatus is precision-tuned to JmJm's exacting standards, and it shows.

## Management Style

JmJm runs rehearsals with the same intensity he brings to everything else. A lighting technician who misses the blue gels, a piece of scenery six inches from its mark, a cue that drops half a beat late — none of it escapes his notice, and all of it earns a response. Stagehands describe working at the JMJMT as exhausting, infuriating, and oddly transformative: whatever his faults as a director, JmJm's dedication to the craft is total, and the shows are genuinely extraordinary.

## Performances

JmJm's performances at the JMJMT are spectacles of high energy, magical enchantment, and captivating artistry that draw crowds from across the Barony. His greatest shows have the quality of a shared dream — an audience leaving in raptures, not entirely sure what just happened to them.
