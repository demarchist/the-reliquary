The Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews Theater — commonly known as The JMJMT or simply the JMJM Theater — is an elaborate performance venue within the Barony of Thunder Bluff. It is owned, operated, and headlined by Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews himself.

The theater features a technologically advanced stage designed by Gundren Rockseeker, combining mechanical ingenuity with magical flair. JmJm's performances are a spectacle of high energy, magical enchantment, and captivating artistry that draw crowds from near and far.

The theater also serves as an important location in the story of Ella, the Harper agent, whose time working there contributes to her personal transformation.
