Und is a small village of roughly a dozen houses nestled in the foothills of the Galena Mountains, situated along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]] a few hours south of [[ironfang-keep|Ironfang Keep]]. Its residents are a mix of Dwarves and Humans who make their living from mining and logging.

## The Dream

When the group arrived, the village appeared impossibly perfect — a pie in every window, flowers on every table, bees humming and songbirds trilling. The residents spoke in halting, dreamy tones, as though each word had to swim up from a great depth. The only dissenter was [[urkle|Urkle]], a dwarven herder who had been locked in enchanted ankle stocks on a silenced platform for "sowing discord."

The source of the village's unnatural contentment was [[the-dreamer|The Dreamer]] — a Beholder living in the mines beneath the settlement. Four cloaked dwarves served as its agents, moving between the mine and the village. When the group dispelled the enchantment and confronted the aberration, the Beholder erupted from one of the agents' bodies, and a second emerged from another moments after the first was slain.

## The Elder Orb

The two Beholders in the village were merely children — spawned copies of a far older and more powerful creature lurking in the [[mines-of-und|mines]] below. After the second Beholder was destroyed by [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]]'s Disintegrate, [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] observed from the [[ethereal-plane|Ethereal Plane]] that the mines blazed with power too immense for any ordinary Beholder. Fëanon identified the true threat as an [[elder-orb|Elder Orb]] — an ancient aberration feeding from a magical source deep beneath the settlement.

The group evacuated the surviving villagers down the mountain to a ranger cabin along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]], where they could recover safely while the group delved into the mines to confront the Elder Orb.
