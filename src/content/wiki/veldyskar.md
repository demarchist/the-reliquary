Veldyskar is a sentient Basilisk who resides within [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]], the legendary demi-plane library. Unlike typical specimens of its kind, Veldyskar possesses the ability to speak and sing — the Heralds first heard its strangely modulated song echoing through the tunnels of the Underdark before encountering it. Veldyskar greeted them warmly and served as their escort down the immense crystalline stairway into the library's geode cavern, explaining the nature of the demi-plane and its Stone Giant archivists along the way.
