Vecna, the Whispered One, is the god of secrets, necromancy, and undeath. Once a mortal wizard of unsurpassed ambition, he achieved lichdom and eventually ascended to godhood through sheer force of will and dark magic. He is known for his missing hand and eye — severed by his treacherous lieutenant Kas — which became powerful artefacts in their own right. He is kin to [[orcus|Orcus]] and brother to [[zhengyi|Zhengyi]], and his followers seek forbidden knowledge and guard terrible secrets.

## Zalaam

On [[al-askar|Al Askar]], Vecna operates under the identity of Zalaam — a god of death whose cult, the Black Skull, has been systematically destroying the world from within. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] deduced Vecna's true identity by connecting the evidence: the [[iron-tome|Tome of Al Azif]]'s visions of a human wizard from another world subjugating the non-magical, the cult's preference for necromancy and its iconography, the revelation that Zalaam was an imposter who orchestrated the Calamity a thousand years ago, and the cult leader's own claim that Zalaam was brother to Zhengyi and kin to Orcus. The Ruby Dragon Jareem reached the same conclusion independently.

The true Zalaam — the god of death as balance, as natural order — still has legitimate worshippers on Al Askar. The druid Liara, whom the party encountered in the wilderness, reacted with fury when the party conflated her faith with the cult, calling the Black Skull heretics who served a false aspect.

## Devouring the Gods

Vecna's threat on Al Askar is existential. He has consumed the deity Zalaam entirely and is slowly absorbing the goddess Fawden — explaining the increasing frequency of wild-magic surges and High Storms that wrack the planet. His forces have systematically hunted down every druid on Al Askar — the beings best equipped to restore the world's shattered balance. He commands two armies: an army of the dead in the city of Sharza, led by Saurian Death Lords, and an army of Shadowfell monstrosities in the Black Sea.

His power on Al Askar may be tied to a Fragment of [[the-last-word|The Last Word]] — a Saurian artefact within the Pyramid of Barzok in Sharza. His cult leader at the Titanic Forge warned the party that the corruption had already reached the [[underdark|Underdark]] of Toril through the alghouls — claiming the death of both worlds was inevitable.

## The Font of Fawden

Vecna's most direct confrontation with the party came at the Font of Power beneath the dying avatar of Fawden. As the group assembled a Ringed Veil and began the ritual with The Last Word, Vecna arrived to investigate. Realising that the artefact changed everything, his form collapsed into a vortex of white and black flame. An impossibly deep note resonated through the cavern before the fiery form blazed through the back wall of the chamber, tearing open a gate to Sharza and unleashing an army of undead Saurians upon the Font.

The ensuing battle was the campaign's most desperate stand — Death Lords with Boneclaws, Death Knights riding undead Rocs, the Lady in White, and a Lich, with Vecna himself whispering curses into the defenders' minds that gnawed at their thoughts, sapping speed and focus. Three of Al Askar's gods intervened: [[vaketh|Vaketh]] restored [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]]'s severed hand, Karthus armoured Jareem, and Horrah — goddess of Time — froze reality itself to give [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] the seconds he needed to complete the ritual and destroy Fawden's corrupted form, flinging the group to Yggdrasil and — eventually — home.
