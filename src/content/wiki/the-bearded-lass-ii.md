The Bearded Lass II is a three-masted caravel painted blue, captained by Dink Duwafu. Lady Faye commandeered the captain's cabin during her stay in Neverwinter's harbour, using the ship as her floating headquarters.

The party rowed out to the vessel to meet with Lady Faye, Lo'Dan, and Lemerlais, where they were briefed on intelligence about Zhengyi's dark messengers and a potential lead on another fragment of The Last Word hidden in the Underdark.
