Ironfang Keep is a fortress of dark stone situated atop the saddle of the mountain along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]] in the Galena Mountains — historically a stronghold of [[shar|Sharran]] worshippers, supposedly held by a small order of fanatics sworn to the Mistress of the Night. [[mister-luko|Mister Luko]] marked it on the group's map as the point where they should turn off the main road and head north into [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]] to find [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]]'s hideout.

The small village of [[und|Und]] lies a few hours south of the keep along the pass — a mining and logging settlement in the foothills.

## Destruction

When the party reached the crossroads at the saddle on their way north, they found the keep already destroyed. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] and [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] climbed the last switchback to inspect the ruin while the others continued on the road.

The destruction was *recent*. There was no smouldering, no smoke, and no corpses anywhere in the wreckage — but a heavy, sickly-sweet scent hung over the melted stone, and the stone itself had melted. Long runnels of vitrified rock streaked the broken walls where something corrosive had washed across them and run downhill before cooling. Great rents had been torn through the curtain wall. Claw-marks the length of a forearm scored the inner courtyard. The damage was consistent with the breath of a Black Dragon — and no other source the group could think of.

In the rubble at the foot of a half-collapsed tower, Patches recovered a single faceted shard of black dragon scale, and among the loose stones a melted ingot, a scorched ring, and a fragment of an iron holy symbol of [[shar|Shar]] half-buried in slag. There were no Sharran corpses anywhere in the keep — neither the bodies of the defenders nor any sign of where they had gone.

Whatever had happened at Ironfang Keep had happened within recent days, and was — together with the [[ashen-white-dragon|Ashen White Dragons]] circling the same range — evidence of a larger movement of draconic forces across the Galena Mountains that the group had not yet fully reckoned with.
