Ironfang Keep is a fortress situated along the [[bloodstone-pass|Bloodstone Pass]] in the Galena Mountains. [[mister-luko|Mister Luko]] marked it on the group's map as the point where they should turn off the main road and head north into [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]] to find [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]]'s hideout.

The small village of [[und|Und]] lies a few hours south of the keep along the pass — a mining and logging settlement in the foothills.
