The Vault of Sages is Silverymoon's great library and archive, occupying a sprawling complex near the Lady's College. Its underground levels extend far deeper than its modest surface entrance suggests. The main hall is a cathedral of knowledge — vaulted ceilings, galleries rising five stories, and a silence so complete that footsteps echo like dropped coins. The air smells of old paper and preservation magic.

The Vault houses extensive cartographic, geological, and historical records. Its restricted archives contain sensitive materials accessible only with the Keeper's permission. Sala Shara serves as the current Keeper of the Vault — a position she has used to monitor intelligence related to Nostalgia for Infinity and the Eye of Aren since the conspiracy decades ago.

The party used the Vault's resources to locate Nostalgia for Infinity's hive, cross-referencing fragmentary sensory impressions from Lei Tan's memory simulation with the Vault's geological and hydrological records to pinpoint Ladypeak as the hive's location.
