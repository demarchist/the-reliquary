Guinevere — commonly called Gwen — is [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren Rockseeker]]'s masterwork clockwork lion, his magnum opus and crowning achievement as an Artificer. She is the envy of Artificers the world over.

## Appearance

To those unfamiliar with Gundren, Guinevere can appear terrifying — glowing, intelligent red-gem eyes staring out from a conglomeration of threshing blades, snicking gears, and armor plates. The clockwork lion is a marvel of both engineering and arcane artifice, crafted by the Rockseeker brothers in the forge of the [[tavern-of-doors|Tavern of Doors]].

## Loyalty and Role

Guinevere is absolutely loyal to Gundren and obeys his every command. She accompanies him at all times, serving as both companion and fearsome combatant. Over the course of the campaign, Gwen has also been lent to other party members as a mount — including [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] and [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] during critical missions. The mechanical lion's durability and loyalty make her one of the Spellforged's most cherished possessions.

## Notable Moments

Gwen has been with the party since their earliest adventures, proving invaluable in countless engagements. She towed Gundren across a chasm during a Myconid ambush in the Bog, carried riders through hostile terrain, and served as a frontline combatant alongside Gundren's other inventions. The dwarven smith [[dogan|Dogan]] eyed Gwen with visible appreciation during the artificer's competition at [[gauntlgrym|Gauntlgrym]].
