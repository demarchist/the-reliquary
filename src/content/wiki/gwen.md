Guinevere — commonly called Gwen — is [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren Rockseeker]]'s masterwork clockwork lion, his magnum opus and crowning achievement as an Artificer. She is the envy of Artificers the world over.

## Appearance

To those unfamiliar with Gundren, Guinevere can appear terrifying — glowing, intelligent red-gem eyes staring out from a conglomeration of threshing blades, snicking gears, and armor plates. The clockwork lion is a marvel of both engineering and arcane artifice, crafted by the Rockseeker brothers in the forge of the [[tavern-of-doors|Tavern of Doors]].

## Loyalty and Role

Guinevere is absolutely loyal to Gundren and obeys his every command. She accompanies him at all times, serving as both companion and fearsome combatant. Over the course of the campaign, Gwen has also been lent to other party members as a mount — including [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] and [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] during critical missions. Gundren's bond with her runs deeper than master and construct — he whispers to her, confides in her, and treats her as a living companion. Standing over the ruins of an ancient vault in the [[underdark|Underdark]], he turned to Gwen and murmured: "Pa would be proud."

## Combat

Gwen is far more than an ornament. Her flashing claws can tear through armour, and she can breathe lightning from her clockwork jaws — a devastating ranged attack that has turned engagements on a single breath. At the Feast of St. Andral in [[barovia|Barovia]], she dragged [[horatio-von-bumidere|Buumi]] free from a vampire's grip and unleashed a torrent of lightning into the giant vampire Beerna while Gundren hammered shot after shot from his [[thundercannon|thundercannon]]. She has towed Gundren across chasms, forced her mechanical body through the organic hull of a [[mind-flayer-nautiloid|Mind Flayer Nautiloid]], and once clawed futilely at the vampire dwarf Kenga — a rare moment where her considerable strength met its match.

## Upgrades

Gundren has continuously improved Gwen over the campaign. The Rockseeker brothers added adamantite plating in the [[moonwood|Moonwood]], hardening her against increasingly dangerous foes. Gundren has sought materials for further upgrades, recognising the need for something more powerful than an enchanted gemstone at her core. The dwarven enchanter [[megno-slatesunder|Megno Slatesunder]] was astounded by Gwen when the party visited [[sundabar|Sundabar]], and the smith [[dogan|Dogan]] eyed her with visible appreciation at the artificer's competition in [[gauntlgrym|Gauntlgrym]] — recognition from master craftsmen that Gundren's creation stands among the finest work in [[faerun|Faerûn]].
