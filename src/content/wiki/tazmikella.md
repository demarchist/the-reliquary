Tazmikella — or Taz, as she introduced herself — runs a magic shop called Tazmikella's Odds and Sundries in the merchant ring of [[helioglabulus|Helios]]. The establishment is a converted house on a quiet cul-de-sac, sitting across from a more impressive competitor called Izzy's Stuff.

## Appearance

She presents as a wizened old woman, bowed by time, leaning on an amber walking stick. She wears fine old robes layered with a colourful silk shawl and has a thick mess of grey hair with a few strands of coppery red still visible. Her face is slender and creased — human in every respect, until it isn't.

## The Shop

The interior is a hodgepodge of random and exquisite antiques. Taz's prices defy mortal economics: she asked for two hundred years of time in exchange for a powerful holy amulet, producing a great hourglass as the receptacle for payment, and quoted a million gold for another piece. She appeared to find the group's incredulity genuinely entertaining.

## True Nature

When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] tested her with a holy medallion, nothing happened — but Taz burst out laughing and winked, her eyes shifting to slitted, catlike pupils. Patches winked back, shifting her own eyes from human grey to drow orange, which earned a chuckle and raised eyebrows from the old woman. Whatever Taz truly is, she is not human, and her going-out-of-business sale appears to be conducted entirely on her own terms.

## The Cat

When the group crossed the cul-de-sac to browse [[izzys-stuff|Izzy's Stuff]], a cat followed them inside and watched from the doorway — its eyes the same green as Taz's. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] suspected immediately that the old lady had simply changed form and trailed them across the street.

The suspicion proved well-founded. During the trade at Izzy's, the Signet of [[house-faen-tlabbar|House Faen Tlabbar]] was given up as part of the price. Patches bent down to the cat and whispered that she would appreciate getting her ring back. The cat chirped and vanished into the back room. It did not return — but when Patches shifted her foot, the signet ring lay on the floor beside her boot.

Whatever Taz truly is, she appears to hold some influence over Izzy's establishment — or at the very least, a casual willingness to intervene in its commerce on behalf of people she finds amusing.

## The Sister

[[lady-isabel|Lady Isabel]] — encountered later at the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]] — revealed herself as the Adult Copper Dragon Izmakella, and explained her own human disguise as part of *a game my sister and I have been playing for some time*. The sister is Tazmikella. Two adult Coppers in human shape have been quietly resident in [[helioglabulus|Helios]] the entire time, running shops on adjacent cul-de-sacs in the merchant ring and watching the capital between them. The slitted pupils, the prices measured in centuries, the cat that delivered a signet ring at a foot's edge — all of it had been a Copper Dragon amusing herself behind a face.
