Tazmikella — or Taz, as she introduced herself — runs a magic shop called Tazmikella's Odds and Sundries in the merchant ring of [[helioglabulus|Helios]]. The establishment is a converted house on a quiet cul-de-sac, sitting across from a more impressive competitor called Izzy's Stuff.

## Appearance

She presents as a wizened old woman, bowed by time, leaning on an amber walking stick. She wears fine old robes layered with a colourful silk shawl and has a thick mess of grey hair with a few strands of coppery red still visible. Her face is slender and creased — human in every respect, until it isn't.

## The Shop

The interior is a hodgepodge of random and exquisite antiques. Taz's prices defy mortal economics: she asked for two hundred years of time in exchange for a powerful holy amulet, producing a great hourglass as the receptacle for payment, and quoted a million gold for another piece. She appeared to find the group's incredulity genuinely entertaining.

## True Nature

When [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] tested her with a holy medallion, nothing happened — but Taz burst out laughing and winked, her eyes shifting to slitted, catlike pupils. Patches winked back, shifting her own eyes from human grey to drow orange, which earned a chuckle and raised eyebrows from the old woman. Whatever Taz truly is, she is not human, and her going-out-of-business sale appears to be conducted entirely on her own terms.
