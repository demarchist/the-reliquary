## Overview

The Knights in Silver are Silverymoon's primary military and law enforcement organization. They maintain order within the city, defend its walls, and conduct patrols throughout the surrounding region.

## Role

Working alongside the Spellguard (who handle magical matters), the Knights in Silver focus on conventional defense and peacekeeping. During the Amnian invasion, they form a crucial part of the city's resistance.

## Significance

The Knights in Silver represent Silverymoon's martial strength and commitment to justice, serving as reliable allies to the party during their time in the city.
