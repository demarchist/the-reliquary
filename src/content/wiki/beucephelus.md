## Description

Beucephelus is a strikingly beautiful red-skinned devil with a distinctly Hobgoblin face, curling horns, and an exaggerated physique — cheekbones too high and sharp, lips too plump, muscles too bulging, teeth too bright. He is a caricature of beauty, and he knows it. He wears a leather legionnaire's skirt with Roman sandals, and can flex his muscular form into sculpted metal armour at will.

He wields an ornate poleaxe that strikes with thunderclaps, hurling targets back with each blow.

## Role

Beucephelus commands the Doomstar Legion's operation around the Ziggurat in the Moonwood, working alongside Ancalagon the Black Dragon. The goblin raid on the Barony was a diversion he orchestrated to keep the defenders' attention south while the Ziggurat completed its purpose. He is known for psychological warfare — taunting Cirrus about his father's torment as a Lemure in Hell and mocking JmJm about his imprisoned father.

When Cirrus offered to buy off the devils, Beucephelus named his price: six hundred and sixty-six innocent souls.
