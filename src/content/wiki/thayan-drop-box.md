A Thayan Drop Box is a seamless metallic purple container crafted by the Red Wizards of Thay. It can only be opened by someone who knows the password — any other attempt triggers a Glyph of Power Word Kill. The box's wards also build in power over time and will eventually detonate, consuming the magic stored within.

One such box was delivered in secret to a watchmaker named Leister Bana in Neverwinter's Tower District, intended for pickup by an unknown agent. The party intercepted it and sent it to Mordenkainen at Helm's Hold, where he, Delly Griplim, and Fëanon Serinde cracked it open by identifying emergency release points and using modified magic missiles to trigger them while Mordenkainen counterspelled the death ward.

Inside they found assassination equipment: a venomous dagger, a cloak of fire resistance, a necklace of fireballs, a mask of clear breath granting immunity to gas weapons, a parrying dagger, and a greater healing potion. Several items had been drained by the box's defensive glyphs. The contents, combined with a half-burned letter detailing a plot against a lord, pointed to an assassination conspiracy operating within Neverwinter.
