The Faerzress is not a spell, a creature, or a place. It is a condition of the [[underdark|Underdark]] itself — a suffusion of wild, unstable magical energy that permeates the deep tunnels and caverns of the world beneath the surface. It manifests as deposits of shimmering crystal that leak constant magical radiation into the surrounding stone and air, and its presence is felt by anyone with arcane sensitivity as a persistent, ambient tingle at the edge of perception.

## What It Does

The Faerzress is responsible for much of the Underdark's fundamental strangeness. It interferes with divination magic — scrying from the surface is unreliable or impossible in areas of heavy saturation — and similarly blocks teleportation, making the deep tunnels a trap for anyone who tries to leave the way they arrived. [[feanon-serinde|Fey'Anon]] noted after his impulsive teleportation topside that leaving the Underdark had been far more difficult than it should have been, as had returning — the Faerzress creating resistance in both directions.

Direct interaction with Faerzress deposits triggers wild magic surges of unpredictable severity. When Fey approached a crystal deposit and attempted to draw on its power, the energy began depositing as heat into the surrounding rock. Within thirty seconds, the stone around the group was glowing red hot. They fled ahead of a flood of liquefying stone. The Faerzress had not "done" anything in any purposeful sense — it had simply discharged chaotically, as it always does when disturbed.

## The Teleport Event

The most dramatic single demonstration of the Faerzress's power was unintentional. After days of travel in the oppressive dark, Fey'Anon declared he could not continue, gathered power, and teleported himself topside to Silverymoon. He arrived in the Guild Circle tower, walked to a cafe, had pancakes, and spent a pleasant night in a hotel while the rest of the party camped in an Underdark grotto. The Faerzress had not stopped him — but the resistance he felt, and the unusual difficulty of the return, was its influence.

## Demonic Interaction

The Faerzress appears to interact in ways that are still poorly understood with the demonic corruption spreading through the Underdark. The [[whorlstone-caverns|Whorlstone Caverns]], which are saturated with Faerzress, proved unusually hospitable to the wild magic used by the [[warlocks-of-ceregorach|Warlocks of Ceregorach]] and seemed to amplify the already-strange properties of that place. Whether the Faerzress is being actively warped by the demonic presence in the deep tunnels, or whether the two forces are simply reinforcing one another, remains unclear.
