Snorbul is a male [[svirfneblin-faction|Svirfneblin]] (deep gnome) Barbarian and a member of the [[faerun-defense-force|Faerûn Defense Force]]. He is the owner, operator, and champion-in-residence of [[snorbuls-hole|Snorbul's Hole]], an underground fighting arena within the Barony of [[thunder-bluff|Thunder Bluff]]. His reputation in the fighting pits of [[gracksuluth|Gracklstugh]] preceded him — among the Svirfneblin, he is something of a legend.

## Appearance

Snorbul is four feet tall with a peculiarly muscular, toned, and well-defined physique. His skin has a stone-like texture and appearance with a dark blue tone. He is bald and beardless with no body hair, and his eyes are red with long, pointed ears. Snorbul tends to wear as little clothing as possible — when he does, he prefers tight-fitting net shirts and leather short shorts that showcase his muscles.

## Personality

As a Barbarian, Snorbul has a tempestuous temperament with a barely-restrained primal rage always lurking below the surface. He views the trappings of civilisation as a sign of weakness, embracing extreme animalism instead. As a Svirfneblin, he is most comfortable underground and in the dark — when the party first reached the surface after the [[underdark|Underdark]], he walked outside, was immediately blinded by sunlight, and screamed in agony before [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] pulled him back inside and gave him sun-shades.

Yet beneath the rage and the animalism lies a deep compassion for the vulnerable. He set up a fighting pit during a debutante ball and lured three aristocrats into a bout against a Flail-Snail — but when the first went down and he realised how badly outmatched they were, he leapt in to end the fight and had them carried to healers. He sleeps in dirt — the dwarves at his mining claim saw he needed a nap and shovelled gravel on top of him — and his connection with stone and earth is fundamental to his identity.

## Combat

Snorbul's fighting style is defined by relentless, methodical aggression and an almost supernatural capacity to absorb punishment. He climbed [[demogorgon|Demogorgon]] axe-stroke by axe-stroke during the climactic battle at Ladueger's Furrow — one handhold at a time, striking as he climbed, working his way up the Demon Prince's body. Against vampire hawks at [[al-askar|Al Askar]], he took blast after blast of lightning but kept climbing, caught a hawk in mid-air, and fell deliberately to crush it against the stone.

His most defining combat trait is his response to injury. When an ancient Saurian dismembered him with two deft cuts, Snorbul rolled to the ground screaming — in rage, not surrender. Even losing limbs, rage is his answer.

## Blingdenstone

Snorbul is a hero of [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]]. When the party arrived, his reputation preceded him — time in the fighting pits was well known among Svirfneblin, and watching him fight was everything they hoped for. He enforced dignity for the wererat newcomers after the [[battle-of-blingdenstone|Battle of Blingdenstone]], hurling a Deep Gnome who spat on them ten feet across the room into a table and declaring that anyone who showed them disrespect would answer to the new Wardens.

His cousin Yuri's corruption — staring at him with blank, empty eyes — showed how the demonic incursion affected him on a family level. In a dreamscape, his uncle Gruttag reported Blingdenstone was tested every day, people were lost daily, and some were acting strangely — distant and quiet, as if something were working on them.

## Snorbul's Hole

[[snorbuls-hole|Snorbul's Hole]] is a labyrinthine complex of underground tunnels and chambers. At surface level, it is an unassuming hole in the ground surrounded by piles of dirt and stones. Underground, it is a bustling hive of activity centred around spectated martial competitions — some with strict rules, others no-holds-barred cage matches. The denizens are a famously rowdy crowd, and gambling is encouraged.
