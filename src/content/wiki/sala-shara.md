Sala Shara is the Keeper of the Vault of Sages, Silverymoon's great library and archive. A human woman of formidable memory and meticulous habits, she was one of Theodemir's five acolytes who participated in the conspiracy to entrust the Eye of Aren to the Illithid Elder Brain Nostalgia for Infinity.

As a young woman, Sala was the most cautious of the acolytes — she argued for hiding the Eye in the Vault of Sages under permanent guard and was the last to agree to Fëanon's radical proposal. She extracted a promise from the others that the arrangement would be reviewed every decade, and catalogued everything she witnessed in the hive with grim precision.

During the Elder Brain's betrayal, Sala dragged the incapacitated Orla Pyntle toward the exit, helping the acolytes escape the hive. She was experienced through the memory simulation by Buumi, who could still feel phantom ink stains on his hands after waking.

In the decades since, Sala rose to become the Keeper of the Vault of Sages, using her position to monitor any intelligence related to Nostalgia for Infinity and the Eye. She assisted the party in locating the hive by cross-referencing the geological and hydrological fragments recovered from the memory simulation with the Vault's extensive cartographic archives.
