Ishin is the senior [[crimson-dawn-knights|Crimson Dawn Knight]] commanding the household guard of [[lord-aimu|Lord Aimu]] at the [[aimu-estate|Aimu Estate]] in [[frostmoore-vale|Frostmoore Vale]]. He is plate-clad to the gorget and carries himself with the unhurried authority of a man who served before he pledged — the look, [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] noted on first sight, of someone whose patience for [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s usual bluffs was already thin.

When the group arrived at the estate at sundown — having been kept on the porch through a peephole-guarded door while their bluff of being a troupe of travelling performers was deliberated — it was Ishin who opened the door, in formation with five other knights of the Order. He carried a brass spyglass and used it to take stock of the situation on the mountainside above the valley. He recognised [[lady-isabel|Lady Isabel]] of [[helioglabulus|Helios]] at distance and could not reconcile what he was seeing — the merchant socialite of the capital was, by his certain knowledge, not the sort of woman who put her shoulder through tree trunks.

He gave the order to defend the estate and the chapel as the sun dipped below the ridge, and his Knights drew enchanted blades and ornate bows in good order. Whatever his deeper loyalties to Lord Aimu's household and to the [[ashbringer|Sunsword]] that sat on the chapel altar, Ishin had his men under discipline and his head clear under pressure.
