Shuushar is a kuo-toa who introduces himself to the party as a pacifist, a remarkable rarity among his violent and superstitious kind. Known as Shuushar the Awakened, he accompanies the Myconid companion Toadstool and guided the party toward the Darklake and his people's settlement at Sloobludop after their escape from Velkynvelve. Despite the chaos erupting around him, Shuushar maintained his commitment to nonviolence, preferring to stand and watch during combat encounters.

During the demon attack on Sloobludop, Shuushar rallied his fellow kuo-toa around the Idol of the Sea Mother, leading them in prayer. The sanctuary spell emanating from their meditation pushed back an attacking demon. Shuushar also demonstrated keen perception, seeing through an Incubus disguised as a kuo-toa that was manipulating [[ront|Ront]]. After the battle, he thanked [[doc|Doc]] for her Plant Growth spell that would enrich the local ecosystem, showing his connection to his community's wellbeing.

After Sloobludop, Shuushar continued with the group as guide across the [[darklake|Darklake]], traveling with them on [[hemeth|Hemeth]]'s Duergar barge. His knowledge of the underground sea proved as essential on the water as it had been on the approach — the Darklake is not a single body of water but an interconnected network of caverns, currents, underground rivers, and vast lightless chambers, and Shuushar navigated it with the fluency of someone who grew up on its shores. The weeks-long passage through the Duergar locks, past Illithid territories, and into the approaches to [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] drew on his experience at every stage. During the journey, he spoke with [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] about his meditation practice, and [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] expressed genuine interest in learning from him.
