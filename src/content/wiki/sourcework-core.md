Sourcework Cores are the power plants of ancient Saurian technology — crystalline energy sources capable of fuelling everything from small automatons to city-sized Colossi. They range in size from fist-sized units found in Flesh Golems and experimental implants to beach-ball-sized power plants extracted from war machines.

The Lady in White's experiments in the Machina Factory involved implanting Sourcework Cores into living subjects. The results were invariably fatal — the cores proved violently unstable when disturbed, detonating with enough force to disintegrate the host. At least two of her subjects died this way: a silver-skinned man who exploded spontaneously, and Talley, who was killed when Gundren attempted to surgically remove the core from her heart.

Larger Sourcework Cores power the most impressive Saurian constructs. The party recovered a massive core from an ancient Saurian Colossus in the Factory's foundry, and Gundren Rockseeker has shown particular interest in understanding and repurposing the technology.
