The Dragonflight pauses to take stock. **Ares** roars somewhere ahead, but the Tornado behind them has kept suspicious pace — shepherding rather than following.

**Asha** peers into the churning walls of cloud and spots fiendish shapes moving within. She calls it out and the dragons attack, tearing Oni from the winds and revealing several of the creatures lurking in ambush. **Fellasin** climbs above the storm to scout from altitude — the eye is not far, still obscured by mist, but reachable. More troublingly, he counts eight tornados arrayed around the storm's circumference, each pulsing with Weather-changing magic of terrific potency. He dives to add his breath to the assault below.

The Oni fall quickly. The dragons force their way through the howling winds into the interior of the twister — and find what lurks at its heart.

A great serpentine tendril of undead flesh and cadavers rises hundreds of feet from the pinnacle of a Black Crystal Ziggurat crawling along the ground far below, propelled by thousands of grasping cadaverous limbs. The meat at the tendril's apex undulates and shapes itself into a skeletal face, jaw opening to speak. Words of Power and blasts of lightning and cold hammer the dragons; **Asha** senses that this thing is tethered through the planes themselves — reaching back to the Shadowfell. Half the dragons dive on the Ziggurat's base while the rest harry the tendril, trading devastating blows with the lurching horror.

**Althul** strikes with holy power and banishes the creature. The Dragonflight quickly assembles, ready to destroy the thing the moment it returns. It does not. The more magically minded among them suspect a Planeshift — the creature escaped its banishment into another plane rather than return to their waiting claws.

Where it went, they cannot say. Where **Ares** went is equally unclear — and it is suspicious that an Ancient Red Dragon would not seize the moment to strike while the Dragonflight was distracted.

Already near the ground, the dragons make a decision. They will borrow Ares's element. **Fellasin** leads the dive into the soft summer tundra and they burrow north, determined to catch the Ancient Red unawares from below. As they move, **Fellasin** can feel the storm overhead — the weight of its potent magic pressing down — and senses the moment they pass beneath the Eye. The wind above them stops.

**Asha** shapeshifts into a small burrowing Molebear and pushes up to investigate.

She surfaces amid an innumerable host of lesser undead, all prostrate and facing toward the epicenter. Two hundred feet above, hovering before the storm-wall, floats **Ares**. Two red-robed humanoids fly out to meet him from a vast Castle of black ice drifting above the ruins of a city at the Eye's center — the same ruins from their earlier scrying of **Themberchaud**. A sour voice echoes within the cylinder of the storm, chanting a dark ritual. The overwhelming scent of blood rises from the wreckage below. **Asha** returns to the others.

There is no subtlety left. They will strike hard and fast, pray that **Themberchaud** remains in the ruins, and reach him before the forces here can be properly marshalled.

They erupt from the tundra directly beneath **Ares**, racing skyward. The Ancient Red is surprised by the angle — but not entirely unprepared. The two red-robed humanoids are knocked from the sky almost immediately, their life-saving contingency magics flashing as they absorb the brunt of the assault and vanish. Surrounding **Ares** high above the plain, the Dragonflight finally sees the full extent of what they have walked into.

A Red Greatwyrm — easily the size of The Father — stands amid the ruins below, its claws resting against the prostrate neck of **Themberchaud**, simmering flames wreathing a gleaming gold exoskeleton. Several Efreeti float in attendance. Nearby, arranged in a ritual ring among the corpses, seven giant Oni raise their arms to the sky in chant, hundreds of humanoid bodyparts stacked before them in a slow, grotesque orbit. Where an eighth Oni should stand instead floats a monstrous abomination of grafted limbs, wielding dozens of Arcane Lances of varying scale. Lines of prisoners thread through the city under the watch of floating red-robed spellcasters, herded toward a vortex of blood and flesh that feeds the orbit of bodyparts around the Oni.

**Fellasin** moves first. A powerful domination spell finds purchase in **Ares**'s mind and holds. The Blue dragon pulls two pieces of intelligence from the Ancient Red's thoughts: the name **Zhengyi** — the Witch King — and the name of the Red Greatwyrm below: **Vulkan**.

A new shape enters the sky — previously eclipsed by the Castle — a great golden Orb that begins to move, slowly at first. And from the west, an Ancient Black Dragon in the same exoskeleton as **Ares** descends through the clouds, changes course upon spotting the foreign dragons, and moves to intercept. **Vulkan** roars and leaps into the air in a blaze, streaking toward the Dragonflight with his Efreeti retinue.

The ritual reaches its climax. A prisoner is lifted screaming from the city and shepherded by red-robed spellcasters up into the great Iron Bell at the Castle's highest tower. The Bell swings. The humanoid is obliterated in a spray of gore. Energy from the Bell focuses through the ritual and detonates around one of the Oni — all the orbiting bodyparts are drawn to it in a hideous burst, grafted to its flesh in an instant. The new abomination takes to the air, rising slowly toward the Dragonflight, Arcane Lances in its many hands.

A thundercrack — a Black Crystal Ziggurat tears into existence outside the city before being swallowed by a tornado, its tendril beginning to grow. Another crack. Another. More Ziggurats rip into view around them.

A figure steps onto a balcony of the dark Castle, three glowing orbs orbiting him. His voice booms across the plain.

*"DRIVE THE CHROMATICS TO ME. KILL THE REST."*

An impossibly long lance of energy erupts from the spellcaster's outstretched finger and strikes **Vakuun**, dominating the Red Dragon in an instant. The dominated **Vakuun** and the already-dominated **Ares** immediately turn on one another — then a second lance re-asserts the spellcaster's control over **Ares** and both Red Dragons turn on the Dragons of Shou Lung together.

**Althul** roars a prayer to Bahamut. The Platinum Dragon does not answer. Tiamat does. A flash of rainbow light and the scent of sulfur — one of **Zhengyi**'s three orbiting orbs tears free and appears in the Gold Dragon's hand. As it does, **Althul**'s strength gutters out beneath him, and the mocking laughter of the Chromatic Goddess fills his mind. She expects him to die for this gift. A fitting sacrifice.

**Fellasin** catches the falling Gold Dragon. **Althul** shifts into a small Hawk and clings to the Blue's scales as **Fellasin** turns invisible.

**Vulkan** looses a pulse of fire that erupts in small explosions across the sky — carefully placed to spare the dominated Reds while scorching everything else. The golden Orb picks up speed. A black-cloaked figure clambers onto its surface, and then both vanish together. Lightning crackles through the Tornado-Ziggurats as they begin to close in from all sides.

**Asha** dives into the undead army below, slaughtering everything within reach, until the awakening horde turns on her. She shifts back into the Molebear and disappears into the churned earth of their tunnel.

The Dragonflight is loathe to leave **Themberchaud** behind. But the forces arrayed against them are overwhelming and they have stolen something of real power from **Zhengyi** with divine help. They fly hard into the storm — eight dragons of Shou Lung fleeing with a stolen Orb of Dragonkind into the howling dark.
