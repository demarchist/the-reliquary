## The Beholder-Alghouls

[[Aliyah Faen Tlabbar|Patches]] descended the stairs first, finding a mouldering barracks that reeked of Mezzoloth. The fiends had abandoned the place, but a shattered Mezzoloth statue suggested something far worse — a Medusa or Gorgon had been through. A perfectly circular hole in one wall had allowed mud to slide in and pile up. She reported back, and [[Tarkathee Daru|Tark]] returned with her to investigate while the others held the stairs.

In the barracks, Patches spotted something lurking in a dark corner. They turned to look — and in doing so, turned their backs on the mud-filled hole.

Two Alghouls attacked. These were unlike any they had encountered before. Covered in eyes — extra orbs encrusting their heads and tipping strange tendrils — they unleashed a devastating array of energy beams. Some rays disintegrated the stone around them.

Tark was mauled viciously, forced to dodge beam after beam. Patches dropped an orb of Darkness on one Alghoul, blinding it. The rest of the group rushed down the stairs to help.

The wounded Alghoul smelled blood and pressed its attack. Tark went down.

Flanked by two of the abominations, the situation was dire. [[Cirrus Al-Kayan|Cirrus]] banished one. Patches gutted the other, but its acidic blood sprayed across the room — and across Tark, who screamed. [[Feanon Serinde|Feanon]] seized him with Telekinesis and dragged him clear. Cirrus healed the monk.

The group rallied, taking eyeblast after eyeblast as they destroyed the remaining Alghoul. Feanon returned the favour with a Disintegration beam of his own. [[Gwen]] was badly damaged in the fighting.

When the banished Alghoul reappeared, the group was ready. They unleashed everything they had and tore it apart.

## The Lady in White

The group barricaded themselves in the barracks for a rest, reactivating the ferrofluid trap above for security.

During the respite, Feanon scried on the owner of the flesh sample he had cut from the clone. He saw a thin, tall woman draped in white robes, a white veil covering her face, and a wreath of white roses at her brow. She floated amongst the clouds high above the [[Machina Factory]], one hand raised, fingers poised mid-snap.

She did not move. She did not snap. But she was ready.

Feanon ended the scrying and told the group what he had seen. The [[Lady in White]] was the artificer who had run this facility — and she hovered above them, waiting to detonate her bombs.

## Talley

As they healed and rested, [[Talley]] reappeared from her invisibility, sweating and pale. Something was clearly wrong with her.

The group tended to her, and she recovered enough to continue. They pushed deeper into the Factory, hoping to find the Lady's medical facilities.

They found a T-junction leading to a strange crystal-coated chamber with a metal mesh floor and scattered metal detritus. Not an infirmary. They pushed on, finding a room with bloodstained beds and piled clothing — closer, but still not what they needed.

Patches returned from scouting. She had found the surgery room.

Talley was deteriorating rapidly. They rushed her through the ancient stone hallways to a massive metal door that folded in on itself when unlocked. Inside, everything was steel — a steel operating table gleaming beneath a large crystal suspended from the ceiling. Medical tools lay scattered about, and among them, a book.

They got Talley on the table. [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] began organising the instruments and grabbed the book — which cursed him on contact, compelling him to read. He flipped through until he found a page showing exactly what was needed: a detailed anatomical dissection of a human chest cavity. The group noticed the compulsion and broke him free of it.

The surgery began. They opened Talley's chest and found what they feared — Machina implants threaded throughout her body, all connected to a [[Sourcework Core]] embedded in her heart.

[[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] worked carefully, separating the core from the surrounding implants. He lifted the device free and reached for the Bag of Devouring to contain it —

The core detonated.

Talley was killed instantly. The group picked themselves up from the wreckage, bloodied and heartsick. Another life they could not save.

## The Stairwell

Patches had been keeping watch in the hallway during the surgery. She found herself drawn to a stairwell leading down to the next level — a void-like darkness looming in the passage. Her feet carried her toward it involuntarily before [[Horatio Von Bumidere|Buumi]] came out to ask her a question, snapping her out of it.

Unsettled, she led Buumi back into the surgery room. Whatever lurked in that stairwell would have to wait.
