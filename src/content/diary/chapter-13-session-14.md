With the front door closed again, the group heads to the stairs down to the lower levels.

Patches descends first, discovering a moldering barracks that smells like the foul Mezzoloths.

The place is deserted of fiends, but a shattered Mezzoloth statue makes her think there may be a Medusa or Gorgon.

A strangely perfect hole can also be seen in the wall, through which mud has slid and piled up.

She informs the group and Tark returns with her to check the place.  The group readies themselves on the stairs in case of an attack.

In the Barracks, Patches spots something in one of the corners of the room just as Tark cautiously moves in to investigate the too-perfect hole.

Patches points out the dark corner, and they *know* something is hiding there.

As they both turn to look into the corner, they turn their backs on the mudslide and the too-perfect hole, and something shifts the mud, leaping from hiding.

Two Alghouls attack, one in ambush.

They are covered in eyes, with extra orbs covering their heads and even tipping the ends of strange tendrils hanging from their heads.

From the eyes comes a destructive array of energy blasts and rays - some disintegrating the stone around them.

Tark is mauled badly and forced to dodge a series of eyeblasts

Patches drops an orb of Darkness on one Alghoul, blinding it and preventing it from shooting its eyebeams.

The rest of the group then rushes down the stairs to assist their friends.

Tark is horribly wounded and the Alghoul, smelling blood, howls and presses its attack.

Tark is taken to the ground.

Flanked by powerful Alghoul abominations, things are not looking good until Cirrus banishes one.

Patches sees Tark go down and rushes to assist, veritably gutting the Alghoul, but sprays the area with its acid blood.

Tark screams and thrashes, near death.

Feanon grabs his friend with Telekinesis and gets him out of harm's way just as Cirrus heals the Monk.

The group then rallies, destroying the remaining Alghoul, but taking eye-blast after eyeblast (though Feanon returns the favor with a Disintegration beam).

Gwen is badly damaged in the battle

The group prepares for the other Alghoul to return from its banishment and as it reappears, they unleash their fury on the creature, tearing it apart and blasting the remains.

Those abominations were absolutely deadly and the group barricades themselves in the barracks for a rest, re-activating the trap the level above for some security.

During this short rest, Feanon scries on the owner of the cube of flesh.

He spots a thin, tall woman, covered in white robes with a white veil covering her head and a wreath of white roses. She floats amongst the clouds, high above the Machina factory, hand raised mid finger-snap.  Though she does not move, nor snap her fingers.

It appears to be the same woman from the cloning vat, though it is hard to make out anything underneath the veil.

He watches for a while, seeing nothing but air and the floating Wizard.

Feanon ends the scrying and communicates what he saw to the group.  He thinks that he has scried the Lady in White.  The artificer/wizard who was running this facility and that she floats, high above, ready to snap her fingers and blow them all up.

The group agrees and they wonder what to do about it.

As they heal up and rest, Talley re-appears from her invisibility, sweaty and pale.

Its clear that something is wrong with her.

The group attends to her and she recovers enough to continue through the Factory,

The group pushes forwards in the hope of finding some of The Lady's Medical facilities so they can maybe help the large woman.

They find a T junction leading to a strange, crystal covered chamber with a metal mesh floor and a number of small metal detritus scattered around.

Its not an infirmary, so they ignore it and push on.

They almost find what they are looking for in the next room - bloodstained beds scattered around, clothing piled up in one corner, but its no medical facility

They are just about to give up, tearing up soiled sheets to make bandages, when Patches returns from scouting.

She's found the Surgery room.

Talley is in a bad state at this point and they rush the large woman down the ancient stone hallways to a massive metal door that folds in on itself when unlocked.

Within the chamber, everything is made of steel and a steel table gleams under a large crystal hanging from the ceiling.

Medical tools are scattered about the room and a book can be seen amongst them.

They get Talley on the table and Gundren begins to organize the medical equipment.

In doing so, he grabs the book and is cursed…

Pulled to read the book, Gundren flips through until he finds a page showing exactly what he needs for the surgery - a detailed anatomical dissection of a human chest cavity.

The cursed book is noticed by the attentive group though.

The delicate surgery begins

They cut open Talley and see that she has numerous machina implants throughout her body.

The hub of the implants is a Sourcework core implanted into her heart.

The surgery goes on for a while and Patches moves out to the hall to keep watch.

She finds herself drawn to the hallway to her right, and the stairs down to the next level.

A voidish darkness looms in the stairway and she finds herself involuntarily moving toward it before Buumi comes out to ask her a question.

Feeling uncomfortable, Patches leads Buumi back into the Surgery room.

Gundren manages to separate the core from the implant and just as he's about to place it in the Bag of Devouring Cirrus has, it explodes.

Talley is killed in the blast instantly

The group recovers and looks over the destruction, distraught that they couldn't save yet another…
