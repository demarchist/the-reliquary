The other Drow in the room let out chuckles and the priestess smiles.

The Wizard, Shoor Vandree turns to the Priestess and says he can take care of it from here.

The wizard goes on to offer (in common) Fey leniency and a good posting as a slave in exchange for his help with the spell books he had in his possession.

Outside the Guard tower and near the prison, the group watch as one of the Drow warriors plays with a Deep Gnome chained to the wall, cutting at the poor wretch.

The Gnome tries to defend himself with a steel rod, and does alright for himself but is looking quite bloodied after the Drow's flurry.

Two corpses can be seen splayed on the wall nearby, a female dwarf and a Quaggoth, both eviscerated and infested with Spiderlings as a warning to those held within the slave pen.

Looking around in the prison, Patches spots the younger priestess leave the tower and notes her being a lower rank than Ilvara.

She sees another couple of Quaggoth servants cleaning and patrolling with refreshments.

Feeling anxious, Patches sits down in a quiet corner to meditate and pray to Elistraee, summoning her bead of mercury to help her focus.

As the hours go by, Patches begins to feel a building tension.  

She understands the message: something is coming, an opportunity and Elistraee blesses her to take advantage of it.

JmJm looks over the other prisoners and spots the Myconid sprout; Toadstool

Smiling to himself, he walks over, buck naked.

The little myconid is standing with Shuushar and the large Kuo-toa spots the Bard approach.  The large fishy eyes study the bard.

JmJm and Shuushar talk for a moment and then JmJm picks up the little myconid which puffs out his rapport spores to talk to JmJm.

They communicate and the little myconid likes what it hears.  It agrees to be the Bard's new squire, in exchange that the group takes him home - to the great Mushroom grove.

JmJm puts the little guy down, satisfied at his new acquisition

Gundren meanwhile begins to investigate the weblike bars of the prison.

Seeing no entrance or exit, the Dwarf heads over to Patches and nudges her, asking if she's seen a cage like this before.

Patches stirs from her meditation and informs the Dwarf that she has: It's a standard Slave cage with an antimagic field within.  

The door appears when a noble Drow uses an insignia to command the bars to open.  The isignia could be a ring, necklace or something similar.

Patches remembers the houses she noted in command here:** Mizzryn**, the fourth house and **Vandree**, the seventh of Menzoberranzan.

She then calls the group over to her for some instruction.

As the group gathers around, the other prisoners grow close to listen as best they can without being invasive...

The Derro however, with the stark white hair and red robes is right in the huddle with the rest of the group, and in undercommon, begins to jabber about getting out.

Patches is not having any of it and sics Mr. Pickles on the Derro.

Both small creatures pull tiny knives, one made of flint and one of a sharpened claw and begin to go at it, knifing one another a circling.

JmJm begins to yell "PRISON RIOT!" but is quickly silenced by Patches as she decks him with a punch.

JmJm looks sullen and goes quiet...and suddenly yells "RIOOOOT!!!"

Patches says to the others that the story to stick to is a variant of the truth: They are down in the Underdark to escort their priestly friend Cirrus to Gracksuluth for religious reasons.

As Patches tells the others the "Story" and JmJm yells out to start a riot, the Drow outside battling the Svirfneblin have both stopped and watch the craziness beginning inside the slave pen: Two small folk having a knife fight and a naked Half Elf screaming to start a larger fight... The Drow shoots his hand crossbow into the pen.

Toadstool feels the riot beginning and rushes over to one of the Gnome twins, bumping into him...harshly.

The Orc begins to bellow and rage at the bars until Sarith, the Drow leaps on his back and begins attacking him.

Shuushar does nothing but stand and watch.

Gundren tries to calm everyone down for a moment, surprising everyone.

Doc B decides to use the moment as a distraction, finding the largest hole in the bars and goes over to see if he can squeeze out.

As he tests by poking his head out the bars, the fired crossbow dart skips off Doc's skull, cutting him.

The drow shakes his head at the Cat-Gnome and then unchains Snorbul, the Svirfneblin he was fighting and shoves him toward the cage.

seeing his chance, Snorbul swings at the Drow with his metal bar.

The Drow deftly parries and ripostes in a flurry, cutting and pummeling the Gnome into submission.  

He picks up the body and throws it toward to bars of the Slave Pens

Far Across the Underdark, the rescue group is wrapping up their long rest, allowing Megno to memorize his spells.

Buumi hands Cirrus a written letter from Li Tan detailing the goings on in the Barony.

The letter says that mages from Silverymoon have been assigned to speed the construction of the keep, all the buildings have laid foundations and construction is going well.

It also mentions that the War seems to have stalled as the Balduran Navy managed to win their engagement and the Dale-Lands have thrown in with the North, giving the aggressive Amnians and Thayans pause.

No one thinks the pause will last, but it is giving the north time to collect themselves.

The final part of the letter says that the Teleport circle being made at the Barony will be operational in 1 week.

The rescue party decides to start heading out, Buumi mentioning that he can only see about 15ft without light.

Cirrus offers to hold his hand...he accepts.

Megno holds up a small opal and begins to cast Locate the Path.

A small beam of light shows him the direction and the group begins to follow, Tark taking Point but keeping darkness wrapped around them with Pass without Trace.

Cirrus begins to relay Patches rules of the Underdark to Megno and Buumi and gets it moooostly right...

They head out and quickly make it back to the crossroads.  Looking into the cavern, they see the carnage of the fight - dead Orcs and Goblins scattered.

Three large, grublike creatures can be seen eating the corpses: Carrion Crawlers.

Remembering that their mucus is a powerful paralytic, they decide to try and kill one and harvest its mucus glands.

They manage to get close before the Crawlers get spooked and start to flee the cavern, but the group springs their ambush.

They make quick work of the Crawler and Tark looks over the ruined corpse.

With his small knife, he manages to extract one of the glands and tie off the ends, securing it for later use.

Following Megno's guidance, they resume their journey.

The unconscious form of Snorbul flies through the air at the Slave pen and the Drow warrior clutches at his chest.

The bars spiral outward, creating an opening as the body falls in.

Doc B leaps out of the hole as it closes and then leaps atop the surprised Drow, grabbing down the front of his armor.

The Drow comes across with a flurry of attacks, hitting Doc badly and the little Druid falls to the floor...clutching the Drow's Insignia.

Thinking quickly, Doc B calls out to the feywilds and finds a creature to answer his call.  He brings forth a giant Scorpion.

The huge arachnid rushes forward, catching the Drow off guard.

The Drow parries both pincers but takes the stinger in the shoulder, poison flooding his system.

Patches and Gundren start pelting the warrior with rocks to distract and give Doc an opening.

Seeing his opportunity, Doc turns and throws the Insignia into the cage...

As he goes, Doc tries to slow his descent, doing a decent job until the last 100ft and tumbling the rest of the way.

Doc hits the water and plunges into the depths, alive, but hurt.

A voice calls into his head: "Flesh for the Faceless Lord!"

JmJm saunters up to the bars and engages the wizard.

Following the Drow, JmJm heads into the guard tower and sees Fey chained up and bleeding.

The Wizard leaves the guard tower, taking the bridge over to the other main Stalactite tower and into the Shrine of Lolth.

Inside, JmJm sees the younger priestess attending the shrine - a place of stone bleachers and pillows arrayed around a large carved statue of a half Drow, half spider.

still they continue, heading down another trap door to the chamber below, a more modest bed chamber belonging to the Wizard.

Far below Velkynvelve, Doc B rushes from the water, desperate to outdistance the Grey Ooze swimming his way.
