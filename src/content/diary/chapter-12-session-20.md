## The Battle for the Forge

[[Horatio Von Bumidere|Buumi]] and [[Jack Mayer-Johnson Mathews|JmJm]] called out to the [[Cult of Zalaam|cult]] leader, taunting him and proclaiming it was the cult who would fail this day. The bluff bought the rest of the group — and [[Aliyah Faen Tlabbar|Patches]], high on the cavern walls — a few more moments to get into position.

The cult leader threw forth his hand, and the battle began in a volley of [[Saurian Firelance|firelance]] fire.

[[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] leapt from cover and unloaded on the cult leader with his own firelance. The cultist crushed a glowing runestone, raising a magical shield that deflected some of the shots. Buumi and JmJm rushed forward under defensive spells. [[Feanon Serinde|Feanon]] cast haste on [[Snorbul]], who rocketed forward on his broom and launched himself from the sky directly onto the cult leader.

Many of the cultists crushed their own defensive runestones — shields, blurs, enchantments — but the most concerning move came from a single cultist who raised a bone whistle to his lips and blew.

The Ruby Dragon cried out a telepathic warning: the cult had a horde of alghouls.

Howls and screams echoed through the forge as monstrosities climbed from cracks, pipes, and every opening — half a dozen adults and over a dozen hatchlings. [[Tarkathee Daru|Tark]] pulled his own bone whistle from a pouch and blew. The alghouls turned as one and gave chase, all but two already engaged with Buumi, JmJm, and [[Thorna]].

## The Crucible

Buumi's hammer began to glow with blinding radiance as he waded into the cultists. Feanon contacted Tark telepathically: *"Draw them to the edge of the lava."* JmJm summoned a Magical Hand construct and shoved an alghoul adult into the molten stone, telling Thorna she could thank him later.

[[Cirrus Al-Kayan|Cirrus]] summoned a radiant celestial — a glowing winged angel with sweeping horns that loosed arrow after arrow into the cult leader. The cultists focused their firelances on the celestial and destroyed it in a concentrated volley, but the cult leader was reeling, driven back by Snorbul, Gundren, and the relentless arrows.

The cult leader attempted a charm spell. Feanon counterspelled it without hesitation. The leader fell back further, desperate for time he would not get.

Tark, beset on all sides by slashing alghouls, shadow-stepped up to Snorbul's abandoned flying broom sixty feet in the air, repositioned to the arena's edge, and leapt down. Feanon created a bridge of ice and flanking walls to funnel the charging horde toward the monk, then released a fireball into the massed enemies.

High above, Patches swung from chain to chain across the cavern ceiling. The heat and light threw off her distance — she plummeted ninety feet to the forge's lower level, barely saving herself from a lava pool. Below her lay the Forge itself: a massive wrought-alloy staircase descending into channels of bubbling lava, a huge mould chamber, steam vents, and the great forge sitting above a pool of magma — angular, alien, ancient, and transcending mortal craft. She rushed for the stairs to rejoin the battle above.

Thorna called down an ice storm that pulverised most of the alghoul hatchlings and turned the ground treacherous. JmJm's Magical Hand flew among the cultists, grabbing and smashing. Snorbul swung the killing blow on the cult leader and, knowing the cult's regenerative powers, hacked the head clean from the shoulders. He picked it up and teleported — surprising everyone — to Tark's side at the ice gateway.

## Death is Only the Beginning

The surviving cultists and alghouls were funnelled toward Tark and Snorbul, who retreated across Feanon's ice bridge. As the alghouls rushed across, Feanon dispelled the bridge and created a singularity — three adult alghouls and several hatchlings were dragged over the lava and dropped screaming to a fiery death.

Then the severed head in Snorbul's hand spoke.

*"Death is only the beginning."*

A long black tongue adorned with gold piercings lashed from the mouth like a python, wrapping around Snorbul's arm. Corruption spread from the contact, turning his skin dark and making him retch.

Cirrus threw powerful healing spells to keep the wounded fighting. Feanon disintegrated another adult alghoul. And Patches reached the chained Ruby Dragon.

She poured every ounce of her blasting powder into the locks and detonated them with her fire bombs. The shackles shattered.

The Ruby Dragon roared — a sound that shook the cavern. It swiped one cultist dead with a single blow. Its glittering ruby-crystal eyes blazed with psionic light, and the remaining cultists' heads exploded in showers of gore.

## The Dragon's Gift

With the last of the Black Skull and their alghouls destroyed, the group stood down and looked up at the massive crystalline dragon towering above them.

*"Thank you, strangers,"* its voice resonated in their minds. *"You came to me in my time of need and set me free from these vermin. I am exhausted and wish no harm to you. I assume you are here for the Forge? The heart of it lies below — take the grand staircase down and experience the wonder of the Titans."*

The dragon's crystals sparkled with vibrant red psionic energy as it shapeshifted into a large male fire genasi — pitch-black scleras, glittering ruby irises. It began to walk away from its site of torture, then stopped and turned. Its eyes locked on Tark. Feanon could feel the dragon's psionics reaching out, reading the monk's chi.

The dragon approached Tark and spoke aloud: *"I can sense the aura of a crystal dragon within your chi, little one. Have you met one of my kind before?"*
