## The Boneclaw Assault

The Boneclaws rushed forward as the [[Saurians|Saurian]] Death Lord advanced behind them. [[Thorna]] summoned a stormcloud over the arena. [[Horatio Von Bumidere|Buumi]]'s holy nimbus began to glow.

[[Tarkathee Daru|Tark]] struck through two of the Death Lord's three illusionary duplicates in rapid succession, but the ancient lord retaliated with a bejewelled, curving blade that left trails of crackling dark energy. The sword cut Tark viciously — then the Death Lord slipped into shadow and reappeared beside [[Feanon Serinde|Feanon]].

In Common, the Death Lord told Feanon to abandon his friends and join [[Vecna]]. This was his only chance. Then the lord slipped into shadow again and rematerialised in melee with Tark.

Feanon gave the offer no thought and responded with a spell, noting the Death Lord's active enchantments — Mirror Image and Haste.

With the Death Lord's teleportation had come a host of other undead. Over two dozen skeletons and more Banshees moved to flank and surround. A Boneclaw caught [[Jack Mayer-Johnson Mathews|JmJm]]'s attention — the bard drove it to the lava's edge, but it teleported away in a burst of shadow and necrotic energy, reappearing in the temple's centre. A nearby Banshee let loose its wail and JmJm dropped instantly, unconscious. Healing spells brought him back moments later, including a regeneration from Thorna.

Buumi called out a warning: the Boneclaws were deadliest in darkness. His holy nimbus could not cover enough of the temple with light. He enacted an Aura of Life to counter the waves of necrotic energy.

## The Big Guns

[[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]], JmJm, and Thorna fought the flanking undead outside the temple. Gundren decided it was time for the heavy arsenal. He pulled out the [[Thundercannon]], summoned [[Arkto]], and shouted to [[Aliyah Faen Tlabbar|Patches]]: *"Let the Cat outta the Bag!"*

Inside the temple, Patches reached for her [[Bag of Holding]] and released Gwenwhyvar. The mechanical lion landed near Tark and discharged a blast of lightning that shattered a swath of advancing skeletons. But the Death Lord raised his gauntleted hand — the purple sourcestone erupted with light, and the fallen skeletons re-knit themselves and rose again.

Gundren flew up on Arkto and lobbed a Daylight gemstone from his Helm of Brilliance into the temple, flooding it with radiance that crippled the Boneclaws' shadow-teleportation.

[[Snorbul]], Patches, Buumi, and Tark converged on the Death Lord and his Boneclaws in the temple's centre, turning it into a chaotic melee. As spells intensified, the unstable Weave reacted badly — a rift tore open and two massive metal spiders walked through from the [[Shadowfell]]. Retrievers.

[[Cirrus Al-Kayan|Cirrus]] and Thorna each banished one, buying time. [[Jareem]], in his genasi form, stepped forward and raised a wall of fire to cut off the flanking undead, then joined the battle.

The group destroyed two Boneclaws under the bright light, but the Death Lord and the remaining two retaliated savagely. Then another rift — this one blazing with vermillion light.

Three massive forms stepped through. Lizard-like, with oversized jaws and feathered hides, their crystalline protrusions glinting red in the lava-light. Fae-touched Tyrannosaurus Rexes. They fixated on the nearest undead, and their massive jaws descended on the surviving Boneclaws.
