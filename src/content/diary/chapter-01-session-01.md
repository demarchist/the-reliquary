 Game began taking goods from Neverwinter to Phandalin town to Barthen's Provisions (2nd brother) for dwarf brothers: GUNDREN ROCKSEEKER.

 went on ahead with SILDAR a hired hand but good friend (Gauntlet Agent).

- along TRIBOAR TRAIL, south of neverwinter. Find signs of ambush.  Attacked by goblins.  All goblins killed, one captured by Jack Meyer Jhonson Matthews.

- find CRAGMAW hideout, kill sentries.

- scout inside, befriend wolves, find klarg the bugbear

- party splits up.

- sentry assassinated

- men go kill all the goblins - noose and magehand the chief, Jedd stabbed in both legs.

- women come to the rescue

- SILDAR saved.

- sneak down chute to suprise Klarg

- charm klarg

- convince Klarg you work for BLACK SPIDER, his boss, he carried his loot out for you, give you a Goblin slave

- SILDAR tells about Wave Echo Cave and Dwarf boss kidnap at Cragmaw castle, no clue where that is.

- Arrive at Phandalin, frontier town, 40-45 buildings, built atop older ruins. Bandit trouble in town: REDBRAND RUFFIANS

- SILDAR heads to the Stonehill Inn to rest, then look for his missing wizard friend IARNO. Sets up a meeting with TOWNMASTER HARBIN WESTER

- Go to Barthen's Provisions and meet BARTHEN, tell him his brother is missing.  Offers to pay 150gp for his brothers return. Loans the wagon and Oxen

- Headed to the other store, Lionshield Coster, met LINENE GREYWIND (mining goods and provisions) to return stolen goods, get paid for it.

- Headed to the Stonehill Inn and convinced TOBLEN STONEHILL, they were bigshots and the bard played for the inn, getting free room and board.

-  Overheard various rumors, followed up on the Halfling child finding a secret tunnel into the Redbrand Ruffian hideout.

- Head to halfling, question child, find tunnel into TRESENDAR MANOR

- Head into secret passage way, enter into cavern beneath manor

- Fight and Kill Nothic, receive loot

- go down side passage, discover room with Bandits, room with Bugbears. Lure both out.

- Lightning blast tunnel with a scroll, kill enemies.

- Unconcious goblin, carry with us - too dumb to know location of Echo Cave or Cragmaw

- Find alchemy chamber, loot thoroughly, Rat Familiar escapes and warns Glasstaff, Bandit Mage leader

- Receive book written in Dwarvish detailing the history and purpose of wave echo cave and mentioning the mace Lightbringer (of Llathander, lost in cave).

- Find Glasstaffs room, loot it, but notice secret passage open

- run to catch Glasstaff

- Patches sniper shots Glasstaff in the knee (critical hit with intent to cripple), group captures him

- Receive his loot (staff)

- Glasstaff is IARNO, SILDARS friend.

- a former member of the Lords Alliance, Iarno seized power for money

- originally supposed to set up a constabulary in town

- instead formed the REDBRAND RUFFIANS to fleece the town

- Was contacted by Black Spider through Noble connections in Neverwinter

- Signed on to help the Drow agent

- tells players that Black Spider is Drow

- Spider sent bugbears to help Iarno, Bugbears knew way to Echo cave, dead now

- Black Spider is in Echo Cave searching for Spellforge

- None of the lords know of Iarno's betrayal (or so he claims - Zhentarim storyline)

- Take Iarno and unconcious Goblin back into town to see the Townmaster

- Redbrands on their way back to the hideout come across the party

- Are convinced to flee seeing their boss as a captive and hearing all their companions were killed.

- Party meets with the Townmaster, takes IARNO into custody, sends to inform SILDAR of what happened

- Party retires to the Inn, awaiting a meeting later that day/night with the Townmaster to talk about what happened.
