## Retaliation

*In the morning, the group heads east—*

Or not.

[[Cirrus Al-Kayan|Cirrus]] was interrupted from his quiet, fervent yodelling by awareness of a threat — an entity moving at speed toward the town from high in the sky. He raced to the roof, certain the dragon had returned. But the entity swerved sharply north and left the area.

Moments later, a series of impacts shattered the pre-dawn calm across [[Selunia]].

[[Aliyah Faen Tlabbar|Patches]] bolted from [[The Establishment]]. [[Jack Mayer-Johnson Mathews|JmJm]] slowly started to awaken. [[Feanon Serinde|Feanon]] scried from [[Feanon's Tower|his tower]] and headed to town.

Patches reached the nearest impact site. A splatter of gore covered the street — enough material for several people, having hit the ground at terminal velocity. The mess had taken out a nearby structure wall.

[[Doc]] pointed out that one particular splatter consisted of someone *dropped* and someone *hit* — [[Ancalagon]] was snatching people from the frontier and dropping them on the town.

The group agreed: this dragon needed to be dealt with.

JmJm pointed out that if Ancalagon was abducting people to the north, he was likely not at home. Cirrus thought he knew where the dragon laired. Doc cast Wind Walk and they rushed south.

## The Lair

They reached the Withered Hill. The goblin survivors guarding the place scattered when Cirrus polymorphed into an adult black dragon and melted a few for good measure. Patches sniped the holdouts peeking from the ruined tower.

Cirrus scouted the surrounding waterways and found a deep crevasse with a spring feeding [[The Bog]] from below. The passage led beneath the hill but was uncomfortably narrow for a dragon.

Patches dived in. She used her new Matron abilities to Alter Self — fingers webbing, feet elongating, eyes filming over, ears forking into gills. She swam through the submerged tunnels, following massive claw marks in the stone until she surfaced in a dry chamber tangled with roots and vines. Glistening venomous needles unfolded toward her. She submerged and returned.

Cirrus, still in dragon form, swam back with Patches. The others followed through the [[Ethereal Plane]] astride [[Steden]]. Cirrus squeezed through the tight confines and surfaced in the vine chamber. The plants lunged at him — strong enough to give a dragon trouble. He ripped them apart.

Patches approached. Cirrus looked down at his companion and felt a powerful urge to rip her apart and feast on her flesh. Disturbed, he ended the polymorph.

In the Ethereal Plane, the rest of the group was beset by ghosts and shades. They rushed through the haunted tunnels and dived into the material chamber — but a ghost came with them, possessing Feanon. They banished the spirit quickly.

Patches scouted ahead, disguising herself as a goblin servant. The slaves — all scarred with acid burns — paid her no notice. She mapped the complex: a cache of eggs, a barracks, and the main lair — a bowl-shaped room beneath an ancient stone dome, excavated into a massive nest.

The nest was packed with gold, silver, gemstones, and items of every sort. Two adult black dragons lay curled atop the hoard, wyrmlings sleeping nearby. An iron cage across the chamber held several humanoid prisoners.

One dragon's nostrils flared. It twitched in its sleep.

Patches retreated.

The plan was elegant: bomb the eggs to lure the dragons out, charge into the treasure room, collapse the entrances, open a demiplane, and shovel as much gold inside as physically possible before teleporting home.

Patches set the charges. The dragons bolted awake, roaring, and tore their way toward their eggs. The group dashed into the lair. JmJm's Arcane Hand scooped treasure into the demiplane in great fistfuls. They opened the iron cage and freed a handful of prisoners kidnapped from nearby roads. Cirrus popped Eldritch Blasts into the tunnels, collapsing them.

The dragons returned fast, roaring with fury. Clawed hands gouged the stone of the remaining entrance. A black horned skull-like head snaked into the chamber, maw agape, dripping acid.

Feanon teleported the group away in a flash of light.
