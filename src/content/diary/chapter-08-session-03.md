## Hulk Smash

Three massive forms rushed into the lightless cavern from their newly made tunnel.

Several of the group recognized them: Umber Hulks. These were bigger than any specimen they had seen before, adorned with long chitinous horns along their heads.

[[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] turned from his potentially infected companions and cast a spell of deep slumber over the chamber. Two of the massive beasts resisted, but the third stumbled and crashed to the floor, incapacitated.

[[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] spun — and out of the darkness rushed one of the Hulks. Unaware of their powerful gaze, he looked into its glittering compound eyes and found himself lost in them, stunned.

The Hulk scooped the monk up in its jaws and turned to take its prey back to its nest. Just as it reached the chamber's edge, a blast of scintillating light hit it, charming the creature and rendering it still. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] was struck as well, and both were incapacitated.

One of the other Hulks rushed toward [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]], but found itself surrounded. Between [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]], [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]], [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]], and [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]], they seriously injured the monster in moments as it charged the smaller creatures, expecting easy prey.

Mr. Pickles rushed over to [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] and slapped the halfling awake. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] extracted himself from the creature's mandibles and joined the fray, laying into the Hulk attacking his friends and reaching into it with his ki. What he sensed was how incredibly tough these creatures were — and that his ability to tear information from a spirit might cause spiritual damage in the process.

The slumbering Hulk stirred and picked itself up just as the one battling the group was pushed back by a volley of eldritch blasts.

Seeing an opening, the Hulk rushed forward to crush [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] against the wall — and slammed head-first into a block of solid ice the warlock had summoned to protect himself.

Seeing the sleep had failed, [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] changed tactics and polymorphed one of the Hulks into a Myconid Sprout. The massive eight-foot beetle-like creature suddenly shrank into a two-foot bipedal mushroom.

The group focused fire on the one remaining Hulk and made short work of it. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] rushed in and jammed [[eclipse|Eclipse]] into its neck armor, severing its head from its shoulders.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] watched through the ice block as the polymorphed Hulk continued to attack — repeatedly nudging and bumping into [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]], bruising her slightly. As the ice tomb dissolved, the little Myconid tried to run into [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]], bumped him, and fell over. Having had quite enough, the warlock picked it up, opened his bag of devouring, and placed it inside.

With only the originally slumbering Hulk remaining, [[doc|Doc]] knocked it out of its incapacitated state. It charged the little gnome and smashed him into the wall. The less than intelligent creature found itself immediately surrounded.

[[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] unfurled his bag of swords, called on his magical brooch, and ten blades leapt to life and flew in slashing. Already surrounded by the group and now assaulted by a storm of steel, the final Hulk was ripped apart in short order.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] spun on the group and began to berate them for breaking one of her rules.

The others explained: the fungal corpse had infected [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] and [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]. They were trying to fix it.

Incredulous, [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] asked if they had touched the corpse.

Turns out, they had.

Sighing, [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] watched as [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] took out a small hammer to crush a diamond. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] took the diamond dust and sprinkled it over the dwarf, casting a Greater Restoration. [[doc|Doc]], meanwhile, placed his paws over [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]]'s eyes and a spray of spring water erupted from them, leaving two leaves over his eyes that fluttered off as butterflies.

They attempted to make sense of the spores and the fungal growth, but arrived at little — only that it was not native to the [[underdark|Underdark]].

Investigating the Hulk corpses, [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]], [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]], and [[doc|Doc]] noticed dozens of massive tumors beneath the carapace. These had not been healthy creatures. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] had never seen anything like it — not the tumors, not mind-altering spores erupting from a body. Neither phenomenon was something she knew.

They harvested a fair amount of undamaged carapace and hide and stashed it in the bag of holding.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] estimated they had until morning before anything came to investigate the smell of the corpses. [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] constructed his Tiny Hut — a perfectly black hemisphere of opaque force — and walked inside.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] placed trip wires. [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] set an Alarm around one of the tunnels. Satisfied, everyone retired into the warmth.

## The Night

With thousands of tons of rock overhead, in absolute darkness and eerie silence, the group began to talk. Worried about the trip ahead, [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] asked why they were going to [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]].

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] responded that her son deserved a better life than living there.

After a few minutes, they all noticed [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] gesticulating wildly but in complete silence. They questioned him.

He finally broke, hissing that the dome did not block noise from escaping — and they had been chatting away for ten minutes.

The group immediately hushed. They broke out rations, ate a meal, and bedded down. No one rested easily.

[[doc|Doc]], [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]], [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]], and [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] were all visited by nightmares and slept fitfully. They did not remember the subject of the dreams, only that they awoke feeling uneasy.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] reported an uneventful night, and [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] had expected nothing less. The dome faded shortly after everyone awoke. They packed their camp and pressed on.

## The Second Day

The group continued on in silence and darkness.

Hours stretched uneventfully, [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] taking the lead, then swapping with [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]]. At one point [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] went ahead to scout a path through a slippery tunnel so the group could follow easily.

Eventually the strangeness of the [[underdark|Underdark]] asserted itself. Descending a tunnel, [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] reeled back, her lungs suddenly burning. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] hauled back on her cape, pulling her from an invisible pocket of chlorine gas.

The group gathered just before the descent into the gas and came up with a plan. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] and [[doc|Doc]] were immune to its effects — the monk's strange psionics and the druid's control over nature allowing them to neutralize it as they breathed. [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] and [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]] put on their gas masks from [[neverwinter|Neverwinter]].

They spent the next two hours ferrying everyone through the gas-filled chambers, two at a time with the masks passed back each time. When the last of them emerged on the other side, the group continued without stopping.

The smooth limestone deposits gave the [[underdark|Underdark]] an oddly amorphous look, the walls flowing into each other without edges. The Upperdark was one of the more desolate environments — devoid of the great mushroom forests found deeper in.

After several more hours, the tunnels widened into a long thin cavern. Up one wall, [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] spotted the small abodes of Svirfneblin — Deep Gnomes. The group moved in cautiously. A trap lay sprung nearby, long ago, and the whole cavern had not been touched in some time.

No Svirfneblin could be seen. Nothing living was present.

Despite [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]]' misgivings, [[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] and [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] moved in to investigate the abodes. They were dusty and devoid of anything of value. The only sign that these had once been inhabited came when [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] fished a small bone from under a pile of refuse. The bone of a Gnome.

Something had happened here weeks or months ago. The group pressed on.

A few hours later, [[doc|Doc]] was shaken from his thoughts by his rumbling stomach. He started keeping an eye out for something to scavenge. Nothing showed itself for several more hours of travel, until his ears picked up a distant hissing and popping.

He called to the others to wait and hopped down a side tunnel. The group followed slowly.

[[doc|Doc]] came around a bend to find the tunnel covered in small barnacle-like creatures. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] identified them as Bore-Limpets, and mentioned they were quite delicious. Avoiding the acid the small creatures secreted, [[doc|Doc]], [[jack-mayer-johnson-mathews|JmJm]], and [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] gathered a large sack of them, intent on boiling them at the next rest.

The group doubled back to a previous cubby and made camp.

## The Nightmares

They set up, cooked the limpets, and ate in quiet conversation.

[[feanon-serinde|Fëanon]] turned to his spellbooks but found it easier to meditate, working through spell equations with Theodemir instead. After a couple of hours, they worked something out — he learned the spell: Manipulate Mass.

Everyone sleeping was visited by nightmares again.

[[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] awoke sweating, feeling a wrongness permeating the air around him. He remembered the last moments of his dream: massive coiling tentacles ripping him apart as two fanged maws tore at his body.

He lay in the dark with the image, and did not go back to sleep.
