Five days until Lady Avelyere of the [[netherese|Netherese]] arrived. Three weeks until the ball in [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]].

[[feanon-serinde|Feanon]] spent the entire night working with his cabal of mages to excise the necromantic undercurrent from [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion's]] ritual spell. The work was sloppy, but it got the job done — the soul-harvesting component was stripped out while preserving the Demon Lord summoning.

## Tark's Dream

[[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] dreamed of his parents. He saw them leaving their home among the Ironwood Trees of [[vaasa|Vaasa]] in the Bloodstone Lands, riding a strange crystalline beast and carrying saddlebags laden with something he could not identify. The vision lingered — warm, distant, and achingly familiar.

## Preparations

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] debated getting Yakuza-style tattoos as a disguise for [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]], then learned he could enter the city without being enslaved — so long as he followed the rules and showed no worship of any god. He had issues with this.

The group headed to [[blingdenstone|Blingdenstone]], stopped by an alchemist there, and traded for a potion of Earthglide. They then struck out from the Deep Gnome settlement into the wilds of the [[underdark|Underdark]], travelling until exhausted.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] summoned [[gravenhollow|Gravenhollow]], and the next day the group entered the library to scour the chambers of Past and Present. [[feanon-serinde|Feanon]] traced the history of [[vizerion-devir|Vizerion's]] ritual spell and discovered the [[drow-faction|Drow]] archmage had begun crafting it long ago — using his time disguised as Elias in [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]] to further the Distortion magic at its core. [[feanon-serinde|Feanon]] believed he had learned enough to continue refining the ritual, diverging it further from the ascension spell it truly was.

[[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] looked in on [[zuggtmoy|Zuggtmoy]] in the Present, seeing the Fungal Queen at the centre of a great mass of fungus in Neverlight Grove — being attended to by numerous demons, clearly preparing for something.

## The Webway

That night, [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] spotted a well-armed patrol of Baenre soldiers passing nearby. They did not detect her. She informed the others in the morning, and the group continued deeper into the [[underdark|Underdark]], reaching the Webway — the vast mass of spider nests surrounding the [[drow-faction|Drow]] city.

[[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] began collecting venomous spiders as they went, picking up four Sulphurwidows. The group managed to push through the Webway and emerge on the other side before needing to rest.

As they settled in, [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] heard motion down a tunnel and moved to investigate, coming across a filthy, reeking human man — an escaped slave, by the look of him. [[tarkathee-daru|Tark]] knocked the stumbling man out and carried him back. [[aliyah-faen-tlabbar|Patches]] woke him to talk, but the man panicked so badly she knocked him out again.

The next morning, they teleported the man back to the [[selunia|Barony]] and continued on toward [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]].
