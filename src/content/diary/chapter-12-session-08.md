## The Blasted Wastes

The ship anchored off a towering two-hundred-foot cliff face. Rather than attempt the sheer rock, Captain Losthook edged the Fiery Flower along the coast until they found a rockslide that formed a steep but manageable ramp. The party rowed ashore in the dinghy, took their supplies, and began the inland trek — leaving the ship moored for at least four days.

The ascent was hard going. [[Cirrus Al-Kayan|Cirrus]] needed to be carried over the final stretch, [[Snorbul]] obliging without complaint. At the cliff's summit, the alien coastline stretched before them — green sky reflected in a dark ocean, and inland nothing but badlands. Red dirt and arid rock as far as the eye could see, save for a gleaming stone edifice in the distance crowned by swirling dark clouds. Purple lightning flickered in those clouds — wild-magic surges, concentrated around the [[Temple of Carthus|Temple of Karthus]].

[[Feanon Serinde|Feanon]] theorized that the temple had been the epicentre of planar-shatteringly powerful magic. Everything around it was dead and blasted outward, as if from a great explosion.

A hunting pack of feathered raptors forced a brief diversion — [[Tarkathee Daru|Tark]] lured half the pack away while the rest of the group killed the remainder, then led his pursuers back into the trap. They harvested meat, feathers, and bone quickly and pressed on.

## The Temple

They arrived hours later. A huge domed structure of ancient [[Saurians|Saurian]] design stood alone in a sandy field — white marble, angular pillars, gold filigree. Its hexagonal sides were open to the elements, revealing massive primordial monoliths cut from solid stone and an intricate dais at the centre. The architecture would have been beautiful if not for the piles of bones, webs of blight, and black spores drifting through the corrupted air.

Feanon felt psionic energy emanating from the very land itself.

[[Liara]] pointed to the bones. No one had succeeded in a pilgrimage in centuries. These were the remains of those who had tried.

The [[Faerun Defense Force]] walked toward the temple anyway.

## The Lost

As they ascended the steps, lightning split the air. Eight blasts of wild magic tore reality open, and rifts to the [[Shadowfell]] of this world gaped wide. Eight wailing creatures poured through.

The party formed a defence at the temple doors. The creatures — the Lost — closed fast and proved deadly, unleashing psychic terror that scattered several fighters before they could master themselves. Cirrus, convinced the shadow creatures could not enter sacred ground, stepped deeper inside. One of the Lost followed him in and attacked. [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] rushed to his rescue.

Cirrus stumbled onto the central dais — and vanished in a flash of light.

Liara moved to follow. Feanon cast a powerful spell but triggered a wild-magic surge. He couldn't tell what it had done until he smelled something foul: their rations had spoiled.

The tide turned when Snorbul flanked the remaining Lost and hacked two apart with a roar. The group crushed the survivors and stood panting in the spore-ridden temple, eyes settling on the dais.

[[Horatio Von Bumidere|Buumi]] stepped forward. A flash — and he was gone.

In the darkness between, a voice asked: *"What do you seek at this font of power? Authority and Control? Justice and Truth? Harmony and Balance? Wealth and Security? Change and Reform? Or Knowledge and Insight?"*

*"Who is most important in your life that embodies this trait?"*

Buumi's mind settled without hesitation on Harmony and Balance — and he thought of his mother.
