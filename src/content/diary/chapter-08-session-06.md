## Waking up and Wind Walking

The night was uneventful, and for the first time, Tark did not have nightmares. Instead, he dreamed.

In the dream, [[kenshi|Kenshi]] visited him in the monastery and they talked. Tark told [[kenshi|Kenshi]] of their quest, their destination and their location in the [[underdark|Underdark]] as well as the strange planar cracks.

[[kenshi|Kenshi]] was most concerned and said he would try and let people know, but his own knowledge of the [[underdark|Underdark]] was very limited.

Tark mentioned the [[darklake|Darklake]] and their destination of [[gracksuluth|Gracksuluth]] and [[kenshi|Kenshi]] said he only had a passing knowledge of the names but would see who he could talk to about the situation.

They spent the remainder of the dream training and sparring, practicing the technique that [[kenshi|Kenshi]] showed Tark last time: Extract Aspects.

The group awakened, half in the glorious mansion, the rest outside in the Tiny Hut.

Inside the magical mansion, Fey left his chambers, clad in silks, and walked to the balcony descending down to the lavish dining area and the massive banquet set up there.

Doc B uncurled from his sleeping position and looked over just in time to get a glimpse inside Fey's chamber, the place filled with duplicates of the elven Wizard Lemerlais — and JmJm sneaking from the room.

[[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] stirred, hacked up a lugie and then downed his morning shot of mama Rockseeker's Gutbuster Brew.

JmJm slunk from the shadows, intrigued at the drink and asked for a taste.

[[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] veritably threw it at JmJm's face and the Bard swore, and then yelled at the Dwarf for being so uncouth.

Doc headed over to the Banquet, but had a hard time reaching the table and accidentally pulled the tablecloth, and food, onto the floor when one of his claws got caught.

Fey walked over, snapped his fingers and the table was re-arranged, now slightly lower to help out Doc.

Patches and [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] both entered the mansion remarking that they had not been bothered by any other Mind Flayers and they were ready to head out for the day.

They swung by the buffet, though [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] was hesitant worried about biting his tongue with his new teeth, but dug in carefully.

Deciding to head out, Fey told them he would end the spell in 10 minutes and everyone should get their things.

Outside, Tark waited, invisible by the bags of holding.

After several minutes, the rest of the group simply appeared next to him as the Magnificent Mansion spell ended.

Patches whispered "Tark?"

The Halfling responded and dropped the invisibility.

Doc B set out a pattern of flower petals and began to cast Wind Walk. As it ended, a great gust of wind picked up the petals and swept up the group, carrying them down the winding tunnels of the [[underdark|Underdark]].

Speeding along, the group made amazing speed.

As they left the area from the night before, they saw the source of the commotion that put them on alert: a Goblin village, deserted. Several corpses lay about, including one or two Intellect Devourers.

The group continued on and many hours later sped past a great stone pillar, scored and torn by massive claws.

Pressing on, they noticed scat piles from something big, filled with the bones of large creatures and humanoids, and they noticed little sparks of electricity and static in the darkness, flickering around the tunnels.

The group pressed on, unwilling to investigate the area, and after a half hour they cleared the area of static.

As the day wore on, they passed through a large section of insect hive, each creature the size of a thumb with a slightly glowing abdomen. The group pressed on.

Just as they began to see Duergar mile markers, the group entered a section of Wild Magic and the Wind Walk spell destabilized and ended prematurely.

The group was deposited at speed and everyone came to a rushing halt, stumbling slightly.

## Faerzress

Fey'Anon looked around and realized that they were in an area suffused with Wild Magic; he tried to look for the source but the area was irradiated, making it hard to gauge direction.

[[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] and Patches both spotted it quickly though — a glint of crystal in the cavern walls — and they headed over to confirm: Faerzress crystals. Crystallized magic.

JmJm had a hard time dealing with the tunnels and decided to storm off.

Patches dropped a globe of darkness over the half elf, who let out a little yelp and walked into the cavern wall.

The rest of the group headed down the tunnel a ways, not wanting to be around the crystals as [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] had decided to try and harvest one.

The darkness on JmJm ended and he watched curiously as [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] went in with a small hammer and pick and hit the stone.

Sparks flew, and to JmJm it looked like the wall simply crumbled and disgorged a large crystal chunk.

[[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] however got a much different view, as after the first hit time simply stopped.

Looking around, [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] saw the sparks mid flight and JmJm mid sentence. He shrugged and went back to freeing the crystal, hitting the right areas to free it quickly.

Time resumed and JmJm let out a gasp as every hit from [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] took effect simultaneously.

[[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] bent down and scooped up the crystal in a gloved hand and then turned to head off toward the group.

JmJm rushed to follow.

Back at the group, [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] showed them the Faerzress crystal, smoke wafting up slightly from his gloved hand where it touched the thing.

[[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] covered it in oil and then wrapped it in heavy leathers until he could craft, or buy, a lead box to properly shield the crystal.

The group continued on and after a few miles, Patches got her bearings and realized they were close to [[the-crossroads|the Crossroads]], a nexus of well travelled tunnels that led to the [[darklake|Darklake]] and [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]] among other places.

Doc stopped, seeing a cluster of small Fireweed, and decided to ask them some questions.

Communing with nature, Doc was told that [[the-crossroads|the Crossroads]] they approached was occupied by Orcs and Giant Spiders and that Fiends had passed through recently.

The group discussed what to do and decided that if they had to say who they were to any people they came across, they should be honest, more or less.

The group continued on, and when they were about 30 minutes from the cavern, Tark wrapped gloom around them with Pass without Trace.

## The Crossroads

They stopped at the entrance to a large cavern, easily two hundred feet across and ascending up beyond sight.

Patches pulled out her telescope and peered into the cavern, the place dimly lit by bio-luminescence. She saw movement near the far end of the cavern, but it was indistinct.

Tark went invisible and shadowstepped into the cavern, then looking to the ceiling, shadowstepped to a stalactite and sniffed. He smelled the sweaty Orcs and something else, some kind of sweet yet earthy perfume.

He returned to the group and they headed into the cavern, ready for a fight.

Just as the group got past the midpoint, a roaring filled the cavern and the Orcs sprang their attack, rushing the group. They looked confused and disoriented though, searching for targets.

Fey reached out to cast a Hypnotic Pattern but was Counterspelled. Fey turned to the others in a panic — Orcs don't Counterspell.

Tark, Patches and Mr Pickles rushed into melee, making short work of the Orcs they were up against.

[[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] started blasting away and [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] unleashed a Sickening Radiance, burning half of the Orcs to ash.

The distraction worked perfectly.

As the group engaged and slaughtered the Orcs, the Drow waiting above in the darkness dropped.

The two priestesses released their Hold Person spells, freezing JmJm, Fey, [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] and Mr. Pickles while the others managed to shake it off.

A Drow wizard dropped to the stone and tried to Banish Tark, but the monk managed to resist the spell, still not sure what was happening as he faced off against the last few goblins in a side tunnel.

Out in the main cavern, the situation was dire as no fewer than sixteen Drow assaulted the group, focusing on the held party members first.

Dozens of attacks rained in, all pommel strikes and punches as the Drow subdued the group, not killing them.

Tark heard a melodic female voice call out to "take them alive."

Realizing what was happening, Tark shadowstepped away and went invisible, unwilling to chance the Artifacts falling into the hands of the Drow. He looked back and saw [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]], Fey and JmJm downed quickly, surrounded by foes and then scooped up and levitated up into the gloom of the ceiling.

Patches grabbed Doc and dashed away, seeing dozens of enemies behind her but fewer ahead, as well as the tantalizing prize of a Drow Priestess.

One of the priestesses summoned an insect swarm of thousands of spiders to fall upon the rogue and druid just as a trio of Drow warriors pulled their hand crossbows and released a volley of poisoned darts into Patches.

She parried the first bolt but took two more. Resisting the first dose of poison, the second took drastic effect and she dropped instantly unconscious.

Doc wailed, not sure what to do, and a massive eruption of earth cascaded out from the angry druid, pulverizing every remaining orc in the area and slamming two Drow, including the Wizard.

The wizard fell to the ground coughing blood but reached out and Banished Doc.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] awoke from the poison dart as the Orc carrying him was destroyed by Doc. He enacted his ice tomb and watched.

The fight was over. The group had lost.

Tark watched invisible, waiting for an opportunity.

One of the priestesses approached the ice tomb holding [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]].

Wearing fine form-fitting mesh armor and flowing robes, layered with a Piwafwi cloak and a full helmet shaped into the likeness of a spider head, complete with mandibles, the priestess had a small shield on one arm, a mace on her belt, a writhing mass of snakes on the other hip and a strange set of rubbery looking tendrils wrapped around her mid section. A single braid of stark white hair fell down her back and was the only thing to show her as Drow as no skin was showing.

Leaning in, she tapped a long claw against the ice and in perfect common, told [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] to "Surrender or Die." The helmet had gem-like eyes and they glittered in the dim light of the cavern.

The tomb ended abruptly and [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] raised his hands in supplication — and then vanished.

Farstepping away and then sprinting down a side tunnel.

Tark saw four of the Drow go in pursuit, but they returned within seconds saying that the strange spellcaster continued to teleport away, outdistancing them instantly.

The two priestesses and the wizard began to talk quietly as the wizard prepared a teleportation circle.

Just as he finished the circle, the levitating warriors dropped from the ceiling with their captives, vanishing in a flash. The other nearby warriors stepped in and vanished as well, leaving the five surviving Orcs, the Drow Wizard, the two Priestesses and another four Drow warriors.

Tark, invisible and using his staff to fly, got in close and listened.

He heard the quiet conversation in Drow, and understood them say that the information they received was accurate, and that it was a powerful group which, had they not attacked with their entire force, they doubted they could have taken. Tark heard a little confusion in the conversation as they wondered just who they had captured, as those prisoners most certainly were not traders or unprepared surface dwellers.

The wizard finished a second teleportation circle and the remaining Drow and Orcs stepped through, vanishing.

Tark did not follow but instead darted off to chase down [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]].

Following the tunnel, Tark darted through them for an hour, praying to find his companion.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] meanwhile had stopped, breathing heavily in a small chamber. Looking around, he saw a crack in the wall and went to hide in it, summoning the Tiny Hut and thinking furiously. They needed help.

Pulling out the small metal cage with a light glowing inside, [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] asked the soul of the Orc where his friends were taken: [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]]. The Warlock found out the direction, distance and general layout of the cavern — Orcs beneath and the Drow living up on the ceiling.

He thanked the Orc and then consumed it.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] looked up when he heard a tapping on the outside of the dome and Tark dropped his invisibility.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] let Tark inside the dome and they discussed the next step: they needed help and they needed to rescue their friends.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] summoned his tiny messenger and quickly penned a letter, telling Buumi of the situation and that he needed him asleep as soon as possible.

He received a letter in reply and found out that Megno was at the Barony with Buumi.

[[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] waited twenty minutes and then enacted a Dreaming, contacting Buumi and showing him their location and further clarifying the situation.

Buumi was shocked, concerned and adamant that they save their friends. Buumi had [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] end the dream and do another to show Megno the teleport location.

An hour later, a flash of light and crack of thunder and Megno and Buumi were standing in the same cavern looking at [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]] and Tark.

Tark rushed over to hug his friend and they embraced.

They began to formulate a plan.

Megno cast a Sending to Fey'Anon telling him they were on the way. They got a short reply that Fey was blindfolded, restrained and alone currently.

Megno switched and Sent to Patches, telling her a rescue was coming and that it was about 20 hours away.

Patches responded: they would be ready.

## Velkynvelve

One hour or so earlier:

Patches awakened, a large clawed hand rolling her over as she lay against the floor.

Looking around, she saw that she was in a large metal cage — a dome with web-like bars, and no apparent door.

The large cavern was lit dimly by purple faerie fire lights covering the nearby stalactites. Below the large ledge that the cage rested on, was a carpet of thick spider-webs, the light giving them an almost luminous quality. At the far end of the stalactite compound, bridges of spider silk connecting each spire, a waterfall roared down to the cavern floor several hundred feet below.

Patches noted the hand on her and the creature staring down at her, its large unblinking fish eyes looking out from a humanoid, ridiculous fish body — a Kuo-Toa.

He introduced himself as [[shuushar|Shuushar]].

Behind the Kuo-Toa, a little walking mushroom, a Myconid Sprout, peeked out inquisitively.

[[shuushar|Shuushar]] introduced the Myconid as Toadstool.

The creature told her to remain calm in a gurgling watery voice.

Patches ignored the thing and looked around, seeing a number of people she didn't recognize as well as the rest of her captured party.

Fey was missing though.

In the cell with the group were a set of young deep gnome twins, a large grey-skinned Orc man with wrinkles around his eyes and salt and pepper hair, the fish-like blue and green Kuo-Toa in brown rags, a sullen-looking Drow male in tattered Drow garb, and a small brown Myconid Sprout with two stumpy little legs and a crazed-looking Derro in a red robe playing with rocks in a corner.

The two gnomes stared at Doc B until he noticed and headed over.

They talked in a strange dialect of Gnomish but they could understand one another.

Doc B found out their village was raided and they ran for it, being captured by the Drow.

The tips of their ears had been docked.

They introduced themselves as [[topsy-and-turvy|Topsy and Turvy]].

Meanwhile, [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] had awoken and sauntered over to the Drow imprisoned with them.

He introduced himself and then promptly started beating the Elf, demanding to know where they were.

The Drow, Sarith, cried out in pain and shock and blurted out the name of the place: [[velkynvelve|Velkynvelve]].

Mr Pickles went up to [[shuushar|Shuushar]] and asked for a Shiv. [[shuushar|Shuushar]] had none but broke off a piece of a claw to give to the little goblin.

JmJm went up to the bars and started to yell, demanding to be seen by the boss.

Out of the darkness, a hand-crossbow bolt struck him in the shoulder and he fell to the ground bleeding.

The large Orc walked over to JmJm and helped him to his feet and introduced himself as [[ront|Ront]].

He liked the group's attitude, arguing and fighting rather than cowering before the Drow.

JmJm smiled and pulled the dart from his shoulder, stowing the shiv away for later.

He turned back to the bars and yelled again. A figure approached the bars from the outside and a Drow came into view.

A male Drow, badly burned across the face and with one hand missing two fingers — the old wounds were not pretty.

JmJm cried out in disgust and recoiled a little. He then proceeded to mock the Drow for his appearance and many of the other group members inside joined in, laughing.

The Drow looked flustered and lashed out, stabbing JmJm in the ribs with a small dagger, spitting on him, then stormed away, furious.

[[ront|Ront]] chuckled and turned to see a commotion at the back of the cage.

Sarith had grown a backbone and turned on [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]], slugging the Dwarf across the face.

[[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] blew out a snot rocket of blood and then went to strike the Drow — until Mr. Pickles leapt on his back stabbing away with a sharp splinter of talon.

Sarith screamed and fell to the ground bleeding badly.

A sharp voice called out "Enough" and a Drow priestess approached out of the darkness, flanked by the Drow Wizard — still bloody — from the battle.

She waved her hand and a flash of light washed over the bleeding Sarith. His wounds scabbed over and he stopped bleeding, eyes fluttering open.

Patches noted her armor and rank and realized this was the Priestess who had led the capture force.

Still outfitted for battle, the priestess had her snake-headed whip in hand, the snakes coiling around her forearm.

The priestess approached the bars where JmJm stood and looked him over, amused. JmJm crooned and began to lay on the charm, not able to resist a pretty face.

Patches hissed at JmJm to watch his step.

The prisoners all turned their attention to the Priestess, who said her name was [[ilvara-mizzrym|Ilvara Mizzrym]] and that they were all prisoners and slaves of [[menzoberranzan|Menzoberranzan]].

She turned to leave and sauntered off with the silent wizard. As she went, [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] said "Bitch..." and she spun, lashing out with a hand as a pillar of flame erupted above the Dwarf.

Diving to the side, [[gundren-rockseeker|Gundren]] was still badly burned and fell unconscious. Doc B rushed to help as [[ilvara-mizzrym|Ilvara]] spun, laughing, and continued to walk away.

Patches watched her go, happy not to have been noticed.

She was broken from her thoughts by a magical message from Megno Slatesunder.

He and Buumi had teleported to the [[underdark|Underdark]] and met up with Tark and [[cirrus-al-kayan|Cirrus]].

They were coming to rescue them in 20 hours.
