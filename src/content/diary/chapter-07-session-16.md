## The Return of Doc B

[[Doc]] B and the [[Leanansidhe]] stepped from a solid stone wall into a natural cavern tunnel — one end curving down into the [[Underdark]], the other climbing toward a faint orange glow. Lea pinned a holly leaf to the druid's coat and told him to prick his finger and call her name three times if he needed to speak with her. Then the Winter Noble vanished, dispersed by an unseen wind. Doc wailed after her that he only remembered her nickname.

Back in the arena, the group was still recovering from the devils. The clerics, paladins, [[Horatio von Bumidere]], [[Cirrus Al Kayan]], and [[Jack Mayer Johnson Mathews]] moved through the wounded with healing spells. JmJm's wild-magic-gifted truesight showed the cracks in the planes still lingering despite the Faerzress crystal's destruction.

Patches and [[Tarkathee Daru]] heard a familiar wailing echoing across the sulphurous arena and went to investigate. Down the far tunnel they found an old friend — a small humanoid cat in a leaf-scale coat, scimitar and walking stick in hand, his gnome hat standing tall. Doc B.

Tark rushed forward and hugged the catman. Patches greeted him with a nod. "How have you been?"

The group received Doc enthusiastically, though with suspicion. Doc explained that [[Demetrius]] had taken him to [[The Bog]] for training by the Fae. JmJm noted he didn't recognise the little gnome before him — and he saw a gnome, not a cat — though it smelled like Doc. Tark activated his blindfold and saw the same. All his instincts said it was still Doc. He trusted his gut.

Cirrus yelled about the army of devils and got blank looks. [[Fëanon Serinde]] nodded — they would investigate. As the group took the elevator up, [[Anaxtrasa]] was leaning against the wall above, asking about the commotion. JmJm winked at the blue dragon. She winked back as the elevator descended.

## The Bumidere Estate

Back in [[Neverwinter]], the group headed straight for the estate. [[Val Tengu]] raised an eyebrow at Doc but accepted the catman with a huff. [[Sera Von Bumidere]] asked Val about Georg and Marta — she hadn't seen them in two days. Val said they were taking time away in the guest house. Everyone headed to bed.

JmJm went to the [[Amnians]]' rooms with Fëanon in stealthy pursuit. The wizard waited an hour, then entered. JmJm was asleep. In the other bed lay two figures — [[Chani]] staring right at Fëanon as he came in. She shot him a questioning look. He shrugged, walked to the unconscious bard, and pulled out his spellbook. Chani rushed silently to grab his hand. Their eyes locked — and she dropped unconscious. Fëanon cast a Geas on JmJm: the bard was not to harm the group in any way.

Patches went skulking. Using Cirrus's loud prayers and terrible singing as cover, she picked the lock on Thessalt's room. The tumbler clicked, waking the halfling. She waited, ready to kick the door in — but silence. She slipped inside, threw a card from her [[Deck of Illusions]], and found an empty room with a warm bed. She felt watched but saw nothing. She flipped off the unseen Thessalt, relocked the door, broke the lock, and strung a tripwire for good measure.

## Tark's Nightmare

In the early hours, Tark woke cold and shivering, breath coming in plumes. The fire was dead. He opened the door to a dark hallway where a young halfling man stood twenty feet away — strikingly familiar, his throat cut. Tark's father, a red ribbon clutched in one trembling hand. The pale face pointed behind his son. Tark spun. [[Li Xian]] grabbed him by the throat and the world erupted in fire.

"To not repeat the errors of history," Mr. Shon's voice echoed as Tark jolted awake, drenched in sweat.

He found Mr. Shon's office and shadowstepped inside. Immaculately tidy — wardrobe, bookshelf, simple desk. Nothing out of the ordinary. Disturbed, he returned to bed.

## The Heroes' Feast

Come morning, Doc woke early and headed to the dining hall. Taking out a small bejewelled bowl, the druid cast a spell — liquid exploding into motes of diamond dust — and the table filled with a Heroes' Feast. The magnificent smell permeated the manor, dragging everyone from their beds one by one.

JmJm woke to find Chani snoring beside his bed. [[Gneiss]] cleared his throat from across the room with a threat: "Not again, Whistler." The bard sheepishly followed his nose to breakfast.

Patches swung by Val's office — Thessalt had left earlier that morning. She told Val what happened the night before and asked him to lie to the halfling if he returned, telling him the Drow was trying to kill him. Val agreed.

The household assembled. The children and Amnians filtered in — Abdul, [[S'Olaa]], Leigham, Gneiss, and Chani. Abdul declined the food but chewed something from a pouch on his hip. S'Olaa took fruit juice, no alcohol.

Breakfast was consumed. The group and the Amnians began preparing to head to [[Helm's Hold]] for the Council. It was ten in the morning. The meeting began at one.
