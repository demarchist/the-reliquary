In 1385 DR, [[Mystra]] dies and the Weave unravels. The event the histories call the Spellplague reshapes the fundamental nature of magic across Toril. For [[Fëanon Sërindë]], standing in [[Silverymoon]], it is the afternoon when the Seldarine's wards on [[Evermeet]] — supposedly eternal, maintained by gods who supposedly cared — fail. The island is pulled into the Feywild. Contact is severed. The scholarly exchange becomes an exile, and the implicit promise to return becomes a door that no longer exists. It is the first crack in Fëanon's faith, and it never heals.
