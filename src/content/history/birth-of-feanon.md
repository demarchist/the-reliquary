In the year 1250 DR, [[Fëanon Sërindë]] is born in [[Leuthilspar]], the crystal-spired capital of [[Evermeet]]. He grows up inside the architecture of divine certainty — the Seldarine's wards are not theology but physical reality, as observable as gravity. The young elf shows extraordinary aptitude for the arcane, drawing the attention of scholars across the island.
