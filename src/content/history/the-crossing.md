Around 1370 DR, [[Fëanon Sërindë]] departs [[Evermeet]] for [[Silverymoon]]'s Lady's College on a formal scholarly exchange. The transition from Leuthilspar's crystalline perfection to a sprawling, multi-racial academic city is jarring — half the library is in languages he has never encountered. He adapts, because he is brilliant enough to adapt to anything, but the implicit promise to return home remains unspoken between him and the colleagues he left behind. In Silverymoon he will eventually find [[Theodemir]] and the [[Conclave of Silverymoon|Conclave]], and the promise will be broken by forces beyond anyone's control.
