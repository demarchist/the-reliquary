Around 1442 DR, [[Theodemir]] sacrifices himself to forge the [[Eye of Aren]] and keep the Witch King Zhengyi unconscious. His students — [[Fëanon Sërindë]], [[Megno Slatesunder]], [[Dean Sejuk|Sajuuk Stomru]], [[Orla Pyntle]], [[Sala Shara]], and [[Elion Loberos]] — are left holding an artifact of staggering power with no master to guide them. When the [[Conclave of Silverymoon]] tears itself apart over the Eye's fate, the acolytes conspire to steal it and entrust it to [[Nostalgia for Infinity]], the ancient Illithid collective beneath [[Ladypeak]], surrendering their memories of the conspiracy in exchange. The Elder Brain honours the deal — then betrays them, seizing Fëanon for enthrallment while the others flee. The process fails catastrophically. Fëanon's shattered magical aspect burns its way out of the hive alone. The psychic link forged during the botched enthrallment is never severed. The whispers will last fifty years.
