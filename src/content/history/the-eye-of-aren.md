Around 1442 DR, [[Theodemir]] sacrifices himself to forge the [[Eye of Aren]] and keep the Witch King Zhengyi unconscious. His five students — [[Fëanon Sërindë]], [[Megno Slatesunder]], [[Dean Sejuk|Sajuuk Stomru]], [[Orla Pyntle]], [[Sala Shara]], and [[Elion Loberos]] — are left holding an artifact of staggering power with no master to guide them. It is Fëanon who proposes the solution: entrust the Eye to [[Nostalgia for Infinity]], the ancient Illithid collective beneath [[Ladypeak]]. The Elder Brain betrays them. The probe shatters Fëanon's psyche, trapping a fragment of his magical essence within the psionic web. His fellow acolytes drag him from the collapsing chamber more dead than alive. The whispers will last fifty years.
