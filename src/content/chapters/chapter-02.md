Tournament sideshows, a guild heist, and a martial contest were interrupted when Bregan D'aerthe drugged the party and led them into the Chasm — a descent into a Netherese celestial vault teeming with moral trials and ancient dangers. At its heart, the golden lich Kangaxx revealed himself as a servant of Orcus, shattered an obelisk containing a shard of the Last Word, and set a chase in motion that ended on a psionically-summoned ship in a raging storm."
